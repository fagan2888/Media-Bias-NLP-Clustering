Sioux Falls, SD

People who like audiobooks now have another option available at the Siouxland Libraries.

OneClickdigital is an app that offers titles that may not be anywhere else. There are over 750 titles available to search on the app for Android and Apple devices, Kindle HD and Nook 7 tablets.

Titles can be check out for 21 days with a library card and can avoid late fees because of the automatic return. Up to ten titles can be checked out at once and holds can be placed on an additional five.

Digital downloads are also available through Siouxland Libraries via OverDrive, Hoopla and Zinio.

For more information, check out the Siouxland Libraries website.
