Wichita police are searching for a thief who punched a woman in the face and stole her brand new car.

It happened around 6:25 a.m. Saturday in the 1900 block of South Greenwood, near Mt. Vernon and Hydraulic. Police said the 27-year-old woman was outside when an unknown suspect approached, punched her in the face and took off in her 2016 Hyundai Elantra.

The woman was not seriously hurt.

Police did not give a description of the suspect, but described the Hyundai Elantra as gold in color with a Kansas tag of 830 HZF.

Anyone with information is asked to call 911 or Crime Stoppers at (316) 267-2111.
