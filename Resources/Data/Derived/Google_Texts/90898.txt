Westar Energy reports power has been restored in Butler County.

The outages topping 3,200 began late Friday morning and continued into the evening. They spanned across several areas near El Dorado, Augusta, Leon and Whitewater.

As of 8 p.m., the number of outages was down to 456 in the El Dorado area.

A Westar spokesperson said accumulating ice and strong winds are likely the cause of the outages.

KAKE News will keep tracking the outage and will post updates as soon as they become available.
