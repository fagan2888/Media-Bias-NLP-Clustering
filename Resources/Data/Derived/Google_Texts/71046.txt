Aurora Police officers are investigating a fatal shooting after a man was found dead in the parking lot of a sports bar Christmas Eve.

Officers received a report of a shooting at Rack’em Billiards and Sports Bar, located at 1919 S. Havana St., according to a news release from the Aurora Police Department.

Shortly after 10:45 p.m., officers found an man in the parking lot with what appeared to be a gunshot wound. The man was taken to a local hospital where he was later pronounced dead.

Witnesses told officers there was an argument in the parking lot between a few individuals. The argument spilled into the street and some witnesses told investigators they heard several gunshots.

The man walked back to the parking lot and collapsed.

Police are looking for more witnesses who have not been contacted yet. They are asking anyone who may have witnessed the incident to contact Detective Mark Yacano at 303-739-6090.

People with information who want to remain anonymous may call Crime Stoppers at 720-913-7867.

The man’s name will be released by the Arapahoe County Coroner’s office once the autopsy is completed.
