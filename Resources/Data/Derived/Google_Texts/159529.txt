SARASOTA — The Sarasota Scullers youth rowing team brought home winning medals from the Head of the South regatta in Augusta, Georgia this weekend. Seventy nine clubs from the southeast region competed.

— Chris Cail and Jeevesh Konoru competed in the men’s junior 2X and finished first. Cail also finished No. 2 in the men's youth lightweight 1x and Konoru finished No. 3.

— Jeffrey Thompson, Andy LeRoux, Kenny Hubbard and Marcel Griffioen finished first in the men's youth 4x.

— Christian Riccobono, Kimia Spikes, Caitlyn Klotz and Katie Beiler took third in the women's junior 4x.
