The victim of an armed robbery says he was giving his friend a ride when his friend robbed him.

Greenville Police say it happened the night of February 23 on the 300 block of Lindsay Drive in Greenville.

The victim told police that the suspect implied he had a weapon and robbed the victim of his phone and wallet.

On February 27, police arrested Christopher Gatlin, 22, and charged him with robbery with a dangerous weapon. The 22-year-old also had outstanding charges for possession of a firearm by a felon and possess/sell/buy a gun with altered serial number.

Gatlin is in the Pitt County Detention Center under a $300,000 bond.
