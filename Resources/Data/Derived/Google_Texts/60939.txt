One person was killed in a crash involving two vehicles on I-71 north in Henry County, according to a WHAS report.

The Kentucky State Police confirmed one person was killed in the crash, but its cause and the identity of the victim is unknown at this time.

TRIMARC tweeted around 8:30 a.m. about the closure alerting drivers that I-71 northbound would be closed for a few hours at I-265 due to "accident reconstruction."
