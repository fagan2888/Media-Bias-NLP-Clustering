On Wednesday’s edition of The Five, co-host Greg Gutfeld asked Chris Wallace point blank if the media was to blame for the ascendance of Donald Trump – now his party’s presumptive nominee.

“Did the Trump support drive the media? Or did the media drive the Trump support by diminishing real estate for the other candidates?” Gutfeld asked.

Wallace said it was a “vicious cycle” or “virtuous cycle” depending on one’s view of Trump. But ultimately, he said, the media were followers, rather than leaders, in this particular phenomenon. The reason news outlets put Trump on so much was “every time we did, it spiked the ratings. We were in a sense following what the ratings were.”

“We are in the news business,” he said.
