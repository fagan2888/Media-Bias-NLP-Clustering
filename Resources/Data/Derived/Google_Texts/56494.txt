A Bradenton man driving the wrong way on State Road 70 was killed when his car hit a semitrailer Friday night in Myakka City, invesitgators said.

The 54-year-old man has not been identified pending notification of family. Toxicology testing for alcohol was pending, the Florida Highway Patrol said.

The man was driving a 2009 Toyota FJ Cruiser east in the westbound lane approaching Wauchula Road at 8:55 p.m., when he approached the westbound Freightliner, investigators said.

The semitrailer, driven by Roman Perez Raya, 23, of Arcadia, swerved to the right to avoid a collision but the Toyota’s left side struck the truck’s left side, investigators said.

Raya and his passenger, Eduardo Lugo, 20, of Arcadia, were not injured.
