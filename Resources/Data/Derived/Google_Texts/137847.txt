Police are searching for the suspect involved in an early morning bank robbery.

The robbery happened just after 9 a.m. Saturday at the Fidelity Bank in the 3000 Block of East Harry.

Police say a man demanded money from a teller before taking off south on Clifton. Police say there was no weapon used in this robbery.

This is the second robbery at the bank on East Harry this month, the first robbery happening on November 3. The branch at 3100 S. Seneca was robbed the same day.

Police also say the description of the suspect involved in this robbery matches other previous robberies.

Saturday's robbery makes a total of four bank robberies in November. On Wednesday, a man robbed the Emprise Bank near Hydraulic and Pawnee.
