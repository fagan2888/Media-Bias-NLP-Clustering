How safe is your information once you lay it all out to find affordable health care?

Last September, federal officials admitted a hacker breached Healthcare.gov over the summer. An investigation determined malware was loaded onto a server but no personal data was taken.

Now, the Associated Press reports the government's health insurance website is sending consumers' personal data to private companies that specialize in advertising and marketing. All of this raises questions about how secure your information is when you sign up through the Affordable Care Act.
