Two people had a rollover wreck on the Sand Springs Expressway Sunday morning.

Oklahoma Highway Patrol and other emergency crews responded to the eastbound lanes of U.S. Highway 412 in Pawnee County after a rollover wreck.

Firefighters told News On 6 two people were in a vehicle that flipped near Bear's Glen early Sunday morning. The cause of the wreck is under investigation.

The driver was taken to the hospital where he's expected to be OK. A firefighter on the scene said neither person in the car had a driver's license.

We will update this story as more information is confirmed.
