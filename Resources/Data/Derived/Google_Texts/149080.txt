IRS Gives Good Directions On ACA Cadillac Tax

Law360, New York (March 25, 2015, 5:36 PM ET) -- On Feb. 23, the Internal Revenue Service released its first publication on the so-called Cadillac tax, a nondeductible 40 percent excise tax on high-cost health coverage that is scheduled to take effect in 2018. IRS Notice 2015-16 describes possible approaches for defining the coverage that is counted, determining the cost of that coverage and applying the annual dollar limits.
