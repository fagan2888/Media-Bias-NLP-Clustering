PLAINSBORO

POSSESSION OF UNDER 50-GRAMS OF MARIJUANIA, 1:23 p.m. Jan. 14. Police stopped a 2003 Mitsubishi Eclipse for a stop sign violation. During the stop, it was discovered the driver, Sean F. Tuohy, 46, Hillsborough, was in possession of under 50 grams of marijuana. Tuohy was placed under arrest and transported to police headquarters for processing. Tuohy was charged with possession of under 50-grams of marijuana and possession of drug paraphernalia. He was also issued traffic summonses for the possession of a controlled dangerous substance in a motor vehicle, and failure to stop. Tuohy was released on his own recognizance pending a future court date.

DUNELLEN

THEFT, 6:13 p.m. Dec. 19. A resident of the 800 block of Dunellen Avenue reported that someone gained access to the home while no one was there and numerous pieces of jewelry were stolen. Police canvassed the neighborhood, but no one reported having seen anything. Patrolman Joe Dudley investigated.

CRIMINAL MISCHIEF, 5:31 p.m. Jan. 13. A 47-year old resident of the 300 block of Fairview Ave reported that holiday lawn decorations were damaged by a golf club. There were no witnesses to this incident. Patrolman Brian Robbins investigated.
