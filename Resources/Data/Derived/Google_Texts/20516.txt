A Ronald McDonald House Charities Wichita family will light the 35-foot Christmas tree in Bradley Fair Plaza today.

The lighting ceremony will take place between 5:30 and 6 p.m. at Bradley Fair, near 21st and Rock Road.

KAKE's Susan Peters will also make an appearance at this event.

The Lights of Love is the annual tree-lighting event that is free and open to the public.
