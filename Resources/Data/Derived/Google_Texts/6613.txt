Emergency Responder: Medical and public health professionals to support medical needs during times of emergency. Must be 18 years or older to apply. The Medical Reserve Corps of Boulder County. Contact Nick Kell at 303-413-7532 or nkell@bouldercounty.org.

Foster Family Helper: Assist with foster family duties, take kids to the park, pick up groceries, make dinner, transport kids, etc. Background check required. Boulder County Department of Housing and Human Services. Contact Bonnie Lloyd at 303-441-1431 or blloyd@bouldercounty.org.

Awards Ceremony Manager: Run awards ceremonies for all of the league races during the season (late August-October) including managing volunteers, assisting with the ceremonies, preparing medals for the ceremonies, and communicating with the executive director. Colorado High School Cycling League. Contact Shawna Lanham at 720-272-9282 or volunteer@coloradomtb.org.

Symphony Volunteer: Assist with events and event management, staffing concerts and/or providing administrative support. Boulder Symphony. Contact Ruth Brand at 303-748-0024 or ruthbrandsoprano@gmail.com.

Animal Care: Help provide quality care for wild birds and mammals including food preparation, hand feeding baby wildlife, cleaning cages, and refreshing food and water. Greenwood Wildlife Rehabilitation Center. Contact Jenny Bryant at 303-823-8455 or jenny@greenwoodwildlife.org.

Translator: Provide housing case manager with Spanish translations. Translate face-to-face conversations, phone conversations and occasionally interpret written documents. Emergency Family Assistance Association. Contact Sue McCullough at 303-442-3042 or sue@efaa.org.

Community Ambassador: Communicate community needs and stay current on Red Cross programs and messaging. Help build, regain presence, and serve as the face of Red Cross at local community events. Recruit and refer prospective volunteers and volunteer groups. American Red Cross. Contact Mickey Ellenwood at 970-310-4511 or mickey.ellenwood@redcross.org.

Citizen Police Patrol (CVP) - City of Longmont: Patrol in pairs in city-provided vehicles, use police radios and cell phones while on duty and act as extra "eyes and ears" for patrol. CVPs have no law enforcement authority and do not carry weapons. CVP uniforms, equipment and extensive training provided. Contact Kay Armstrong at kay.armstrong@longmontcolorado.gov.

Dining Companion: Provide companionship for a resident living with dementia in our Memory Care neighborhood during meals. Frasier Meadows Retirement Community. Contact Molly Briggs at 720-562-4339 or mbriggs@frasiermeadows.org.

Venus de Miles: On Aug. 29, event volunteers needed to help as course marshals, rest stop captains, finish line motivators and festival management. Greenhouse Scholars. Contact Angie Vermillion at 303-459-5467 or venus@greenhousescholars.org.
