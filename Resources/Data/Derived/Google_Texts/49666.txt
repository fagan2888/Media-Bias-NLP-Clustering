Police are investigating a stabbing at a home in Norman, Oklahoma, early Monday morning.

According to police, at about 1:13 a.m., officers responded to a reported burglary in progress in the 1700 block of N. Crawford. When officers arrived, they found a female victim with multiple stab wounds.

The victim told officers that a male suspect entered the home and began attacking her with a knife. The victim then ran across the street and called for help.

The victim was transported to OU Medical Center for treatment. The male suspect's name is not being released at this time, police said.

The incident remains under investigation by the Norman Police Department's Criminal Investigations Division.
