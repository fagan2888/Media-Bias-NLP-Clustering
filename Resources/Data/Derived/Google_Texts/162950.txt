Biloxi, Mississippi (Ammoland) Paul Markel and the crew at Student of the Gun TV and Radio are pleased to welcome Lauer Custom Weaponry, the creators of DuraCoat custom finishing products, to Student of the Gun Radio. DuraCoat has long been a sponsor of SOTG TV and this expands their support of firearms education, enjoyment and enlightenment.

DuraCoat recently introduced “DuraBlue” a do-it-yourself firearms bluing finish, all in a single can. This product takes advantage of Lauer’s revolutionary Can-in-Can technology that allows any person to apply a professional looking finish to their favorite gun or accessory in their workshop or garage.
