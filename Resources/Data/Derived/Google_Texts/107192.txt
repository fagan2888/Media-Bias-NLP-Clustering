Sioux Falls, SD

A crash involving a Sioux Falls police car resulted in the arrest of a teenager on drug charges.

The crash happened just before 10 p.m. Saturday at 12th and Westport.

Investigators say the driver of a Nissan Pathfinder turned in front of the police cruiser, causing the crash.

The police officer was treated at the hospital and released.

The 17-year-old driver of the Pathfinder was treated at the scene.

Police say they found marijuana and a controlled substance inside the Pathfinder.

They arrested the teen for driving under the influence, numerous drug-related charges and driving without a license.
