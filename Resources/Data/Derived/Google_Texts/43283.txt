A pedestrian was struck by a vehicle and killed Saturday afternoon in west Oak Cliff, police said.

According to police, around 4:45 p.m., officers responded to an intersection at Hartsdale Drive and Fort Worth Avenue where they found a pedestrian who had been struck by a vehicle.

The pedestrian, who was not identified, died at the scene.

Police said no charges have been filed against the driver, who remained at the scene after the accident.
