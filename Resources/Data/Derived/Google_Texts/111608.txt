× Two bodies found in Lancaster Township home

LANCASTER COUNTY, PA (WPMT) Police are investigating the deaths of two people found inside a home in Lancaster Township. The home is in the 500 block of South West End Avenue. The county coroner says the bodies were discovered in the home earlier Thursday evening.

A neighbor said that a man in his mid 60’s to early 70’s lived in the home. Manheim Township Police spent hours at the scene gathering evidence.

An autopsy will determine the exact cause of death.
