PHILADELPHIA (AP) - Police say an 18-year-old woman was shot and killed on a west Philadelphia porch on a night that saw several other people shot or stabbed.

Officers found the unnamed victim on the porch with a gunshot wound to the chest at about 11:30 p.m. Saturday. She was pronounced dead just after midnight at Presbyterian Hospital. Several males were being sought.

Nearby, a 37-year-old man was shot four times in the leg and twice in the arm. He drove himself to the hospital and was listed in stable condition.

In north Philadelphia, police said a 52-year-old man was critically injured when he was stabbed three times.

Just before 8 a.m. Sunday, a 53-year-old man was found dead on a north Philadelphia street in what police are calling a suspicious death.
