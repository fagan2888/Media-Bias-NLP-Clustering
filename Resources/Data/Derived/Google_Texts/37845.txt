A Tucson man died Sunday night in a single-vehicle accident on North Country Club Road.

Tucson police identified the victim as 28-year-old Joseph Two Two.

Police say Two Two lost control of the 2004 Chevrolet Silverado that he was driving south on Country Club near East 10th Street at about 8:15 p.m. on March 22. The vehicle hit a curb on the west side of the street and rolled over, coming to rest in a front yard in the 100 block of N. Country Club Road.

Police say Two Two was driving at a high rate of speed, and police are investigating whether alcohol was a factor in the accident.
