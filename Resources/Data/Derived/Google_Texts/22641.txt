What do you get when advances in information technology are coupled with the creativity that comes from managing tight budgets in an era of increasing demands on government services? The answer is innovation.

Every day, there are new reports of state, county and municipal officials leading the way to more efficient and effective service delivery.

GovExec State & Local's new ebook, "Innovation in Action," written by Senior Editor Michael Grass, captures five stories from the frontlines of state and local government, from advances in open data and digital services to transportation.
