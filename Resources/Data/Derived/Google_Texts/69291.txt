Sand Springs police are hoping the public can help them locate a convenience store robber.

Detectives said a man went into the Kum 'N Go on Adams Road Monday, pointed a gun and took money.

Officials said they believe just before the robbery the man walked in without a disguise on. They describe him as a white male with dark colored hair that is balding on top with sideburns and a goatee. They said he was wearing thick glasses, a white shirt, blue jeans and gray shoes.

Police said after seeing video, they believe he has a limp or distinct movement with his right leg, and they said he may be driving a white Dodge pickup.

If you recognize him, call Sand Springs police at 918-246-2539.
