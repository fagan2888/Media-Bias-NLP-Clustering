Updated at 8:30 p.m.:

Three people died in a fiery car crash early Sunday in North Richland Hills, police say.

The names of the victims had not been released Sunday night. No other vehicles were involved in the accident.

The driver failed to stop at a light about 2:30 a.m. after exiting Northeast Loop 820 at Meadow Lakes Drive, police said.

The vehicle struck a guardrail and concrete wall before going over another wall and landing on the main lanes of 820.

One person was ejected from the vehicle, which was engulfed in flames. All three died at the scene, police said.
