Rapid City, SD

Authorities in Pennington County have made an arrest in the stabbing incident that left a man dead Saturday in Rapid City.

Previous Story: Rapid City Man Dies After Stabbing

Twenty-one-year old Samuel Tyburec was taken into custody and faces second degree murder charges.

Twenty-five-year old Matthew Wassenaar was stabbed around 1:30 p.m. Saturday in the 2300 block of Ambush Ranch Road. He later died at Rapid City Regional Hospital.

Wassenaar and Tyburec were roommates who had recently moved to the area.

An autopsy is scheduled to be performed next week. More information will be released as it becomes available.
