Two Wichita hospitals have released the conditions of eight patients still being treated following Thursday's shooting rampage in Newton and Hesston.

Via Christi St. Francis tells KAKE News one person is still listed in critical condition, and five others are in fair condition.

Wesley Medical Center said Sunday afternoon that one person is in serious condition while another is in fair condition.

Newton Medical Center reports no patients from last week's tragedy are being treated there.

Three people were killed and 14 were injured in the shootings. Hesston Police Chief Doug Schroeder fatally shot the gunman, 38-year-old Cedric Ford.
