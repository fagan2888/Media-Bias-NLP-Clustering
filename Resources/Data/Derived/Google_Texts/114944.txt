The official weigh-ins for Ultimate Fighting Championship’s UFC 189 take place at 5 p.m. Friday in Las Vegas.

UFC is broadcasting the full event live via YouTube.

The card is headlined with Chad Mendes (17-2) fighting Conor McGregor (17-2) for the interim featherweight title. Denver’s Brandon Thatch will take on Gunnar Nelson in a welterweight bout.

The main card will be broadcast at 8 p.m. Saturday on pay-per-view.
