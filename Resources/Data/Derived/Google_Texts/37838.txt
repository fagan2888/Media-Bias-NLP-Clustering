Tucson Police need the public's help in finding a missing 60-year-old man who is in need of medication.

Carlos Palomo-Yescas was last seen in the area of West Ajo near Freedom Drive at 8 a.m. on Saturday, March 19.

Police said he is a Hispanic man, 5'6, about 190 pounds with brown hair. He has brown hair, brown eyes and speaks mostly Spanish but may know some English, according to Tucson Police Public Information Officer Sgt. Kimberly Bay.

Palomo-Yescas was last seen wearing jeans, green alligator shoes and could be wearing a black ball cap.

Bay said he does not have a vehicle or bicycle but knows how to use the bus. He may have been trying to head to Valencia Road near Mark Road, according to Bay.

Anyone with information is urged to call 911.
