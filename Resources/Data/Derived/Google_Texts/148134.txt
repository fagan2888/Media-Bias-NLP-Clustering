It was 7:15 Friday evening when emergency personnel received a call about a shooting on Old Highway 45. Once police and emergency workers arrived, they say they discovered a man had been shot.

The victim was inside of the vehicle which was precariously parked on the side of the road near the 4500 block of Old Marion Rd. Detective Dareall Thompson says the man was the driver of the vehicle and had a passenger with him when the shooting happened.

The passenger was not injured in the incident. Once at the hospital, police say the victim immediately went into surgery. Police say they are currently searching for the suspect or suspects responsible for this shooting. The victim remains in the hospital in stable condition.
