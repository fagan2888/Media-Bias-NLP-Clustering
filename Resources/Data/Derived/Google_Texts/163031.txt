A 23-year-old man has died after he was hit by a car early Saturday morning near Derby.

It happened around 5:30 a.m. on 47th Street South between Woodlawn and Rock Road. Sgt. Lanon Thompson with the Sedgwick County Sheriff's Office said the man was walking when the car hit him.

The man died at the scene. His name was not released.

The driver remained on scene and is cooperating with the investigation, Thompson said.

Further information was not provided.
