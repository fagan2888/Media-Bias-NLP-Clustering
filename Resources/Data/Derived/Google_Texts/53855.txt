Q&A With Hunton & Williams' Douglas Mancino

Law360, New York (February 5, 2014, 6:31 PM ET) -- Douglas M. Mancino is a partner in Hunton & Williams LLP's tax practice in the firm's Los Angeles office. For more than 35 years, he has represented health care and nonprofit organizations on tax, business and financial matters, including audit, appeals and tax controversy issues. He served as counsel to health care clients in a number of cases that defined the limits of tax-exempt organizations participating in health care joint ventures.
