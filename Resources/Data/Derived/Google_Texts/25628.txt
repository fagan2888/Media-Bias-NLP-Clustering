Emergency crews responded to the scene of an injury accident on the Turner Turnpike near Luther.

The accident was reported Saturday afternoon on eastbound I-44 near N. Indian Meridian.

Lt. John Vincent with OHP said the accident was a single-vehicle, rollover accident with injury. An EagleMed helicopter was requested for this accident.

The accident shut down the roadway for more than an hour, OHP said.
