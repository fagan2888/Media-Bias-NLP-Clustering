The Lee County Sheriff’s Office has identified the body inside the plane that crashed into the Gulf off North Captiva Island as the pilot; 62-year-old Gregg Howard of St. Petersburg, Florida.

The medical examiner made the identification and also determined the official cause of death was drowning.

The Lee County Sheriff’s Office received the 911 call about the plane crash at around 5:52 p.m. Wednesday night. Shortly after the crash, a group of divers were sent to the wreckage.

The divers located the body of the pilot. Through an extensive search, divers were able to determine that Howard was alone in the plane when it crashed.

The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) assumed the investigation and is working to determine the cause of the crash.
