U.S. Women Soccer legends Mia Hamm, Kristine Lilly, and Tisha Hoch are in the Lowcountry this weekend!

The three stars took part in Team First Soccer Academy hosted by Mount Pleasant Recreation.

The two-day camp for soccer players ages 6-18 focused on different forms of teamwork.

Players will go through educational sessions and "feet-on" elements.

There is also a separate section during the training for athletes with disabilities.

Combined Hamm, Lilly, and Hoch have five Olympic gold medals, five World Cups Championships, and 12 NCAA titles.
