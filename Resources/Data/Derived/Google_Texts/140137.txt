The Colorado State Patrol is investigating a fatal crash involving a van that killed a 4-year-old boy in a Monument driveway Friday night.

The name of the boy has not been released. The driver of the 2003 Chevrolet van has been identified as Derrick Chamberlain of Monument.

The crash happened at 4:27 p.m. as Chamberlain was backing out of a private drive in the 800 block of Lancer Court and did not see the boy walking beside the van.

The child was pronounced dead on the scene, according to a news release.
