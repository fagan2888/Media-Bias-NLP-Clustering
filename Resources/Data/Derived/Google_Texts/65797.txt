Southeast Nashville residents are fed up with always getting the less than desirable projects dumbed on them, including moving the Sheriff’s Office and a new jail to their part of town.

Residents told the Sheriff they do not want the jail to move into their backyard

Antioch residents are tired of being Nashville’s dumping ground. One resident said everyone knows Antioch already has a asphalt plant, a waste management center, a rock crushing plant, and a jail.
