Police are looking for three men they say robbed a couple at gunpoint outside their Tulsa apartment early Tuesday.

Officers said they were called at 3:45 a.m. to the Lakeside Place Apartments in the 2100 block of South 99th East Avenue about an armed robbery.

The victims told officers as they got out of their car, three men, one armed with a handgun approached and demanded money, their keys and their cell phones.

One of the suspects wore a mask which had the eye holes cut out of it. Another had a shirt pulled up over his face and the third man stayed in a car, according to police.

Police said after getting the money and cell phones the suspects left in that car.
