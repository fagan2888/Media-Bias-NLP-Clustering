Frederick Koester, 71, of Barnegat, died Tuesday morning on Route 18 near Exit 10.

Koester was hit and killed as he crossed the highway from the northbound shoulder to the center median, according to Monmouth County Prosecutor's spokesman Charlie Webster.

Nobody has been arrested.

Police shut down the northbound side of the freeway, directing traffic to Route 33. A body covered in a white sheet could be seen near the center median.

Neptune Lt. Michael McGhee confirmed officers were dispatched to the scene at 9:53 a.m.

Investigating agencies include Monmouth County Prosecutors Office, Monmouth County Serious Collision Analysis Response Team and the Neptune Township Police Department.
