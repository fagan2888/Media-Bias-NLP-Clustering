Without Will Thornbury, Erroll Garner’s “Concert by the Sea” would not have become one of the most beloved and best-selling jazz albums of all time. Thornbury was a recording engineer for the Armed Forces Radio Network on Sept. 19, 1955—the day the pianist performed for servicemen at Fort Ord near Carmel, Calif. When the concert began, Thornbury switched on his Ampex mono reel-to-reel tape recorder with plans to air the concert later...
