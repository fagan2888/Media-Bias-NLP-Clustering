COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo. -

(12/19/15) A KRDO Newschannel 13 viewer sent us surveillance video of a package thief he says took items off his front porch.

It happened around 6:30 pm, at a home on Pear Tree Court, near Union Blvd. and Fillmore Ave.

The homeowner says he has notified police.

The incident is one of several package thefts that have been reported in the Colorado Springs area this year.
