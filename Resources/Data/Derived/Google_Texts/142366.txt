Jimmy Fallon reprised his new Vladimir Putin impression Wednesday in order to reenact one of President Barack Obama’s recent phone calls with the Russian leader. On Fallon’s version of the call, Putin slugs Vodka, cracks an Obamacare joke and sings “McCain in the Ukraine” and a Cold War edition of “Let It Go” from Frozen.
