KSDK - Mercy Medical Center is set to cut between 300 and 350 positions across four states, the company announced Wednesday.

The cuts, which a Mercy attributed the cuts to financial challenges with " reimbursement structure as we adjust to reductions mandated by the Affordable Care Act and other budget cuts, as well as the lack of Medicaid expansion in most of our states."

The release said most of the cuts will be made in leadership roles and the individuals will be notified next week. According to the release Mercy's total workforce numbers nearly 40,000, including approximately 2,500 leadership positions.
