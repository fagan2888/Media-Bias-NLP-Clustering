Wichita Police are searching for a man who robbed two fast food employees of their bank deposit.

The employees were trying to deposit money at a bank in the 3200 block of North Rock Road when their car was rear ended. A man with a gun walked up to the car and demanded the money. The robber left the scene driving west on 32nd Street.

The robber is described as a black man 28 to 32 years olf, 5'8", 165 pounds with a medium build. He wore a black hoodie. He was driving a 2003-06 dark blue Suburban or Yukon with blackened tail lights.
