Health Care, Utility Providers Request TCPA Exemptions

Law360, New York (June 8, 2015, 10:08 PM ET) -- Representatives from Pennsylvania and Texas' health care and utility industries filed support Wednesday for petitions to the Federal Communications Commission asking the agency to ensure the industries' unsolicited calls will be protected from the Telephone Consumer Protection Act.



The FCC is set to take up Commissioner Tom Wheeler's call to strengthen consumer protection laws against unsolicited robocalls and texts this month, and industries are calling in to make sure they’re protected.
