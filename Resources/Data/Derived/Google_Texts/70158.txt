Authorities found a wanted man in Duluth's Lincoln Park neighborhood Thursday night.

An arrest warrant had been issued for 38-year-old Darrell Treandre Dalton in February. The Douglas County Sheriff's Office had listed Dalton as "armed and dangerous" on its website.

Duluth police officers and St. Louis County sheriff's deputies arrested Dalton in the 2300 block of West 4th Street at around 8 p.m. Thursday after a brief struggle.

Dalton faces four felony charges in Douglas County, including strangulation or suffocation and intimidate a victim/use or attempt force, as well as misdemeanor battery.
