Miss Enid Alex Eppler is crowned Saturday night at the Mabee Center in Tulsa.

Alex Eppler was crowned Miss Oklahoma 2014 Saturday night in Tulsa. Eppler entered the scholarship pageant as Miss Enid.

First runner-up was Miss Okmulgee County Georgia Frazier. Sarah Klein, Miss Tulsa, is the second runner-up. The Miss Oklahoma pageant is sponsored by the Kiwanis Club of Tulsa and provides over $2 million in scholarships to contestants each year.

Eppler's cause is Care: Random Acts of Kindness, and her talent is dance.

The pageant was held at the Mabee Center on the ORU campus.
