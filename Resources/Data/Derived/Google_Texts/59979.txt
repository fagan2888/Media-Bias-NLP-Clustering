Niceville Police and the Florida Department of Law Enforcement are investigating a shooting that occurred Saturday morning at a home on 48th Street.

According to reports, police responded to a disturbance at the home, and when they arrived, shots were being fired.

The suspect, whose name has not been released, was shot, but has sustained non-life threatening injuries, and no officers were injured.

Both agencies, along with the State Attorney's office are investigating both the shooting, and the disturbance call. At this time no other information has been released. We will bring you more information when it becomes available.
