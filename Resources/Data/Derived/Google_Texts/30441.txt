The Meridian Police Department is investigating a shooting that happened on State Boulevard Saturday night.

The Meridian Police Department responded to the 2700 block of State Boulevard just before 5:30 p.m. MPD Detective Dareall Thompson says that one person was shot after an altercation possibly over a firearm. Thompson says police have recovered a weapon and have made contact with the shooter.

This is the second shooting that has happened near the same location on State Boulevard in the past three days.

We will update you with more information once it becomes available to us.
