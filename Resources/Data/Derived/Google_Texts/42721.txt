DOJ Must Block Health Insurance Mergers, AMA Says

Law360, New York (November 12, 2015, 1:59 PM ET) -- The U.S. Department of Justice must block proposed megamergers among health insurance giants to avoid disastrous cuts to physician reimbursement, the American Medical Association said Wednesday in an escalation of its anti-consolidation efforts.



The AMA voiced its position in a letter to the DOJ’s Antitrust Division, which is reviewing proposals for two epic mergers — a $37 billion deal between Aetna Inc. and Humana Inc., and a $54 billion deal between Anthem Inc. and Cigna Corp.
