Secular Anti-Abortion Org Can't Avoid Birth Control Mandate

Law360, New York (December 10, 2015, 8:44 PM ET) -- A Pennsylvania federal judge on Thursday ruled that a secular but strongly anti-abortion group cannot receive an exemption to the Affordable Care Act’s contraception mandate.



Even though Real Alternatives Inc.’s beliefs may be sincerely held, allowing a group with a single moral objection to receive an exemption could lead down a dangerous path, as under that logic, all moral exemptions to laws with religious objections would have to be upheld, U.S. District Judge John E. Jones III said. Judge Jones dismissed the case on summary judgment....
