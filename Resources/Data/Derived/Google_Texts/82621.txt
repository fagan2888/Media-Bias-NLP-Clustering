One man is dead after his motorcycle crashed in South Walton County.

It happened around 6 p.m. Saturday night near Mack Bayou Road and Bayou Landing Road.

Officials with the Florida Highway Patrol report that 59-year-old Michael Philbin was heading north on Mack Bayou Road.

They report that Philbin failed to make a sharp turn on a curve and crashed into a tree. According to the report, Philbin was not wearing a helmet.
