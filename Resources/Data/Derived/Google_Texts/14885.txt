Multiple earthquakes continue to rattle Oklahoma residents.

An earthquake, measuring a 3.0, was recorded about eight miles northeast of Langston at 4:21 a.m.

A 3.2 magnitude earthquake was recorded near Stillwater. According to the U.S. Geological Survey, this earthquake was recorded at 6:19 a.m. Its epicenter was located six miles west of Stillwater and 20 miles northeast of Guthrie.

At 6:43 a.m., another 3.0 magnitude earthquake was recorded seven miles west of Stillwater. It was about two miles deep.

No injuries or damage were immediately reported.
