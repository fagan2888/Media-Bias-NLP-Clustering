A water quality swimming alert put in effect Friday has been lifted one day later for a sound-side site in Craven County.

State recreational water quality officials say the alert affected the swimming area on the Neuse River located at 818 Neuse Drive near New Bern.

Officials say the water is now safe for swimming and water play.
