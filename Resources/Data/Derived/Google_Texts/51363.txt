A seasonal weekend in Denver comes before a likely Monday night snow storm, forecasters said.

Saturday is expected to be dry with a high of 60 degrees, according to the National Weather Service in Boulder.

On Sunday, temperatures could get slightly warmer with a high of 64 degrees and sunny skies, the weather service said.

The next storm will bring snow to the mountain areas beginning on Monday. The Front Range should get a taste of the storm starting on Monday night, forecasters said.

Forecasters are not yet certain on the intensity or duration of the snow storm, but they added that Tuesday night through Friday should be drier.
