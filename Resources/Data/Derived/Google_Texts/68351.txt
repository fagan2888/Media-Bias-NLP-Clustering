One person is dead after a motorcycle crash on County Road 167 in Jackson County Saturday afternoon.

Florida Highway Patrol officials say a motorcycle was traveling northbound on County Road 167 when the rear tire lost air pressure.

It swerved back and forth, and then fell on its side. Both the driver, 62-year-old Charles Crumpton, and the passenger, 53-year-old Stephene Crumpton were ejected.

Stephene Crumpton died, and Charles Crumpton was taken to Bay Medical with serious injuries.

Reports say they were both wearing helmets.
