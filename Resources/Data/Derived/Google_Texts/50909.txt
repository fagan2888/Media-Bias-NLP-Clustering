The Minnesota House has created a website where people can share their horror stories regarding Minnesota's Health Insurance Exchange, MnSure. MnSure was launched in July of 2014 to comply with the federal Affordable Care Act, otherwise known as Obamacare, and gained notoriety for the many problems people had connecting with the program, as well as difficulties finding affordable and reliable health insurance policies. The Affordable Care Act forces every American to have health insurance or face stiff fines from the IRS.
