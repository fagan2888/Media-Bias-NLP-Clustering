Finals week has wrapped up, exams have been taken, papers have been turned in, now its time for the graduates to celebrate.

Florida State Panama City had their commencement ceremony Sunday afternoon at the Marina Civic Center.

More than 200 graduate and undergraduate students participated in the ceremony.

Panama City businessman and tax consultant Floyd Skinner was the ceremony's keynote speaker.

"I hope to inspire the kids to take what they have learned here and capitalize on it," explained Skinner. "They have gone to a really great school, FSU Panama City, and they have all the tools they need to go out and get a really good job."

FSU Panama City had 342 graduates this academic year.
