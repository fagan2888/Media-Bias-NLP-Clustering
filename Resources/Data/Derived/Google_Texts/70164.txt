Updated at 8 p.m.: The Tarrant County Medical Examiner has identified the woman as 52-year-old Marcia Smith.

Original item at 4:50 p.m.: A woman was killed in a motorcycle accident Sunday afternoon after witnesses told police she lost control of the bike.

Police were called to eastbound State Hwy 121 by North Beach St about 2:10 p.m. Witnesses who stopped to help the woman said she lost control and fell off, said Officer Sharron Neal.

The bike continued for another 1,000 yards before coming to a stop, Neal said.

The woman was pronounced dead en-route to a hospital.

She was not wearing a helmet, Neal said.
