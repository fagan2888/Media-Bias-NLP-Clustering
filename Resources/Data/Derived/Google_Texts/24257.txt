The man suspected of killing five people in Modesto was the father of one of the victims, Modesto police said Sunday morning.

Vehicle of person of interest in Modesto case Photos

Martin Martinez was arrested and charged for murder Sunday morning, officials said.

Modesto police sent a tweet out about 2 a.m. that said San Jose police found Martin Martinez, who was identified as a person of interest in the deaths of two adults and three children who were found dead Saturday in a Modesto home.



Officers found the five people in a home in the 2600 block of Nob Hill Court about 3:30 p.m. Saturday, after officers responded to a welfare call.

Police identified Martinez, 30, as the person of interest late Saturday evening.



The investigation is ongoing.



No other information was immediately available.
