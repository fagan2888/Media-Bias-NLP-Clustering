Police say a man has been shot to death at an apartment complex in Birmingham.

Birmingham Police say a Hispanic man was found on the balcony Thursday morning with a gunshot wound to his chest. Police say the man was arguing with a woman before he was shot.

Authorities say the woman left before police arrived. The man was found unresponsive at the scene and was taken to the UAB Hospital where he was pronounced dead.

The identity of the man has not been released.
