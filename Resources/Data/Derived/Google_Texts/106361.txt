A woman is dead after a car crash Saturday night at the intersection of State Road 20 and County Road 275 in Altha.

Florida Highway Patrol reports state, Wesley McHenry, 25, was traveling south on County Road 275, approaching State Road 20. Meanwhile, a second car, being driven by Mark Laramore was traveling westbound on State Road 20 approaching the intersection of County Road 275.

Reports say McHenry failed to observe the stop sign and collided with the passenger side of Laramore's car.

The passenger in Laramore's car, Candie Laramore, was pronounced dead on scene.

Officials say alcohol was a factor, and at this time charges are pending.
