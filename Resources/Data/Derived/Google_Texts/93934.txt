The learning is in the journey: That could be the motto for Road Scholar, the nonprofit travel and education program for adults 50 and older that is celebrating its 40th anniversary this year.

In 1975, the organization—originally known as Elderhostel—had 220 people sign up for programs based on the then-novel idea of lifelong learning. Today, Road Scholar offers about 4,600 trips that accommodate approximately 100,000 people annually...
