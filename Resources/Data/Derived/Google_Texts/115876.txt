The Arizona Wildcats' Pac-12 opener against UCLA will be played on the national stage.

ESPN's College GameDay is headed to Tucson for the game, which is set for 5 p.m.

Arizona (3-0) is coming off a 77-13 rout of Northern Arizona while UCLA (3-0) edged BYU 24-23.

The Bruins beat the Wildcats 17-7 last season in California.
