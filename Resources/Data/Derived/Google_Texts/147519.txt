Brookings, SD

The South Dakota State University football team will make its third consecutive appearance in the Football Championship Subdivision playoffs, traveling to Montana State in first-round action Saturday afternoon.

Both the Jackrabbits and Montana State enter the postseason with 8-4 overall records and are at-large selections to the 24-team playoff fields. SDSU is ranked 14th in the latest Sports Network media poll, while the Bobcats are 17th.

Kickoff is slated for 2 p.m. Mountain Time (3 p.m. Central) at Bobcat Stadium in Bozeman, Montana. The game will be televised on ESPN3.com.
