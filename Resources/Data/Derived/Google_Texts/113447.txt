Police are asking the public if anyone recognizes two suspects accused of stealing marijuana with an estimated value of a quarter-million dollars from a Denver dispensary.

The Denver Police Department said the suspects broke into Fox Street Wellness, at 4773 N. Fox St., on Sunday and stole the marijuana.

Anyone with information on this incident or the suspects is urged to call Crime Stoppers at 720-913-STOP (7867) Tipsters can remain anonymous and could be eligible for up to $2,000 in reward money.
