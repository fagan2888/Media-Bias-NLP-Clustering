Over the past 25 years, Henry Schein Inc. has transformed itself from a family-owned business with $125 million in annual sales into a $10 billion provider of products and services to dental, medical and veterinary offices world-wide.

Steven Paladino, who joined the firm in 1987 and became chief financial officer in 1993, has been there through it all, helping steer an expansion that has included more than 200 acquisitions.
