Update at 5:15 p.m., Sept. 4, by Tristan Hallman: The Tarrant County Medical Examiner has identified the other victim as Roland Warren, 55.

Update at 8:40 p.m., by Tristan Hallman: One of the victims has been identified as Roland Holloway, 33, according to the Tarrant County Medical Examiner’s office.

Original post at 9:55 a.m.: Arlington police are investigating a single-vehicle crash that killed two people on Interstate 30 Sunday morning.

Police say the car crashed into a concrete barrier near the HOV lane on East Interstate 30, near Ballpark Way. A passerby called police around 5:40 a.m. to report a car on fire in the westbound lanes.

When fire officials arrived, they found two people dead inside the car. Police and the Tarrant County Medical Examiner’s Office have not identified the victims.

Police are investigating the cause of the accident. Investigators and emergency responders have temporarily closed several lanes of Interstate 30 near the crash site.
