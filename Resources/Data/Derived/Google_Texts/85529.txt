A a suspicious package in the highway 19 Walmart parking lot put a halt in business for the a few hours Saturday afternoon.

Meridian police say around 2 pm customers in the parking lot of Walmart noticed a package that appeared to be out of the ordinary. The customers contacted management who called police. Officials say the package was placed in the parking lot. A bomb dog out of Jackson was called in to investigate the package. The Meridian Police Department, Homeland Security, LEMA and the Meridian Fire Department were all on scene tending to the situation.
