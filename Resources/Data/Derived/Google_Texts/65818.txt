Mayor Mitch Landrieu will hold a news conference to announce new community leaders whom he is appointing to serve on the New Orleans Recreation Development Commission.

The mayor will be joined by city councilors and other community leaders for the news conference, set to begin at 1:30 p.m. Wednesday at City Hall.

Until a charter change in 2010, NORDC was previously known as the New Orleans Recreation Department. NORDC is comprised of 13 members who are nominated by Landrieu and approved by the New Orleans City Council.
