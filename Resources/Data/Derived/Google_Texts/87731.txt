Musical performance by Some Like It Hot Jazz Club and Music to My Ears

From 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. June 13, Somerset County Park Commission Colonial Park Gardens will be celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Rudolf W. van der Goot Rose Garden Rose Day. The event will be held at the Rudolf W. van der Goot Rose Garden on Mettlers Road (Parking Lot A and F) in the East Millstone section of Colonial Park.

The Rose Garden will be in peak bloom and visitors will enjoy a festival for rose enthusiasts, their families and all others who enjoy the beauty of the great outdoors.

Scheduled events include:

10 a.m.: Guided tour of the Rudolf W. van der Goot Rose Garden

11 a.m.: Marta McDowell, award-winning historical garden writer, will lecture on “The Gardening Life of Beatrix Potter”

Noon: Manfred Pawlak, international fragrance expert, will lecture on “The Chemistry of Garden Fragrance”

1 p.m.: Musical performance by Some Like It Hot Jazz Club

3 p.m.: Program ends.

Additional attractions include: live children’s music by Music to My Ears (10:30 a.m. to noon); JJ the Balloon Guy (12:30 to 2:30 p.m.); Colonial Park Gardens Rose Plant Sale; ongoing floral design demonstrations; vendors and local artisans; food trucks and more.

Admission to the event is free, but the Rose Garden welcomes donations.

For further information, call 732-873-2459, or NJ Relay Service at 711 for individuals with a hearing or speech impairment. Information on this event and other Somerset County Park Commission activities may be found at www.somersetcountyparks.org.
