Gender Surgery Bias Suit Should Stay Alive, Court Told

Law360, New York (March 25, 2016, 6:47 PM ET) -- A woman alleging that Minnesota-based health insurance company HealthPartners illegally refused to cover her teenage son’s gender reassignment surgery told a Minnesota federal court to keep the suit afloat, saying the company’s arguments that she lacked standing had missed the mark.



Plaintiff Brittany Tovar asked a federal court in Minnesota to reject bids by HealthPartners Inc. and her employer Essentia Health to toss her suit.
