Police have arrested a man accused of shooting and killing a pregnant woman in Colorado Springs on Monday.

Officers said they arrested Andre Jones, 32, on suspicion of first degree murder.

Jones is accused of shooting Lakeisha Jones, 32, at the Summit Apartments. Police did not say if the victim and suspect were related.

Lakeisha Jones, who was in the third trimester of pregnancy, was taken to Memorial Hospital. She died, but doctors were able to deliver the baby. The infant was still in critical condition on Tuesday.
