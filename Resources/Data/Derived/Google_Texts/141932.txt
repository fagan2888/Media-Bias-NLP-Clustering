An Atchison, Kansas man was killed when the car he was driving rolled New Year's Eve. The accident happened in Winchester, Kansas in Jefferson County.

Kansas Highway Patrol troopers said the man's car was westbound on Kansas Highway 192 when it missed a curve. The car entered a ditch and rolled several times. The driver was ejected through a rear window.

Killed was Isiah Jackson, 27. He was the only person in the car.
