Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway Inc. said Friday it is being sued by two present Acme Building Brands Inc. employees and one former employee over Acme's decision to restructure its employee retirement plans.

Berkshire acquired Acme 14 years ago, and the lawsuit contends that the acquisition agreement allows retirement plan participants to accrue additional defined benefits forever, at the same rate that benefits were being accrued at the time of the acquisition, Berkshire said.
