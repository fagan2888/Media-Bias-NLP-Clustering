STILLWATER, Okla. (AP) - A former Oklahoma State University professor who had been missing since February has been found in a hospital in Las Vegas.

The son of Marc Krein told the Stillwater NewsPress (http://bit.ly/1n0YVmH ) that Krein called a family member Saturday from the hospital. Phillip Krein says he then flew to Las Vegas.

Phillip Krein says his father told him he had been in some kind of accident.

Marc Krein was a broadcast professor at OSU and had been living in Stillwater.
