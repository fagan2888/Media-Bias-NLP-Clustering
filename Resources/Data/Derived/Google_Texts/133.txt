WALTON HILLS, Ohio - A fire broke out Friday morning at a youth baseball academy in Walton Hills. It was originally thought thought that the fire started next door at the Affordable Car Care, but that was not the case

The fire department responded to the commercial complex at about 6:30 a.m. on Northfield Road.

The landlord said the office of "Players I" caught fire. Some minor damage was also caused to a bakery business next door.

There is heavy traffic between Forbes and Alexander roads.
