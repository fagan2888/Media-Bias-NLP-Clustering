A woman suffered critical injuries in a stabbing Friday evening in northeast Wichita.

It happened around 6 p.m. at 14th Street North and Grove. Officers were called to the report of two people fighting in the street. When they arrived on scene, they found a woman in her 30s with a stab wound to her left thigh.

The victim was taken to a local hospital. Officer Chad Cooper said the woman's condition has improved, adding she should be released from the hospital Friday evening.

Officers arrested a suspect who was booked into the Sedgwick County Jail for aggravated battery. Officer Cooper said the incident was domestic-related.

Police released no further details about the victim or the suspect.
