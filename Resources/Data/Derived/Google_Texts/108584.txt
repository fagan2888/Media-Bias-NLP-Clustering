The South Carolina Highway Patrol is investigating an incident that killed a child inside a state park.

The incident happened along State Park Road in Ninety-Six at the Lake Greenwood State Park.

According to investigators the incident happened at 11:15 a.m. Saturday.

State troopers said the incident happened when an SUV turned onto State Park Road off of private property, and struck a pedestrian.

The coroner identified the victim as a 1-year-old girl.

An autopsy is scheduled for Sunday to determine a cause of death.

Troopers have not said if charges will be filed.
