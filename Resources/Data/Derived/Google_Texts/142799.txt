Killed killed in traffic accident at 65th Avenue and Bethany Home Road. (Source: KPHO/KTVK)

A man was arrested for drunken driving Saturday night after he struck and killed a 58-year-old woman, Glendale Police Sgt. David Vidaure said.

Police said they arrested the driver, 34-year-old Erick Vazquez-Barragan. He was also charged with driving on a suspended license.

The victim, Demonia Sanders, was not in a crosswalk, Vidaure said.

The accident happened at 65th Avenue and Bethany Home Road just before 9:30 p.m.

Bethany Home Road was closed for a while between 63rd and 67th avenues while police investigated.
