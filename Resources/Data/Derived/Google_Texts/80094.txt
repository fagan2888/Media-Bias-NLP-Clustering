The Gulf Coast men and women, along with the Northwest Florida Lady Raiders all won both their games this weekend at the the Commodores' Thanksgiving Classic.

Jay Powell's program topped Walters State 76-71, improving to 7-4 on the season. Jeremy Harris led GC with 28 points and eight rebounds. Donovan Franklin added 15 points while Curtis Weathers added 12.

The Lady Commodores continued to stay unbeaten in 2015, going 9-0 by besting Eastern Florida 103-63, topping the century mark on the scoreboard for the second straight game.

The Lady Raiders beat Albany Tech 89-51 to improve to 9-1 on the campaign.
