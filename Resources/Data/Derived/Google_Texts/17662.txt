Two vehicles were involved in a rollover crash Saturday night in downtown Oklahoma City, police said.

The crash was reported shortly before 8 p.m. near W Reno Avenue and S Hudson Avenue.

Witnesses told News 9 that one of the vehicles was speeding and ran multiple red lights before the driver lost control and crossed the median. That vehicle hit at least four trees before hitting a flipping another vehicle.
