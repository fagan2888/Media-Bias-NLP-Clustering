A man is in jail after breaking into his estranged wife's home, assaulting her and hitting her 8-year-old son.

It happened at about 5:15 a.m. Friday in the near Waco and Funston. Police responded after the woman was able to call 911.

The 32-year-old woman told officers her husband forced his way in without permission, damaging the door. She said he hit her and tried to strangle her, while threatening to harm her further, before going after the boy. Both victims suffered minor injuries.

The suspect was arrested and booked into Sedgwick County Jail on charges of domestic violence, aggravated burglary, multiple battery charges and criminal damage to property.
