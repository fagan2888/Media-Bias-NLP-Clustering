An 18-year-old Wichita woman was killed in a crash Saturday morning in Riley County.

The crash happened around 8:45 a.m. on Interstate 70, just southeast of Manhattan. Troopers say a Chevy pickup was heading east, crossed the median and went into a ravine.

The driver died at the scene. She is identified as Alexis Champ of Wichita.

Her 21-year-old passenger was taken to a local hospital for treatment. Troopers identified him as Darius Person of Wayne, Michigan.

The crash remains under investigation.
