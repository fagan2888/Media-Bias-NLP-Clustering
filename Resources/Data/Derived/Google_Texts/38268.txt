According to the Wayne County Sheriff's Office, 37-year-old Danielle Lee Hagans is charged with identity theft, forgery of an instrument, and uttering of a forged instrument.

Deputies were called to a business in the Mar Mac community on January 25. Employees told deputies that Hagans attempted to cash several fraudulent checks on various occasions.

Investigators determined the woman was using the name of someone else while trying to make the transactions.

Hagans was arrested February 13. More charges and arrests may be pending.

Hagans is in jail with bond set at $10,000 secured.
