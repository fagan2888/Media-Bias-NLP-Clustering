UPDATE 1:29 P.M.

The victim from Sunday morning's deadly shooting on Okaloosa Island has been identified as 21-year old Timothy Stocker Junior of Fort Walton Beach.

Investigators with the Okaloosa County Sheriff’s Office say Stocker died at Fort Walton Beach Medical Center after being shot multiple times outside the New Green Frog nightclub on Santa Rosa Boulevard around 3:30 a.m.

Callers reported hearing a series of gunshots near the business at that time. Stocker reportedly fled into the entryway prior to being transported to the hospital.

----------------------------------

The Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office is investigating a shooting that led to the death of a Fort Walton Beach man early Sunday morning.

Deputies were called to The New Green Frog on North Santa Rosa Boulevard around 3:30 a.m. in response to multiple shots fired outside the establishment.

The victim, in his early twenties, entered the club bleeding profusely and died later at Fort Walton Beach Medical Center.

Officials have not released the victims name.

Anyone with information on the crime is asked to contact the OCSO at 850-651-7400 or contact Emerald Coast Crime Stoppers at 850-863-TIPS, emeraldcoastcrimestoppers.com, or text “TIP 214 plus your message to 274637 (Crimes).
