A three-acre brush fire in Camarillo broke out Sunday night amid a Red Flag Warning, Ventura County Fire officials said.

The brush fire broke out in the 1400 block of W. Avenida De Aprisa.

Winds were blowing 20-30 mph.

Initially, fire officials said the fire threatened homes, but later said it was not.

The fire was burning up about three acres of medium brush.

The Red Flag Warning was issued for mountain and canyon areas Sunday.

A compost fire also broke out in Fontana in the 13300 block of Napa Street.

Large flames were reported as green waste burned.
