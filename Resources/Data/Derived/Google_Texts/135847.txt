A Niceville man is dead after an early morning crash on State Road 20 on Rocky Bayou Bridge in Okaloosa County.

Florida Highway Patrol (FHP) officials say the crash happened around 3:45 Sunday morning.

Reports state, Richard Alan Terry, 28, was driving westbound on State Road 20 on the inside lane, when his car hit a concrete barrier at the

beginning of the Rocky Bayou Bridge and his car overturned.

FHP officials say alcohol was not a factor, but Terry was not wearing a seatbelt at the time of the crash.
