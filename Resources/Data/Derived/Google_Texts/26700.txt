The first baby born this New Year is a healthy baby girl, Anderson Hospital had the honor of delivering her. After seven hours of laboring Ariella Faith Haddox was born at 3:27 AM weighing 5 pounds 11 ounces.

Her mother, Amanda Haley and father, Anthony Haddox, were both at home when mom's water broke. This is the couple's first child. They say they called their parents and rushed to the hospital to deliver the baby. The baby's father, spent the entire time in the hospital by his girlfriend's side.

“I think what we strive for is health, that’s all matters really. As long as she is healthy,” said Haddox.

Both mother and baby will remain in the hospital until the doctor releases them.
