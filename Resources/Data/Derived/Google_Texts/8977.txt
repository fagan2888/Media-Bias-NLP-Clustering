WELD COUNTY — At least one person was killed Tuesday in a semi truck versus car crash northeast of Gill.

The crash happened at about 11:50 a.m. Tuesday at the intersection of Colo. 392 and Weld County Road 57, according to a news release from the Colorado State Patrol.

One person died at the scene, according to the release, but their identity has not been made available by the Weld County Coroner’s Office pending notification of family.
