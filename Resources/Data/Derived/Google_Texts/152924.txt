× Mayor Wharton introduces Project ConnectHome

MEMPHIS, Tenn. — Memphis Mayor A C Wharton, along with several other community leaders, announced a new project that would give lower-income families access to the internet.

The new project is called ConnectHome.

According to the Mayor, it will provide internet access to more than 4,500 low income homes by the end of the year.

He said the move would bridge the digital divide and “improve their quality of life tremendously”.

The city is partnering with the Memphis Housing Authority, Shelby County Schools AT&T, Comcast, HUD and the IT Council to make this project happen.
