Police said a hospital security guard led them to the man wanted in a robbery and shooting outside a Tulsa gas station last week.

Officers say Dawan Stigger, 22, shot the victim in the leg then took his car at the Peoria Mart in the 6300 block of South Peoria on November 14, 2015.

On Wednesday, a security guard at the OSU Medical Center recognized Stigger, detained him and called police. In their arrest report, officers said Stigger admitted to the crimes when he was questioned.

Police booked Dawan Stigger into the Tulsa County jail on complaints of robbery with a firearm and assault and battery with a deadly weapon. Jail records show Stigger has a court appearance set for November 25.
