SÃO PAULO—Vale SA, the world's leading producer of iron ore, agreed to form a joint venture with power company Cemig to manage some energy assets and to sell part of its stake in the controversial Belo Monte hydroelectric dam in the Amazon forest, part of the Brazilian mining company's efforts to cut costs and focus on its main business.
