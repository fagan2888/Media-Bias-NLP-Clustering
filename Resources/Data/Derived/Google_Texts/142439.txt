Oklahoma City police are investigating a shooting on the northwest side.

It happened around 4:30 a.m. Monday in the 7500 block of N.W. 6th Street at the London Square Village Apartments between Council and Rockwell.

Police said a group of people fired several shots into the apartment from the outside.

A man was hit by the gunfire. Authorities said he is being treated at the hospital for non-life-threatening injuries.
