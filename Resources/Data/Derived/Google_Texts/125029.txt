25 McKenna Long Health Attys Decamp To BakerHostetler

Law360, New York (May 14, 2015, 5:05 PM ET) -- BakerHostetler on Thursday announced it's hiring 25 Atlanta-based health care attorneys from McKenna Long & Aldridge LLP, a bid to capitalize on the sharp rise in provider integration tied to the Affordable Care Act.



When the lawyers start work on June 1, they'll almost triple the size of BakerHostetler's Atlanta office, which has roughly a dozen attorneys and debuted following last year's merger with intellectual property boutique Woodcock Washburn LLP.
