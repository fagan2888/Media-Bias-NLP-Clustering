Anyone interested in riding the bus to Raleigh to participate in the march on Feb. 14 can contact Amos McClorey at 704-560-1877. The deadline to register is Monday and the trip is free, but space is limited.

For those who plan to attend the march and are driving separately, there will be a pre-rally at 9 a.m. Feb. 14 with everyone gathering across the street from the Raleigh Memorial Auditorium at 2 East South St., Raleigh. The march begins at 10 a.m. with the assembly held near the State Capitol on Fayetteville Street. For those who can walk in the march, there will be trams available to take people to the assembly.
