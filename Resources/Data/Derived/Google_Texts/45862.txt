Two people are arrested after allegedly threatening a car dealership in west Wichita according to police.

Wichita Police say the couple went to Midwest Kia to purchase a car and became angry when they were denied financing.

A man allegedly pulled a gun and threatened employees. No one was injured.

The couple were arrested on charges of aggravated assault.

The girlfriend contacted KAKE News. She says that the incident happened after a car deal went bad. She said that her boyfriend pulled out an unloaded gun after feeling threatened while trying to leave the parking lot.
