A Delta Airlines flight departing from Baltimore and on its way to Atlanta landed early when a passenger noticed a baboon tarantula escaped its container. Fortunately, it was captured by Delta employees and all passengers were transferred onto another plane. [Complex]

In case you forgot, it’s not necessary to give the police your phone password if you get arrested. A Pennsylvania court just ruled that forcing citizens to give up their passwords violates the Fifth Amendment. For phones that have a fingerprint lock, this doesn’t apply, as the Fifth Amendment does not protect biometric data. [Gizmodo]
