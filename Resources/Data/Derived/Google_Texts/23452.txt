Tiffany, Mary Etta Littrell

Mary Etta Littrell Tiffany, 74, of Interstate Drive, Covington, and formerly of Clifton Forge, Va. passed away Wednesday, May 25, 2016 at Lewis Gale Medical Center in Salem.Mary Etta was born September 18, 1941 in Clifton Forge to the late Robert S. and Josephine Harris Littrell. She was a self employed child care provider, and a member of Temple Baptist Church in Covington.In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by two sisters, Sarah Littrell and Diane Williams.She is survived by her husband of 56 years, William Adair "Bill" Tiffany Jr. of Covington; two daughters, Amy Tiffany of Leesburg and Karen Benoit and husband, George of Clifton Forge; a son, Robert Tiffany of Covington; three grandchildren, Brett Benoit, Amanda Benoit, and Derek Benoit, all of Clifton Forge; and a great grandson, Brady Benoit.In keeping with her wishes, her body has been cremated. A memorial service will be held at 7 p.m. on Friday evening, May 27, 2016 in Temple Baptist Church in Covington, with the Reverend Bill Hartsfield officiating. The family will receive friends from 6 p.m. until service time Friday evening at Temple Baptist Church. Interment of her cremains will take place at a later date in Arlington National Cemetery.Nicely Funeral Home, Clifton Forge is handling arrangements.The family suggests memorial tributes take the form of contributions to Temple Baptist Church, 300 Stratton Street, Covington, VA 24426 or the Myasthenia Gravis Foundation of America, 355 Lexington Avenue, 15th Floor, New York, NY 10017.To send condolences to the family online, please visit www.nicelyfuneralhome.com
