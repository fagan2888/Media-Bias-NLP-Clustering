PRINCETON - A Trenton man working at Princeton House Behavioral Health has been charged with sexually assaulting a patient, authorities announced Monday.

Jonathan D. Hodges, 36, faces one count of second-degree sexual assault. He is free on $50,000 bail and is scheduled to appear in Princeton Municipal Court on Feb. 9.

On Jan. 28, a 34-year-old woman reported that prior to being discharged that morning, an employee came into her room, pushed himself against her, put his hand inside her pants and underwear and sexually assaulted her, the Mercer County Prosecutor's Office said in a release.

Video surveillance showed Hodges, a mental health associate, entering the victim's room several times during a 14-minute period, staying various lengths of time, prosecutor's said.

The investigation remains ongoing. Anyone with information should contact Detective Marlon Webb of the Mercer County Prosecutor's Office Special Victims Unit at 609-989-6568 or Princeton Detective Annette Henderson at 609-921-2100.
