Commerce City Police are investigating a suspected murder/suicide after a man and woman were found dead in their home Sunday afternoon.

Officers responded to a call about a shooting at 5761 East 65th Way around 3:55 p.m. Sunday. They found a man and a woman, both in their 50s, dead in the house.

“It appears to be a murder/suicide,” said Detective Christian Rasmussen, spokesman for the Commerce City Police Department.

A juvenile female in the house called in the shooting, Rasmussen said.

Rasmussen said there was no history of domestic disputes at the address.

The investigation is ongoing.
