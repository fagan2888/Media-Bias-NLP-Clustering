M&A executives’ bullish expectations for deal-making activity this year are being tempered by concerns over global economic concerns, financial market volatility tighter debt markets and regulatory pressures, according to Deloitte’s 2016 M&A Trends Report. The survey of U.S. corporate and private equity M&A executives also revealed a continued focus on strategic deals, divestitures and cross-border opportunities and a rising use of data analytics in M&A decision-making.
