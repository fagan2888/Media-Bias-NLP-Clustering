An El Dorado teen is killed in Marion County when her vehicle hit a semi head on.

According to the Kansas Highway Patrol, 19-year-old Sally Rowell was driving south after 8:45 a.m. on U.S. Highway 56, south of Herington, when she crossed the center line and hit a semi.

Rowell was killed in the crash.

The driver of the semi, 45-year-old Tommy Tibbets, was taken to the hospital with injuries.
