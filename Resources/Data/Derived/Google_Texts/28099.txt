WAYNE, Pa. — The Emerson Group announced Friday that Vince DeTurris, a 21-year veteran of Johnson & Johnson, would be joining the company’s Walgreens team as its sales manager. In his new position, DeTurris will manage several categories within the company’s health and wellness, as well as beauty and personal care areas.

During his time at Johnson & Johnson — where he was most recently director of its Natural Drug Team and the national account director for its Diabetes Solution Company — DeTurris also served as the company’s customer development director overseeing its beauty team at Walgreens.

DeTurris has three children and resides outside Chicago.
