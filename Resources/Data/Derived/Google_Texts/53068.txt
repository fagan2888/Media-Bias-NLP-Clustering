RIVERTON, Wyo. (AP) - Authorities say a 19-year-old woman was killed and another person was injured in a two-vehicle crash south of Ethete.

The Riverton Ranger reports (http://goo.gl/kO3m7L ) Taylor Harris, who graduated from Wyoming Indian High School this year, was killed in the crash Tuesday evening. Fremont County Coroner Mark Stratmoen says a person in the other vehicle was treated at a nearby hospital.

Harris was a varsity basketball player for the WIHS Chiefs. She previously attended Riverton High School.
