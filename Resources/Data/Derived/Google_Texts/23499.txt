Despite the Republican Party’s business-friendly reputation, the word “technology” has barely been mentioned in the first two Donald Trump-centric debates. Though the tech industry drives the nation’s economy, when leading candidates have passed through Silicon Valley, they’ve been here to hit up techies for cash — not talk policy.
