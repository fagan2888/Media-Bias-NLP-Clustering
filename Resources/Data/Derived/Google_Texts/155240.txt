A Dodge City man is dead after running his pickup truck into a tree near Cimarron Sunday morning.

The Kansas Highway Patrol reports at just before 11 a.m. 91-year-old Henry R. Wagner was traveling eastbound on US-Highway 50 when his Chevrolet S-10 left the roadway, swerved into the south ditch and struck the tree.

Wagner was pronounced dead at the scene. The KHP reports he was not wearing a safety-belt.

No one else is listed as being in the truck at the time of the crash.
