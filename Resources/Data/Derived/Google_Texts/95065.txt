As if on cue, the sun peeked over the neighboring high-rises just as Patricia Rodriguez rose from her park bench to read the Gospel.

Only four of the dozen regulars had gathered on this frigid day to celebrate an unofficial, open-air Roman Catholic service. Ms. Rodriguez, 51 years old and the unofficial leader of this group, was bundled head to toe and read quickly, bouncing up and down to keep warm.
