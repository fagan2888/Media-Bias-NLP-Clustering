BRAMPTON A Brampton man working at a Mississauga wellness centre has been charged with sexual assault, Peel Regional Police report.

Police say a 46-year-old woman went to the centre on Hurontario Street for a massage appointment on June 27. She reported to police she was sexually assaulted.

Police have arrested and charged an employee of the centre.

Ali Khan, 42, faces one charge of sexual assault.

Anyone with information is asked to call 12 Division detectives at 905-453-2121, ext. 1233, or Peel Crime Stoppers anonymously at 1-800-222-TIPS (8477).
