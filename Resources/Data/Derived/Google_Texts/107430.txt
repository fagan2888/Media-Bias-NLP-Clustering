GAINESVILLE, Fla. - The Gainesville police department arrested a woman Saturday morning on DUI charges.

Police say Summer Crosby, 29 was involved in a traffic crash several hours prior to her arrest. Police say Crosby told the other party in the crash that she would pay for the damages if law enforcement wasn't called.

Police say Crosby then left the scene and failed to give proper information and never returned to the scene. Several hours later Crosby agreed to meet the other party at a Publix Shopping Center on 53rd and 43rd Streets to meet.

Crosby was observed leaving the scene and was stopped. Police say she showed signs of impairment and performed poorly of field sobriety test. She was then arrested. Police say two breath samples registered .247 and .248.
