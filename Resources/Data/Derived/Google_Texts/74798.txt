On the 50th anniversary of the March on Washington this past August, MSNBC’s hosts and contributors shared what they were doing to “advance” Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.’s “dream.” Today, the crew over at Fox News banded together for a similar social media blitz, posting 6-second Vine videos of their new year’s resolutions.

Below is a look at what all of your favorite Fox News personalities plan to change over the next year, from Sean Hannity’s plan to “drink more, party more,” to Shepard Smith’s desire to play less Candy Crush. One voice conspicuously missing is Fox’s reigning ratings champ Bill O’Reilly. Guess that means there’s nothing he needs to change in 2014?
