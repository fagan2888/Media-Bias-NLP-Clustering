Charges are pending against a driver who State Troopers say was speeding when he caused a rollover crash in Calhoun County that critically injured a man.

Danny Carnley

According to the Florida Highway Patrol, Danny Odell Carnley Jr., 27, of Fountain and Pedro Simon Chavez, 39, of Altha, were both traveling south on State Road 71. About 5:45 a.m., Carnley rear ended Chavez’s van, forcing it off the road. The van overturned several times into a tree line.

Chavez was transported by helicopter to Bay Medical Center/Sacred Heart in Panama City. He’s listed in critical condition. Carnley

was transported to Calhoun Medical Center in Blountstown with serious injuries.
