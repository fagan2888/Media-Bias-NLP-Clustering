Authorities are looking for a Weld County man who escaped from the Colorado Mental Health Institute on Sunday in Pueblo.

Celestino J. Ureste, 30, has been at the hospital since 2007. He was committed after an arrest by the Greeley Police Department on several charges, including first-degree motor vehicle theft with a weapon, two counts of possession of a dangerous weapon by a previous offender, armed robbery and resisting arrest. Ureste had several previous arrests dating to 2000, including charges related to drugs, theft and assault, records show.

He is described as 5-foot-8 and 154 pounds with brown hair and green eyes. He was last seen wearing jeans, a black shirt and a jacket with a Native American symbol that is yellow, red and green.

Ureste has a tattoo on his chest and is missing part of his left thumb, according to the hospital.
