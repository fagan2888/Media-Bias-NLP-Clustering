EUREKA SPRINGS, Ark. (AP) - Arkansas State Police say a 23-year-old Eureka Springs man has been killed in a head-on collision in Carroll County.

A police report says Christopher Reeves died in the crash on Highway 23 near Eureka Springs shortly before 1 a.m. Sunday.

The report says Reeves was southbound and negotiating a left-hand curve in the road when a northbound vehicle also entered the curve and crossed the centerline - colliding head-on with Reeves.

The driver of the second vehicle was hospitalized in undisclosed condition.
