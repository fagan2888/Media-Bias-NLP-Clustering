Event Date: Wednesday, April 30, 2014, 7:30 pm

WBUR is a presenting sponsor of IFFBoston, the Independent Film Festival Boston 2014, taking place from April 23-30.

Tickets are on sale now, and the full festival lineup is at the IFFBoston website. Don’t miss Only A Game Reporter Doug Tribou at The Somerville Theater on Thursday night when he hosts an IFF screening and talk with the filmmakers and cast of Fight Church, a new documentary about Christian pastors who’ve embraced mixed-martial art “MMA” fighting. The film was recently featured on Comedy Central’s show The Colbert Report in a special segment titled: Extreme Measures for Boosting Church Attendance.
