Durham police were searching Saturday morning for a 23-year-old who is believed to be cognitively impaired.

The North Carolina Center for Missing Persons issued a Silver Alert for Antwan Lashawn Davis at about 3:20 a.m.

Davis weighs about 150 pounds, short, black hair and brown eyes. When he disappeared, Davis was wearing a turquoise shirt, black jeans and white, black and red sneakers.

He was last seen getting in small, black sedan in the 100 block of Basset Hall Drive. It's unclear where Davis may be headed.

Anyone with information about Davis' whereabouts should call the Durham Police Department at 919-825-8364.
