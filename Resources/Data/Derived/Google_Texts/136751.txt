ARIZONA REPERTAORY SINGERS' - Winter Concert series



Arizona Repertory Singers (ARS), Annual winter concerts happening Dec. 14, and 19



Tucson's premier vocal choral group, is celebrating 25 years under the leadership of Music Director Dr. Jeffry A. Jahn with a concert season featuring his favorite songs.



ARS will honor Jahn's silver anniversary season with four performances of Heavenly Light featuring new arrangements of well-known Christmas carols as well as classical choral music. The program includes holiday pieces by Johannes Brahms, 20th century composers Francis Poulenc and Gustav Holst and contemporary composers John Rutter and Morten Lauridsen.



Concertgoers will enjoy the music from inside the lovely Benedictine Monastery, usually closed to the public. Concerts at the Benedictine Monastery, 800 N. Country Club Road, typically sell out and ARS expects the same from this year's performances Sunday, Dec. 14 at 3 p.m. and 7:30 p.m. and Friday, Dec. 19 at 7:30 p.m.



Visit www.arsingers.org for more information.
