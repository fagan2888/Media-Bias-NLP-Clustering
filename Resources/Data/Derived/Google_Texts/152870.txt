Three people were shot and injured, including one seriously, in two separate shootings in Milwaukee that occurred 15 minutes apart overnight Saturday.

Gunfire broke out around 12:25 a.m. at a party in the 1800 block of N. 6th St, police said in a news release. Police didn't provide a specific location, but WISN-TV reported it was at the Days Inn & Suites on N. 6th St.

A 20-year-old woman was seriously injured and another female victim also was hit by gunshots that followed a fight.

Then at 12:40 p.m., an 18-year-old Milwaukee man was hit by gunshots fired by a moving vehicle in the 3800 block of W. Rhor Ave.

No arrests have been reported in either shooting.
