A day after some protesters

turned to violence in Baltimore

, the Maryland Transit Administration (MTA) announced several service changes for local transportation Tuesday.

Baltimore's Metro Subway is traveling to the Johns Hopkins station, but is bypassing the Mondawmin, Penn-North and Upton Market stops.

Local Bus is operating with several diversions, and the Mondawmin stop is closed.

Light Rail is operating on a regular schedule, as is MTA Mobility services, MARC Train and MTA Commuter Bus.
