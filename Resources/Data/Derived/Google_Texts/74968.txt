WEST DES MOINES, Iowa -- West Des Moines police are investigating after a body was found in what they called a "shallow ravine" near a townhome complex and they're calling the woman's death suspicious.

Police identified the woman as 48 year-old Lisa Marie Hulsing, of West Des Moines.

The call came in just before 9 a.m. about a woman found face-down in the water at 8610 E. P. True Parkway. Police say the body was found between a group of townhomes and homes and the ravine divided the area.
