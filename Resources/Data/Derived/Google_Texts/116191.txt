Missouri authorities are investigating the death of a Kansas man whose body was found inside a burned portable restroom.

On Friday, Jackson County authorities identified the victim as 30-year-old Jackson Guzman of Shawnee, Kansas. A Jackson County park ranger found the body Thursday night in the parking lot of the Frank White Jr. Softball Complex near Longview Lake in Lee's Summit.

Authorities are asking anyone with information to come forward.
