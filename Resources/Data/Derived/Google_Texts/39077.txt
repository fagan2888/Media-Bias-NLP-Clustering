Police and firefighters from Tuckerton Fire Department responded Thursday to a vehicle crash on Green Street involving a person on a bicycle.

The man on the bicycle was not identified and information was not immediately available from police. The man was taken to the hospital by Quality Medical to be checked out, a fire official said.

Tuckerton Fire Department responds to all motor vehicle accidents within the borough where injuries occur and all EMS calls requiring Advanced Life Support, according to Fire Chief Lee Eggert Jr.
