Engaged in a reelection campaign, U.S. Rep. Leonard Lance (R-7) will go up on cable television tomorrow with “Our Kind of Conservative,” his first ad of the season.

The ad notes Lance’s opposition to Obamacare and the President’s Iran nuclear deal.

Lance is running for reelection in the June primary against fellow Republicans David Larsen and Craig Heard.
