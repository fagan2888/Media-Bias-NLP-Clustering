A fire in Westminster burned through six apartments Sunday, displacing the residents.

The Westminster Fire Department was dispatched to the 1400 block of 116th Ave., at 3:02 a.m., according to a department press release.

Firefighters had the blaze in control within 35 minutes. There were no injuries, though emergency workers assessed two residents for smoke inhalation.

The Red Cross and Westminster Victims Advocates are assisting displaced residents with temporary housing, the release said.

At this time the fire is under investigation and there is no estimated dollar loss.
