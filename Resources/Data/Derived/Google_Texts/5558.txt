Notre Dame Leans On Hobby Lobby In ACA Birth Control Feud

Law360, Chicago (April 22, 2015, 8:52 PM ET) -- The University of Notre Dame on Wednesday returned to the Seventh Circuit seeking an exemption from the Affordable Care Act’s contraception coverage mandate, saying the U.S. Supreme Court’s Hobby Lobby decision confirms that the law is a substantial burden on its Roman Catholic beliefs.
