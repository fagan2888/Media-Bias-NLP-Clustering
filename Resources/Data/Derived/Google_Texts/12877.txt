Meridian Police responded to the report of an armed robbery at CVS on Highway 19 North Saturday evening.

The call came in around 8:40. Detective Dareall Thompson of the Meridian Police Department said an individual was robbed on the outside of the CVS Store.

Thompson says the victim left the scene after the robbery happened, but returned to speak with officers. The suspect is described as a skinny black male, wearing a zip-up hoodie, and dark grey colored pants.

If you have any information that can lead to an arrest you can call Crimestoppers 855-485-TIPS.
