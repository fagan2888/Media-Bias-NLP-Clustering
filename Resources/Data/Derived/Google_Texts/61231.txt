Twenty million people now have health coverage under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, according to a new report. That includes an additional 12 million people who gained coverage through other provisions of the law along with the 8 million who enrolled in coverage through the exchanges since the spring.

A study from the Commonwealth Fund, published Wednesday in the New England Journal of Medicine, detailed new enrollees in the law, as of May 1.
