SuperChefs restaurant at 106 Fairfax Ave. in St. Matthews has been damaged after a fire around 4 a.m. this morning.

WHAS reported about 50 firefighters responded to the two-alarm fire. Two other businesses in the strip mall where SuperChefs is received smoke damage.

Firefighters experienced delays due to snow and had to frequently change crews because of the cold temperatures, according to a WHAS report.

No injuries were reported. Fire officials are still working to determine what caused the blaze.
