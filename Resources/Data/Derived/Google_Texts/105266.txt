Westminster Police issued a missing person bulletin Tuesday for 19-year-old Lea Chali Porter. Porter, who was reported missing by her mother on June 5, lives in Pueblo County but was visiting friends in the Westminster area when she disappeared.

Porter is 5-foot-5 inches tall and weighs 90 pounds. She has brown hair and brown eyes, has a tattoo on her right wrist and a piercing in her lower lip.

Anyone who has seen Porter is asked to contact the Westminster Police Department 303-658-4360, or Detective Calhoon at 303-658-4237. Individuals can also contact Crime Stoppers at 720-913-STOP (7867) or text tips to DMCS 274637.
