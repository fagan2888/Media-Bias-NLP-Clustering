Nightly revival

Circle Of Kingdom Builders Ministries, 604 La. 3043, Opelousas, is hosting a revival at 7 p.m. nightly through March 31, featuring recording artist Apostle Dr. O’Meal O. Reid. For more information, call 337-351-5817 or 337-384-7775.

Baptist Fellowship

The Baptist Fellowship of St. Landry and Vicinity will meet at 7 p.m. Monday at the Greater Mt. Calvary Baptist Church, 134 W. C Alley, Opelousas. The Rev. Kyle Sylvester will do the lesson and the Rev. Kirk Gradnigo will do the sermon. Fellowship President the Rev. Calvin Moore invites all to attend.

Christ Centered Recovery Group

Father’s Embrace Worship Center, 608 S. Market St., Opelousas, is offering a Christ-centered recovery group from 6:30 to 7:30 p.m. every Monday and Friday. The group is open to anyone dealing with problems of drugs, alcohol, gambling or other addictions. Transportation is available. For more information, call the group facilitator Darrell Latin at 337-210-6186 or email him at Darrellatin11@yahoo.com.

Lenten Revival

Holy Ghost Catholic Church, 747 N. Union St., Opelousas, will host a Lenten Revival March 23-25 beginning at 6 p.m. each evening. The Rev. Maurice Nutt will lead the revival that will include praise and worship. For more information, visit hgcatholic.org or call 337-942-2732.

Mount Olive Baptist revival

Mount Olive Baptist Church, 227 W. Church St., Opelousas, will host its “Youth and Revival Gospel Fest” from 6-8 p.m. nightly, March 26-28, with the theme “Are You Listening Youth?” Guest speakers include Sister Vera Hollier, Opelousas Police Chief Donald Thompson and the Rev. Marcus Lazard, with closing remarks by the Rev. Joel S. Greene Sr. For more information, call 337-351-0839 or email sclavine@att.net.

Couple’s Retreat

The Acadian Baptist Center, 1202 Academy Dr., Eunice, is hosting a couples’ retreat on March 27-28 with Jerry and Bayne Pounds as guest speakers. Dr. Bayne Pounds is the assistant dean for academic support and associate professor of psychology at Louisiana College in Pineville. Dr. Jerry Pounds is professor of psychology at Louisiana College and pastor of Baptist Temple Church in Alexandria. Registration with lodging is $75 or $45 without lodging per couple.

Women’s Spiritual Retreat

The Our Lady of the Oaks Jesuit Retreat House in Grand Coteau will host a Women’s Spiritual Retreat May 28-31. A $25 deposit is required to reserve your spot with a suggested donation of $210; however, all are welcome regardless of ability to pay. For more information, call 337-662-5410 or email oloaks@centurytel.net.

Easter Musical Drama

The First Baptist Church of Opelousas, 3835 I-49 S. Service Road, Opelousas, will present the musical drama “Come See the Lamb” for Easter and the public is invited. The free, roughly 90-minute program will be presented at 10:30 a.m. March 29 and then again at 7 p.m. April 3. Call Jackie at 337-942-6794 for more information.

Good Friday Walk

Holy Family, St. Ann and St. Bridget Catholic churches will host a “Walk with the Cross” on Good Friday, April 3, and the public is invited. The event will begin with a Rosary at St. Ann’s, 7248 U.S. 190 in the Mallet community, with the walk beginning at 7 a.m. For more information, call 337-945-3394.
