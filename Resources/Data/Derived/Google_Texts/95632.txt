WILLIAMSON – Jason Allen, Project Manager for Veolia Water, has released the trash collection schedule for the week of Thanksgiving.

Due to the upcoming Thanksgiving Holiday Veolia Water will be picking up the garbage route in the City of Williamson as listed below:

Monday November 23, 2015 – Regular Schedule

Tuesday November 24, 2015 – Tuesday & Wednesday routes

Wednesday November 25, 2015 – Thursday & Friday routes

Thursday November 26, 2015 – Veolia Water OFF

Friday November 27, 2015 – Veolia Water OFF

If you have any questions please feel free to contact City Hall at 304-235-1510 or 304- 235-1626.
