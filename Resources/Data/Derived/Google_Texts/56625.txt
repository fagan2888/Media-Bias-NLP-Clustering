JOHANNESBURG—South African prosecutors will appeal the verdict and sentence in the case of athlete Oscar Pistorius, who shot and killed his girlfriend on Valentine’s Day, 2013, the National Prosecuting Authority said in a statement on Monday, marking a fresh twist in a case that has gripped global attention for over a year.
