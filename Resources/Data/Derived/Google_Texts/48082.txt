One person is behind bars after a hit and run crash Saturday morning in Fort Walton Beach.

According to Fort Walton Beach Police officials, around 2 a.m., they arrived to the intersection of Beal Parkway and Hollywood Boulevard to find a motorcyclist was laying unconscious in the road.

Witnesses were able to identify the car and driver involved in the crash, and officials were able to find the suspect.

Officials arrested Andre Bush, 27, and charged him for leaving the scene of a traffic crash.

At this time police have not released the name of the motorcyclist. The case is still under investigation.
