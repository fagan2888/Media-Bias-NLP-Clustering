A Meridian woman died in a car accident Monday morning in Neshoba County.

Mississippi Highway Patrol says Angeline Wess was traveling north on Highway 15 near County Road 351 at about 12:25 a.m., when she ran off the road and hit a tree. She was pronounced dead on the scene due to the impact of the accident.

Highway patrol officials say no one else was in the vehicle when she wrecked.
