Harris Health spending $70 million on more Ben Taub operating rooms But six rooms will go unused because of a lack of funding

Photo: Cody Duty, Staff The Emergency Center for Ben Taub General Hospital is seen, Friday,... A $70 million expansion project will increase the number of operating rooms at Harris Health System's Ben Taub Hospital from 11 to 18, but funding challenges will leave six of the rooms unused, hospital officials said at a board meeting on Thursday.
