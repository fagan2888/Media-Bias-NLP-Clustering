WASHINGTON (MarketWatch) -- The House Ways and Means Committee voted Tuesday to repeal the Affordable Care Act's tax on medical devices. The vote for repeal of the 2.3% excise tax on sales of devices including pacemakers and stents was 25 to 14. The bill now goes to the House floor. The Senate is considering similar legislation.
