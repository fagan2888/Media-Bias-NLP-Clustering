MILWAUKEE — Milwaukee police need your help locating a vintage car that was stolen from a garage on Milwaukee’s northwest side.

Police say the suspect(s) entered a garage in the 3900 block of N. 79th Street on Wednesday evening, August 26th and stole the vehicle — a two-door, light yellow 1962 Chevy Impala convertible.

The vehicle has Wisconsin license plate: 209-VAX.

Anyone with information in this case is asked to give police a call at 414-935-7360.
