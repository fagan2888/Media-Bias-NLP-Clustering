By Cindy Von Quednow of the Ventura County Star

One person was rescued from a vehicle after a crash in Moorpark Thursday morning, officials said.

The crash was reported about 8:25 a.m. at the intersection of Moorpark and Read roads.

The person who was trapped in an overturned sewage suffered major injuries, according to the Ventura County Fire Department. One other person in the vehicle suffered minor injuries.

The roadway remained blocked hours after the crash, the California Highway Patrol reported.
