Post-Grant Challenges In Life Sciences: A Midyear Assessment

Law360, New York (June 29, 2015, 12:00 PM ET) -- The America Invents Act established inter partes review and post-grant reviews mechanisms to challenge the validity of issued United States patents. These procedures were created to improve patent quality, and were introduced in part because of perceived abusive enforcement by nonpracticing entities in the consumer electronics space. As with most legislative changes, the consequences of these post-grant challenge mechanisms may not be fully appreciated until long after the law has been enacted.
