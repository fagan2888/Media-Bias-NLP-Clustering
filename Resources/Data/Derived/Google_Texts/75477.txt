The Prime of Miss Jean Brodie

By Muriel Spark (1961)

1. It would be churlish to be ungrateful for Maggie Smith's unforgettable film performance as Miss Jean Brodie, but the book has complications and subtleties that the movie can't approach. It is concerned with the dangers and seductions—and possibilities—of glamour, of a charismatic personality.
