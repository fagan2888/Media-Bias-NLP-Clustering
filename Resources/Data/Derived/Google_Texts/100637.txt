If you’re looking for the James Carville and Mary Matalin of the Obama years, consider the Woodhouse brothers.

First, there’s Brad Woodhouse, the former communications director for the Democratic National Committee. His brother is Dallas Woodhouse, an alumnus of the Koch-funded group Americans for Prosperity's North Carolina chapter who led tea party charges against President Barack Obama and Obamacare. This cycle, through his group Carolina Rising, Dallas is aiding Republican candidates his Democratic brother loves to hate.

The siblings are the subjects of a forthcoming documentary, “Woodhouse Divided,” which premiered in Washington Thursday night with the hope it will eventually find its way to a film festival and widespread distribution.
