A 26-year-old Killeen man was killed late Saturday in a single-car crash in southwest Arlington.

Rajarad Williams was pronounced dead around 9:20 p.m. in the 7800 block of Calender Road near Shady Lane, according to the Tarrant County medical examiner.

Williams was driving around 9 p.m. when he lost control of his vehicle and struck two trees. Two boys, ages 4 and 5, were also in the vehicle at the time of the crash. They were transported to the hospital with injuries that are not believed to be life-threatening, Arlington police said.

Police are investigating the cause of the crash.
