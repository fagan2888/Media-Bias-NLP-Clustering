Lehigh, IA

Three firefighters in the central Iowa town of Lehigh have lost their homes to fire this year.



The latest on Friday destroyed the fire chief's home.



Fire Chief Kirk Kelley says he heard a loud banging noise from a closet near his front door just before the fire broke out at 3 a.m. Friday. Kelley, his wife, their two children, ages 2 and 4, and the family dog escaped unharmed.



The cause remains under investigation.



On Oct. 5, Lehigh volunteer firefighter Cody Frazier lost his home in a fire. The cause of that fire also remains under investigation.



A third Lehigh firefighter, Milton Friedel, lost his home in Burnside in April. The cause of that fire was determined to be electrical.
