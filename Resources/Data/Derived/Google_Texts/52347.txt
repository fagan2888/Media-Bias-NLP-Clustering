Key & Peele were too busy to help Obama promote Obamacare last year

Apparently comedy duo Key & Peele were too busy to help President Obama promote Obamacare last year, leading to the president’s Emmy-winning sketch with Zach Galifianakis.

To promote Season 4 of “Key & Peele” on Comedy Central, Keegan Michael-Key and Jordan Peele took over Entertainment Weekly as guest editors. The latest edition also includes an interview with the duo where they revealed their Obama story.
