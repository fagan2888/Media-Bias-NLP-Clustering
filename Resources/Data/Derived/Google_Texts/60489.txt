Obamacare may be one of the largest expansions of the U.S. safety net in the country's history, but a lot of people will have a hard time noticing the difference.

Some states will benefit much less from President Obama's health-care reform law than others, for various reasons -- like whether or not the state opted to expand Medicaid, the general health of the state's population and how many people there already have insurance.
