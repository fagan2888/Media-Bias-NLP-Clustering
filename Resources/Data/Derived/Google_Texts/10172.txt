Police seek help in identifying the suspect in an armed robbery of a Lebanon County pharmacy. It happened at 5:02 pm this evening at the Medicine Shop in the first block of West Main Street in Palmyra Borough.

A man, wearing a fake red beard, and armed with a pump action shotgun with a pistol grip walked into the store and handed a clerk with note demanding prescription pharmaceuticals. Two female clerks were on duty, neither were injured. No customers were in the store during the robbery.

The man ran out the front door headed towards West North Avenue. Borough Police released several surveillance photos of the suspect. He is a white male, about 6 feet tall. He wore black gloves, sunglasses, a grey hoodie, and black bib overalls.

Anyone with information on the case is asked to call Palmyra Borough Police at (717) 838-8189.
