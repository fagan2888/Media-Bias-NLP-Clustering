It was a mushy Valentine's Day message, but they played Barry White in the background... By #teamJET



It makes our hearts melt to see the President of the United States pitch woo to the First Lady for Valentine’s Day on national television. You didn’t get that kind of romantic feeling from FDR and Eleanor, JFK and Jackie, or Ronnie and Nancy.

But Barack and Michelle have no problem being mushy in front of the whole world. So on “The Ellen DeGeneres Show,” they both professed their love for each other — “I Obamacare about you more than you even know,” said the POTUS. There was even Barry White music in the background!
