First-degree murder charges have been field against a Westminster man in the disappearance of a Pueblo teen.

Christopher Adam Waide is also charged with sexual assault and tampering with physical evidence in the disappearance of 19-year-old Lea Chali Porter, according to the Adams County District Attorney’s Office.

Porter was reported missing to Westminster police on June 5.

Waide is scheduled to appear at 10 a.m. Friday in Adams County District Court to be advised of the charges.

Investigators have not said whether they’ve found Porter’s body.
