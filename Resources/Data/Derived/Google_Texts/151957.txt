Fathers who have gone above and beyond were honored today with a celebration of dads in Meridian.

An enrichment program called the wave honored fathers in this area by spotlighting men who have had an impact on the community. Steve Mosley and Henry Granger are among those who were recognized. Those who were recognized at today's celebration, say they feel honored to have been a part.

"Throughout my life, I've received many honors," Father Henry Granger says. "This ranks among the highest, to know someone thinks well enough of you of the contributions that you're making in your home community to bestow an award like this. I will forever cherish this ward."
