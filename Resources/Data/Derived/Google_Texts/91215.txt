SALT LAKE CITY—A leader of polygamous sect pleaded not guilty on Wednesday to fraud and money laundering charges that prosecutors say stemmed from a wide-ranging food-stamp fraud scheme.

Lyle Jeffs—who runs the day-to-day operations in the polygamous community of Hildale, Utah—entered the plea Wednesday during his first court appearance in Salt Lake City, wearing a jail jumpsuit and looking somber. A judge ordered him to stay in jail...
