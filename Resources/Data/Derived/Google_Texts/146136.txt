The 5 Practice Areas Scrambling To Hire

Law360, New York (September 3, 2015, 9:24 PM ET) -- Five practice areas are currently inundated with work and, as a result, are fighting for talented attorneys by offering higher pay, signing bonuses and other perks, a report released Wednesday said.



New hires with backgrounds in these booming fields are in short supply, intensifying competition and driving up salaries, Robert Half Legal's "2016 Salary Guide for the Legal Field" said. The practice areas include litigation, general commercial law, health care, real estate and intellectual property.
