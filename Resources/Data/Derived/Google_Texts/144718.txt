Actor Sunny Leone accepted ALS Ice Bucket Challenge to spread the awareness for ALS (Lou Gehrig's Disease).

The Ice Bucket Challenge campaign involves dumping a bucket of ice water over one's head and then challenging someone else to do the same.

The initiative has gone viral, with prominent celebrities throughout the world attempting it and even filming themselves doing the act.

Sunny Leone dared Yo Yo Honey Singh to take up the challenge.
