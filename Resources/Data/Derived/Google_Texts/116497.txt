On Saturday, Ken Jordan and Scott Kirkland of The Crystal Method sat down with Journal Sentinel music writer Piet Levy before the DJ duo's headlining performance at the Miller Lite Oasis.

The EDM duo has been performing for over 20 years and released a new self-titled album at the beginning of the year. In the video, Jordan and Kirkland discuss how electronic dance music has changed over time and what's in their future.
