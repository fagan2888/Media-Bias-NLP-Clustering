UPDATE 11:51 A.M.

Panama City Beach Police are investigating the death of 21-year old Coty Trenell Haywood of Perry, Florida.

A little after 8 a.m. Saturday police received a call of an unresponsive person in a hotel room at the Boardwalk Beach Resort.

When they arrived on scene they found Haywood dead.

No foul play is suspected at this time.

An autopsy is set for a later date.
