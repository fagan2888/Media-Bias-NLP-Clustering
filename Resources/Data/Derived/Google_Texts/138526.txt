Average demographic characteristics for the average Anchorage Project Access patient in 2015 are the single Caucasian female 50-59 years of age. Although many of our patients may be unemployed for a variety of reasons, over 30 percent are working full or part time or are self-employed without health benefits. Education remains fairly consistent for our patients with the majority being high school graduates. However, over 38 percent have attended some college or are college graduates. The homeless situation continues to be a major problem in Anchorage and accounts for more than 48 percent of our patients. The typical Anchorage Project Access patient is the person who helps you with your groceries, cuts your hair or sits next to you at church. She is your neighbor.
