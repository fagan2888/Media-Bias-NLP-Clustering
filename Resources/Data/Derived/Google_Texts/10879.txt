Double Take 'Toons: Obamacare Migration?

Republicans have been linking their refusal to enact comprehensive immigration reform to the problems plaguing the implementation of the Affordable Care Act. Lisa Benson argues that President Obama lacks the necessary focus to do a good job, while Nate Beeler advises the GOP to think twice before it sacrifices the opportunity to play a role in landmark legislation in the name of opposing the ACA.
