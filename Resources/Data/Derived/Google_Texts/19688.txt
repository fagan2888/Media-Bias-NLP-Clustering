DO YOU KNOW HIM?

Police serached the area Saturday morning but did not locate a suspect. (Source: KPHO.KTVK)

The suspect is a white man in his 40s. He is between 5 feet 8 inches and 5 feet 10 inches tall with a 'muscular' build. (Source: Gilbert Police Dept.)

The Gilbert Police Department released a sketch of a man in his 40s who attempted to sexually assault a 14-year-old girl near a community pool Saturday.

The suspect is further described as between 5’8” and 5’10” tall with a muscular build. He has brown and gray slicked-back hair and a goatee. He was possibly wearing jeans, police said.

Saturday’s attempted attack took place just after 7:40 a.m. while the girl was sitting in a park near a community pool in the area of 3700 E. Yeager Drive.

[RELATED: Gilbert PD investigates reported sex assault of young girl]

The suspect fled on foot and the girl notified police.

Officers searched the area Saturday but did not locate the man.

If you know the man in the sketch, please contact the Gilbert Police Department at 480-503-6500. (Click or tap the number to call from this story on your mobile device.)
