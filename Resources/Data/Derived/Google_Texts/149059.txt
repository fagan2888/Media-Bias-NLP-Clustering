With Arizona's Presidential Preference Election just more than a week away, a list of 60 polling places has been released by the Maricopa County recorder.

Unlike typical elections, voters on March 22 will be able to go to any of the 60 polling places located throughout the county to vote.

These polling places are for this election only, Maricopa County Recorder Helen Purcell said.

[LIST: Find a polling place near you]

Voters will have to go to their assigned polling place to vote in the Aug. 30 Primary and Nov. 8 General Election.

Polling places will be open from 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. on Election Day.
