Driver Killed After Crashing Into Pond Near CU South Boulder Campus

A driver was killed in Boulder Sunday after he apparently lost control of the vehicle, went off the road, crossed a bike path and crashed into a pond.

The accident happened shortly before 8 a.m. as the driver was taking the ramp onto eastbound US 36 off of southbound Foothills Parkway. The area is south of CU Boulder.

The driver was pulled from the vehicle and rushed to the hospital, but did not survive. His name has not been released. Investigators say he was not a student.

Colorado State Patrol is investigating the accident.
