Today

• The 18th annual Erie Town Fair and Hot Air Balloon Festival is today. Balloons will launch at 6 a.m. at the Colorado National Golf Club, 2700 Vista Parkway, Erie, weather permitting. The fair is from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. in downtown Erie on Briggs, Wells, Kattel and Moffatt Streets. Evening activities with music and barbecue are from 4 to 9 p.m. at Coal Creek Park, 575 Kattell, Erie. Go to eriechamber.org.

• Toddler-Palooza is at 10 a.m. at WOW! Children's Museum, 110 N. Harrison Ave., Lafayette. All activities, including snacks, arts and crafts, and hands on activities, will be geared toward younger children. Cost is $9. Go to wowchildrensmuseum.org.

• Wildflower Hike of Rabbit Mountain begins at 10 a.m. at Rabbit Mountain Open Space, northeast of Lyons on North 55th Street. Learn about the wildflowers on a two mile hike. Contact Larry Colbenson 303-678-6214 or lcolbenson@bouldercounty.org.

•The Strawberry Festival and Antique Show is from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. today and 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Sunday, May 18, at the Boulder County Fairground, 9595 Nelson Road, Longmont. Enjoy barbecue, strawberry shortcake, art show, bake sale and more. Cost is $5; kids under 12 are free. Go to facebook.com/stvrainhistory.

• The History and Mystery Tour of downtown Erie is at 5 p.m. Saturday. Cost is $12.50 per ticket, $20 for couples, and $10 for those 12 and under or 60 and over. Call 303-775-7137 or email spookslongmont@gmail.com for reservations.

• The Longs Peak Chorus presents "The Saga of Sweetheart Sally: A Story of Love Lost and Found" at 2:30 p.m. and 7 p.m. at Vance Brand Auditorium, 600 E. Mountain View Ave., Longmont. The performances feature special guest quartet Fastlane. Cost is $15 in advance, $17 at the door, $10 for students; 12 and under are free. For tickets, email Tickets@LongsPeakChorus.org.

Upcoming

• Longmont Sculling Club is hosting an open house at 9 a.m. Sunday, May 18, at the southeasy corner of Union Reservoir, 461 Country Road 26. Watch demonstrations, see the facilities, and learn about boats. Go to longmontsculling.com.

• Crafts and Trades of the Olden Days is from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the Agricultural Heritage Center, 8348 Ute Highway 66, Longmont. Learn about the jobs and skills the pioneers had to have for everyday life. Contact Larry Colbenson 303-678-6214 or lcolbenson@bouldercounty.org.

• Frank's Ride for Children poker run sign up is from at 8 to 10 a.m. Sunday, May 18, at the Longmont Loyal Order of the Moose, 2200 Pratt St., Longmont. Proceeds go to Make-A-Wish Colorado. The run starts at 11 a.m. Breakfast is available for a donation of $5, registration is $20. Go to franksride.com to register.
