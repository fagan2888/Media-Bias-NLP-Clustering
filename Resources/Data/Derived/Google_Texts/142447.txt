Sioux Falls, SD

A casino in central Sioux Falls was robbed Saturday night.

The Recovery Room Casino located at 1004 W. 18th street was robbed by a male suspect with a handgun just after 7 p.m Saturday. The man displayed a gun and demanded money from an employee. He then fired one shot into the wall of the building and ran out without any cash. No one was injured in the incident.

The suspect was described as a Native American male, 5'10", and 165 lbs, wearing a dark grey or black hooded sweatshirt, dark pants, and a blue bandana over his face.

Anyone with any information is asked to call Crime Stoppers.
