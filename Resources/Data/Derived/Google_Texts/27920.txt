A car accident at the intersection of U.S. Highway 231 and East 24th Plaza in Panama City causes a lane closure Sunday afternoon.

According to Florida Highway Patrol reports, 49-year old Bruce Baxter was stopped at the stop sign on East 24th Plaza waiting to turn North onto U.S. Highway 231.

Meanwhile, 17-year old Angelica Mathes was traveling southbound on U.S. Highway 231.

Baxter failed to yield to the other vehicle, and the two cars collided.

No one was injured.
