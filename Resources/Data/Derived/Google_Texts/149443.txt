Aunt Martha's Health Center in partnership with The South Suburban Health Alliance invites you to attend a FREE Community Health Fair on Saturday, October 24, 2015, at Martin Luther King Park, 150th and Dells Parkway, Harvey, Illinois, from 11 a.m. - 3 p.m. This event is open to the public, and everyone is welcome to attend.

The health fair will offer free health screenings and assessments for blood pressure, body mass index, blood glucose, cholesterol, stress, and more.

Join us for a day of food, fun and fitness for the whole family.

The fair also includes a wide variety of other activities, including:

• Health related services for seniors 65 and older

• Affordable Care Act (Obamacare) health insurance enrollment

• Confidential HIV testing

• Corrective and therapeutic chiropractic care

• Life insurance and financial planning consultation

• Raffle and giveaway prizes

• Fitness/Workout classes

• Much more

Local vendors will be at the health fair to share health information, including:

Babie Shea by Renee, Oak Street Health, Access Community Health Network, Lupus Society of Illinois, Ingalls Hospital, IDCFS-children Family Services, Catholic Charities - South Suburban Senior Services, Baps Bags & Bling, Cook County Department of Public Health, Primerica Financial Services, Aunt Martha's Youth Service Center, Cook County Health & Hospital Systems, The Cradle, South Suburban Family Shelter, Illinois Public Health Association and Chiro One.

For more information, please call (708) 836-5457 or visit www.auntmarthas.org/healthfair
