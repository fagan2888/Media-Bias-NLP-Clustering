A 22-year-old man arrested on Denver’s 16th Street Mall on Sunday is facing arson and murder charges in Golden, where a fire destroyed the home where he lived.

As firefighters entered the home at 711 Iowa St. on Saturday, they found the body of man. His identity has not been confirmed, Golden police said Sunday night.

Thomas Kevin Laperch is being held in the Jefferson County Detention Facility.

Golden police are asking for the public’s help to determine Laperch’s whereabouts around the time of the fire. Anyone with information can call 303-384-8045.
