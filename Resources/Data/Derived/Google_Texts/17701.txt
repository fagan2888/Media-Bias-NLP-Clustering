A Kentucky man is in the Bay County Jail for several counts of sexual abuse.

44 year old Robert Eric Price was arrested by the US Marshal Florida Regional Fugitive Task Force at 9 Friday night.

He was found at the Value Place Motel in Callaway.

Price was wanted by the Henry County Sheriff's Office in Kentucky for three first degree sexual abuse charges.

Price is now at the Bay County Jail pending extradition back to Kentucky.
