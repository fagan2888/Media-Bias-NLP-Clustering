(Carie Canterbury / Daily Record) Hundreds of children of all ages lined up Saturday to take part in the annual Trunk-or-Treat event hosted by the Colorado Department of Corrections employees at Macon Plaza. Trunk-or-Treat organizers Jim Moore and Sarah Cordova said about 1,200 pounds of candy was collected by the Colorado State Penitentiary and Centennial Correctional Facility, and Canteen Services also donated a 4' X 4' X 4' pallet of treats.
