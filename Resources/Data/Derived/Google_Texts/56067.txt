Law360, New York (March 18, 2015, 12:39 PM ET) -- After becoming subject to rehabilitation in Iowa and losing its insurance license in Nebraska only a couple of months earlier, CoOportunity Health was ordered into liquidation on March 2, 2015. The liquidation of CoOportunity marks the end of an insurance company that was licensed barely two years ago.
