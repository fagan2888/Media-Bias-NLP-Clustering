SNL didn’t pull any punches with its most recent skit about Obama announcing the underqualified Ron Klain as Czar for the Ebola crisis.

Played by comedian Taran Killam, Klain is introduced by Obama (Jay Pharoah) to journalists for questions, only to bumble and make a fool of himself.

He gives way to Al Sharpton (Kenan Thompson) who shows off his ability to handle pronunciations like the best of them.
