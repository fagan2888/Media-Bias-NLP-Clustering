One man was seriously injured in a crash on southbound Interstate 25 near County Line Road on Saturday afternoon, officials said.

The Lone Tree Police Department responded to a two-vehicle accident in which one man was ejected from his vehicle and transported to an area hospital with serious injuries, according to a Lone Tree Police Department news release.

All but one lane of southbound I-25 was shut down for about one hour along with one lane of northbound I-25 due to concrete debris being in the roadway from the crash.

All lanes are now open.

The accident remains under investigation, and police have no further details about the crash at this time.
