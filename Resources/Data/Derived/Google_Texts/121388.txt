Four people suffered minor injuries after a city bus and car collided Saturday afternoon in downtown Wichita.

the accident happened around 2:30 p.m. at the intersection of Central and Broadway.

Police said a car was heading westbound on Central when the driver likely ran a red light and drove in front of the bus.

Four men on the bus suffered minor injuries and were taken to area hospitals to be checked out.

The driver of the car was not hurt.

The intersection has since reopened.
