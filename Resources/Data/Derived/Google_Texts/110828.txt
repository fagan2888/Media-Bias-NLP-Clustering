A man has been hospitalized and his father is in jail following a stabbing early Sunday morning in Wellington.

Officers were called to the report of a stabbing in the 1100 block of South Washington, near Kansas Street. Sumner NewsCow reports a 43-year-old man and his 22-year-old son were involved in a physical altercation when the father allegedly stabbed his son in the chest.

The son was taken to a Wichita hospital for treatment. His name and current condition are unknown.

His father was booked into the Sumner County Jail. He faces charges of aggravated battery and domestic battery.
