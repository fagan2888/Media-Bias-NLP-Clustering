MANITOU SPRINGS — All roads in and out of Manitou Springs are closed because of high water and flood danger. There are no mandatory evacuations.

Colorado Springs D-11 is evacuating Howbert Elementary School on 31st Street.

U.S. 24 is closed westbound between 31st Street and Cascade and eastbound will stay closed from Cascade to Cave of the Winds. The Emergency Operations Center communicated a break in the weather for the moment, adding that a new storm cell was expected to travel over El Paso County bringing heavy rain over the region between 3 p.m. and midnight.

The body of a man was recovered from flood waters in Fountain Creek just before 5 a.m. Thursday by the Colorado Springs Fire Department’s heavy rescue unit.
