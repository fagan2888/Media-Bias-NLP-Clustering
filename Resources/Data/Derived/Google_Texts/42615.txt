Animals, Easter eggs, cotton candy, Zoo World has just about everything for the Easter Weekend and now they also have new logos to show off.

Zoo World revealed three new logos Saturday, the main logo will be the silhouette giraffe in front of the world.

In February, the zoo started a logo contest and the winner was selected last month.

Zoo World's director talked about what they have going on during the rest of their Easter celebration.

"We will have all of our friendly little animals around to say hi to people," explained Kate Wonko, Zoo World's Director. "We have bunnies, chicks, baby llamas, baby alligators. We have clowns, face painting, a magician tomorrow, we have balloon animals, cotton candy. There is so much to do, it's just a great time."

Zoo World will open Sunday at 9:30 a.m. for more Easter celebrations.
