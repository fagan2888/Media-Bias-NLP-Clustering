"I'm one of those people who believes that austerity is not necessarily bad. I think that there is some good that comes from this. I've been in organizations before where in times of declining budgets, we have actually been able to innovate and do the kind of thinking about the future that we weren't able to do when there was a lot of money out there."

Lt. Gen. Robert E. Schmidle Jr., U.S. Marine Corps, Principal Deputy Director of Cost Assessment and Program Evaluation, U.S. Defense Department...
