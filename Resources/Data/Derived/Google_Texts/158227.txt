Family and friends put their skills to the test bright and early this morning at Pima Community College.The college partnered up with the U.S. Army and Cholla High Magnet School to host its first Desert Mayhem fitness challenge.

It included several different physical challenges and endurance activities like flipping a giant tire and carrying water jugs.

All the money raised at the event goes to the southern arizona cancer foundation.
