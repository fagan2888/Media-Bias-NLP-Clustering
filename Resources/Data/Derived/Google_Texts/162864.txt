Police are searching for a man who allegedly robbed a Dollar General store near Wichita's Delano District.

The robbery happened around 10 p.m. Friday at the Dollar General in the 1200 block of West Douglas. That's just west of Seneca.

Sgt. Roger Runft said two employees were in the store when a man in a clown mask entered the store. The employees said the suspect had a weapon, possibly a stun gun, and demanded cash from the register.

The suspect was able to get away with an unknown amount of money. No injuries were reported.

Officers described the suspect as standing about six feet tall and weighing 200 pounds. Along with the clown mask, he wore cover-alls.

Anyone with information is asked to call 911 or Crime Stoppers at (316) 267-2111.
