The Oklahoma City Thunder announced Friday its roster for the 2015 Southwest Airlines Orlando Pro Summer League.

The Thunder's roster includes Mitch McGary, Dakari Johnson, Semaj Christon, Frank Gaines, Richard Solomon, Marquis Teague and Talib Zanna. Assistant coach Darko Rajakovic will be the team's coach during summer league play.

The Thunder's summer league team will play four games between Saturday, July 4, and Friday, July 10, at the Amway Center Practice Court in Orlando.

Oklahoma City plays Charlotte at 2 p.m. Saturday, Orlando's Blue squad at 2 p.m. Monday, the Clippers at 4 p.m. Tuesday and Memphis at 4 p.m. Thursday. The Thunder's final opponent and tipoff time for its July 10 game have not been announced.
