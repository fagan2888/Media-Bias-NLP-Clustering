Jen Torpey and Tim Myers were married in Skaneateles, N.Y., on Sept. 26, 2015.

Laurie Durgan, an associate minister from the Unity Church in Delray Beach, Fla., officiated.

The matron of honor was Melissa O'Malley, the bride's sister.

The best men were Teddy Myers and Daniel Myers, the groom's brothers. Annie Myers, the groom's sister, was the flower girl, and Liam O'Malley, the bride's nephew, was the ring bearer.

Jen is the daughter of Terry and Linda Torpey, of Boulder. Tim is the son of Tim and Kim Myers, of Vesta, N.Y.

The couple honeymooned in Paris.

They reside in Delray Beach, Fla.
