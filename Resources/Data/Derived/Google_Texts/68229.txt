Three presidential campaigns visited Dutchess County in the last week.

On April 12, Democratic candidate Bernie Sanders appeared at Marist College in Poughkeepsie. Former REM frontman Michael Stipe introduced Sanders, who blamed income inequality and economic hardship on Wall Street greed and cost-prohibitive college education.
