Health Care Regulation To Watch In 2016

Law360, New York (December 24, 2015, 8:37 PM ET) -- Major regulatory initiatives in 2016 will profoundly affect False Claims Act liability, Medicare reimbursement and enforcement of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, attorneys say.
