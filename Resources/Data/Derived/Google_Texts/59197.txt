Miami Hospital Says Humana Underpaid For Services

Law360, Miami (April 23, 2015, 5:09 PM ET) -- Miami-area hospital network Baptist Hospital of Miami Inc. on Wednesday sued Humana Health Insurance Co. of Florida Inc. for allegedly underpaying for services and excluding it from the network of providers established for the insurer's plans offered through the state's health care exchange.



In a suit filed in Miami's Eleventh Judicial Circuit Court, Baptist, which operates a number of hospitals and surgery centers in the area, said that Humana breached a hospital participation agreement by paying improper amounts for goods and services provided by the hospital...
