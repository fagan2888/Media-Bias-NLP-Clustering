Letter: GOP to Blame for Lack of Health Care

The News Herald published an outstanding article Sunday, May 8, thanks to Katie Landeck, on people who fall “Between the Cracks,” because Florida chose not to expand Medicaid for the indigent. I suppose our governor doesn’t think poor people here need medical care, or, more likely, he doesn’t care.
