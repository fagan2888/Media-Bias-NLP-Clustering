The man reported missing in Knowlton Township on Friday has been found, unharmed.

According to State Police at the Hope station, the Silver Alert was issued for 80-year-old George Anderson. He was last seen at 109 Mt. Pleasant Road, Knowlton, driving a 1994 red Dodge Grand Caravan.

The alert was issued Friday evening. Anderson was found safe several hours later in West Point, N.Y., an officer at the Hope barracks reported Saturday afternoon. Further details were not available.
