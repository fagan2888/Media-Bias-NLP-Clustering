PLYMOUTH -- A Plymouth man was arrested late Tuesday after allegedly threatening his wife with a toy gun.

According to a release from the Marshall County Sheriff's Department, officers were called to a house in the 13000 block of Nutmeg Ridge Drive shortly after 11 p.m. Tuesday on report of a man threatening his wife with a gun in the presence of a child.

Officers talked to the man who admitted having a toy gun, according to police. David A. Tanner, 46, was arrested and charged with intimidation with a deadly weapon. Tanner was booked into the Marshall County Jail on a $1,500 bond.
