Rapid City, SD

Rapid City police say an officer has shot and killed a man who attacked him with a knife.



The officer was called to remove an unwanted man from a house at the Lakota Community Homes just after 6 p.m. Saturday.



Police say Officer Anthony Meirose was standing in the door when he was attacked by a knife-wielding man. Meirose shot the man multiple times.



The man was pronounced dead at Rapid City Regional Hospital. He is identified as 30-year-old Allen Locke of Rapid City.



Meirose and other people in the house were not hurt.



Meirose, who joined the force in July 2013, is on standard administrative leave.



The South Dakota Division of Criminal Investigation and the Pennington County Sheriff's Office are investigating the shooting.
