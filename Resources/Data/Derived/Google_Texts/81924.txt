Aetna Inc. boosted its profit outlook for the year as the health insurer’s revenue rose in the most recent period, although rising medical costs continued to weigh on its earnings.

The company’s profit was in line with analysts’ expectations, while its revenue topped them. Aetna said it now expects at least $7 a share in earnings for 2015, up 10 cents from its previous projection.
