The 2015 Girl Scout cookie kickoff was held in the EMEPA auditorium today. This marks the official start date of cookie sales. Cookie rally is designed to educate the scouts on cookie selling tips, names of cookies and marketing techniques. Booth sales for cookies will begin in February at Walmart, Lowes and Petco. "We just have a whole bunch of activities going on. One of them is talking about keeping you safe when you go door to door selling cookies or on the road,” Emma Taylor says. “Just doing some fun conversations. We made this little lanyard that has the prices and allergy information."

The Girl Scouts will be selling cookies until March, so you better go ahead and start ordering!
