One person has died following an accident at Museum Boulevard and the Central and Seneca street change.

It happened shortly after 2:30 a.m. when a white-colored vehicle was involved in a one-vehicle crash.

"Appears at this time that did not make the curve here and collided into the wall," said Capt. Dan East with the Wichita Police Department.

The driver was pronounced dead at the scene. Another man was transported to Via Christi St. Francis with minor injuries.

Both men are in their mid 30s. Their names have not been released at this time.

Police are still investigating on whether speed or alcohol were factors in this accident.
