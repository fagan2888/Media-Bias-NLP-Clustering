Larry Robbins, founder of Glenview Capital Management, bet big on Obamacare on won.

Larry Robbins, a self-made billionaire form Alpine, is the lone Bergen County resident on the annual Forbes 400 list.

Robbins, 45, resides in Alpine. He founded Glenview Capital Management and has been “one of the hottest hedge fund managers in recent years,” the magazine said, because of his big bet on Obamacare.

He bought shares of hospital companies and health insurers, thinking they would benefit from the Affordable Care Act.

Robbins moved up 75 spots on the Top 400 list from 2014 when he was ranked No. 368. He is married with four children.
