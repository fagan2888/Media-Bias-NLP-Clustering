A Goldsboro man is wanted for holding up a store while armed with a knife.

Goldsboro police say 30-year-old Quentin Barnes robbed the Family Dollar store on Wayne Memorial Drive in Goldsboro in December of 2015.

In a renewed effort to catch him, police put out a notice on social media over the weekend.

Police say that his last known address was 1303 North George Street in Goldsboro and that he also may frequent Terrance Court on South John Street.

Police say that Barnes is considered armed and dangerous.

Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to contact Investigator Zwirblia at (919) 580-4242.
