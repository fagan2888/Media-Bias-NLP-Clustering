MOSCOW, Oct 20 (Reuters) - The Russian embassy in Damascus said on Tuesday it had no information about reports that three Russians had been killed fighting alongside Syrian government forces.

"There is no information about the alleged 'deaths of three Russians' or 'three Russian servicemen' in Syria, as reported by Reuters," RIA news agency quoted the embassy as saying.
