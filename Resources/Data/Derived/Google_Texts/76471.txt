Police officers with the Meridian Police Department were called to a strong armed robbery Sunday evening.

It happened around 6:15 p.m. at the Econo Lodge on South Frontage Rd. Det. Dareall Thompson says a man stated he was assaulted by three black females and one black male.

Metro Ambulance was called to the scene and transported the victim to an area hospital to be checked out. The case is under investigation.

If you have any information on this strong armed robbery, you are asked to contact the Meridian Police Dept. or Crimestoppers at 855-485-TIPS.
