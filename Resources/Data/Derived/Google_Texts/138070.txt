The US Geological Survey confirms a 4.4 magnitude earthquake at 4: 20 a.m.

The quake is centered around Medford, Oklahoma, about 21 miles north of Enid and 53 miles from Arkansas City, Kansas.

Several KAKE News viewers have called and messaged to report feeling the quake around the viewing area.

Sedgwick County dispatchers said they received two calls of people reporting the earthquake and no reports of damage or injuries.
