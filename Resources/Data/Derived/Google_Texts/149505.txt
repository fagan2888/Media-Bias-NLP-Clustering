Mishawaka Police say they got a call about a fire at the Village Green Mobile Home Park in the 1400 block of Candlewood Lane around 3:30 Wednesday morning.

A mobile home there was fully engulfed in flames when crews arrived to the scene.

Fire officials on scene say two dogs and a cat may have died in the fire.

They say everyone else got out of the home safely.

Investigators are still working to figure out what started the fire.
