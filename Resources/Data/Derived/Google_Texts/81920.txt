MUMBAI: Jammu & Kashmir Bank today sold 1.12 lakh shares of UB group company McDowell Holdings for an estimated Rs 35.42 lakh through the open market route.

According to bulk deal information available with the bourses, Jammu & Kashmir Bank offloaded the scrips of McDowell Holdings at an average price of Rs 31.63 apiece. Earlier this week as well, Jammu & Kashmir Bank had sold 70,000 shares of McDowell Holdings for Rs 22.68 lakh.
