A Bay County man is in jail after sheriff's officials say he shot his father a little before midnight Friday.

Reports state, 18-year old Carlos Peebles, and his father had been arguing earlier in the evening, the two went outside when Peebles allegedly shot his father, and then fled.

When sheriff's officials arrived to the scene on Bob Little Road in Callaway, they foiund the victim on the porch with a gunshot wound.

He was taken to the hospital, and is in stable condition.

Bay County Sheriff's officials found Peebles around 3 a.m. walking along Tyndall Parkway.

When officials approached him, the handgun used in the shooting was found on him.
