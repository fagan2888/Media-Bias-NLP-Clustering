Neshoba Co. Sheriff Tommy Waddell says authorities responded to the Golden Moon Casino around 1:00 p.m. Sunday.

He says they found a juvenile with a gunshot wound. The victim was inside of a vehicle in the parking lot. Waddell says the shooting is believed to be an isolated incident and authorities are not looking for a suspect. He says they will be reviewing surveillance footage to try and figure out what happened. More information on this investigation will be provided when it becomes available to us.

Pearl River Resort released this statement Sunday evening:

“Pearl River Resort is heartbroken by the tragedy that occurred earlier today in the Resort’s parking lot. Our thoughts and prayers go out to the victim’s family. We are fully cooperating with the Neshoba County Sheriff’s Department in the investigation of this incident.”
