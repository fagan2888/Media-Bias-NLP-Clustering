BigLaw Engaged in 'Stealth' Benefits Cost Shifting

Law360, New York (April 4, 2014, 7:34 PM ET) -- BigLaw benefits managers are increasingly engaged in “stealth” cost shifting to employees, according to a recent survey and analysis, as firms cut their health care overhead while trying to keep the most visible parts of their benefit plans status quo.



The moves in recent years by many large firms to offer restructured plans and put more financial burden on associates and staffers has helped put inflation for the legal industry well above the national average, according to report by health care broker and consultant firm Frenkel...
