Knoxville Panthers are at it again.

The seventh annual Coaches vs. Cancer event broke its own record.

Knoxville has been the No. 1 high school in the nation for the fundraiser the past two years.

Preliminary totals estimate that Panthers have raised more than $60,000 this year, bringing their seven-year total to more than a quarter-million dollars.
