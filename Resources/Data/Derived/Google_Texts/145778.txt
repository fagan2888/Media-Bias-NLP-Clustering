Charleston County deputies named a man they are looking for in connection with a deadly shooting Saturday evening on James Island.

Deputies are looking for Clifford Whaley, Jr., and say he is considered armed and dangerous.

The shooting happened Saturday afternoon on the 1000-block of Honeyhill Road. Deputies responded just after 5 p.m., and say they found one victim who died of gunshot wounds.

The Charleston County coroner has identified the victim as 43-year-old Irvin Green of James Island.

Anyone with information on the whereabouts of Whaley should call the Charleston County Sheriff's Office's detective office at (843) 554-2475 or Crime Stoppers at (843) 554-1111.
