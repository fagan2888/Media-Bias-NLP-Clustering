An arrest warrant has been issued for a man accused of sexually assaulting multiple children at a metro daycare.

Police issued a warrant for D’angelo Evans Friday, February 19.

According to court documents, Evans is accused of touching and kissing children while working at Winning Beginnings near NW 122nd and Rockwell.

He has since been fired.
