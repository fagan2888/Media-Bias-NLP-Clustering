Gymnasts from across the country competed Sunday in the 11th Annual Edgewater Classic held in Panama City Beach.

Sunday, was the final day of competition for the event, where more than 500 athletes from seven different states competed.

Teams came from as far as Michigan for the regional meet. Athletes showed off their skills on the balance beam, uneven bars, vault and even some floor exercises.

"It's so exciting that these kids come from so far, as far as Texas and Michigan, because they get to see the beautiful beaches, the great attractions, and a great gymnastics competition at Edgewater Beach Resort. It's very well run and everyone just keeps coming back," said Pam Kitchens, a coach for Edgewater Gymnastics
