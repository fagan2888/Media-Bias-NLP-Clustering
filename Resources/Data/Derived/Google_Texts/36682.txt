Two DeFuniak Springs residents are behind bars after they were arrested during a traffic stop Sunday morning.

Douglass Harvell, 31, and a 17-year-old white female were near the intersection of Highway 331 South and Highway 90 when they were stopped by Walton County Sheriff's officials.

Deputies used a K-9 during the traffic stop and found a stolen firearm, a number of narcotics and $2,500 in cash. Harvell was charged with nine counts; including possession of narcotics, and possession of a stolen firearm. The female juvenile was charged with three counts, including possession of a controlled substance and drug paraphernalia.

Both were booked into the Walton County Jail.
