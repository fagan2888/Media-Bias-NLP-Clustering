Sisseton, SD

Colter Richard Arbach has been located and identified as one of the four people shot and killed this morning in Sisseston.

According to authorities, Arbach was the shooter of the other individuals before taking his own life.

Investigators are continuing to search for individuals who may have been at the scene at the time of the shooting.

Authorities do not believe the public is in danger at this time.
