A 6-year-old girl is killed when the vehicle she was riding in was struck head on yesterday morning in Sheridan County.

According to the Kansas Highway Patrol, Rileighann Fisher, 6, was killed when a vehicle driven 28-year-old Jami Stevenson, of Quinter, was struck head-on by a tractor trailer.

Stevenson was driving on Kansas Highway 23 when she tried to pass a tractor trailer in dense fog and was hit by the other semi.

Stevenson and semi-driver John Mosier were taken to the hospital with injuries.
