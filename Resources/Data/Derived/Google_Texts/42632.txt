Police have identified a suspect in the fatal shooting of a man in southeast D.C. on July 24.

They're looking for Eric McDuffie, 23, who has been charged with murder.

Police say he shot and killed Antonio McCallister, of southeast Washington, in the 2700 block of Langston Place SE, a few blocks east of Fort Stanton Park and north of a police station.

Anyone who sees McDuffie should call 911.

If you have information that could help find McDuffie, call police at (202) 727-9099 or submit anonymous information by texting 50411.

There's a reward of up to $25,000 for anyone who provides information which leads to the arrest and conviction of someone responsible for any homicide committed in D.C.
