Good Luck Guessing With The Gov't On Gainsharing

Law360, New York (December 19, 2014, 3:31 PM ET) -- It’s frustrating when the government opposes a good idea and makes it illegal. But gainsharing presents an even more frustrating situation for hospitals and physicians. Why? Because the government continually sends signals that it approves of gainsharing, but it won’t take concrete steps to change the law that flatly makes gainsharing illegal.
