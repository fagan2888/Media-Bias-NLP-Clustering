The Kansas City Royals drafted Thomas Jefferson alum Brennan Henry in the 17th round with the 513th pick.

Henry — at 6-foot-4, 205-pounds — graduated with Rockies first-round pick Kyle Freeland in 2011. He had a 9-2 record with a 3.59 ERA and 101 strikeouts last season.

Ralston Valley RHP Jordan Holloway was selected by the Marlins in the 20th round with the 587th overall pick. The 6-foot-4, 190-pounder went 6-1 with a 2.60 ERA and 50 strikeouts in 43 innings pitched for the Mustangs last season.

Oral Roberts RHP Gavin Glanz, a 2011 Arvada West graduate at 6-foot-2, 205 pounds, was selected by the Colorado Rockies in the 23rd round with the 683rd overall pick. He finished the year with a 3-5 record, but had a 3.27 ERA and 50 strikeouts.

Glanz is the third local player the Rockies have selected in the draft, along with Freeland (first round) and Regis Jesuit SS Max George (sixth round).

Toronto selected Grand Junction first baseman Owen Taylor as the 804th pick in the 27th round. Taylor hit .266 with two doubles, two triples and 15 RBIs.

Colorado Mesa SS Nate Robertson, a 2011 Montrose grad, was selected by Arizona in the 27th round with the 810th pick. Robertson hit .356 with 13 doubles, six home runs and 48 RBIs. He also had 25 stolen bases.

Texas-Arlington RHP Jess Amedee, a Rocky Mountain grad, was selected by Pittsburgh in the 27th round with the 821st pick.

Another Rocky Mountain graduate, first baseman Carl Stajduhar, was chosen by Atlanta two picks later. Stajduhar led the state in home runs (13) and led Rocky Mountain to the 5A state title.

Kansas RHP Jordan Piche, a 2011 Resurrection Christian graduate, was selected in the 28th round by the Los Angeles Angels with the 839th overall pick.

Regis Jesuit LHP David Peterson was selected vy Boston in the 28th round with the 854th pick.

Kansas OF Tucker Tharp, a two-sport athlete at Fairview ’11, was selected by the New York Mets in the 30th round with the 895th pick.

Other high-schoolers — Pueblo South catcher Cory Voss, by the Rockies, 34th round; Mountain Vista pitcher Nick Leonard, by Atlanta, 34th round; ThunderRidge infielder Brody Wetsmoreland, by the Rockies, 35th round; Fairview pitcher Ryan Kokora, by Atlanta, 35th round; Legacy pitcher Lucas Gilbreath, by the Rockies, in the 36th round; and Chaparral outfielder Keenan Eaton, by Philadelphia, 39th round.
