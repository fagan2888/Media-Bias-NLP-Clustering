Topics: Elliot Rodger, Fox News, Gun Control, mass murder, shootings, Video, Entertainment News

What will it take to get real, serious gun reform in America? Jon Stewart, so worn out by the NRA, Fox News and politicians who believe that the answer to (what they see as inevitable) mass shootings is more guns, has relented.

“What about the real victims — the news networks?” Stewart asks, brilliantly skewering the likes of pundits like Bill O’Reilly, “who are still going to have to waste valuable time going through the motions of covering these inevitable, clearly unstoppable, everyday, ordinary, soul-destroying slaughters?”
