Tucson Police officers are on the scene of a shooting that happened Sunday afternoon.

Sgt. Pete Dugan said it was a neighbor dispute in the 4400 block of E. Glenn.

One man in his 80's grabbed a rifle and shot at the other neighbor, Dugan said.

The bullet missed but it hit something because shrapnel hit the victim. Victim is hurt, but injury is not life-threatening.

The suspect is in custody

We have a reporter at the scene gathering more details.
