Upshot: Employers on the bubble of employing 50 full-time employees, and thus being subject to the play-or-pay requirements, may be looking for a strategy to ensure that they’re ACA compliant. One strategy that’s popped up on the Internet is forcing employees into the plan.

Can you force employees into the plan? The short answer is Yes, but you’ll have to make some changes to your cafeteria plan and, quite possibly, to your 401(k) plan, as well.
