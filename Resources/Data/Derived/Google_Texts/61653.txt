Cesar Castello III, 31, of Wayne, was driving west on Route 80 near Totowa just after 7 a.m. when he lost control of his 1996 Toyota and crashed into a concrete barrier, said Sgt. Adam Grossman, a State Police spokesman. Castello was ejected from the vehicle and hit by a another vehicle, Grossman.

Castello was pronounced dead at the scene, Grossman said.

After Castello was ejected, his vehicle struck another car. No one in that vehicle was injured, Grossman said.

The accident is still under investigation.

Three lanes of traffic were shut down for several hours while police investigated the crash. No charges have been filed, police said.
