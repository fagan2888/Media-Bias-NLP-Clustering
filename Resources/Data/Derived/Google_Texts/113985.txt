Port St. Joe Police have arrested a man for robbing a Chevron store Sunday.

Reports state, 20-year old Josiah Quaranta is being charged with robbery, and possession of drug paraphernalia.

Police say, Sunday, they responded to a robbery on Monument Avenue, where a man came in appearing to make a purchase.

When the clerk opened the cash draw, the man jumped over and grabbed the cash, and then ran out.

The Express Lane across from the chevron store was also robbed.

Officials found a car in an alley fleeing the scene, and they immediately arrested Quaranta.

Quaranta confessed to the robberies, and said his drug habit was the cause of robbing these stores.
