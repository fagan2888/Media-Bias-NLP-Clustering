Wholesale brokerage firm R-T Specialty has named Meredith Dyson senior vice president for its property division.

R-T Specialty is a unit of Ryan Specialty Group, headquartered in Chicago. Dyson will work out of R-T Specialty’s New York office.

Dyson has more than 23 years’ experience in wholesale and retail property brokerage. She specializes in real estate, hospitality, financial institutions, habitational, construction and energy.

Dyson most recently served as a senior vice president in Swett and Crawford’s New York office. Prior to that, she was a senior vice president and Northeast property manager for Mercator Risk Services. She also helped found RMC2, a wholesale insurance brokerage firm that was sold to American E&S in 2003.
