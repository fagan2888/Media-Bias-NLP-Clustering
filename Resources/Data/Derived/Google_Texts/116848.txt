Nathaniel Herz

Nathaniel Herz grew up in Maine and moved to Alaska in 2013 to work at the Anchorage Daily News after surviving two years in New York City, where he received a masters degree from the Columbia Graduate School of Journalism and reported for Newsday and the New York World. He started his career in journalism in 2009 covering cross-country skiing and biathlon for the website FasterSkier.com, and enjoys a good day out on the trails at Kincaid Park.
