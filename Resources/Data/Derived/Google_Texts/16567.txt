Police are investigating a fatal shooting at a Fort Myers apartment complex.

The shooting happened in the 3500 block of Dale Street around 7 p.m. Saturday.

According to Fort Myers Police, the department received multiple 911 calls in reference to a disturbance and shooting at the East Pointe Place Apartment.

When officers arrived, they found a man suffering from a gunshot wound. The victim died at the scene.

Friends identified the victim as Lamarris Jackson.

Born and raised in the Fort Myers neighborhood, Leo Idlette said the violence is usually targeted.

"It can be about a girlfriend or somebody crossed somebody with a girl. Just hating. It don't have to be about drugs all the time," said Idlette.

A friend of Jackson's said a fight led to the shooting.

The gunman is still on the loose. Police say it was an isolated incident and community members are not in danger.

The investigation is ongoing.
