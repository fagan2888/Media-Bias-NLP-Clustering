Big Red F, which owns The Post Brewing Co. and a handful of other restaurants including Jax Fish House, will open the first outlet of its fast-casual GoodBird Kitchen concept in Longmont.

The Boulder-based company has leased a defunct Mexican restaurant space near the redeveloping Twin Peaks mall and plans to open GoodBird in November. A second store is planned for the Stanley Marketplace, scheduled to open near Stapleton next spring, serving beer from The Post and a fried-chicken focused menu.

“We are super excited about the location,” chef and partner Brett Smith said. “We are anticipating a lot of movie-theater goers enjoying delicious fried chicken and craft beer before and/or after their movie. It’s a high traffic area and is the perfect spot for us.”
