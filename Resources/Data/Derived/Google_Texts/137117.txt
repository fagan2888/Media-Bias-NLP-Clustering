Fatal accident in the 1500 block of N. Valleyview Ct.

One man is dead after a single vehicle crash in a west Wichita neighborhood.

It happened around 11:30 p.m. Friday night in the 1500 block of N. Valleyview Ct, north of 13th street.

Sgt. Nichelle Woodrow stated that a single vehicle traveling northbound on Valleyview hit a curb, while turning a corner, then hit a mailbox and struck a tree; turning the car around in front of an unoccupied house.

The 55-year-old male was pronounced dead at the scene.

Detectives are still investigating the exact cause of the crash.
