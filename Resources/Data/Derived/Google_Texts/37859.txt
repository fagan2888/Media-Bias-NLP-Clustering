Longtime Estero High School coach and athletic director Jeff Sommer passed away Saturday morning, according to the Lee County Schools spokesperson.



Sommer was attending the FHSAA state track meet in Jacksonville.

“There are now words to express how deeply saddened the Estero family is over the loss of Jeff. He leaves us a legacy of love, leadership and passion that will endure forever in the many lives he touched,” said principal Clayton Simmons.

On Saturday morning, the Estero girls 4 x 800 relay team won the state title – a lasting testament to Coach Sommer's leadership and commitment to his students and athletes.
