Showers and storms in western Oklahoma will be in the Oklahoma City metro later Tuesday evening.

Any chance for severe storms should stay to our south and west with the threats of hail and strong wind. Rain moves east overnight with some clearing by Wednesday morning. Temperatures drop to around 50 degrees.

A mix of sun and clouds are expected Wednesday. Temperatures will climb to the low 70s with a northwest wind between 10 to 20 mph making for a fairly comfortable day.
