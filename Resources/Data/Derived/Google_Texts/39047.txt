Nicholas Schorsch's newly listed American Realty Capital Healthcare Trust Inc. is being acquired by giant health care REIT Ventas Inc. in a stock and cash transaction valued at $2.6 billion, or $11.33 per share. With the ticker symbol HCT, the ARC Healthcare Trust Inc. launched as a nontraded real estate investment trust in 2011 and listed in April. The offer by Ventas (VTR), represents $1.38 more than ARC Healthcare Trust's closing price Friday, or about a 14% premium to investors. Mr. Schorsch is executive chairman of the ARC Healthcare Trust.
