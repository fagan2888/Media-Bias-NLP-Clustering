“Saturday Night Live” alum Bill Hader returned to the set this weekend to host the show and he brought with him some of his most beloved characters from his previous eight-year run.

Audience favorite and “Weekend Update” City Correspondent Stefon was one of the characters that made a special appearance.

“Weekend Update” viewers were finally able to hear what New York City hotspots they needed to be hitting up.

“New York’s hottest club is…,” he announced.

Hader might have been just as excited about bringing the character back as his viewers. He couldn’t help but breaking out into laughter a few times during the sketch and even gave Stefon quite the jawdropping announcement to end the segment.
