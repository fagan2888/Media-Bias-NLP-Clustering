New Bill Would Shield H-2A Farmers From ACA Mandate

Law360, New York (March 19, 2015, 2:39 PM ET) -- U.S. Reps. Renee Ellmers, R-N.C., and Ed Whitfield, R-Ky., on Wednesday introduced legislation that would exclude seasonal agricultural workers from being counted toward health insurance mandates under the Affordable Care Act, a measure they said would shield many farmers from potentially crippling fees and penalties.
