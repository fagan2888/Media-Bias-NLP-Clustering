SEOUL—Shin Dong-hyuk, a North Korean refugee who helped publicize alleged human-rights abuses inside his country, has admitted to changing parts of his life story, putting his credibility in question.

Mr. Shin is among the highest-profile activists against North Korea’s prison camps, in which he says he was born via an organized breeding program.
