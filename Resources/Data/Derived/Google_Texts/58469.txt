IRS Misallocated Millions In ACA Tax Credits, Watchdog Says

Law360, New York (May 4, 2016, 2:36 PM ET) -- Although the Internal Revenue Service has been accurate in determining 93 percent of Affordable Care Act tax credits, millions of dollars were either improperly withheld or handed out to taxpayers, according to an audit by the U.S. Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration.
