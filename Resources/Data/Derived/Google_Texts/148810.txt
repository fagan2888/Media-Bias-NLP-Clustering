Police in Marana are searching for a man suspected of armed robbery at a Walmart northwest of Tucson.

The suspect gave a note to an employee at the store, located at Ina and Thornydale roads, at approximately 11:30 a.m. on Saturday, March 26. Investigators say the man displayed a handgun that was in his waistband.

The employee handed over some money and the suspect left the store.

He's described as an African-American man in his mid-30s, anywhere from 6'1" to 6"3" tall. He was wearing a white construction hat with a blue bandanna underneath. He had blue jeans and black shoes, according to police.

Anyone with information about the suspect or about the crime should contact the Marana Police Department at 520-382-2000 or report it anonymously through 88-CRIME.
