Wichita Police investigate a shooting that seriously injured a teenaged boy.

It happened around 3:30 a.m. in the 1500 block of East Del Mar, near Hydraulic and I-135.

Police found a 17-year-old boy with a possible gunshot wound to the leg.

The teen was transported to a local hospital. Police say he is in stable condition.

Police are still investigating and no one has been arrested at this time.
