The D.H. Conley volleyball team has advanced to the East Regional final after topping Hoggard 3-0 Saturday.

Conley won convincingly in all three sets (25-18, 25-11 and 25-12) as they have yet to drop a set throughout the playoffs.

Conley (28-0) will host Tuesday's regional final against Wakefield.

The 4-A state championship is at Reynolds Coliseum at N.C. State University on Saturday, November 2nd at 7:30 pm.
