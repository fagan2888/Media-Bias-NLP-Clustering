Denver police are asking for the public’s help in finding an 11-year-old boy last seen Monday afternoon.

The Denver Police Department said Jayden Williamson-Brown was last seen in the 1100 block of Decatur Street around 12:30 p.m.

He is described as black with brown eyes and black hair about 4-foot, 11-inches tall, weighing about 70 to 80 pounds. He was last seen wearing an orange shirt, red shorts and sandals.

Police got the call about the disappearance around 11:30 p.m. and detectives were put on the case an hour later.

Anyone with information on Jayden’s whereabouts should contact Denver police at 720-913-2000.
