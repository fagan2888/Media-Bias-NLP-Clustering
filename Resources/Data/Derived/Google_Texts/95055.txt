From Sea To Shining Sea: French And US Sunshine Laws

In the U.S., intense scrutiny in recent years has focused on relationships between drug and medical device manufacturers on one hand and physicians and teaching hospitals on the other. New requirements by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, effective this year, will require public disclosure of these relationships.
