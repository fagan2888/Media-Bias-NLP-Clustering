Crews respond to crash on I-35 in Edmond

Troopers and police responded to an injury accident in Edmond on the southbound lanes of Interstate 35.

The accident happened Saturday night on the southbound lanes of I-35 near 15th Street. The accident involved three vehicles and traffic was being diverted, troopers said.

The extent of injuries was not known Saturday night.
