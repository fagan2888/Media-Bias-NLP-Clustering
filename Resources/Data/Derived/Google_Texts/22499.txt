Arkansas City police are asking for help in locating 16-year-old Jaylen Jones.

On October 21st, Jones was reported as a runaway or missing person by his mother. She said Jaylen left the residence that day, sometime between noon and 9 p.m. Since then, he has not communicated with family members.

Jaylen does have friends in South Haven, Winfield and possibly Wichita.

He's described as a white male, about 5 foot 6 inches tall, weighing about 145 pounds, with blonde hair and blue eyes.

He was last seen wearing a red jacket and black tennis shoes.
