Cali Patty Melt

8 slices sourdough bread

4 hamburger patties, cooked and grilled

4 slices Monterey Jack cheese

2 tablespoon Sriracha sauce

1/4 cup mayonnaise

Sliced avocado

Micro greens

Preheat the grill or grill pan to medium high heat. Cook the hamburgers until desired temperature is reached and then remove from the pan. Brush the bread with olive oil and top the In a skillet, place one piece of bread then top with the cooked hamburger, top with Monterey jack cheese, micro greens and avocado slices. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise and Sriracha sauce. Brush the mayo on the bread and place on top of the sandwich. Cook the sandwich on both sides till browned and the cheese is melted. Serve!
