Obamacare architect Jonathan Gruber infamously discussed the “stupidity” of the American people. Now we know which kinds of voters he was specifically referring to.

Gruber was the first one quoted in the 2008 Boston Globe obituary of his former MIT political science professor Louis Menand III. What did Gruber remember about Menand?

“He would say in class, ‘There are two types of people: There are Democrats and there are stupid people,'” Gruber said. (RELATED: Gruber: The Affordable Care Act Will NOT Be Affordable)
