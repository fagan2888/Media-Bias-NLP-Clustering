Aberdeen, SD

Boys basketball teams at high schools across South Dakota are boasting players who bring sizeable advantages to the court.



Data from the South Dakota High School Activities Association shows 57 players who stand at least 6-foot-6 are listed on rosters this season. 20 of those players are on Class AA and A teams, while 17 of them are on Class B teams.



Several boys basketball teams, including those at Washington and Lincoln high schools in Sioux Falls, have three players who are at least 6-foot-6.



Veteran coach Todd Neuendorf says his current team at Roncalli High School in Aberdeen is the tallest he's ever coached. The team is capped by 6-foot-8 center Zach Hammrich.



The tallest player in the state is 7-foot-1 Nathaniel Brown Bull of Little Wound School in Kyle.
