A man has died following a crash Saturday night in south Wichita.

It happened at about 9 p.m. near Seneca and I-235. Wichita police said a 30-year-old man was driving eastbound on Cary Lane when he ran a stop sign and drove through a fence. He then crashed into an embankment, and his vehicle came to rest in a drainage ditch.

Police said man had to be extracted from the vehicle. He was taken to a nearby hospital with life-threatening injuries.

Via Christi hospital staff confirmed Sunday morning that he died of his injuries. His name was not released.

The crash is still under investigation.
