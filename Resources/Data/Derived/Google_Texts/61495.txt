Police are searching for a man who allegedly robbed a QuikTrip early Saturday morning in Wichita's Delano district.

It happened around 1:40 a.m. at the QuickTrip at Douglas and Seneca. A clerk told officers a man entered the store, produced a small handgun and demanded the money from the register.

The suspect was last seen running from the store with a small amound of cash.

Police did not provide a detailed description of the suspect, but Sgt. Roger Runft said police are speaking with three patrons who witnessed the robbery. They are also reviewing surveillance video.

Anyone with information is asked to call Wichita Crime Stoppers at (316) 267-2111.
