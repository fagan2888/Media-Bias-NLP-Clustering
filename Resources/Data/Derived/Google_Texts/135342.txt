Under pressure from U.S. short seller Carson Block, French supermarket group Casino released details of its real estate operation in Wednesday’s annual results. But these raise as many questions as they answer.

Gains on property disposals contributed €167 million ($184 million) to the company’s earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization in France in 2015, 23% of the total.
