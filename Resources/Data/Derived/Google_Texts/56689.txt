Eckstrom-Parker wedding

Annie Eckstrom and Cory Parker. both of Lake Stevens, Washington, were married in Maui, Hawaii, on May 4, 2014.

Annie is the daughter of Jerry and Julie Eckstrom of Welch and Jim and Jill Bray of Snohomish, Washington. Cory is the son of Rich and Becky Parker of Apple Valley, Minnesota.

Annie graduated from Red Wing High School and the University of St Catherine's physical therapy program. She works at Cascade Rehabilitation in Mukilteo, Washington.

Cory graduated from Apple Valley High School.

Reception will be held in Welch in August.
