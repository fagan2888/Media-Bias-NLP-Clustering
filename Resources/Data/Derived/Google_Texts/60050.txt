Molina Healthcare Will Repay $19.2M To Wash. Authority

Law360, Washington (September 5, 2014, 4:52 PM ET) -- Molina Healthcare Inc. revealed on Friday that its Washington subsidiary has agreed to pay approximately $19.2 million to settle overpayment allegations with the state's health care authority.



In a U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Molina said the deal would end two outstanding overpayment issues with the Washington Health Care Authority over psychotropic drugs and claims for Washington Community Options Program Entry System health care plan members.
