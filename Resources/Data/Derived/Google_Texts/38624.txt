Mary Ann (Blakemore) and John Farrington were married on Dec. 28, 1950, in the First Presbyterian Church in Bedford, Iowa, Mary Ann's hometown.

In 1965, the couple moved to Boulder, John's hometown, where he started his medical practice specializing in internal and pulmonary medicine.

Mary Ann and John have three children, Nancy (Paul) Reid, of Waltham, Mass.; Steve Farrington, of Deer Trail, Colo., and Julie Ann (Robert, recently deceased) Goette, of Hoffman Estates, Ill. Mary Ann and John also have nine grandchildren.

Although retired, Mary Ann and John remain very active. Mary Ann is a 33-year volunteer at the information desk at Boulder Community Health and belongs to three bridge clubs. John is a 17-year volunteer in the print shop at Boulder's First Presbyterian Church and is active in a book club.

The couple enjoy traveling, golf, fishing and gardening and just being with each other and their family.
