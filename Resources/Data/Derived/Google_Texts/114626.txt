LAKE BUTLER, Fla. - The Union County Sheriff's Office responded to a bomb threat Friday at a CVS Pharmacy on Main Street in Lake Butler.

The threat was phone in Friday morning, and police could be seen blocking off the area.

Deputies said bomb units and bomb-sniffing dogs were brought in to search the CVS and the parking lot, and nothing was found.

The roads surrounding the CVS were blocked as a safety precaution but have been reopened.
