Sioux Falls, SD

Freezing rain has left eastern South Dakota and southwest Minnesota with a sheet of ice on roads and sidewalks.

Roadways in Sioux Falls are covered in ice and Metro Communications has taken several calls for vehicle crashes.

Sioux Falls School District will be on time today, but tardies will not taken:

School on time today but due to overnight conditions roads are slippery in some spots. Tardies will not be taken but school will be on time. — Sioux Falls Schools (@SFSchools) January 19, 2015
