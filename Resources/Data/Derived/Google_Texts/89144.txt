The South Dakota Highway Patrol shared a little girl's powerful message about seat belt safety Friday.

Highway Patrol shared a photo and a message on their Facebook page from a girl named Amelia and her mother, who had been in a car accident.

According to the message, 7-year-old Amelia suffered a broken leg and a lacerated liver, but thanks to her seatbelt, was still alive.

South Dakota Highway Patrol was the first to respond to the crash.
