Police are looking for a man who groped two women Arcadia. (Published Thursday, Aug. 27, 2015)

Police on Thursday released a sketch of a person suspected of forcing two women into a "bear hug" and groping them near schools in Arcadia within a two-hour period.

Both assaults were reported Tuesday morning, the first near Dana Middle School, and the second two hours later at Arcadia High School, Arcadia police said.

Police said the man approached two women from behind as they were either walking to or away from their cars and placed them in a "bear hug" without saying a word.

The victims felt several pelvic thrusts from behind before being released, police said. The man then ran away.
