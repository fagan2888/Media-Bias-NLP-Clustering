The Federal Reserve on Friday said it has approved Zions Bancorp's resubmitted capital plan, reversing a decision to reject the plan in March.

The move clears the way for Zions to reward shareholders with Fed-approved dividends or share buybacks if the bank chooses to do so. Shares of the bank rose 1.3% in recent trading on the news.
