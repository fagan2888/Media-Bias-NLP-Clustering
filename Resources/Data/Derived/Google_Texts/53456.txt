One man is dead after a shooting in Scott county Saturday night.

Sheriff Mike Lee told WLBT-TV in Jackson that Bradley Paul Harrison was shot twice after an altercation at a home on Old Highway 80 in Lake. The sheriff said Harrison then left the home belonging to Allen Thomas Barnes and was later found on the side of the road near his four wheeler. Harrison was airlifted to UMC in Jackson where he was pronounced dead.

No charges have been filed at this time and the case is still under investigation.
