Below, U.S. News highlights some of the most important numbers related to the Affordable Care Act.

$7.4 Billion: The Amount of Money Hospitals Saved on Uncompensated Costs Last Year

Health and Human Services Secretary Sylvia Burwell announced in Arlington, Virginia, on Monday that hospitals saved $7.4 billion in uncompensated care costs last year as a result of patient enrollment through Obamacare health insurance exchanges and Medicaid . Before the law, hospitals would take on the cost of treating people who were uninsured or underinsured. In 2013, they provided more than $50 billion in uncompensated care.
