Two people were injured, one of them seriously, after one vehicle involved in a two-car accident Sunday burst into flames on E-470 at East Colfax Avenue, the Colorado State Patrol said.

The accident happened at 3:12 p.m., said patrol spokesman Tim Sutherland.

“One vehicle (was) fully engulfed” in flames, Sutherland said.

Shortly after 4 p.m., Sutherland said, the vehicles were being moved from the roadway.

This story will be updated when more information is available.
