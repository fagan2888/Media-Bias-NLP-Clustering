The borough is looking for volunteers to help plant 15,000 dune grass seedlings on along a mile of beach.

The project is a partnership between the borough, the American Littoral Society and the Surfrider Foundation. The planting will happen rain or shine starting at 10 a.m. on March 28.

Email questions to Capt. Alek Modjeski at alek@littoralsociety.org.
