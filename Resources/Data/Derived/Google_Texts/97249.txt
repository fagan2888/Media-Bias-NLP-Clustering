PUEBLO, Colo. -

Pueblo police arrested two people in connection to a drive-by shooting Sunday.

Kamisha Chavez, 23, and Paul Kushner, 18 face several charges.

Police said officers found the two walking away from a vehicle reported stolen and involved in a drive-by shooting Sunday morning.

A woman originally reported the vehicle stolen around 7:30 a.m. Sunday from a convenience store on Jerry Murphy Road.

That vehicle was spotted at a drive-by shooting around 12:30 p.m. in the 2400 block of West 13th Street, police said.

The vehicle was found abandoned near City Center and Greenwood. Police said Kushner is charged with stealing the vehicle.
