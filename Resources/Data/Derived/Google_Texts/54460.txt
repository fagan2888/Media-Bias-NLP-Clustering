Bay County Fire crews spent Saturday afternoon putting out a vehicle fire on South Campflower Road on Highway 231.

The fire occurred before 3 p.m. Saturday on the northbound lanes of Highway 231.

Bay County fire officials say the fire started in the engine compartment.

The driver stated to officials that smoke began coming out of the air vents as he was driving, he eventually pulled over and the car caught on fire.

The male driver was the only person in the vehicle at the time, and he was not injured.

The northbound lanes on Highway 231 were closed off for a few hours as crews put out the fire.

Fire officials want to remind drivers to get routine check-up's for their vehicles, especially if it is an older model.
