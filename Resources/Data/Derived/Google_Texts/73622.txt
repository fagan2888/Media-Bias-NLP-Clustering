Citizens Financial Group Inc. scored in its return to the U.S. stock market Wednesday, rising 7.4% to cap off the regional lender's first day as a publicly traded company in 26 years.

The shares' gains came a day after the Providence, R.I., firm concluded a 10-day roadshow that saw active debate over the price being asked for the shares.
