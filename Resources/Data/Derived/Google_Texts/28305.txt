Washington Post columnist and author George Will stated that he would “bet against” Jeb Bush running for president in 2016 on Tuesday’s broadcast of “The Laura Ingraham Show.”

He added that Bush “has four strikes against him, his last name, Common Core, immigration, on which I happen to agree with him … but fourth he will come into the race with a huge bullseye on his back that says ‘Establishment Choice’ and the base is going to say ‘well, we know who we’re not for.’”
