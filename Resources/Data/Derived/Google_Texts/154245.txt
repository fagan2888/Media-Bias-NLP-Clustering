Police are searching for a 19-year-old man in connection with what's being investigated as an intentional hit-and-run that left a Milwaukee woman dead on the city's south side Thursday.

According to police, the striking vehicle in the incident was found in Franklin on Friday. Two passengers in the car, a 17-year-old boy and 17-year-old girl, were taken into custody, according to the Milwaukee Police Department. Officials are searching for the driver, a 19-year-old man.

The hit-and-run left Reeanna L. Ramazini, 35, dead. The crash happened about 8 p.m. Thursday in the 1900 block of W. Bolivar Ave, police say. Ramazini was in the road when she was hit by a white four-door SUV. The vehicle then fled north on S. 20th St.

A case against the two passengers is expected to go to the Milwaukee County district attorney's office, police said.
