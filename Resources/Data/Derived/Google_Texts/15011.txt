Daniel Roy Plummer, 34, of Toms River (Photo: OCEAN COUNTY JAIL)

A township man was arrested Tuesday on charges that he sexually assaulted four girls, according to the Ocean County Prosecutor's Office.

Authorities say Daniel Roy Plummer, 34, sexually assaulted four minors known to him, according to a news release from Ocean County Prosecutor's Office spokesman Al Della Fave.

Plummer was charged with three counts of sexual assault and four counts of endangering the welfare of a child. He was taken to Ocean County Jail. Judge Wendel E. Daniels set bail at $250,000 with no 10 percent option and implemented a no-victim contact order on behalf of the victims.

Members of the Division of Child Protection and Permanency and Ocean County Sheriff's Department assisted the Prosecutor's Office in the two-month-long investigation.
