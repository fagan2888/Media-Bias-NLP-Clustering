Regarding Scott Atlas's "Where ObamaCare Is Going" (op-ed, Aug. 14): I am a U.K. citizen now living in New York. I agree the British National Health Service isn't perfect, far from it, but it does allow the vast majority of patients to get medical treatment without fear of being bankrupted. That should be the right of every citizen in a civilized society in the 21st century.

In the U.K., no one has to think twice before seeing a doctor. This opportunity prevents patients from getting diseases and illnesses...
