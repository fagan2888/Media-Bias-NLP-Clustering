A Colorado Mountain College student is missing, and officials are seeking information regarding his whereabouts.

Colton Shane Wilson, 19, was last seen on a security camera leaving his dorm at the Leadville Campus in plaid pajama pants and loafers at 1 p.m. Thursday, according to a Facebook post by the Lake County Office of Emergency Management.

His vehicle remains parked at the dorm, the post said.

Wilson has not been seen since.

He is described as a white male, 6’4″, 160 pounds, with brown hair, brown eyes and light facial hair, according to the post.

Anyone with information about Wilson is asked to contact the Lake County Sheriff’s Office at 719-486-1249.
