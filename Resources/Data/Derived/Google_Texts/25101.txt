Katherine Haigh, daughter of Brian and Veronica Haigh, former Broomfield residents now residing in Arkansas, is engaged to be married to Jonathan Thornton, son of Kenneth and Ann Thornton, of Pine Bluff, Ark.

Katherine is a 2006 graduate of Legacy High School; Jon is a 1998 graduate of Riverside Baptist School in Vidalia, La.

Katherine is a corporate payroll administrator for WEHCO Media in Little Rock. Jon is an account manager for the Brickman Group.

The couple will wed in spring 2016 and will reside, along with Jon's daughter, Kendel, in Little Rock, Ark.
