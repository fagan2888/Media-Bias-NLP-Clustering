The incident happened near the Prince Road exit on Interstate 10 on March 19. (Source: Twitter/ Quentin Mehr, DPS PIO)

Two people died Saturday, March 19, after a man lost control of his vehicle and hit a pole near the Prince Road exit on Interstate 10.

The incident happened at 10:38 a.m., according to Department of Public Safety spokesman Quentin Mehr.

The driver died at the scene. Two female passengers were taken to a hospital for treatment of critical injuries, Mehr said. One of the passengers later died.

Mehr said they are working to notify the families of the victims before releasing their names.
