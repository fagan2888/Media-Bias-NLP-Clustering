One man died in a car accident near Middletown Christmas Eve morning.

Ricardo Diaz Nevarez, 25, was involved in a single-car accident in the 12600 block of Old Henry Road around 6 a.m. Thursday, according to a news release from the Jefferson County Coroner's Office.

Nevarez was transported to University Hospital from the scene and died during surgery around 8:45 a.m.

The Coroner's Office said Nevarez died from multiple blunt force injuries.
