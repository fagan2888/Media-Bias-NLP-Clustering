NBC5 reports:

Police say a driver is in custody after crashing into a garage in Far North Dallas early Saturday morning.

The crash occurred at the VV&M Apartments on Verde Valley Lane. KXAS-TV (NBC5) found the scene thanks to a viewer’s tip.

The driver of a Chevrolet SUV ran a stop sign, and crashed through a resident’s garage, hitting the car parked inside, according to police.

The driver, who was not identified, was taken into custody. Police did not confirm whether alcohol may have been involved.
