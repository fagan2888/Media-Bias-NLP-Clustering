Wichita Police are searching for two suspects involved in a home burglary Saturday night.

The burglary happened at a residence in the 1500 Block of South Old Manor.

The victims told police the suspects both had guns. No injuries were reported and police estimate the theft and damages were minimal.

The suspects are described as two males, approximately 19 years-old, but the victim could only give police a vague description. The suspects left by car, but no description was given on the type and make of the vehicle.
