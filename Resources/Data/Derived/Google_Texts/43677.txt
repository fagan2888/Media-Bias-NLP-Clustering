Healing Hands Health Center in conjunction with ETSU Quillen College of Medicine will host its annual

Free Health Fair open to the public on Wednesday and Thursday, November 18 & 19 at 245 Midway

Medical Park Bristol, TN. Appointments are now being taken from 9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. both days for

physical exams, school and sports physicals, lung function exams, hearing tests, cholesterol & blood

sugar checks and more. A trained navigator will provide information about the Affordable Care Act and

how to sign up for health insurance. There is no charge to attend. Free tshirts will be given away to

everyone attending as well as 3 $100 Food City gift cards. Call (423) 652-0260 for an appointment.

“We are excited to be hosting our 15th Annual free health fair, but the first one in our new location,”

said Scott. Thousands of people have received free health care at our health fair and many lives have

been saved because they took the time to get examined. The health fair is open to anyone whether

they have insurance or not.”
