During his 18 years in the U.S. Senate, Democrat Russ Feingold made a reputation for pushing campaign finance reform.

But now, with Feingold running for the seat he lost to Republican Ron Johnson in 2010, he is being attacked by the National Republican Senatorial Committee.

In a TV ad, the committee said Feingold "wanted to get money out of politics. Now he's profiting from that same dark money."

PolitiFact Wisconsin checks whether Feingold made money off a so-called dark money political group.
