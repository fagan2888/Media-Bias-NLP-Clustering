A home is destroyed in Panama City after it is engulfed in flames Sunday evening.

Panama City fire officials responded to a home on East 9th Street.

They were able to contain the fire in 15 minutes, but the house is being called a total loss.

Reports state that three people were home at the time of the fire, but were not injured.

The Battalion Chief that worked the call says they arrived on scene within minutes.

"Had a single residence catch fire, said Kent Taylor, Battalion Chief. "The crews arrived with in two minutes of being toned out. Knocked the fire out in about 15 minutes. Nobody was injured and it's still under investigation."

The investigation is being conducted by a Florida State Fire Marshal.
