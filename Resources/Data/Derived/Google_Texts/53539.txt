The annual Bobcat Booster Club and Sunrise Rotary Casino Night attracted a large crowd Saturday. Held in the MacGregor Room of the Stanley Hotel, the fund-raiser generates funds for Bobcat athletic programs within the school district.

Poker, craps, black jack, horse racing and dancing were a part of this fun adult evening. There was also a Texas Hold 'Em tournament.

Many of the high school and middle school coaches served as dealers.

Participants played with funny money that could be redeemed for prizes.

There was also a silent auction that included items donated by local businesses.
