The Republican who represents Orange, Louisa and Culpeper in the House of Representatives wants a second term. Dave Brat (pictured) wants to repeal the Affordable Care Act, which he calls Obamacare

Brat has a growing campaign warchest, including recent contributions from former Congressman Virgil Goode, University of Virginia Rector William Goodwin, and Albemarle businessman Carter Myers.

Brat is being opposed this year by attorney Eileen Bedell, a Chesterfield County Democrat. Brat was a recent guest on the Schilling Show.
