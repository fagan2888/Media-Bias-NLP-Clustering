International Paper Co. said Friday it will invest between $60 million and $70 million to double the size of an Ohio plant to meet rising demand for paper products from the food industry.

The Memphis, Tenn., paper company said the project will add 250,000 square feet and about 125 jobs to the factory in Kenton, Ohio. The expansion should begin operations by mid-2015.
