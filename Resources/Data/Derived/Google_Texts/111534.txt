The Bonnet Carre Spillway Road and all recreational areas in the spillway in St. Charles Parish is closed until further notice, parish officials announced Saturday (Jan.9). Water from the rising Mississippi River has covered the road and the recreation areas.

The Army Corps of Engineers has announced that it will open the Bonnet Carre Spillway structure starting at 10 a.m. Sunday to help lower the river by diverting water to Lake Pontchartrain.
