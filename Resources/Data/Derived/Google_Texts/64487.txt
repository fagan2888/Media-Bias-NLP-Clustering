Surveillance video catches a man walking away with a delivery that was left on a front porch in Bethany late Thursday afternoon.

This crime occurred just after 4:00 p.m. near NW 30th and Council. Police say the suspect drove by the home once, then came back to steal the package.

If you recognize him, call Bethany police.
