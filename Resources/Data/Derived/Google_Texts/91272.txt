Tea, SD

A Marion woman has died from injuries she received in a two vehicle crash on Saturday.

88-year-old Caryl Rand was heading eastbound on 272nd Street in a Chevrolet Impala. Rand failed to stop at a stop sign and was hit by a GMC Acadia driven by 56-year-old Glen Danielsen. Both vehicles went in to the northeast ditch.

Rand was taken to a Sioux Falls hospital where she died from her injuries.

Everyone involved was wearing their seat belt.

No further information is available.

The South Dakota Highway Patrol is investigating.
