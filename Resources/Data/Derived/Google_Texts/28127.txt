Nelson Mullins Snags Health Care Pros From LeClairRyan

Law360, New York (November 14, 2013, 5:34 PM ET) -- Seeking to bolster its health care and pharmaceutical strength, the Washington, D.C.-based firm of Nelson Mullins Riley & Scarborough LLP snagged a pair of veteran health care attorneys from LeClairRyan, the firm said last week.



Partner Michael Ruggio and of counsel Sarah McIntee both represent hospitals, health care providers and pharmaceutical and medical device companies. Ruggio, who has 32 years of experience, said he is looking forward to expanding the health care practice at Nelson Mullins.
