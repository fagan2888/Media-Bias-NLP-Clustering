Parade of luxury



Eight homes, starting at $2.5 million, have been selected for the Parade of Luxury Homes Evergreen on May 31. The adult-only tour, open 11 a.m. to 6 p.m., also features original works of art on display and for sale by 21 artists who will be in attendance to discuss their pieces. Proceeds benefit the Center for Arts Evergreen. Don’t miss the hors d’oeuvres and refreshments, and be sure to enter the drawing to win one of two works of art. Visit the website for more information, including a map. evergreenluxuryparade.com
