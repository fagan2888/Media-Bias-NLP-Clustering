A huge fundraising rugby tournament will take place today, organised by motor neurone disease campaigner Lee Newton.

The L6ve Life Foundation Rugby League Tournament will be held all day at East Side, Salt End, the home of Skirlaugh Rugby League Club.

The tournament will involve 16 teams of over-35s.

Rugby union players are taking part and Hull FC have also raised a team.

The tournament will also be attended by the likes of former Hull City and England player Nicky Barmby and a host of ex-professionals and rugby league legends.

Full details are available on the L6ve Life website.
