The Gundersen Health System in LaCrosse, Wisconsin had a mission: Go green or go home.

Millions later, Gundersen has been able to offset its entire fossil fuel footprint with a host of environmentally friendly systems including dairy digesters, wind turbines, landfill programs and thermal heating systems.
