Sturgis, SD

As the 75th Sturgis Motorcycle Rally comes to a close Sunday, final numbers from the South Dakota Highway Patrol list another fatal crash.

Saturday afternoon 57-year-old Larry Kaduce of St. Peter, MN was killed driving his motorcycle northbound on SD 79 trying to pass two pickups pulling campers. Kaduce collided head-on with a southbound semi truck and was pronounced dead at the scene. He was not wearing a helmet.

The report from the last day of the rally lists a grand total of 220 DUI arrests, 203 misdemeanor drug arrests, 99 felony drug arrests, and over $28,000 in seized cash over the course of the entire rally. A total of 1,501 citations were issued, along with 4,557 warnings.

Injury accidents totaled to 152 compared to last year's 69. Thirteen people were killed over the course of the rally, more than triple from 2014.
