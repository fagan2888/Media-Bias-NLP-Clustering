An Independence, Missouri man was killed when the truck he was driving crashed in eastern Kansas. The accident happened Wednesday afternoon near LaCygne, in Linn County.

Kansas Highway Patrol troopers said the truck driven by David Mayfield was pulling away from a power plant. The truck left the east side of the roadway and struck a fence.

Mayfield, 53, died from his injuries. Troopers said he was not wearing a safety belt.
