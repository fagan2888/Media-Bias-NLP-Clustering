KELOLAND News has learned a man was hurt from an electrical shock at the Sioux Empire Fair Sunday night.

It happened after the fair had closed and employees were taking down the rides around 8:00. A 50-year-old Texas man received an electrical shock, according to the Minnehaha County Sheriff's office.

He was an employee of Reed Expo and was rushed to Sanford Hospital. He is listed in stable condition. The cause of the shock is being investigated.
