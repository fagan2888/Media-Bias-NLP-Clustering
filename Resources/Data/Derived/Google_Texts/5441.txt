A four-year-old girl died after a driver crashed into a daycare in Winter Park, Florida. Paramedics took 11 other children and one adult to the hospital. They treated two other children on the scene.

Troopers are looking for Robert Corchado. They say he forced another driver off the road and she crashed into the daycare just before 3:30 Wednesday afternoon. According to troopers, after the crash Corchado ditched the SUV and rented a car in Winter Park.
