Whether North Korea tested a hydrogen bomb or an “ordinary” nuclear fission device, the basic truth remains the same—North Korea has crossed the nuclear threshold and is never going back. At this point, the only acceptable response is to beef up our ballistic-missile defenses so Pyongyang’s nukes cannot put American cities at risk (”The New Nuclear Proliferation Age,” Review & Outlook, Jan. 7).
