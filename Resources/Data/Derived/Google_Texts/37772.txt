As the world continues to mourn the loss of Blues legend B.B. King, we had the chance to talk with someone who knew him.

Bill Cosby was in Alabama yesterday talking to high school students and Newscenter 11 had the chance to ask him about the King of Blues.

"The thing about him, he had the greatest line pertaining to the blues I’ve ever heard. The quintessential sentence, “nobody loves me but my mother and she might be jivin' too," Cosby says.
