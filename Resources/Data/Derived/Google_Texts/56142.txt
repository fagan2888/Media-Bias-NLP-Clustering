Devin Kelly

A native of Sacramento, Calif., Devin grew up traversing Northern California and the western United States in a Volkswagen camper bus and hiking the Sierras. She decided to be a writer when she was 7 and started working for her local newspaper, the Davis Enterprise, a decade later. Devin majored in history at UCLA and has worked as a reporting intern at the Oregonian and the Los Angeles Times. She moved to Alaska in November 2013 for her first full-time reporting gig.
