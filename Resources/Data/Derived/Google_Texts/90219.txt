Three people were hurt in a crash Sunday afternoon on a highway in east Wichita.

It happened around 2 p.m. in the eastbound lanes of Kellogg near Webb Road. Pictures sent to KAKE News from the scene show the crash involved two pickups and an SUV.

Two women and a man were taken to the hospital with potentially serious injuries. Police later said at the scene that their injuries ended up being minor. No names were released.

The cause of the crash is under investigation. Eastbound lanes of Kellogg have reopened.
