Two teens were seriously hurt in a crash near Burrton on Friday night.

The crashed happened just after 9 p.m., on Rayl Rd. and US 50, four miles west of Burrton.

Highway Patrol reports show a Dodge Grand Caravan with three teens ran a stop sign, hitting the trailer of a semi. The driver of the semi was not injured.

Both teen boys, one 16-year-old and a 17-year-old, were taken to a local hospital with serious injuries.
