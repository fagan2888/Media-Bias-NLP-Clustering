JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - The Jacksonville Sheriff's Office is searching for a suspect after a man was shot in the leg.

It happened just after midnight Saturday at the Pines of Mindanao Apartments, on Mindanao Drive, which is off of Atlantic and St. Johns Bluff Road.

Police say they found a 19-year-old man who had been shot in the leg. He was taken to UF Health Jacksonville with non-life threatening injuries.

Little is known about the suspect except that he is a black man possibly driving a small red four door car.

If you have any information about this shooting- you're asked to call crime stoppers at 866-845-TIPS
