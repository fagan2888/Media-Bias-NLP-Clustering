Emergency crews responded to the scene where a cement truck has rolled over Tuesday morning in west Oklahoma City.

According to authorities, just after 9:30 a.m., a cement truck rolled over on the Kilpatrick Turnpike southbound off-ramp near Wilshire.

The off-ramp was blocked for more than three hours as crews worked to clear the scene.



The driver of the truck was treated at the scene for minor injuries.
