Bay County Sheriff's officials are investigating the death of a Bay County man.

A little after 10:20 Saturday night, Sheriff's officials received a call about a domestic violence dispute in the area of Shannon Circle and County Road 2301.

The call came in from 34-year old Erin Goodwin who told officers, her husabnd, Christopher Williams had attacked her in the living room in their home on Lory Lane.

Goodwin tells officiers she ended up in the kitchen, where she believes she stabbed him. Deputies went back to Goodwin's home and found Williams on the floor dead.

Investigators at this time believe Goodwin acted in self-defense. The investigation is ongoing.
