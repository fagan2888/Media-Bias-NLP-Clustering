A man has died and two other people were hurt in a crash Saturday night in Trego County.

It happened just before 11:30 p.m. on U-283 north of Wakeeney. The Kansas Highway Patrol reports a 2001 Chevy Blazer was heading south on 283 when it crossed the center line and entered a ditch.

The SUV went over a concrete drain and overturned several times.

The driver, 36-year-old Donald Mizer, Jr., of Wakeeney, died at the scene. Reports state he was not wearing a seatbelt.

Two passengers in the vehicle, a 19-year-old man and a 32-year-old woman, were taken to a local hospital for treatment.

Troopers are still investigating the crash.
