DES MOINES, Iowa --Not everyone is complaining about the snow. Many kids are out of school for the Thanksgiving holiday and they had plenty of time to take advantage of it.

For most, this is their sled’s maiden voyage for the season and they say the snow is perfect. 7 –year – old, Zoe Gard, of Grimes giggled her way up and down the hill. She says “I’m having is 100 percent fun.”

Parents say they’re enjoying the snow too, it give them a chance to get the kids out of the house.

“They were driving each other crazy inside the house and you know winter really hasn’t even started yet, so we gotta get them out the house as much as possible, “says Lee Gard.
