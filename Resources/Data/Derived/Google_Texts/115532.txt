One person has been arrested on suspicion of DUI following a two- car wreck on Middle Beach Road Friday morning.

According to officials with the Panama City Beach Police Department, two others were sent to the hospital with serious but not life-threatening injuries.

It happened just before 10:30 a.m. Friday in front of Hutchison Beach Elementary School. According to police, one driver ran a stop sign on Clara Avenue onto Hutchison Boulevard.

A car and pick-up truck collided. The intersection at Clara Avenue and Hutchison Boulevard was briefly closed. Crews were working to clear it up.
