JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - A 49-year-old Jacksonville man was found guilty Tuesday of sexual battery on a teen.

In July 2012, the victim disclosed that she and Kevin Elliott had engaged in inappropriate sexual activity.

The investigation revealed that in 2010, Elliott had sex with the victim, who was 16 at the time.

Elliott now faces 30 years in prison. He will be sentenced the week of July 14.
