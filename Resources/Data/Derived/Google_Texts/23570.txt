Habitat for Humanity of York County is organizing a Global Village trip to Comanesti, Romania, Oct. 2-11. A few openings remain for volunteers. The trip involves teams from around the world and includes sightseeing and cultural activities. Deadline to sign up is March 30. Cost is $1,850 plus airfare. For information, contact Tim Veeck at tveeck@yorkcountyhabitat.org or call 803-985-9241.
