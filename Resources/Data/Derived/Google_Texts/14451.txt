A crash involving two motorcycles killed one rider and seriously injured another just before 5 p.m. Sunday in Denver.

The cause of the crash on West Colfax Avenue on the bridge over Interstate 25 is under investigation.

Westbound lanes of West Colfax Avenue are closed for the investigation, and delays are expected while the investigation takes places.

The names of those on the motorcycle were not released.

This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
