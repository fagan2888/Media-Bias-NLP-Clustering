By Staff Reports

Oxnard police issued 45 citations and made one arrest Sunday afternoon during a four-hour motorcycle safety enforcement operation.

Between noon and 4 p.m., officers issued 16 citations for various motorcycle violations, 29 citations for various vehicle violations and arrested one person on an outstanding misdemeanor warrant.

Police said they conduct the operation, in part, to raise awareness of motorcycle safety and reduce the number of motorcycle collisions and their severity. The safety check was paid for by a grant from the California Office of Traffic Safety and through the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.
