SARASOTA — A leading motorsports industry executive is scheduled to kick off the University of South Florida Sarasota-Manatee's Knowledge-A-Bull Speaker series.

Joie Chitwood III, the president of International Speedway Corporation's flagship facility, Daytona International Speedway, will speak about his many roles in the racing industry.

When: 5:30 p.m. on Wednesday, Oct. 8

Where: USF Sarasota-Manatee's Selby Auditorium

Cost: Free

A reception follows the speaking event.

Here is a bio of Chitwood:

Chitwood III, a USF MBA alumnus, has reached the pinnacle of success in his career. He sits on the Motorsports Hall of Fame America Board of Trustees and the Sports Executive Leadership Conference Advisory Committee. Among his many roles in the racing industry, Chitwood was previously president and COO of the famed Indianapolis Motor Speedway. He was the third generation of his family to perform in the legendary Chitwood Thrill Show, which entertained audiences across the United States for decades. He was named to Sports Business Journal’s “Forty Under 40” in 2009, Indianapolis Business Journal’s “Under 40” in 2007 and was a University of South Florida College of Business Celebration of Free Enterprise Honoree in 2005.
