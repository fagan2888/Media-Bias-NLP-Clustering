India's finance ministry is targeting the country's top iron ore mining company, NMDC Ltd., as the next government company it plans to push into a big, one-time dividend payout to help the country deflate its ballooning budget deficit.

On Tuesday, state-run monopoly Coal India Ltd. said it would pay a one-time special dividend of about $3 billion. The federal government holds a 90% stake in the company.
