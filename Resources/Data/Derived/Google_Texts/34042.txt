New Castle County police were investigating Saturday four deaths at a Wilmington apartment complex a day earlier from suspected carbon monoxide poisoning.

Found Friday evening in two units of Building G of the Evergreen Apartments at Riverfront Heights (also known as Hidden Valley Apartments) on Homestead Road, police said, were Carl Dunfee, 60; Veronica Mousely, 58; Andrew Spanakos, 72, and Nancy Uniacke, 56. A dog also was found dead, police said.

In addition, five adults and three children were stricken and treated at Wilmington hospitals. One of those remained hospitalized on Saturday, listed in stable condition.
