The Douglas County Sheriff’s Office released a composite sketch on Sunday of a suspect sought in connection with a sexual assault that happened Thursday after a woman’s car broke down.

The woman was driving in the east Parker area when her vehicle broke down and she was approached by a man, who assaulted her, according to a news release.

The suspect is described as fit, 6 feet 3 inches tall and between 20 and 30 years old.

His vehicle is an older model white or light gray pickup truck with flood lights above the cab.

Anyone with information about the man should call the Douglas County Sheriff’s Office tip line at 303-660-7579.
