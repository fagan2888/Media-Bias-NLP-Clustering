An earlier Silver Alert has been canceled for a missing Surprise woman.

At 7:15 a.m. on Christmas Eve, Virginia Fink was found in the area of 27th Avenue and VanBuren, according to the Arizona Department of Public Safety.

Virginia A. Fink, 77, had last been seen on Wednesday, Dec. 23 at 10 a.m., when she left her home in the 13000 block of 181st Avenue in Surprise for an unknown location, according to DPS officials.

She had been reported missing by her husband, Henry Fink at 1:40 a.m. on Dec. 24.
