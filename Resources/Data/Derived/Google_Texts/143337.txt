An Oklahoma City convenience store owner was arrested for allegedly selling synthetic marijuana.

Authorities took Kiem Bao Nguyen into custody on charges of distributing synthetic marijuana and felony money laundering.

Police just raided the business last week.

They seized 554 packets of synthetic marijuana and $11,332 of proceeds believed to be related to the distribution of the seized drugs or involved in money laundering at KN Food Mart on N.W. 10th Street.
