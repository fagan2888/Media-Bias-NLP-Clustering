FFA and agricultural education students from all over Oklahoma are at State Fair Park in Oklahoma City for the Oklahoma Youth Expo.

Oklahoma Youth Expo is recognized as the largest youth event in the state of Oklahoma.

The livestock show brings over 7,000 exhibitors along with their educators and families from all of Oklahoma's 77 counties. They'll show 14,000 animals over several days.

The expo wraps up on Friday, March 18 with the sale of champions.
