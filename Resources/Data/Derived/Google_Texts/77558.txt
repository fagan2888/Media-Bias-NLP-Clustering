Colorado Springs Police are searching for a man believed to be involved in more than one armed robbery.

Around 9:30 Saturday night at the 1100 block of North Academy Blvd. a man showed two employees at Captain D's restaurant a handgun and demanded money.

The suspect ran away with a few hundred dollars.

Police arrived on scene and did a canine search. They were unable to locate the suspect.

There were no injuries reported.

According to CSP, the suspect is described as a tall black male around 200 pounds.

They say he matches the description as well as images from surveillance videos and could be a suspect in two other robberies in Colorado Springs.

If you have any information, please call police right away.
