Heather Truesdall and Dave Smith were married in an outdoor ceremony on Aug. 30, 2014, at Spirit Mountain Ranch in Grand Lake.

Heather's godfather, Father Bruce Swinehart, officiated the personal and heartfelt ceremony, which also included original music played by the couple's friends.

Friends and family from all over the world attended the celebration, which was followed by dinner, more music and dancing.

Heather is the daughter of Jeffrey Truesdall and Reesa Porter, of Boulder. She's a graduate of the University of Colorado College of Music. Dave is the son of Derek and Jenny Smith, of Norwich, England.

The couple live in London, where they are both musicians.
