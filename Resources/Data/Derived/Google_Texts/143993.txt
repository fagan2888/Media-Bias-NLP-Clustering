Ohio Republican Governor John Kasich joins Channel 13's Dave Price in this week's edition of The Insiders.

The U.S. Supreme Court ruled that taxpayer financed subsidies are OK to help people under the Affordable Care Act. Governor Kasich, a likely presidential candidate, angered some conservatives when he expanded Medicaid in Ohio. Other governors running for president like Scott Walker, Bobby Jindal and Rick Perry declined to expand Medicaid in their states.

Governor Terry Branstad did not rip Kasich for his decision. However, he said Iowa Caucus goers will want to know why Kasich did what he did.
