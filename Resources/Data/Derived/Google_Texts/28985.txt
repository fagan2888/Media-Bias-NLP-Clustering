American Standard Brands, a maker of toilets, sinks and other kitchen and bath products, said Ferguson Enterprises Inc. agreed to distribute its products in the U.S., starting Jan. 1.

American Standard, based in Piscataway, N.J., was acquired in June by Japan's Lixil Group Corp., one of the world's biggest makers of toilets and related items.
