A man died in a car crash on Harry Hines boulevard late Friday, police said.

About 11 p.m., the man was driving northbound in the 6000 block of Harry Hines, near Dallas Love Field Airport.

He lost control of his Chevy Tahoe and the car struck a tree.

Dallas police spokeswoman Chinh Le said police believe the man, who has not been identified, was speeding before the crash.
