A lawmaker backing Donald Trump for president says he hopes the number of Capitol Hill endorsements for the candidate doubles.

“I hope we can double our number this week ... I think after next Tuesday, the sense of inevitability will be very real,” Rep. Kevin Cramer (R-N.D.) said in an interview with The Hill's Molly K. Hooper.
