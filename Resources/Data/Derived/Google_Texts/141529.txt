One person is dead and a teenager is under arrest after an early morning altercation led to a shooting, according to the Union Springs Police Department.

According to investigators, the shooting occurred at the intersection of March Street and Hardaway Avenue around 10:30 a.m. on Saturday.

Police say the 17-year-old suspect claimed that 29-year-old Danny Adrian Parker cut him off in traffic and almost hit his vehicle. This led to a verbal altercation between the two individuals, which then turned physical.

According to police, a firearm belonging to Parker fell to the ground during the altercation. The 17-year-old reportedly retrieved the gun from the ground and shot Parker twice. Parker died from his injuries.

The 17-year-old is being charged with murder, and police say he will be charged as an adult. He is being held at the Bullock County Detention Facility.
