CHENNAI: President Pranab Mukherjee has appointed former Andhra Pradesh Governor C Rangarajan as the Chancellor of University of Hyderabad.

Mukherjee, the Visitor of the university, appointed Rangarajan for a term of three years, a release from Madras School of Economics (MSE) said here today.

A former Reserve Bank of India governor, Rangarajan is currently serving as Chairman of the MSE and President of Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.
