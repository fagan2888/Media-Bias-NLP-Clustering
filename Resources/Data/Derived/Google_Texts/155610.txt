Mitchell, SD

Hundreds of bikes gathered at the Depot in Mitchell for a poker run today; But something else happened for the first time.

Glen Shoemaker of Mitchell rode a modified motorcycle equiped to fit his electric wheelchair. Shoemaker lost both of his legs five years ago after a battle with MRSA. With the help of his friends and family, today is the first day he's able to ride a motorcycle again.
