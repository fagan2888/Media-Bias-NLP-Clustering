Find out exactly when your next Light Rail train is scheduled to arrive with the OnTime Phx transit app, created for iOS smartphones and watches and coming soon to Android devices. The app uses geo-location to give data on the closest station and arrival times for Valley Metro Light Rail.

If you have an Apple Watch, you can also find out train arrivals by merely glancing at your smartwatch. OnTime Phx was developed in the New Media Innovation & Entrepreneurship Lab by students at the Cronkite School of Journalism. Download it now:
