One person was hospitalized with a non-life threatening bullet wound early Saturday morning in a shooting in Lower Downtown Denver.

The name, gender or age of the victim has not been released.

“We have no suspect information at this time,” Denver police spokeswoman Mary McIver said Saturday.

The shooting happened on the 1500 block of Market Street at 3:45 a.m., McIver said.
