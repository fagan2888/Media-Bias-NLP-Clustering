Health Group Of The Year: McDermott Will & Emery

Law360, San Francisco (January 27, 2014, 7:21 PM ET) -- Guiding health-care solutions organization Premier Inc. through an initial public offering that yielded $820 million is just one of the accomplishments that earned McDermott Will & Emery LLP a place among Law360's Health Care Practice Groups of the Year.



McDermott has represented Premiere for the past 20 years, so it was natural to help the company reorganize and get ready for its $27-a-share IPO, said David Klatsky, a partner in the firm's health care group.
