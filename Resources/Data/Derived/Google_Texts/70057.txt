Shots rang out at a Guthrie VFW overnight.

Around 1:08 a.m., police were called to the VFW Post 4578 at 5223 S. Division St., to a report of a female being shot three times. Upon arrival, emergency crews located two victims, a female and a male who both had been shot.

According the the release, the victims were in attendance at an after party for the Kappa Alpha Psi fraternity's "Probate" ceremonies. Witnesses say a fight broke out inside the building and moved outside where the shooting took place.

At this time, police have no suspect information. OSBI has been called in to investigate.

The two victims are being treated at a local hospital and are listed in stable condition.
