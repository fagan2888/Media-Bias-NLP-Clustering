Antitrust Professors Back Rehearing In St. Luke's Merger Fight

Law360, New York (April 7, 2015, 6:06 PM ET) -- A group of prominent antitrust professors filed a proposed amicus brief Monday supporting an en banc rehearing of a Ninth Circuit decision that forced St. Luke’s Health System Ltd. to divest a local physician practice group, expressing concern that the decision will adversely affect consumer welfare.
