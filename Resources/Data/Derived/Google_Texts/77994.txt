Sioux Falls, SD

A concerned citizen in Sioux Falls helps police stop a drunk driver. The caller was following the suspect and updated police as the drunk driver drove recklessly through business parking lots near 41st St and Louise Avenue at 7:21 p.m.

Once police arrived on scene, they were able to get the driver pulled over shortly after she drove over a concrete parking block. She was highly intoxicated and refused to follow the commands of the officers. They eventually put her in custody.

52 year-old Thea Johnson of Rapid City faces a DWI charge, aggravated eluding, reckless driving, obstructing, and resisting.
