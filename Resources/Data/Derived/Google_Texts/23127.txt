Two-day beach party

The Arvada Chamber of Commerce presents the second annual Sand in the City Festival 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. June 28-29. Family-friendly festivities include a children’s zone with buried treasure, bouncy castle, giant slide, live music, vendors, food, craft beer and wine, and — more than 117,000 tons of sand that has been trucked in. Watch as more than a dozen teams create giant sand sculptures. Admission is free. Proceeds are used to raise awareness and funds for the Arvada Chamber of Commerce and its many community programs and events. Ralston Park, West 64th Avenue and Simms Street, 720-898-3380, visitarvada.org/events/sand-in-the-city/
