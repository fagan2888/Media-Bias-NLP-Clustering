Mr. Billy Lee Foster, age 67, passed away at Countryside Health Care in Lawrenceburg, Tennessee in the early morning hours of Saturday, February 22, 2014 following an extended illness.

Mr. Foster was born on July 14, 1946 to the late Clarence Lee Foster and the late Mary Glen Willodean Hughes Foster in Lawrenceburg, Tennessee. He married Lydia Ann Copper who preceded him in death in 1997. He was a brick layer by trade and of the Church of God faith.

Billy is survived by his children: Patricia (Quincy Suttle) Foster of Lawrenceburg, Gloria Foster, Belinda Foster, Delton Foster of Lawrenceburg and Anthony Foster of Lawrenceburg; siblings: Bobby (Barbara) Foster and Mike (Carrie) Foster of Lawrenceburg and Judy (Bobby) McPherson of Lewisburg; 11 grandchildren and 3 great-grandchildren. In addition to his parents and wife, he is preceded in death by three sons: Kevin, Karlton and Jonathon Foster.
