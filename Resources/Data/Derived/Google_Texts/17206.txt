A fire at a Colorado Springs church is being investigated as a possible arson.

The fire was reported just after 6 a.m. Saturday at the St. Mary's Cathedral on 22 W. Kiowa St. Authorities tell 11 News construction around the church caught fire.

There were no injuries, but there was some unspecified structural damage.

No other information has been released at this time. We will update this story when new details are made available.
