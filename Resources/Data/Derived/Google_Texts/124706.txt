A man who was ticketed for reportedly driving erratically and without a license was arrested after police found what they believe is heroin in the vehicle.

Police say at first Joshua Kirby, 24, was ticketed and released on the traffic violations, but while his vehicle was being prepared for impound, police say they found the drugs.

Police tracked down Kirby again and arrested him. He is charged with unlawful possession of a schedule one substance and unlawful distribution of a schedule one substance.

Police first pulled over Kirby at approximately 3:40 a.m. Sunday near Austin Bluffs Pkwy and Union Blvd. in Colorado Springs.
