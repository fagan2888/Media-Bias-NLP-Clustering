The mystery photo? From a Jan. 4 article, “Another Silent Protest of Mayor de Blasio as Officer Liu Is Laid to Rest,” the caption reads, “Hundreds of officers participated in the silent protest outside the funeral home by turning their backs when Mr. de Blasio spoke.” Police union officials, already upset with the mayor for, in their view, seeming to condone criticism of the police, said after the shooting of Officers Liu and Ramos that the “blood on the hands” started at City Hall.
