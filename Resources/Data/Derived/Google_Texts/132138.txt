Northbound Highway 99 between Taft Highway and Panama Lane is expected to be closed tonight from 11:00 p.m. to 5:00 a.m. as crews remove temporary support structures from the new Hosking Avenue bridge.

Traffic will be diverted at Taft Highway to South H Street, where drivers can re-enter northbound Highway 99 at Panama Lane.

And tonight between 9:00 p.m. and 5:00 a.m., the two inside eastbound lanes of Highway 58 between Real Road and H Street are expected to be closed.

The eastbound lane connecting northbound Highway 99 to eastbound 58 will remain open.

Access to 58 from Real Road will also be closed.
