Police are investigating a homicide after a man’s body was found Monday evening in Commerce City, officials said.

The body was found at 4900 Sandcreek Drive South around 6:30 p.m., said Commerce City police spokesman Dave Cubbage.

“We don’t have anything else to go on,” Cubbage said.

This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
