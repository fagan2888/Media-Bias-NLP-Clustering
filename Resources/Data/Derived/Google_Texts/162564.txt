Health investments rose slightly for the third day in a row as of 2:40 p.m. Thursday.

Overall investments in the industry increased 0.08 percent. Only investments in the medical device industry had fallen, dropping 0.07 percent. Shares in the payers and providers sectors saw the largest increase, rising 0.39 percent.

Investments in NASDAQ-listed biotechnology stocks also creeped upward 0.04 percent and shares in the pharmaceutical industry grew 0.15 percent.
