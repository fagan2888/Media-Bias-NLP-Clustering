Insurance Co. Asks Court to Nix Transgender Bias Suit

Law360, New York (March 7, 2016, 4:27 PM ET) -- Minnesota-based health insurance company HealthPartners has urged a Minnesota federal court to dismiss a suit brought by a woman who says the company illegally refused to cover her son’s gender reassignment surgery, arguing that her lawsuit is moot because the exclusion of such surgery has been removed from her health plan.
