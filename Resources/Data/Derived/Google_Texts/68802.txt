Tulsa police are looking for a man who stole frozen pizza and beer from area convenience stores more than 30 times over the past few months.

TPD's Organized Retail Crime Unit said the man is targeting QuikTrip convenience stores.

According to police, the man picks out beer or frozen pizza from the store then leaves without paying. Surveillance photos shows the man leaving one store while carrying five frozen pizzas and another store carrying beer.

Police say the man has several accomplices who drive him to a QuikTrip in late-model vehicles.

Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 918-596-COPS.
