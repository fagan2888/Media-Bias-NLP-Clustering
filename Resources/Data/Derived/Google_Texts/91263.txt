Update at 8:11 p.m.: Irving police said Gutierrez has been located and is safe.

Original post: Irving police are asking for the public’s help locating a missing 20-year-old who was last seen Saturday morning after he left his home for a walk.

Police said around 11:30 a.m. Jesus Gutierrez left his home in the 1000 block of Blaylock Circle, near North McArthur Boulevard, to go for a walk in a nearby park.

Police said Gutierrez has been diagnosed with Asperger’s syndrome, walks with an unusual gait and may be lost or confused. He typically walks in parks and on major thoroughfares in south Irving, police said.

He is described as a Hispanic male who is about 5-foot-2, weighs about 160 pounds and has brown hair, brown eyes and wears glasses. He has a mustache and a light beard, and was last seen wearing a gray shirt and black jeans.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Irving Police Department at (972) 273-1010.
