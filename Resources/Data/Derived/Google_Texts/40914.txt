WASHINGTON—Strutting, proud, and given to noisy, chest-puffing displays, Plains-states legislators in the U.S. Senate on Wednesday considered the fate of the Lesser Prairie Chicken, one of their most controversial constituents.

While it’s become more scarce out West, the threatened Lesser Prairie Chicken has been popping up all over Capitol Hilland Republicans have declared open season.
