Early on in Showtime’s new half-hour comedy “Happyish,” an advertising agency pitch-maker basically sums up the show’s theme: “How do we sell happiness in the age of disillusionment?” Or, in the case of the show’s star, Thom Payne (Steve Coogan) how does an acutely sensitive ad exec in a midlife crisis stay true to his principles and not get left behind in the age of social media and rebranding? Especially when his agency’s creative team has been taken over by a pair of accented Swedish whiz kids who challenge the staff with insights such as: “First came black and white, then came color. What comes after color?”
