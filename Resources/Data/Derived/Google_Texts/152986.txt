The Bluffton Police Department are investigating after a shooting in a parking lot left one person dead.

Police say shortly after 4:30 on January 3rd, one person was shot in the parking lot of the Dollar General on May River Road.

Witnesses say that there were two vehicles in the parking lot when the victim was shot. They are currently looking for a suspect who fled the scene in a dark-colored vehicle.

Anyone who has information is being asked to call Lt. Joseph Babkiewicz or Sgt. Kelly Heany at (843)706-4560.
