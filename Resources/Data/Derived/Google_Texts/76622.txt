A Tarboro man with a history of breaking and entering was arrested at an apartment complex Friday.

Greenville police say it was around 5:30 p.m. when they apprehended Ladarius Powell, 23. He was at a University Suites apartment in Greenville.

Police say Powell had several warrants for first degree burglary and felony larceny for a September 27th incident at the Bellamy Apartment Complex.

Powell is in the Pitt County Detention Center under a $410,000 bond. He will go before a judge Monday.
