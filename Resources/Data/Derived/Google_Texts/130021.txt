Princeton took the top spot in U.S. News & World Report's annual ranking of national universities. (Photo: Princeton)

U.S. News & World Report released its annual college rankings this week with Princeton University taking the top spot among the best national universities.

The report ranked colleges and universities in four different categories: national universities, national liberal arts colleges, regional universities and regional colleges.

Rutgers in Piscataway ranked number 72 among national universities and Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken came in at number 75.

College of New Jersey in Ewing took the third spot for best northern regional universities; Rowan University in Glassboro ranked at number 19 and Rider University in Lawrenceville was number 22. Ramapo College of New Jersey in Mahwah was 26, Montclair State University was 34 and Monmouth University in West Long Branch was 36.

To view more rankings visit the report at www.usnews.com.
