Greenville police arrested two people and are searching for two more in connection with a string of car break-ins throughout the city.

Officials said 17 cars have been broken into since Thursday.

After an investigation, authorities identified three suspects.

Two suspects -- James Robert Hudson and Dianna Breeze Strickland -- were apprehended. Items such as laptops, credit cards, debit cards and a stolen vehicle were recovered.

Justin Edward McLeod and a fourth unidentified female are wanted by police. The unidentified female is pictured above.
