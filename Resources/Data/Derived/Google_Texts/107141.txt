WIDE RECEIVERS (12)

Jordy Nelson (Ht.: 6-2 1/2; Wt.: 211; Age: 30; Acquired: D2-'08; College: Kansas State)

Came of age in 2010 playoffs. Based on Pro Football Weekly's positional rankings, he was unranked entering '11, 19th in '12, 20th in '13, eighth in '14 and fifth this year.

Randall Cobb (Ht.: 5-10; Wt.: 195; Age: 24; Acquired: D2-'11; College: Kentucky)

Dropped nine of 145 targeted passes (6.2%) in 2014, zero of 46 in '13, 10 of 110 (9%) in '12 and two of 34 (5.9%) in '11.

Davante Adams (Ht.: 6-1; Wt.: 214; Age: 22; Acquired: D2-'14; College: Fresno State)

Played just nine snaps as No. 3 compared to Jarrett Boykin's 49 in opener. The pair each had 70 snaps in Weeks 2-3 before Adams outplayed Boykin, 777-119, in Weeks 4-18.

Ty Montgomery (Ht.: 6-0; Wt.: 216; Age: 22; Acquired: D3-'15; College: Stanford)

Fourteenth WR selected. Made 26 starts in four seasons, missing substantial time with knee and shoulder injuries. Vertical jump of 40½, broad jump of 10-1, 16 reps on the bench and Wonderlic of 24.

Jeff Janis (Ht.: 6-3; Wt.: 215; Age: 24; Acquired: D7-'14; College: Saginaw Valley State)

Tremendous testing athlete: 4.42 40, 37½-inch vertical jump, 10-3 broad jump, 20 reps on bench press. Posted scores of 23 and 32 on the Wonderlic intelligence test.

Jared Abbrederis (Ht.: 6-1; Wt.: 193; Age: 24; Acquired: D5-'14; College: Wisconsin)

Blew out his right ACL July 30 in fourth practice of rookie camp. First Packers' draftee from UW since G Bill Ferrario (fourth round) in '01.

Myles White (Ht.: 6-0 1/2; Wt.: 192; Age: 25; Acquired: FA-'13; College: Louisiana Tech)

Played 123 snaps in Games 6-13 as a rookie but beaten out for No. 5 job by Janis in second training camp. Began collegiately at Michigan State. Deep threat with 4.46 speed.

Larry Pinkard (Ht.: 6-0; Wt.: 196; Age: 23; Acquired: FA-'15; College: Old Dominion)

Caught 160 passes (14.6) for 25 TDs at ODU before being dismissed from team in June 2014 for failed drug test (marijuana). Practiced at Bluefield (Va.) in '15 but wasn't eligible to play.

Adrian Coxson (Ht.: 6-0 1/2; Wt.: 207; Age: 23; Acquired: FA-'15; College: Stony Brook)

Fastest of five free-agent rookie WRs (4.46). Spent time at Florida and Maryland before starting two of three years at Stony Brook, catching 84 for 1,335 (15.9)_and 10 TDs.

Jimmie Hunt (Ht.: 6-0; Wt.: 208; Age: 24; Acquired: FA-'15; College: Missouri)

Backed up in 2012 before playing extensively in 2013-'14. Had 74 receptions (16.3) and 12 TDs. Posted career bests (6-169) against Alabama in 2014 SEC Championship Game.

Ricky Collins (Ht.: 6-0; Wt.: 201; Age: 23; Acquired: FA-'15; College: Texas A&M-Commerce)

Played two JUCO seasons, six games at Midwestern State in '12 and then 2013-'14 for Division II Lions. Terrific senior year: 71 catches, 1,187 (16.7) yards and 14 TDs.

Javess Blue (Ht.: 5-11 1/2; Wt.: 194; Age: 22; Acquired: FA-'15; College: Kentucky)

Started two years for Wildcats, catching 72 for 1,111 (15.4) and nine TDs. Used mostly in the slot. Eager to join Cobb and former UK P Tim Masthay.
