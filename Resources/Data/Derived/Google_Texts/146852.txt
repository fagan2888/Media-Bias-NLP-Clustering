The Charleston County Sheriff's Department is working with the Coast Guard and the North Charleston Police Department on a water rescue in the Ashley river.

Officials say they are searching for a black male who was last seen near Drayton Hall on the river.

Right now, deputies say the search for the man has been called off for tonight but they will resume tomorrow.

This is a developing story.
