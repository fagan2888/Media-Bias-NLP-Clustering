Video was released Friday showing a bar brawl in Beaver Falls.

The Jan. 31 fight happened near the Evergreen Cafe and was caught by surveillance video.

According to Beaver County police, the fight involved two biker groups known as The War Dogs and The Rebels.

"Our investigation determined that several people were assaulted physically," Sgt. Thomas Lococo said. "Two people did receive wounds from gunshots and one person was stabbed."

So far, six people have been charged and more arrests are pending.
