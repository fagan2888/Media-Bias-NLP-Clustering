Attys Can Use 'Confidential' Vocera Docs In Investor Suits

Law360, Los Angeles (August 1, 2014, 8:38 PM ET) -- A California federal judge on Thursday refused to block investors in two securities class actions accusing Vocera Communications Inc. of artificially inflating its stock prices from using purportedly confidential internal company documents, ruling investigators working for the plaintiffs' counsel didn't obtain them improperly.
