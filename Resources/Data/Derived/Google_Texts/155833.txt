Do you know the way to San Jose? Satisfied employees certainly do, according to the second annual Employment Satisfaction Report Card by City (2014), which was released by Glassdoor.

The California city ranked No. 1 for the second consecutive year in a comparison of the 50 largest metropolitan areas by overall employee satisfaction, number of employers hiring, business outlook expectations and other criteria.
