(KTVI)- A 95-year tradition is coming to an end at Yacovelli's in Florissant. The banquet hall has been handed down from generation to generation and has moved all over the metro area. Jack and Jan Yacovelli smile at employees while fighting back tears, remembering all the wedding receptions and graduation parties the hosted through the years.

Many employees have worked at the banquet hall for over 40 years.

Family, friends and employees will gather for their "Last Chance Dinner Dance" on December 26, 2014. The banquet's final party will be on New Year's Eve.
