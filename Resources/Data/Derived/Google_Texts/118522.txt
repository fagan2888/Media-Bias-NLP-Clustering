Two state insurance commissioners who normally support Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (PPACA) requirements and programs are at odds over a PPACA fixer bill.

Mike Kreidler, the Washington state insurance commissioner, testified today at a House Energy & Commerce health subcommittee hearing in opposition to H.R. 1624, a bill that would let each state choose whether to set its small-group size limit at 50 employees or 100 employees.
