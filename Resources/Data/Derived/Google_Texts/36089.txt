Under the 2010 health care law called the Affordable Care Act, small businesses with fewer than 50 employees can enroll in the D.C. health insurance exchange. But more than 13,000 employees of the U.S. Congress are also enrolled in the exchange. Roll Call reporter Bridget Bowman joined WAMU 88.5's Matt McCleskey to discuss why all those staffers use the exchange, and explain the legal challenge that the setup is facing.
