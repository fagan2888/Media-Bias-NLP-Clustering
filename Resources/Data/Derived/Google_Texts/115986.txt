Wings, pizza and nachos? Some of New York's Super Bowl fans may be too highbrow for such run-of-the-mill tailgating food.

Several chefs around the city, an unofficial host for Sunday's game at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, N.J., are offering party menus for those with more global, gourmet palates: spicy tripe-ragu hot dogs, buffalo chicken pierogi and a 3-foot sub (of the Cambodian variety).
