2nd Circ. Case Will Have Far-Reaching ERISA Implications

Law360, New York (June 23, 2014, 11:15 AM ET) -- When a general liability policy expressly provides coverage for employee benefits liability and the plaintiff in the underlying suit alleges a violation of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act, does the insurer have a duty to defend the suit? Resoundingly, yes, according to the Second Circuit in a well-reasoned opinion handed down on June 10.
