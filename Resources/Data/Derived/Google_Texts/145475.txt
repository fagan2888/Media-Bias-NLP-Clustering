A 26-year-old man was killed during a robbery early today in Wimauma and authorities are looking for two suspects seen by witnesses leaving the scene.

Authorities said the body of Christino B. Galvez was found at Mi Amigos Mobile Home Park, 610 Railroad St., where he lives.

The Hillsborough County Sheriff’s Office said another person was injured during the incident. Information about him was not released because he is considered a witness.

The robbery took place about 3:30 a.m., deputies said

No description of the suspects or the vehicle they were in was available.

The sheriff’s office said an investigation is ongoing.

No other information was released.
