A 9-year-old Kansas boy has died in an all-terrain vehicle crash in Lyon County.

The Kansas Highway Patrol identified the victim as Ryan Lane, of Reading. He was riding on the ATV on Saturday night when the 11-year-old driver lost control. The patrol says the vehicle toppled onto its side about three miles north of Miller.

Lane was taken to a hospital, where he died. The patrol says the driver and another 9-year-old passenger also were injured.
