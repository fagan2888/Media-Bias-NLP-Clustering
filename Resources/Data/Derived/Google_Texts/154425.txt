KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia—Malaysia’s police chief said Monday that 17 suspected militants were detained for allegedly plotting to carry out acts of terror in the country’s largest city, Kuala Lumpur.

Inspector General of Police Khalid Abu Bakar said the 17 people were detained Sunday. Mr. Khalid tweeted that two of them had just returned from Syria. He didn’t give further details and a police official said a statement will be issued...
