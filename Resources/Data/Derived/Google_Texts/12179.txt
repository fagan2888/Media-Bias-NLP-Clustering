Highway 54 east of El Dorado has reopened following a head-on crash that left five people injured.

The crash happened just before 10 a.m. in the 2100 block of Highway 54, east of the El Dorado Correctional Facility. The Butler County Sheriff's Office said two vehicles crashed head-on, causing one to overturn.

A female who was trapped inside one of the vehicles was freed and taken to the hospital with potentially serious injuries.

The sheriff's office said four others had minor injuries.

Further details were not provided.
