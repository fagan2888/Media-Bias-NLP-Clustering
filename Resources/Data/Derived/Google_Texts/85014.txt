In an industry not noted for career longevity, movie mogul Sir Run Run Shaw, who died last week at the age of 106, has set a gold standard for durability. Truly the last of the titans, Shaw was born when cinema was in its infancy. His own life and career grew alongside movies' evolution from the black-and-white silents to today's global media industry.
