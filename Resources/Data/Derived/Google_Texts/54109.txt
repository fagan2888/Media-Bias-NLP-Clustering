A holiday hole-in-one

Go miniature golfing — at night — on the four-acre Adventure Golf & Raceway in Westminster. “A Glowing Winter Adventure” is open 5:30 to 9 nightly through Jan. 3. The course is decked out for the holidays with thousands of lights and attractions. Weekend entertainment includes visits with Santa. Refreshments, including s’mores, hot chocolate and a beer garden, are also available. General admission is $10, discounts available for seniors and groups. 9650 Sheridan Blvd., Westminster, 303-650-6587, adventuregolfandraceway.com
