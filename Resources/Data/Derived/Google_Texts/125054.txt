Sioux Falls, SD

Over 250 Sioux Falls area kids will compete in the First LEGO League in the Elman Center at Augustana College.

The festivities kick off at 8 a.m. and last until 5 p.m.

The regional tournament will put 16 weeks of research, design and programming to the test as the teams face off with their custom built robots.

The winners will make a trip to the FLL World Festival on April 22 through the 25 at the Edward Jones Dome in St. Louis.
