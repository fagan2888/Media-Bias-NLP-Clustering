With the 2016 presidential race heating up, people are taking to as many outlets as possible to voice their opinions about potential candidates. One Democratic candidate, Bernie Sanders , is getting especially high praise from young people through social media. It seems new, young voters are attracted to Sanders' focus on the middle class , genuine interest in people's needs and high level of intelligence .
