SNL’s opening featured a bumbling, wide-eyed Tom Brady denying any knowledge of deflate-gate, and exclaiming incredulously, ““Whaaaattt?!”

Then absurd, total denials: “I’m not worried about the air pressure in the ball, that’s nerd stuff. That’s honestly above my pay grade.”

“You make 26.5 million a year.”

“Is that a lot? Guys, I don’t know things!”
