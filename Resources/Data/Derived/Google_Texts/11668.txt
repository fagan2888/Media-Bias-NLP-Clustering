At the request of the Kinston Department of Public Safety, the N.C. Center for Missing Persons has cancelled the Silver Alert for Norma Jean Sutton-Brown.

A Silver Alert is issued for a missing local woman.

Kinston Police are looking for 70-year-old Norma Jean Sutton-Brown, who was last seen at 633 East Washington Avenue in Kinston.

Police say Sutton-Brown is about 5'2" and weighs 90 pounds. She was last seen wearing a lavender shirt, blue jeans and croc shoes.

Police say they don't know where she may be headed, but we're told she drives a white 2002 Dodge van with North Carolina plates BAP2571.

Anyone who sees her is asked to call the Kinston Department of Public Safety.
