Last night Jimmy Fallon managed to work in a nice, short little send-off to Jon Stewart in the middle of “Thank You Notes.”

Amid jokes about the Pope and peas in guacamole, Fallon brought up Stewart’s departure from The Daily Show on Thursday, which saw the return of almost every single correspondent ever.

He thanked Stewart for taping his final Daily Show episode before saying, “Or as Fox News called it, their moment of zen.”
