Houston Freeze Stifling Health Care Fraud, DOJ Atty Says

Law360, New York (January 6, 2015, 3:15 PM ET) -- A groundbreaking moratorium by Medicare on new ambulance providers in Houston appears to be sharply reducing fraud involving emergency transportation, but a similar freeze on home health care providers hasn’t yet delivered clear-cut results, a top official in the U.S. attorney’s office for southern Texas told Law360 in an interview.
