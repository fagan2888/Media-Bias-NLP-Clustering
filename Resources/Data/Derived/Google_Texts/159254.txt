In this installment of Cooking Healthy in Indian Country, Meagen Baldy shows viewers how to replace pasta with “our favorite summer friend” spaghetti squash, which is a good source of vitamin C, vitamin A, potassium as well as helping with prostate health.

The recipe is a simple one that involves any spaghetti sauce you like, and shredded spaghetti squash cooked on the stove. This is a good recipe for a hot day when you don’t want to turn the oven on.
