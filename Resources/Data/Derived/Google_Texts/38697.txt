Murdo, SD

A 24-year-old Rapid City man is dead after crashing head on into a semi-truck.

Charles Thesing was heading eastbound in a Chevy pickup with 22-year-old Quintez Provancial. The Chevy went into a construction zone with head to head traffic. Thesing crossed into the westbound lane and hit a Freightliner semi-truck driven by 36-year-old Trygg Andrew Johnson.

Thesing died at the scene. Provancial was taken to a Pierre hospital and later transferred to a Sioux Falls hospital. Neither was wearing a seatbelt.

Johnson was not injured. He was wearing his seatbelt.

No further information is available.

The South Dakota Highway Patrol is investigating.
