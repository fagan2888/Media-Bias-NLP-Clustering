A man was arrested in San Antonio, Texas, on a first-degree murder complaint in connection to the death of an Oklahoma City woman.

According to Oklahoma City police, on Tuesday, 61-year-old Merwin Hill was arrested in San Antonio on an arrest warrant for a first-degree murder charge, in connection to the death of 54-year-old Joyce Grant.

On Friday, February 26, 54-year-old Joyce Grant was found dead inside her home in the 3000 block of N. Robinson. Police said Grant had injuries consistent with homicide. Her vehicle was missing from her home, but was later found unoccupied near N.E. 23rd and Broadway.
