Rapid City, SD

Around 5:30 a.m. police in Rapid City responded to a call on Mountain View Road for an unknown problem. Upon arrival, police spoke with several subjects and found a dead body behind Cold Stone Creamery.

Police consider the death suspicious and two people are currently being interviewed by detectives. No arrests have been made.

The victim has not been identified and is pending an autopsy.

No further information is available.
