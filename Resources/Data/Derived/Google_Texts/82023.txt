Sioux Falls, SD

Anyone eager to see the inner workings of the Sanford Pentagon in Sioux Falls can take one of several upcoming tours planned in the coming weeks.



Open houses will be held Oct. 12, Oct. 17, Oct. 22, Oct. 30 and Nov. 4.



The Pentagon is located at the new Sanford Sports Complex. It's a 160,000-square-foot, five-sided building featuring nine basketball courts, including the 1950s/1960s-inspired Heritage Court.



The complex is on Westport Avenue between Benson Road and West 54th Street.
