Colorado Springs Police stopped a suspicious only to find the driver not only had multiple warrants out for his arrest, but he also had meth, heroin, prescription pills, and an illegal handgun on him.

The stop happened around 5:40 Sunday morning in the 3800 block of Van Teylingen Drive.

Police say that Damon McDuffie was the only person in the car.

McDuffie had multiple active warrants for his arrest including: burglary, assault, menacing, child abuse, harassment, and reckless endangerment.

Police say McDuffie had a handgun in his waistband when they stopped him.

He was arrested and booked into the El Paso County Jail for the outstanding warrants as well as new charges for illegal possession of a firearm by a previous offender, and the drug possession charges.
