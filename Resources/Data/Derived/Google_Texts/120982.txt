A Walton County man is dead after officials say he crashed his car into a tree Saturday morning in DeFuniak Springs.

Florida Highway Patrol (FHP) reports state, around 2:30 a.m., Mark Unterborn, 63, was driving west on County Road 185, approaching the intersection of State Road 83.

FHP officials state, Unterborn drove off the road when coming to the intersection and crashed into a tree.

A report shows he was not wearing a seatbelt. Unterborn was pronounced dead on scene.

At this time officials are investigating if alcohol was a factor.
