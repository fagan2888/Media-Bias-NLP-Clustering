LITTLE ROCK, Ark. - An individual was hospitalized after a shooting in Little Rock on Wednesday night, police say.

The shooting took place in the 4200 block of Apple Avenue around 7 p.m., according to the Little Rock Police Department.

The victim was taken to a local hospital in a private car.

As of 7:50 p.m., the condition and identity of the victim had not been released.
