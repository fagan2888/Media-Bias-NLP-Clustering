Published Tuesday, March 29th. 2016 -- 10:56 p.m.

JANESVILLE, Wis. -- Presidential Candidate Donald Trump spoke at a town hall event at the Holiday Inn Express in Janesville.

About 1,000 people were let inside for the event. Trump's speech was played over loud speakers for thousands more waiting outside after the event space reached capacity.

Trump explained how he would make better international trade deals to help improve Wisconsin's economy and lower the state's unemployment rate.

He also emphasized the importance of providing better resources to veterans, bringing manufacturing jobs back to the United States and ending Obamacare and Common Core.

Trump says a Wisconsin win could secure his nomination.He says he plans on traveling around the state throughout the week.

The event remained peaceful. One protester was escorted out by security.
