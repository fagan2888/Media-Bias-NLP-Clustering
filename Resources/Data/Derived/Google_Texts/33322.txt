Oklahoma City police arrested a man on prostitution charges Tuesday night.

According to police, a Vice Enforcement officer made contact with the suspect, 21-year-old Bryan Christopher Phillips. Police said Phillips agreed to provide a specific sexual contact in exchange for $200.

Phillips was arrested and booked into the Oklahoma County Jail on charges of use of a computer to violate state statutes (Internet prostitution,) and offering to engage in prostitution.

He is being held on $5,000 bond.
