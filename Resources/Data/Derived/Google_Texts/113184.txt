Open Door Health Center in Mankato is the recipient of a very competitive grant.It comes from the Affordable Care Act; the aim is to invest in the behavioral health concerns of citizens across the country.

Open Door received a Behavioral health integration grant worth two hundred and fifty thousand dollars.

The money will add behavioral health team members... allowing more patients who need behavioral health services to be seen. ((20))

Open Door Health Center CEO, Sarah Kruse says, "So we're very happy to be the recipient of this behavioral health integration grant. It's a very special grant because not only is it focusing on the behavioral health needs it also integrates that in primary care."

Open Door Health Center will also be able to provide chemical health assessments as well as therapy.
