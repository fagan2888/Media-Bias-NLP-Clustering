Wichita Police are now investigating five armed robberies occurring at local business since Friday evening. The latest incident happening Saturday night at the CD Tradepost near Central and Ridge.

Officers responded to the call at about 8:30 p.m. Two employees told officers two men entered the store around closing time. The armed suspects demanded cash then left with the money in a gold-colored SUV travelling southbound on Ridge Road.

Police say it is unclear whether this incident is connected to the four other robberies occurring at Wichita businesses since Friday night.
