Larry and the former Barb Ruth were married March 18, 1955, in Emmanual Lutheran Church in Hermantown.

Their children are Kathy and Rick Johnson and Dave and Terri Huttel, all of Island Lake; Connie and Paul Richardson of Redlands, Calif.; and the late Duane (Julie) Huttel of Hermantown .

Secret to staying married: Just love each other and spend time together.
