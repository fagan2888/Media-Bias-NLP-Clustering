A man fired a gun at a another man fleeing a fight at a west Wichita complex Friday morning.

A neighbors tells Wichita police he left his apartment in the 400 block of S Meridian at just before 6:30 a.m. to check on some commotion after hearing a woman screaming loudly outside. That's when he saw two men fighting nearby.

The witness telling officers one of the men then got up and ran down the stairs toward the complex parking lot. The man he was fighting pulled out a gun, firing at least two shots as the victim fled.

None of the bullets hit either the man running away or another man who was also nearby. The victim then got into a car that also had two young children in it at the time.

Police did not give a detailed description of the shooter.
