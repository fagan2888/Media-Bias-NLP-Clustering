John Kasich is calling himself the "only hope" to stop Hillary Clinton in a new Star Wars Day-themed ad.

The 90-second advertisement paints a picture of the year 2017, as yellow text mimics the opening crawl of the "Star Wars" movies.

In this not-so-distant future, Clinton has just defeated Donald Trump "in the largest landslide since Reagan in 1984."

Also in this future, Nancy Pelosi is the speaker of the House planning new tax hikes and Chuck Schumer leads a Democratic Senate majority.
