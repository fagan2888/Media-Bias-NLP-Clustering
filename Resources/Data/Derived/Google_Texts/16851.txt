Loeb Adds Ex-Porzio Partner For New FDA Regulatory Practice

Law360, New York (January 27, 2014, 3:18 PM ET) -- Loeb & Loeb LLP has snagged an experienced health and life sciences attorney to lead the firm’s new U.S. Food and Drug Administration regulatory and compliance practice in New York and Washington, D.C., the firm announced Monday.



Scott Liebman joined Loeb as a partner Monday from Porzio Bromberg & Newman PC, where he worked as principal and served as vice president of the firm’s subsidiary, Porzio Life Sciences LLC.
