Last evening, a friend on Instagram shared a photo of orange leaves gracefully littering an outdoor staircase. It was captioned, appropriately, “This is bullsh*t.”

I second the sentiment. And as if this fleeting summer weren’t already ending too soon, Starbucks had to go ahead and announce, via Twitter, that it’s ready to reintroduce its seasonal Pumpkin Spice Latte on Aug. 25 — a full month before the official start of autumn. Some branches, apparently, are already serving them.
