BILLINGS, Mont. (AP) - A 24-year-old Billings man has pleaded guilty to posting nude photos of his ex-girlfriend on Craigslist and pretending to be her seeking sexual encounters.

Mathew Christopher Connery was charged with misdemeanor privacy in communications for listing her phone number in the casual encounters sections of Craigslist. A nude photo she had provided him while the two were in a relationship was also posted.

The Billings Gazette reports (http://bit.ly/24itAlz ) that Connery entered his plea before Yellowstone County Justice of the Peace David Carter this past week.

Connery is scheduled to be sentenced Feb. 25.
