First Avenue is back open after a fatal motorcycle accident earlier today.

The investigation by Tucson Police shut down the road between River and Wetmore for several hours..



We're told the motorcyclist hit a pickup truck that was turning out of a driveway.



Police say he knew he was going to hit the truck so he laid the bike down and ended up sliding underneath the truck.



No name for the motorcyclist killed has been released.



Police say speed was a factor in the accident.



This is the second fatal motorcycle crash this year.
