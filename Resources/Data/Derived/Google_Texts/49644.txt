The moment has come to share the exiting news of The Charleston Gaillard Center’s pending completion and Grand Opening Gala!

The inaugural concert of the Martha & John Rivers Performance Hall on October 18, 2015 will feature world-renowned cellist Yo-Yo Ma, accompanied by the Charleston Symphony Orchestra and its new Music Director, Ken Lam.

The Gala starts on Sunday, October 18 at 2:30 pm, at which point the doors open and Champagne is served. At 4:00 pm the concert will commence. Patrons may opt to join the following Cocktail Reception and Gala Dinner in the magnificent Grand Ballroom.

Formal invitations have already been issued this summer for the Opening Performance and Gala.
