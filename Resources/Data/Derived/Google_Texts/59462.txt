BigLaw Eyes Benefits Cuts Amid 'Cadillac' Tax Wrangling

Law360, New York (November 3, 2015, 10:44 PM ET) -- Growing uncertainty about the fate of the Affordable Care Act’s “Cadillac” tax won’t stop some in BigLaw from following in the footsteps of Debevoise & Plimpton LLP and making employee health care cuts this year, experts said.



And while law firms remain reluctant to do anything that appears to be skimping on associate benefits or fall behind competing firms, many will be looking to employees to kick in more for health coverage before any form of the controversial Obamacare tax hits in 2018.
