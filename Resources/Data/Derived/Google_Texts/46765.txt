One person was seriously hurt in a two-vehicle crash near the intersection of Tangerine and Twin Peak roads on Saturday, March 19, authorities said.

Brian Keeley, spokesman for the Northwest Fire District, said the crash happened around 9 p.m. in Marana.

Keeley said the driver of one of the vehicles was taken to the hospital for treatment of serious injuries. First responders could not find anyone in the other vehicle.

Keeley said the Marana Police Department and DPS are investigating the accident.
