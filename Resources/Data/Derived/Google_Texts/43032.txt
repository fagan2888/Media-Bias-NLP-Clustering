A man is electrocuted while working on a sign at a car dealership Thursday afternoon.

According to Oklahoma City fire officials, David Emo was working on a sign at Reynolds Ford located at 600 W. Memorial Road in Oklahoma City when he was electrocuted.

Firefighters used a ladder to get Emo down and he was transported to Baptist Hospital where he was pronounced dead at 2:45 p.m. The accident is under investigation.
