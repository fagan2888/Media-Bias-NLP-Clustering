Two historic rivals face off on Saturday at Howard Wood Field.

Sioux Falls Washington will take on O'Gorman at the 35th Annual Dakota Bowl. Both teams are 1-1 this season.

If you can't make it to the game, you can watch it live on KELOLAND.com. Kickoff is scheduled for 8 p.m. and that's when our live coverage will start as well. You can watch the action and listen to play by play from ESPN 99.1.
