There is no way to know from the opening scene of “The Driver,” involving a wild car chase, that what’s about to unfold is a drama that encompasses great themes—the claims of conscience, the call of temptation, the indissoluble ties that bind families. Or that the death grip in which it holds your attention will never for a moment relax in this three-part tale of Vince McKee (a sterling David Morrissey).
