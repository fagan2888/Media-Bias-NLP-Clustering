Denver police are seeking a suspect in connection with a Saturday homicide.

Police are asking the public to call 911 immediately if they see Alonso Nunez-Cabrera, 32, who is wanted for first-degree murder.

The body of an adult man was found Saturday morning outside a nightclub called The Stone on Morrison Road just south of Westwood Park.

Tyrone Campbell, a police spokesperson, said police received a call about the body at 6:11 a.m. Saturday. Officers arrived on scene shortly thereafter.
