NY Tells Insurers They Must Cover Transgender Treatment

Law360, New York (December 11, 2014, 7:32 PM ET) -- The state of New York on Thursday issued regulatory guidance to insurance providers in the state saying it is illegal under state and federal law for them to deny medically necessary treatment for transgender people.
