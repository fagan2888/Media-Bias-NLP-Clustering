Sturgis, SD

The South Dakota Department of Public Safety has released initial law enforcement statistics for the days prior to the 75th annual Sturgis Rally.

Authorities in western South Dakota have arrested 25 people for DUI. 22 arrests were made in Sturgis, two made in the Rapid City district.

Officials have made 29 misdemeanor drug arrests (24 in Sturgis, 5 in Rapid City), and 17 felony drug arrests (8 in Sturgis, 9 in Rapid City). 289 citations have been issued, along with 765 warnings. $5,872 and one vehicle has been seized by law enforcement.

The statistics are current as of 6 a.m. Saturday, Aug. 1.

The Sturgis Motorcycle Rally is set to officially begin on Monday, and runs through Sunday, Aug. 9.
