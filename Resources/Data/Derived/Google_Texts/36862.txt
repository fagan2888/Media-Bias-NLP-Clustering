The Kansas Highway Patrol and Hutchinson Police were involved in a chase Sunday afternoon.

It began shortly before 5 p.m. in the 900 block of West 4th Street when a trooper began pursuing a suspect that he knew was wanted by law enforcement. Hutchinson Police assisted in the chase, which ended in the 100 block of East 10 Street, where the suspect entered a home.

Police set up a perimeter were able to talk everyone out of the house. The suspect, 35-year-old Dominique Vargas, was arrested for felony flee and elude, leaving the scene of an accident, fail to give information at motor vehicle accident, and driving while suspended.

A Hutchinson Police vehicle suffered minor damage when the suspect clipped the SUV with his vehicle, but there were no injuries.
