Panama City, FL---A day after falling to .500 in Conference play, losing on the road at Pensacola Friday night, the Gulf Coast baseball team returned the favor Saturday.

The Commodores (28-13, 6-5) powered past the Pirates with a pair of home runs from Christian Williams and another from Wes Roberson to win 12-3 in eight innings. GC tallied together 15 total hits.

Starter Matt Foster earned his ninth win of the season.

After splitting the first two matchups, the rubber game of the series will take place in Pensacola this Monday at 6 PM.
