A 19-year-old woman died Sunday in a one-vehicle accident in Cass County.

Kayla S. Boyd of Astoria was traveling along Illinois Route 125 just west of Shiloh Road about 5:40 a.m. when her car went off the road and into a ditch, causing it to roll several times.

Boyd, who was wearing a seat belt, died at the scene.

She was the only person in the car, according to an Illinois State Police report.
