Police investigate a deadly drive-by shooting early this morning in Topeka.

Officers rushed to a hospital around 2:10 a.m. after a victim was taken there by a private vehicle, states Lt. Bryan Wheeles in a press release. The victim, identified as Deljuan Laron Patton, had a gunshot wound. Patton, 25, died from the injuries he sustained.

Investigators believe the shooting occurred near SE 23rd and SE Madison. It appears Patton was in a vehicle and was hit by a drive-by shooter. The suspect's vehicle is possibly a silver car, Wheeles states.

We'll bring you details on this investigation as they become available.
