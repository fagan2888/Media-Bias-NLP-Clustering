Emmy-winning comedian and satirist John Oliver weighed in on a local Virginia Senate race during his HBO show Sunday night, noting the impact the Dick Black-Jill McCabe contest could have on the future of Medicaid in Virginia.Sen. Black of Virginia's 13th District is one of the most vocal opponents of Medicaid expansion, while his Democratic challenger, McCabe, supports it. The commonwealth is one of 19 states that has neglected to expand Medicaid under the Affordable Care Act.The 13th District contest is seen as one of three swing contests within the Virginia Senate that could shift the chamber from the Republicans to Democrats. Democratic Gov. Terry McAuliffe is hoping a Democratic power shift will help him expand Medicaid, an issue central to his campaign.
