The Securities and Exchange Commission is alleging that a former consultant for the parent company of Panda Express shared nonpublic information in 2011 and 2012 about a potential acquisition of P.F. Chang’s China Bistro Inc.

The SEC’s insider-trading complaint against Richard Condon and Jonathan Ross alleges that the consultant, Mr. Condon, “repeatedly” shared information with Mr. Ross and another man who died in December 2014.
