High School Football: John Stark squeaks by Hollis/Brookline



Jackson Easter's 35-yard field goal with eight minutes remaining in the fourth quarter secured John Stark of Weare a 17-14 road victory over Hollis/Brookline Saturday afternoon.



Easter's field goal gave the Generals (2-3) a 17-7 lead. Following the field goal, Wyatt Farewell ran in a touchdown for Hollis/Brookline (1-4) but the Cavaliers were stifled the remainder of the game by the Generals' defense.

..

Ryan Gordon made a team-high 15 tackles for John Stark while Cody Ledoux and Zachary Hamilton combined for 23 tackles.



Drew McQuarrie and Cooper Gorskie connected for a touchdown and the Generals also recovered an offensive fumble recovery in the end zone for a score. Gorskie finished with eight receptions for 111 yards and teammate Jacob Fitts made seven receptions for 80 yards.

..

McQuarrie suffered a high-ankle sprain in John Stark's loss to Monadnock of Swanzey Sept. 26 but played all but three series in Saturday's victory.



Trinity 29, Plymouth 25: In Manchester, the Pioneers (3-1) scored the game-winning touchdown on a 40-yard reverse option pass from Pat Mackey to Saikou Bah with two seconds remaining in the game.

..

Danny Carey kicked a 31-yard field goal with three minutes left in the fourth quarter to give Plymouth a 25-23 advantage.



Will Sheppard ran in two 4-yard touchdowns and Nick Perdue had a 19-yard touchdown run for Trinity. Bah ran for more than 100 yards in the victory.

..

Garrett Macomber scored two touchdowns and Brandon Roy added another for the Bobcats (2-3).



St. Thomas Aquinas 47, Laconia 6: In Exeter, Stephen Hedberg passed for 225 yards and four touchdowns for St. Thomas Aquinas of Dover (5-0).

..

Teammate Sam Andolina rushed for 137 yards and a touchdown. Nate Grunbeck ran for a 72-yard score on the Saints' first play from scrimmage. On their second play from scrimmage, Hedberg and Tom Bouchard connected for a 48-yard touchdown.

..

Laconia (2-3) scored its lone touchdown on a pass early in the second quarter.



Milford 28, Lebanon 6: In Lebanon, Maxwell Urda threw two touchdowns and ran for another for Milford (4-1). He finished with 153 yards passing. Teammate Bryce Walker rushed for 82 yards and a touchdown.

..

Mark Madigan added 91 total yards and a touchdown for the Spartans. He also recorded three interceptions on defense.



Kirk Palladino caught one of Urda's two touchdown passes. Ryan Milliken ran 75 yards to the end zone early in the second quarter to score the lone touchdown for Lebanon (4-1).

..

The Raiders turned the ball over six times on four interceptions and two fumbles.



Bow 50, Farmington/Nute 0: In Bow, Gavin Lacourciere ran in touchdowns from 1, 2 and 1 yard out for the Falcons (5-0). He finished with nine yards rushing.

..

Teammate Jack Corriveau ran in a 60-yard touchdown in the second quarter and finished with 80 yards on the ground. Ben Kimball also rushed for a touchdown and brother Mac Kimball made four of his five PATs for Bow.

..

Falcons backup quarterback Mark Borak tossed a 15-yard touchdown to tight end Nate Alford. Starting quarterback Matthew Harkins was injured during Bow's victory over Epping/Newmarket on Sept. 26. Farmington/Nute fell to 1-4.

..

..

Aidan Plumpton rushed for a 3-yard touchdown and recorded two interceptions.
