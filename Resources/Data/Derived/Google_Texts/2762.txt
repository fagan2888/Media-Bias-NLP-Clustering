Will Rogers College High School will be opening its doors to the public for a tour of the building Monday evening.

The building features a lot of Art Deco architecture and is one of few schools on the National Register of Historic Places.

Guided tours will begin at 6:30 p.m. in the auditorium, and should take about 90 minutes. Historical almanac-calendars with photographs and facts about Rogers High will be available to buy.
