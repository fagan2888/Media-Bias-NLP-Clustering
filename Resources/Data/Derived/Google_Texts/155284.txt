Colorado Therapeutic Riding Center celebrates 35 years

Owen Ulbrich rides over obstacles with his horse, Choice, during his turn in the outdoor arena at the celebration of the 35th anniversary of the Colorado Therapeutic Riding Center. The center put on a horse show Sunday with all of the riders who currently use its programs, from youths to adults.
