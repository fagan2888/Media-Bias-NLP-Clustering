A fiery accident left a portion of U.S. Highway 400 blocked in Butler County for about three hours on Friday afternoon.

The accident was reported east of Leon at 3:20 p.m.

Butler County officers said two drivers were involved in a head-on collision.

The drivers were able to escape their vehicles with the help of other people. Their vehicles eventually caught on fire.

A 53-year-old woman was rushed to the hospital via an ambulance.

A 19-year-old woman was airlifted to a Wichita hospital.

Officers said both drivers suffered critical injuries but were in stable condition.
