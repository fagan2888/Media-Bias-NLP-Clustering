A man was in critcal condition after being shot multiple times in Pomona Friday afternoon.

The shooting occurred around 4:45 in the 200 block of West Willow Avenue, police said.

Police Investigate Pomona Shooting

Police were investigating a shooting in Pomona that left a homeowner injured. Beverly White reports for the NBC4 News at 11 p.m. Friday, Sept. 26, 2014. (Published Friday, Sept. 26, 2014)

Police believe this was a targeted attack and that the shooting was not random.

According to an NBC4 report, the victim, who is in critical condition, had not been cooperative with police prior to undergoing surgery.

Neighbors told NBC4 that the victim worked as a painter.

Police have not caught the shooter or shooters involved in this attack.
