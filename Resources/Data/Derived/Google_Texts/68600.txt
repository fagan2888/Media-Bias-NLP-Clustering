RED BANK - A mother and daughter were hospitalized Tuesday night after they were hit by a southbound car while crossing Shrewsbury Avenue near Herbert Street.

About 7:45 p.m., police responded to the call, according to Lt. Mike Frazee. The 39-year-old mother suffered back injuries. The 11-year-old daughter suffered a facial injury and complained of pain in her right leg.

Both were taken to Riverview Medical Center for treatment, according to Frazee. Their injuries were not life-threatening.

Frazee said there isn't a crosswalk at that intersection; the mother and daughter were near a corner while crossing.

The driver of the car, Ryan Fields, 41, of Holmdel was issued a summons for careless driving.
