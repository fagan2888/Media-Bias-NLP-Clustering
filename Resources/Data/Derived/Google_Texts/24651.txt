Ramirez, Yoshiko Kinsei

Yoshiko Kinsei Ramirez of Roanoke, Va., passed away on Tuesday, May 24, 2016, after battling valiantly against cancer. She passed peacefully at home surrounded by loved ones.Born in 1964 in Kobe, Japan, she would have been 52, next month on the 29th. She joins her mother, Fusako Kinsei; father, Matsuhiro Kinsei; and sister, Haruko Kinsei, in a more peaceful place.Yoshiko was an eternally optimistic woman who put her love into everything she did and everyone she met.She is survived by her older brother, Hiroaki Kinsei; her husband, Johnny Ramirez; her daughter, Brenna Ramirez; and her son, Glen Ramirez. She forever adored and cared for her precious granddaughter, Penelope Aiko Ramirez, with all her heart and Penelope's mother, Shannen Fitzgerald.Special thanks to her closest friend, confidante and partner-in-crime, Twyla Elliot, for always being there for her.A viewing will be conducted on Friday, May 27, 2016, from 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. and from 6 to 8 p.m. at Oakey's South Chapel. The Funeral Service and Celebration of Life will be held at 10 a.m. on Saturday, May 28, 2016, at Oakey's South Chapel. We invite people to bring fond memories to share with all at the service. Online condolences may be expressed to the family at www.oakeys.com.
