Accident near the Lincoln exit on I-135

A man has been seriously injured in a collision with concrete bridge support.

The Kansas Highway Patrol says it happened around 12:30 a.m. on northbound I-135 near Harry. Troopers say 24-year-old Diamond Wilson was driving northbound in a Buick Rainier when for an unknown reason he lost control and collided with a pedestrian bridge support.

Wilson was taken by EMS to Wesley Medical Center where he remains in critical condition.
