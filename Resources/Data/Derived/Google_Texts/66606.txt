Irene, SD

Two Sioux Falls area men are dead after a crash just west on Irene this afternoon.

30 year old Brad Johnson was driving a 2005 Dodge Pickup with 28 year old passenger Cody Kollars east on Highway 46 when the vehicle struck the guardrail, went into the other lane, then went over a bridge and landed by a creek and rolled a couple times. Both men were dead at the scene.

Yankton Sheriff Department, Yankton Fire and Rescue, Irene Fire and Rescue and the South Dakota Highway Patrol all responded to the crash.

The cause of the crash is still under investigation.
