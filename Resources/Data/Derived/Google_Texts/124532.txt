Firefighters with the Yukon and Oklahoma City Fire Departments are working to put out a large hay bale fire at the Express Family Ranch.

Crews responded to a fire near Cemetery Rd. and Wilshire at the Express Family Ranch.

Firefighters from Yukon and Oklahoma City were called to the scene around 5:00 p.m. Monday.

Fire crews say the fire burned close to 100 bales of hay right in the middle of the ranch.

No word yet on what caused the fire.
