Regarding Laura Reathaford and Daniel L. Saperstein's "San Francisco Handcuffs Employers to Give Ex-Cons a Break" (Cross Country, Aug. 2): Discrimination and mental illness are big reasons for incarcerations in our country. Society must come to terms with people unjustly jailed and how they re-enter the workforce. I believe in the dignity of work and know firsthand that some ex-offenders can be successfully rehabilitated. I also know some can never be helped.
