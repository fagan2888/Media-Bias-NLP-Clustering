One person is dead after a traffic crash in Crestview Saturday morning.

Crestview Police officials are investigating the incident that occurred at the intersection of South Wilson Avenue and Martin Luther King Boulevard.

While investigating the scene, officers learned that two individuals were pulling a cart on Wilson Avenue, when they were both struck by a car, which was also traveling down Wilson Avenue.

One person died on scene, the other was taken to a Pensacola Hospital.

At this time the condition of the person taken to the hospital is unknown, and police have not released the name of the person who died on scene.

The crash is still under investigation, and we will bring you more information when it becomes available.
