MTV celebrated the 25th anniversary of the MTV Movie Awards on Saturday night.

Hosted by Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson and Kevin Hart at Warner Brother Studios in Burbank, California, the awards show named "Star Wars: The Force Awakens" Movie of the Year.

Melissa McCarthy made history, becoming the first woman to receive the Comedic Genius Award. The highest honor at the MTV Movie Awards -- The Generation Award -- was given to Will Smith for his career.

Aside from handing out movie popcorn trophies, artists such as Ariana Grande and Halsey performed.

Presenters also took to the stage, including Charlize Theron, Kendrick Lamar, Chris Hemsworth, Jessica Chastain, Seth Rogen, Zac Efron, Olivia Munn and Gigi Hadid.

Watch all of the fun for yourself when the 2016 MTV Movie Awards airs on the network tonight at 7 p.m. CT.
