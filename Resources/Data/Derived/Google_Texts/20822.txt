Dell Barbee, visiting from North Carolina, looks at the artwork of Mitch Berg of Santa Fe, N.M. at last year's Open Arts Festival in Downtown Boulder. The festival will continue on Sunday from 10 am to 5 pm on the Pearl Street Mall ( Cliff Grassmick / Daily Camera file )

Open Arts Fest, presented by Downtown Boulder, Inc. and OpenArts, is a celebration of Boulder's visual arts unlike any other. The Pearl Street Mall will host over 100 artist booths featuring stunning collections of original artwork in a variety of mediums This fine arts festival highlights 56 Colorado-based artists in addition to artists from 20 other states. Works range from jewelry to handcrafted wood & furniture, photography, sculpture and more, 10 a.m. - 7 p.m., Pearl St. Pub and Cellar, 1108 Pearl St., Boulder, free; 303-939-9900.

The Amazing Acro-Cats Boogie in Boulder One of only four "performing domestic cat troupes" in the United States, The Amazing Acro-Cats perform nationwide to sell out venues. The Amazing Acro-Cats delight and astound audiences of all ages as they perform their remarkable feats of agility, pushing carts, riding skateboards, rolling barrels, ringing bells, turning on lights, walking tight ropes, jumping through hoops, and much more, 5 p.m., Nomad Theatre, 1410 Quince Ave., Boulder, $15 - $18; 303-443-7510.
