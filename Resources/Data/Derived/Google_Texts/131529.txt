During Monday’s White House briefing, press secretary Josh Earnest dropped a truth bomb about the Affordable Care Act.

Despite what you might have heard, the health insurance market has improved since Obamacare’s implementation. (RELATED: Majority Of Americans Want To Replace Obamacare With Single-Payer System)

White House: Insurance Market Is Doing 'A Lot Better' Than Before Obamacare (YouTube)

“This is an argument we’ve been making for six years,” Earnest explained. “The health insurance market is a lot better for private citizens now than it was before the Affordable Care Act.”

“That’s just a fact,” he added. “And I know that’s a fact that Republicans like to deny, but, um, it’s the truth.”
