The U.S. Chamber of Commerce Foundation invites you to participate in its 2015 Health & Wellness Forum. This year’s forum theme is Better Health through Economic Opportunity: Engaging Business to Strengthen Community Wellness. This half-day event will explore how to empower health and wellness in communities by leveraging the influence of local and national businesses. Additionally, panels will explore the links between economic growth and community health, as well as how businesses can drive a greater culture of health in the United States. This year’s speakers include:

Hugh Welsh, General Counsel & President, DSM North America

Barbara Bush, CEO & Co-founder, Global Health Corps

Hank Cardello, Director, Obesity Solutions Initiative, Hudson Institute
