Remote patient monitoring devices monitor patient from long distance. Remote patient monitoring device are used for ambulatory healthcare that allows the patient to use an internet capable device to perform a routine test and send the test data to healthcare professionals in real time. Remote patient monitoring devices are used mainly for elderly and chronically ill patients. Remote patient monitoring devices are used for patients with chronic illness to monitor vital signs such as blood pressure, heart rate, ECG, blood glucose, weight and oxygen content.
