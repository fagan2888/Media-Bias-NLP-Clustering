Tesla Motors Inc. told investors and analysts in a note Tuesday it would announce a grid-scale battery pack as well as a home battery in its announcement scheduled for April 30.

Tesla’s Chief Executive Officer Elon Musk had previously announced through Twitter the company would announce a new product line—not a vehicle—on April 30.
