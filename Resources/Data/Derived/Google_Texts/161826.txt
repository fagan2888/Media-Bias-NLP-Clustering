Anthony and Nancy Smith of Brookfield have announced the engagement of their son, Jason Tyler Smith, to Sarah Marie Long, daughter of Robert and Jeanne Long of Mt. Pleasant, Mich.

The future bridegroom is the grandson of Robert and Gladys Benn of Ridgefield and Donald K. Smith of Seascale, England.

Mr. Smith, of New York City, N.Y. is a graduate of Brookfield High School. He graduated from The Ohio State University in 2008 and is employed by GE Capital in Norwalk.

Ms. Long, of New York City, is a graduate of Mt. Pleasant High School. She graduated from Michigan State University in 2007 and is employed by Pepsico.

A June 21 wedding is planned in Bay Harbor, Mich.

The couple will live in New York City.
