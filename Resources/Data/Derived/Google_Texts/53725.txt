Regarding Dr. David Agus’s “Saving Corporate Cash by Hiring a Chief Health Officer” (op-ed, Jan. 8): Such a physician could help to achieve improved productivity. Recognizing that many existing wellness programs based on screening blood tests are repetitive, obtrusive, disliked and of little benefit to anyone, I suggest a focus on the issues of absenteeism, presenteeism and long-term disability.
