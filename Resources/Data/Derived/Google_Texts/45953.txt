DAVISON TWP, MI -- A variety of full- and part-time job positions are up for grabs during a hiring fair at Menards in Davison Township this week.

The hiring fair is set from 8 a.m.-3 pm. Monday, May 2 at the Davison Township Menards, 7410 E. Court St., off Irish Road. Applicants should bring a resume to the event.

On-the-spot interviews and some hiring is expected to take place at the event. Available positions include cashiers, manager trainees, lumberyard, stocking, as well as sales and receiving.

Anyone with questions about the hiring event may contact the Davison Township Menards locations at 810-653-8618.
