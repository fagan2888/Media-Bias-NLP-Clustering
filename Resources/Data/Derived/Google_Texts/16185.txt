A 31-year-old Salina man is in jail, accused of threatening a woman with a knife and strangling her almost to the point of unconsciousness.

Salina Police Captain Mike Sweeney told the Salina Post that Clinton Cheney allegedly attacked the woman and make threats at a south Salina home on Thursday. He also reportedly did not allow her to leave the home.

Cheney is an acquaintance of the woman, who is in her 30s. He left the home after the woman was able to call police. He was arrested in Lincoln County Thursday evening, the Post reports.

Cheney was booked into the Salina County jail for aggravated assault, aggravated burglary, domestic battery, aggravated kidnapping and criminal threat.
