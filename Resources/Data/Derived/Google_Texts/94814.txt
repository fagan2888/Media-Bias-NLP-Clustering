Oil futures pared losses after data from Baker Hughes BHI, +3.62% released Friday showed that the number of active oil-drilling rigs fell 13 to 662 as of Sept. 4. The total active rig count, which includes natural-gas rigs, was at 864, also down 13 rigs. Compared to last year, the total rig count has fallen by 1,061, with the oil rig count down 922. Oct. crude clv5 fell 27 cents, or 0.6%, to $46.48 a barrel on the New York Mercantile Exchange. It was trading at $46.02 just before the data.
