BERLIN—Prosecutors in Germany have charged a 93-year-old man with at least 300,000 counts of accessory to murder while a member of Adolf Hitler's SS unit at the Auschwitz concentration camp.

Prosecutors accuse Oskar Gröning from Lower Saxony of having collected money from Nazi victims' luggage on their arrival at the concentration camp, which he turned over to the SS headquarters in Berlin.
