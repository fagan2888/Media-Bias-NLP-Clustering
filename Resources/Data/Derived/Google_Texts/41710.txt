Some have called 2013 a “solar renaissance,” but not all industry giants have recovered in the same way.

From SunPower’s vertically integrated business model to SolarCity’s broad domestic penetration, the movers and shakers of the sector are re-establishing their presence in an industry that’s seen some turmoil in recent years. With solid fourth quarters behind them, the future is bright, and possibly tumultuous: international market penetration, government subsidies and a highly competitive marketplace are all par for the course for both SunPower and SolarCity.
