JACKSONVILLE, Fla - The Jaguars preseason schedule is out with home games on Friday and Saturday nights and three of the four games will be shown on WJXT, Channel 4, the official station of the Jaguars.

The Jags will host the Pittsburgh Steelers on Friday, August 14 at 7:30 p.m. (WJXT-TV)

On August 22, the Jags will travel to the Meadowlands to play the N.Y. Giants at 7:30 p.m. (WJXT-TV)

The Jaguars will return to EverBank Field to host the Detroit Lions on August 28th at 8 p.m. That game will be nationally televised on CBS.

The the Jaguars wrap up the preseason in Washington on September 3 at 7:30 p.m. (WJXT-TV)

The 2015 regular season schedule is due out Tuesday evening.
