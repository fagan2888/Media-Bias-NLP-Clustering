McDermott Joins BigLaw Trend Of Secretary Cuts

Law360, New York (June 3, 2014, 7:23 PM ET) -- McDermott Will & Emery LLP has laid off 25 legal secretaries, the firm said Tuesday, following dozens of others in BigLaw who have pointed to the need to lower staff overhead amid changing work habits and technology investments.



“It is clear that the role of legal secretary has changed significantly in recent years as technology has allowed lawyers to work with less need for administrative support,” McDermott Chief Operating Officer Bob Koenen said in a statement.
