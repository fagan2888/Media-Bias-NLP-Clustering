Jacksonville Police say shots were fired this morning inside a residence.

Public Safety Director Michael Yaniero says his department got a call around 11:05 a.m. that a person had a weapon at 101 King Richard Court in the Sherwood Forest subdivision.

Officers say that person fired a weapon inside the residence.

Police no one was hurt and officers were able to take the subject into custody without incident.

The person in custody was taken to a medical facility for evaluation.

Yaniero says the investigation in this case will continue.
