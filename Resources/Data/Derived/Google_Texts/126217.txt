Sioux Falls, SD

A 25-year-old man has been charged in a fatal motorcycle crash. It happened just after 4 p.m. Sunday near 41st St. and Westbrooke Lane.

Police say Trevor W. Blankartz of Sioux Falls was driving a 1991 Buick Regal that attempted to pull out onto 41st from Westbrooke Lane when he collided with a westbound 2012 Harley Davidson Motorcycle.

The motorcycle was driven by a 56-year-old Sioux Falls resident. He was pronounced dead at the scene. He was not wearing a helmet. His name has not yet been released.

Blankartz faces vehicular homicide, DWI, and careless driving charges. The investigation will continue and more details will be available at a later time.
