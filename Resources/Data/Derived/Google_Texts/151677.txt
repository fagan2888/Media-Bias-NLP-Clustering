UPDATE 9/13/15 5:52 P.M.

Around 12:15 pm Sunday, Panama City Beach Police arrested Barrett Ryan Beasley at his home. Police have been searching for Beasley since Friday night in regards to a shooting on El Centro Boulevard in Panama City Beach.

Beasley has been charged with, attempted murder with a firearm, tampering with evidence, theft of a firearm and two counts of violation of probation. The victim who was shot, Michael Taylor, is still in critical condition.

Police have not released what the shooting was about. Beasley was booked into the Bay County Jail Sunday afternoon.

UPDATE 6:48 P.M.

An arrest warrant has been signed charging Barrett Beasley with attempted murder of Michael Taylor.

The Panama City Beach Police Department is investigating the shooting of Michael Clay Taylor, 26, of Panama City Beach.

Around 6 p.m. Friday, police responded to a home on Centro Boulevard where they located Taylor shot in the residence.

Taylor was transported to Bay Medical Center where he is listed critical condition.

After investigating, police say Barrett Ryan Beasley, 33, is a person of interest.

The Panama City Beach Police Department is requesting anyone with information regarding this investigation or the location of Beasley to please contact the Police Department at (850) 233-5000.
