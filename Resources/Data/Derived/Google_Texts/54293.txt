Texas doctors wary of Aetna-Humana merger

Photo: Jessica Hill, FRE FILE - In this Tuesday, Aug. 19, 2014 file photo, pedestrian walks...

The Texas Medical Association has asked the U.S. Department of Justice to further investigate the proposed merger of insurance giants Aetna and Humana, complaining such an alliance would be damaging to both patients and physicians in the state by further reducing competition.
