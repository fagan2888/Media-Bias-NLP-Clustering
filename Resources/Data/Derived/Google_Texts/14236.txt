A west Wichita resident received a rude awakening early Saturday morning when a drunk driver lost control of his vehicle, crashing into their house.

Wichita Police say it happened at about 2:20 a.m. in the 900 block of N Redbarn Ln. A 27-year-old man was arrested for DUI after running his Jeep into the home.

No injuries were reported. No damage estimates were given.
