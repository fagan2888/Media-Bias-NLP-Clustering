A man is in the hospital Monday night after a traffic crash in northern Bay County.

It happened about 2:30 Monday afternoon, on Highway 231, just south of highway.

State troopers say the driver of a white SUV was heading north on 231, when he apparently lost control of the vehicle. It ran off the shoulder of the road, began flipping, and wound-up on the other side of the Bay line railroad tracks.

Authorities say he was throw from the vehicle, landing near the side of the highway. Paramedics took him to Bay Medical-Sacred Heart.

The have not release his name of his condition.
