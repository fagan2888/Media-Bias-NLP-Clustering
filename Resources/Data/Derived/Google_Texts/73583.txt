President Bill Clinton will travel to Oklahoma on Sunday, February 21, and will hold a Get Out the Vote event in Oklahoma City to encourage supporters to vote for Hillary Clinton in the March 1 primary.

According to Hillary Clinton's campaign office, the event will take place at 8 p.m. The location has not been determined. President Clinton will lay out what is at stake in this election and discuss why he thinks Hillary Clinton is the best candidate to take on the tough fights on behalf of American families and to create shared prosperity for all.

Additional details about this event will be released in the coming days. The event will be free and open to the public.

News 9 and News9.com will keep you updated as more information becomes available.
