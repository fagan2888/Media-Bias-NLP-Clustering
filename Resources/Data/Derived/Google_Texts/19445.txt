You couldn't miss the theme of Wild West World. The Park City amusement park hoped to turn Kansas history into cash.

The amusement park promised two roller coasters and a log ride. A nearby business park had plans for a restaurant and hotel. Wild West World was expected to bring 100 million dollars a year in business to Sedgwick County.

Wild West World opened in 2007. It closed two months later, blaming wet weather for low attendance. Owner Thomas Etheredge would be found guilty of securities fraud.
