On Wednesday, May 27th, drivers will see some changes on State Road 388 in Bay County.

The Florida Department of Transportation will increase the speed limit on State Road 388 from S.R. 79 to S.R. 77 in Bay County.

Crews will be putting up new speed limit signs in the coming days.

The speed will increase from 45 MPH to 50 MPH, and then from 55 MPH to 60 MPH.

If you have any questions, you are urged to call the Florida Department of Transportation department.
