New Mexico Department of Transportation crews will close the eastbound I-40 on-ramp at Louisiana beginning at midnight Monday.

The on-ramp is expected to be closed for approximately 10 days.

There will also be partial lane closures on northbound Louisiana starting Monday.

From March 29-April 17, the eastbound I-40 off-ramp will be closed to traffic.
