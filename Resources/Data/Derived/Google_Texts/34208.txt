Two people were left injured in a roadway after a driver hit them and kept going.

State Patrol has identified the two victims as 50-year-old Amy Paschal and 55-year-old Michael Paschal. Both were taken to Penrose Hospital; their condition Saturday is unknown.

The crash happened around 6:40 p.m. on at the intersection of Jackson Creek Parkway and Lyons Tail Road in Monument. Troopers say the victims were crossing the street in a crosswalk when they were struck.

Troopers do not have a suspect vehicle description, and are calling on the public to help them locate it. If you know anything about the crash you are asked to call 719-544-2424.
