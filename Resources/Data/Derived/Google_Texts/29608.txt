PATNA: AAP leader Parveen Amanullah and six party workers were arrested today for allegedly violating the poll code of conduct, police said.

Amanullah, who quit the Nitish Kumar government to join AAP recently, is tipped to contest from Patliputra Lok Sabha seat presently represented by Lalu Prasad's friend-turned-foe and JD(U) leader Ranjan Yadav.
