Print Email Font Resize Font Resize BVSD to recognize outgoing board members, elect officers on Tuesday

A reception to recognize the service of outgoing Boulder Valley school board members Laurie Albright and Jim Reed is set from 5 to 6 p.m. Tuesday.



Following the reception, there's a special meeting to swear in the new board members and elect board officers. Incumbents Sam Fuqua and Shelly Benford were re-elected, while Richard Garcia and Kathy Gebhardt are joining the board.



The reception and meeting are at the Education Center, 6500 E. Arapahoe Road, Boulder.
