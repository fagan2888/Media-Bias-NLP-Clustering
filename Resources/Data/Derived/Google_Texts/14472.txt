A man suffered critical injuries Saturday evening after being hit by a pickup in south Wichita.

It happened around 7:45 p.m. near Seneca and Jewell. That's just north of Pawnee. Witnesses told police a pickup truck was traveling northbound on Seneca in the curb lane when the man walked in front of the truck and was hit.

The 46-year-old man with critical injuries was rushed to Via Christi St. Francis hospital.

Police said detectives have talked to the driver of the pickup and are still gathering information on the accident.

KAKE News reached out to the hospital for an update on the man's condition. None was available as of 11 p.m. Saturday.
