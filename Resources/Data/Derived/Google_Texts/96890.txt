Police are searching for the suspect in an armed robbery at a Wichita Dollar General store.

It happened around 10 a.m. Sunday at the Dollar General at 1919 East Pawnee, just west of I-135. Witnesses told police they saw a man walk up to the building and pull a mask up over his face.

That's when the suspect entered the store and demanded money from the clerk. the alleged robber was able to get away with an unknown amount of cash.

Police said the suspect had a weapon, but no one was hurt.

Police are interviewing witnesses and reviewing surveillance video from the store. They did not provided a description of the suspect.
