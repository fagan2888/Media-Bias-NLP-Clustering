One woman is dead and a man is critically injured after a crash in Bethany, police said.

The crash happened about 2 p.m. Saturday, a sport utility vehicle was traveling westbound on NW 39th Expressway and departed the roadway. The vehicle hit a tree and ended up in a field near NW 39th Expressway and Council Road, police said.

A woman was found dead inside the vehicle and the man was taken to OU Medical Center.

Police did not release any details of what led up to the crash.
