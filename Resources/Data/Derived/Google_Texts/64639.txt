Ben Cohen, co-founder of Ben & Jerry's ice cream is a big Bernie Sanders booster, even going so far as to create "Bernie's Yearning," a special edition ice cream to raise funds for Sanders' campaign.

Reason TV caught up with Cohen at the McIntyre-Shaheen 100 Club Celebration in Manchester, NH, where he told us if Sanders falls short of winning the Democratic nomination for president, he won't vote for Hillary because she's "part of the system that has created this government run by the corporations and the ultra-wealthy."
