March 5 -- 87 calls for service

A resident in the 500 block of Rudd reported theft of a firearm. Information was taken for a report.

A female party in the 800 block of South Fifth reported that a male party was making threats towards her. James Aguilar, 55, was issued a summons for harassment.

Officers responded to the 700 block of Oak Avenue for a report of a 1-year-old female child not breathing and unresponsive. The child was transported to St. Thomas More Hospital where she was pronounced deceased. The investigation is ongoing at this time.

A female party reported a traffic accident in the 3200 block of U.S. 50.

Two juveniles ran away from the 700 block of Four Mile Parkway. Officers took information for a report.

An employee in the 3100 block of U.S. 50 reported theft of a sleeping bag. Information was taken for a report.

A resident in the 100 block of Greenwood reported that their wallet had been lost or stolen. An officer gathered information for a report.

A two-vehicle traffic accident occurred in the area of Ninth and Main Street. One of the drivers involved in the accident fled the scene on foot prior to officer arrival. Officers gathered information for a report. The investigation is under way.
