For the third time in recent weeks, a Wichitan has been abducted and forced to withdraw money from an ATM.

A woman told police two men approached her in an apartment complex parking lot at 1400 N. St. Paul around Midnight. One of the men had a gun and told the woman to drive to an ATM and withdraw money.

The woman wasn't hurt. She described the suspects as black men, both in their mid twenties and 5'8" tall. One wore a white hoodie, a black bandana and had dreadlocks. The second man wore a black hoodie.

Police don't know if the cases are related. If you have information call Crime Stoppers at (316) 267-2111.
