To assist with parking needs for the Church Service at Hesston High School Sunday evening, school district buses will shuttle members of the community from the Hesston golf course parking area as well as from the King Park/Methodist Church parking areas.

The service is set to begin at 6 p.m.

Also, a reminder to Excel employees who were directly affected that they are strongly encouraged to attend a trauma group meeting hosted by EMPAC. Sessions are planned 10 a.m. and 1 p.m. Feb. 29 at Whitestone Mennonite Church, 629 Crescent Dr., Hesston.

Sessions will not be held at Prairie View.
