PT Astra Sedaya Finance, a unit of PT Astra International Tbk , issued bonds worth S$100 million ($76 million) with three-year tenure, Investor Daily reported, quoting a statement by Credit Guarantee and Investment Facility (CGIF).

The Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation acts as lead manager.
