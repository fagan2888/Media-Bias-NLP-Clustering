Baltic, SD

South Dakota Highway Patrol has released the name of the man who died in a single-vehicle crash west of Baltic Friday afternoon.

Eighty-nine-year-old John Jensen of Dell Rapids was driving on 250th Street near Interstate-29 around 4:40 p.m. when the vehicle left the roadway and hit the bridge pillar that supports the southbound lane of I-29.

Jensen was pronounced dead at the scene. Authorities are still investigating the accident.
