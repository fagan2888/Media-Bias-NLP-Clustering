Authorities have arrested a man in Castle Rock who is believed to have robbed two banks Saturday morning in the Denver metro area.

The suspect was arrested following robberies at the Chase Bank at 13620 Orchard Parkway in Westminster and at First Bank, 8600 E. Arapahoe Road in Arapahoe County, the FBI announced Saturday night. He is being held on unrelated state charges.

The Westminster Police Department, Castle Rock Police Department, Arapahoe County Sheriff’s Office, Douglas County Sheriff’s Office and the FBI Rocky Mountain Safe Streets Task Force investigated the robberies.

Bank robbery is punishable by a 20-year prison sentence for each offense.
