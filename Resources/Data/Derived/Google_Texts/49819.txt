FCA Suit Against For-Profit Educator Survives With Cuts

Law360, New York (May 11, 2016, 8:14 PM ET) -- A New Jersey federal judge on Wednesday allowed whistleblowers to proceed with portions of a False Claims Act suit accusing Premier Education Group, the operator of several for-profit colleges, of submitting fraudulent claims to receive money for federal student aid programs, but trimmed several claims.
