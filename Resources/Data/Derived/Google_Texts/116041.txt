A single vehicle car crash in Trego County on I-70 has killed one person and injured two others.

The crash occurred at 2:19 p.m. near the town of Ellis.

Kansas Highway Patrol Trooper Tod Hileman says one person was killed and two others injured.

One of the injured was taken to Hays, the other was taken by helicopter to a Wichita hospital.

The cause of the accident has not been released.
