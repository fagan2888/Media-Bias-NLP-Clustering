Meridian police have someone in custody Saturday after they say he robbed the business he worked at.

Police got the call about 12:45 a.m. from the Sonic on North Hills Street. Detective Dareall Thompson says thanks to quick work from officers, minutes later, they were able to take Dominic Tuck, a Sonic employee, into custody. Tuck was charged with armed robbery. His bond was set at $25,000. No injuries were reported during the crime. Police were able to recover all that was taken and return it to Sonic.
