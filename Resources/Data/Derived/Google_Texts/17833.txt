The mother of a missing Madison County child has been arrested on an unrelated matter.

WLBT-TV reports Ericka Lewis was arrested on Friday, relating to a felony charge for carrying a concealed weapon. The Madison County Sheriff's Department says Lewis was on probation for the charge.

Lewis' 2-year old daughter Myra has been missing since last weekend. A news conference on the search for Myra is set for Sunday.
