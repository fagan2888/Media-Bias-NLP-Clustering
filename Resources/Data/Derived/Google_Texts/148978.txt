10 years after Katrina, New Orleans is vibrant but wary

Saturday was a day to remember what “all the other people” went through during one of the country's deadliest and most costly natural disasters. Those who were lifted from rooftops by helicopters, those who came home to find only concrete steps as evidence of where their house used to be, those whose bodies were never claimed after the storm.
