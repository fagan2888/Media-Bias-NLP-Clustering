A man was struck by a car and killed on Back Beach Road at Argonaut Street Saturday morning.

According to Panama City Beach Police, when they arrived on scene, they saw a Hispanic male lying on the North shoulder of the roadway, partially in the right hand turn lane going into Nautilus Station.

Officials say the pedestrian walked into the path of a silver Hyundai that was westbound on Back Beach Road, and was being driven by Andrew McElvie of Troy, Alabama.

The pedestrian, whose names has not been released at this time, died of his injuries at Bay Medical Center.

The incident is still under investigation.
