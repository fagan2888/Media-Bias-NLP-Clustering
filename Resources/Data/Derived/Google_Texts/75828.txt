What the 1998 study found:

• 63 percent of the people who participated in the study had experienced at least one category of adverse event.

• More than 20 percent experienced three or more categories of trauma.

• 11 percent experienced emotional abuse.

• 28 percent experienced physical abuse.

• 21 percent experienced sexual abuse.

• 15 percent experienced emotional neglect.

• 10 percent experienced physical neglect.

• 13 percent witnessed their mothers being treated violently.

• 27 percent grew up with someone in the household using alcohol and/or drugs.

• 19 percent grew up with a mentally-ill person in the household.

• 23 percent lost a parent due to separation or divorce.

• 5 percent grew up with a household member in jail or prison.
