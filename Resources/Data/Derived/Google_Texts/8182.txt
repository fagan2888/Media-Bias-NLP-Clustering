Lest you were under the impression the 2014 midterms concerned issues or policies or anything, Morning Joe cohost Mika Brzezinski reduced the voting public’s gauzy discontent to a single sound-of-one-hand-clapping petition:

“But people want — I don’t even know if they care what gets done, they want something done competently.”

DO SOMETHING WHO CARES WHAT was the bipartisan rallying cry of this election. Here’s hoping it isn’t for the next.
