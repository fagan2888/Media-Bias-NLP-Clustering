Compliance with evolving government regulations is a priority for your small and midsized business clients (SMBs) who are, by and large, expected to remain agile and thrive while abiding by many of the same rules that govern much larger enterprises. But keeping up with the barrage of these regulations can be a challenge.

According to the latest ADP Midsized Business Owners Study, the mounting level of government regulations is the number one business concern of midsized employers.
