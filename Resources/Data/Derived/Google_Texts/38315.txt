He smiled constantly, but not the ecstatic smile of a 21-year-old who had just done something few 21-year-olds have ever done: battle the Boston Red Sox at Yankee Stadium. Instead, it was the easy grin of a man who knew he belonged, and never doubted that he would reach this level.
