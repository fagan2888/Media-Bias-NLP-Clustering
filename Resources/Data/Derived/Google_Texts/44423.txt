According to the report, this district manager is now been sacked after he got caught peeing in the sink of kitchen on CCTV camera of Pizza Hut.

The Italian food chain are apparently “embarrassed” by the actions of the employee, and reiterated their “zero tolerance” approach to such matters in a statement.
