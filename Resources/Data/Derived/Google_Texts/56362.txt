How To Woo Native American In-House Counsel

Law360, New York (April 21, 2015, 2:52 PM ET) -- Law firms that are new to working with Native American tribes or looking to expand their business must be willing to learn the unique nature of tribes’ legal needs, tribal in-house counsel say.



Establishing a successful practice working with a tribe means resisting the urge to hurry solutions in favor of gradually coming to a know a particular tribe’s structure, values, goals and preferred ways to approach legal problems.
