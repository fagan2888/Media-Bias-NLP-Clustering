Transgender Medicaid Recipients Hit NYS With Class Action

Law360, New York (June 20, 2014, 5:29 PM ET) -- The New York State Department of Health was hit with a proposed class action on Thursday in New York federal court that alleges a state Medicaid regulation wrongly denies necessary medical treatment to transgender people.



Angie Cruz and another Medicaid recipient claim the department created a regulation in 1998 that denied the transgender women health care coverage for a medical condition known as gender dysphoria, or gender identity disorder, discriminating against them in violation of the Medicaid Act and the Affordable Care Act.
