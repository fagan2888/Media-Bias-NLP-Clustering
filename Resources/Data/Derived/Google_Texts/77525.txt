× Man arrested for home invasion in Franklin County

(KTVI)– Jefferson County deputies arrested a man accused of a home invasion earlier this week in Franklin County.

Michael Murphy faces assault charges after allegedly breaking into a home on Virginia Mines Road on Tuesday.

The victim told police Murphy had a handgun and demanded money.

He also allegedly pulled the trigger twice, but the gun didn’t fire.

Jefferson County sheriff deputies found him after getting calls about a suspicious person Friday afternoon.
