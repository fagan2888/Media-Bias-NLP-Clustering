Sioux Falls, SD

A mom accused of dealing drugs from her home appeared in court Wednesday.

Jennifer Darger pleaded guilty for possessing and using methamphetamine at her in-home daycare in southwest Sioux Falls.

Previous Story: Investigation Underway At Sioux Falls Day Care

In March, the Sioux Falls Drug Task Force received a tip from Darger's 17-year-old daughter about what was happening inside the home.

Darger had previously pleaded not guilty. She faces at least one year in prison when sentenced in October.
