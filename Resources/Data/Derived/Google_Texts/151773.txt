Birth Control Mandate Unlawful, Texas Again Tells High Court

Law360, Los Angeles (January 12, 2016, 8:55 PM ET) -- Attorneys general for Texas and 19 other states on Monday again urged the U.S. Supreme Court to nix the Affordable Care Act’s contraception mandate, arguing in a consolidated appeal that it makes certain religious nonprofits complicit in providing birth control.



The states claimed in an amicus brief that the mandate violates the Religious Freedom Restoration Act and creates a double standard by allowing only some religious organizations, such as churches, to get an exemption from the Affordable Care Act’s contraception mandate without providing paperwork.
