Humana is offering free help to Palm Beach County residents looking to sign up for health insurance before the Feb. 15 deadline to purchase a health plan or risk being charged a federal tax penalty.

Humana agents will help sign up applicants and find out whether they qualify for financial assistance. Applicants are asked to come to Humana's office near Macy's at the Boynton Beach Mall from 5 to 9 p.m. Feb. 13 and from noon to 5 p.m. Feb. 14.

The Boynton Beach Mall is at 801 N. Congress Ave., Boynton Beach. For more information, go to humana.com.
