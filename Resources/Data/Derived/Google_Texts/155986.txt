A woman has died after trying to cross I-135 in south Wichita.

The accident happened just before 10 p.m. Saturday in the northbound lanes of the interstate near Southeast Boulevard. The Kansas Highway Patrol said the woman was standing in the left lane when she was hit by a pick-up.

The woman was pronounced dead at the scene. Her identity was not released, but Sedgwick County dispatchers said Saturday night she was 50 to 60 years old.

The driver of the pickup was not hurt.
