Denver police are investigating a two-car accident Saturday morning that happened near Denver International Airport in which one person was killed and several people were taken to hospitals.

The name of the victim, who was pronounced dead at the scene, has not been released.

The injured had potentially serious injuries, according to a Denver police Tweet around 7 a.m.

Westbound 40th Avenue near Airport Road has been closed and traffic was diverted onto eastbound 40th Avenue.
