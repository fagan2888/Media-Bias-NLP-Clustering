Midwest City police are investigating after a bicyclist was hit by a vehicle Monday morning.

According to police, it happened in the 8300 block of S.E. 29th. The female victim was riding her bicycle to work when she was struck by a car.

The vehicle involved in the crash then left the scene. The bicyclist was transported to the hospital in an unknown condition.

It is unclear at this time if police found the vehicle involved. Stay with us for updates.
