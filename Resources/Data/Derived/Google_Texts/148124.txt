A Missouri man has died following a crash Saturday evening in northwest Kansas.

The crash happened around 9:20 p.m. on westbound K-9 about a mile west of Lenora. That's in Norton County. The Kansas Highway Patrol said a 2005 Ford Crown Victoria left the roadway and rolled several times in a field.

James McKinley, 74, of Reeds Spring, Missouri, died at the scene.

The crash remains under investigation.
