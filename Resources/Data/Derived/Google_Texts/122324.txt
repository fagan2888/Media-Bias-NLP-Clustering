6th District Job Fair Returns To Lawrenceburg Aug. 7

By Kelcey Mucker

(Lawrenceburg, Ind.) – The 2nd Annual 6th Congressional District Job Fair and Employer Seminar with WorkOne Southeast, Ivy Tech Community College, and the Dearborn County Commissioners will be held at Ivy Tech’s Riverfront Campus located at 50 Walnut Street, in Lawrenceburg on Thursday, August 7.

This event will include an optional Employer Seminar with catered lunch including speakers from Old National and the Department of Workforce Development. They will provide information on the Affordable Care Act and also WorkOne services.

Messer expects hundreds of job seekers to participate in the event. The first hours has been set aside for veterans of the U.S. armed forces to have one-on-one interaction with employers.

8:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. Booth set-up

10:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. Employer seminar with catered lunch

1:00 p.m. Job Fair kick-off

1:00 p.m. – 2:00 p.m. Veterans only

2:00 p.m.- 4:30 p.m. Open to public

There are no fees for participation or admission to the job fair. If you have any questions, please contact Tim Hawkins at (317) 421-0704 or email [email protected].
