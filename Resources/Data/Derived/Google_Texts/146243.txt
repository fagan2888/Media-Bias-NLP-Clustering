Dallas

Over 600 miles east in Dallas, the scene is far from the beautiful snowfall Texans are experiencing to the West. Rowlett suffered major damage from storms that spawned tornadoes across the region. More photos and video of the damage have come to light today as the sun revealed the destruction tornadoes left in their wake Saturday evening.
