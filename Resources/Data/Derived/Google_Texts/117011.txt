A man wanted by police following a standoff last month in Hutchinson is now in custody.

The Hutch Post reports 34-year-old Jeremy Frischenmeyer was arrested Friday in the 500 block of East Avenue C.

The standoff took place on the evening of Tuesday, March 15 near Sherman and Pershing. A woman had called police saying a man wanted on felony warrants was inside his mother's home by himself. Police believe he slipped out of a window during the hours-long ordeal.

Frischenmeyer was wanted on a warrant out of Reno County for violating his terms of release stemming from felony drug charges, The Post reports. He was also arrested for a warrants through Hutchinson Municipal Court as well as Saline and Gray counties.

His bond was set at $25,000.
