Arlington police are investigating the death of a woman whose body was found near a creek Saturday night.

Police said they believe the remains found may be those of a missing woman who was reported missing by her family earlier in the evening.

Police responded to a report of a missing 83-year-old woman around 5 p.m. in the 2000 block of Mayfield Villa Drive, near East Mayfield Road and State Highway 360.

Family members told police that the woman went for a walk around 9 a.m. and had not been seen since.

Around 7:45 p.m. police received a call about a body that had been found in a creek area near Mayfield Road and Sherry Street.
