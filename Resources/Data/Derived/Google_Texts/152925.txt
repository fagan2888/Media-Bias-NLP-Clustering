GENEVA â€”The Geneva School District 304 Board approved a new two-year working agreement April 25 with Geneva Educational Support Personnel groups, officials announced in a press release.

Those included in the contract are office administrative assistants, reading tutors, technology assistants, technology support, transportation support, hallway and study hall supervisors, and registered nurses, the release stated. The contract covers the 2016-17 and 2017-18 school years.

The working agreement covers approximately 110 employees who will get 3.5 percent pay increases in each year, costing the district an additional $200,000, district spokeswoman Kristy Poteete-Kriegermeier said.

Language changes to the working agreement are related to the categories of educational support service employees, as well as personal leave requests, the release stated.

Additionally, the structure for sick leave allotment was modified, and the health reimbursement arrangement for employees who do not take district insurance was revised to stay compliant with the Affordable Care Act, the release stated.
