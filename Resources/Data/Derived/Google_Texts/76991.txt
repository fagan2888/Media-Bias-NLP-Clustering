U.S. Senate candidates Bruce Braley and Joni Ernst took part in their first debate at Simpson College Sunday night.

Click here to watch it online

Moderators Kevin Cooney and Kathie Obradovich asked hot-topic questions about issues related to attack-ad claims, Social Security, minimum wage and foreign threats to the U.S.

A recent Iowa poll found Ernst leading Braley 44 percent to 38 percent.
