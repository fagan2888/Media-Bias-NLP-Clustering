An escapee was captured Friday by Jefferson County Sheriff’s deputies in Mount Olivet Cemetery near West 44th Avenue and Youngfield Street.

Earlier, officers had been asked to be on the lookout for an escaped prisoner in Wheat Ridge.

Dispatchers tell 7NEWS the inmate was being transported from Las Vegas to Illinois when he escaped from a truck stop in Wheat Ridge.

The inmate was tracked by a K-9, but the man’s scent disappeared.

Police have not released a description of the suspect, but the BOLO alert said the man’s name is Turhan Robinson. The BOLO said Robinson is a black man with corn rows in his hair and that he was handcuffed in the front and had shackle on his left leg.
