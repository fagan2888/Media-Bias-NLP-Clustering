A man was punched in the face with brass knuckles at a party in east Wichita.

It happened at 12 a.m. Saturday morning in the 1000 block of N. Williamsburg.

The 22-year-old victim was at a party hosted by the two 20-year-old suspects when the victim was accused of stealing phones. He was then punched several times in the face with brass knuckles and taken to St. Francis for a medical evaluation.

No arrests were made.
