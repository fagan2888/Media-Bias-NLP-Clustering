A Panama City Beach woman is dead and a man is charged with vehicular homicide after a car accident Saturday afternoon in Panama City Beach.

Florida Highway Patrol officials arrived on scene on Beach Drive and Upas Street a little after 4 p.m.

According to FHP officials, 23-year-old Adam Bailey was driving westbound on Beach Dr., approaching the intersection with Upas St.

At that time, the driver and passenger in the second vehicle were traveling northbound on Upas St., approaching that same intersection.

Bailey failed to stop at the stop sign and hit the other vehicle coming through the intersection.

The failure to stop resulted in the fatality.

Bailey was arrested on scene and charged.
