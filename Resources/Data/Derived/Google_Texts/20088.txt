(Photo : NBC/"America's Got Talent") NBC announces the 2015 premiere date for season 10 of "America's Got Talent"

NBC has just announced the season 10 return date for their popular variety show "America's Got Talent." Filming is currently underway with contestants auditioning across across the country for the chance to become the most talented person in America!



Howard Stern, Heidi Klum, Mel B and Howie Mandel return as judges and Nick Cannon returns as host. Season 10 will premiere with a two-hour episode on Tuesday May 26th.



Per the NBC Press Release:



"NBC's top-rated summer series 'America's Got Talent' celebrates its landmark 10th season with the hottest performers from across the country competing for America's vote.



With the talent search open to acts of all ages, 'America's Got Talent' has brought the variety format back to the forefront of American culture by showcasing unique performers from across the country. The series is a true celebration of the American spirit, featuring a colorful array of singers, dancers, comedians, contortionists, impressionists, jugglers, magicians, ventriloquists and hopeful stars, all vying for their chance to win America's hearts and the $1 million prize.



Last year, for the first time in the show's history, America chose a magician as their winner: Rhode Island native Mat Franco, who went on to headline the 'America's Got Talent Live' stage show in Las Vegas."
