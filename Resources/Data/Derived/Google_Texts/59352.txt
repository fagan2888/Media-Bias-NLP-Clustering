A 5-month-old who died Wednesday from multiple blunt-force injuries has been identified as Noah Jones, according to Jefferson County Deputy Coroner Jim Wesley.

Noah was brought to Kosair Children's Hospital on Monday from his home in the 2200 block of Date Street.

Louisville Metro Police are conducting a homicide investigation into the child's death.

The funeral will be held at Neurath-Schoppenhorst Funeral Home on West Market Street.
