Fairfax County police are investigating a reported sexual assault at a Falls Church area community pool on Friday.

Police said a 20-year-old woman was working at the counter when she was approached by a man on Friday night. The suspect allegedly displayed a weapon and sexually assaulted the victim before fleeing.

Fairfax Police Investigate Sexual Assault at Community Pool

Police are investigating the sexual assault of a 20-year-old woman who worked at a community pool in the Falls Church area. (Published Sunday, Aug. 3, 2014)

The victim was taken to a local hospital where she was treated and released.

The suspect is described as a white man in his late 20s to early 30s, 5’10” tall with an average build and clean shaven.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Crime Solvers at 866-411-8477.
