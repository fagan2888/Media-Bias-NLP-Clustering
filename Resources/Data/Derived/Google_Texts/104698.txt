A young teenage girl is missing, and her family and Meridian police need your help to find her.

13-year-old Kaeli Winstead went missing Wednesday morning. She is a white female with black hair and green eyes. She is about 5 feet 4 inches, and weighs 126 pounds. Winstead was last seen wearing a three-quarters length blue shirt with orange heart on it with a hood. She was wearing a black Aeropostale jacket and black stretchy pants with a green 4 leaf clover and Guinness written on them. She may be headed to Alabama with 16-year-old Luiz Ibarra, who is also known as Chucky.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts can contact Meridian police at (601) 485-1893.
