A man was killed after his car struck a tree in Fort Worth on Sunday night.

Police said the driver veered off Interstate Highway 30 about 7:20 p.m. and his car struck a tree along the 3900 block of West Freeway service road.

The man was pinned in the 2001 Chevrolet sedan and later pronounced dead at the scene, police said.

It’s unclear why the man, who was the only person in the vehicle, drove off the road, police said.

The man’s identity has not been released.
