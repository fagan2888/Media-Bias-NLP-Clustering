The Urgent Care Center of Richmond Hill will host Business After Hours from 5:30-7:30 p.m. Thursday, June 18, at 60 Exchange St., Suite B-7.

Hors d’oeuvres and drinks will be served, Stewart Marshall will entertain guests with music and attendees will have a chance to meet the center’s staff and learn more about the business.

There also will be a drawing for every staff member nominated for the HEART Award. A team member will win cruise tickets for two people plus spending money. The HEART Award honors the staff member who best exhibits and/or values humility, energy, awareness, relationships and trustworthiness.

RSVP by calling 912-220-2976 or emailing ashley@lesleyfrancispr.com.
