SPRINGFIELD, Mo.-- Crosslines of Springfield will distribute Christmas food baskets and toys Dec. 20 to an anticipated 3,500 people.Volunteers from the community including churches, businesses, and schools will help to distribute the Christmas food baskets and toys to Greene county residents who have pre-registered for assistance.The distribution will be held at Ozarks Empire Fairgrounds E-Plex , located at 3001 N. Grant Ave. in Springfield from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.Each basket contains enough food for a nutritious holiday meal, breakfast and a snack for everyone in the household. Parents are able to shop at no charge for age-appropriate toys in the Crosslines Holiday Center Toystore. Each child in a family receives three toys, a book, and winter knits such as hats, gloves, and scarves.There are approximately 18,000 toys and gifts for this year's event.Crosslines of Springfield has seen a 15 percent increase in applications for assistance fop this holiday season.For more information, visit CCOzarks.org or contact Crosslines at 417-869-0563.
