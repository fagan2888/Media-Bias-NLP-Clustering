A man is in critical, but stable condition after being struck by a vehicle in southeast Wichita.

Wichita police say the man was crossing the street at East Lincoln and Ida Street at just after 6 p.m. Saturday at the intersection area of Lincoln and Ida. Police said an eastbound driver did not see the man and hit him.

There is no crosswalk at the intersection.

Sgt. Roger Runft told KAKE news the 33-year-old driver stopped his vehicle, assisted the victim and called 911.

The 54-year-old victim is recovering at Via Christi St. Francis hospital.
