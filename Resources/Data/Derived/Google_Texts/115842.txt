A Marianna man is behind bars after police say he was in possession of the drug 'Flakka'.

Marianna Police officials arrested Michael Allen, 60, Saturday night.

Officials say they received a call in reference to a verbal altercation in the area of Jackson and Bertram Streets in Marianna. When they arrived on scene, the complainant told police that Allen had been repeatedly threatening him.

Police searched Allen and that is when they found a pill bottle filled with 'Flakka'.

Allen was booked in the Jackson County Jail and awaits his first appearance.
