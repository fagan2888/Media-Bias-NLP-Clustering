A 20-year-old Tucson man died after he was ejected from his vehicle when he struck another car from behind, causing a rollover accident on the westbound Interstate 10 off-ramp at Orange Grove Rd., according to the Northwest Fire Department.

Public information officer Adam Goldberg said the man was driving northbound towards Orange Grove Rd. when he hit another car from behind, lost control and overturned on the side of the road.

Goldberg said he was not wearing a seat belt. He said the vehicle overturned and died on the scene when he was partially ejected.

The other people involved was a couple in their 70's. Goldberg said both had their seat belts and suffered no injuries.

The Orange Grove Rd. reopened at around 10:45 p.m.
