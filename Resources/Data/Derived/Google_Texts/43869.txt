A motorcycle driver died in a collision near Colorado Mills Mall with a tour bus in Lakewood on Sunday, according to Lakewood police.

Police dispatch received multiple reports of the accident at 14500 W. Colfax Drive at about 12:26 p.m., said Andrea Joo, police spokeswoman.

The accident is still under investigation, Joo said. “At this time it is undetermined if the motorcycle hit the bus. Detectives are on the scene, trying to piece everything together,” she said.

Joo said the bus is owned by Arrow Stage Lines.
