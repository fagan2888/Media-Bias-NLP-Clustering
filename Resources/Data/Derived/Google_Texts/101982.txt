The intersection of Hooper and Bay avenues was closed "for an extended period of time" Thursday morning after a tractor-trailer crashed into a traffic signal, knocking it to the ground, Toms River police said.

The crash, close to the Ocean County Mall, had knocked out power to the area.

The accident was was cleared by Thursday afternoon.
