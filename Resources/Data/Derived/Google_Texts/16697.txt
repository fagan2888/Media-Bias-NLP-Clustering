Lawmakers want to know more about the problems plaguing the federal website that’s supposed to enroll people in Obamacare.

Duplicate enrollments, spouses reported as children, missing data fields and incorrect rulings: all among the problems reported with the federal site.

(Lance) “How can we tax people for not buying a product from a website that doesn’t work?”

New Jersey Republican Congressman Leonard Lance hopes to get answers to that at an Energy and Commerce Committee hearing next week. But Health and Human Services Secretary Kathleen Sebelius has said she will not testify. Sebelius has said the problems with the federal website are being fixed.
