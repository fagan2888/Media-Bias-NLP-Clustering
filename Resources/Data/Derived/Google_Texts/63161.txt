Rep. Charles Rangel and Sen. Charles Schumer ranked among the 10 most productive lawmakers in the last five full sessions of Congress, according to a bill tracking service.

In the 10 years between 2003 and the end of the 2012, Rangel was the most productive member, based on total bills passed, according to a list compiled by TrackBill, a legislative tracking service.

The Harlem Democrat sponsored 31 bills that became law, along with 10 resolutions. He saw 10.7% of 290 bills he offered become law.
