Extinct reptile extravaganza

Anything and everything about dinosaurs is covered at “Jurassic Quest” taking over the National Western Complex this weekend. Life-size dinosaurs that move, inflatables, rides, fossil digs, tours, science stations and crafts are included from 9 a.m. to 8 p.m. Dec. 20 and 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. Dec. 21. Tickets vary, VIPs are available. Additional “Jurassic” shows are scheduled Jan. 3-4 in Greeley and Jan. 10-11 in Colorado Springs. 4655 Humboldt St., jurassicquest.com
