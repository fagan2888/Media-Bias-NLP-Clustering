Law Professor Replaces Quinn Emanuel In GOP's Obama Suit

Law360, New York (November 18, 2014, 2:11 PM ET) -- House Republicans on Tuesday said they’ve enlisted constitutional scholar Jonathan Turley to replace Quinn Emanuel Urquhart & Sullivan LLP in a planned lawsuit against President Barack Obama over the delay of the Affordable Care Act’s employer mandate.



Turley’s hiring marks the third time Republicans have brought aboard legal counsel to litigate the matter. Two previous firms — Quinn Emanuel and BakerHostetler — jumped ship amid speculation from some observers that accepting the politically charged case could harm their reputations.
