Authorities are investigating after a woman was hit by two vehicles while she was standing in the middle of the street Tuesday morning.

According to Oklahoma City police, it happened at S.W. 59th Street and Stewart Drive in Mustang. The first car that hit the woman reportedly left the scene. The driver of the second car that hit the woman stopped and called police.

The woman has been transported to OU Medical Center in serious condition. But police said she was alert.
