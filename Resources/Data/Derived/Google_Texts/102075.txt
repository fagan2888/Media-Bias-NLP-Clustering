Rapid City, SD

Authorities in Pennington County are currently investigating a case involving a police pursuit of a stolen vehicle that ended in a fatal crash early Sunday morning.

Rapid City law enforcement responded to a call of suspicious activity in the area of Elm Street and East Anne Street around 2:40 a.m. Sunday. Upon arrival, the subjects drove off in what ended up being a stolen vehicle, leading to a pursuit by law officers.

Authorities pursued the vehicle for about two minutes before it lost control at the corner of E. St. Patrick Street and Highway 44 East. The vehicle rolled and ejected two passengers. One of them died at the scene.

Another occupant of the vehicle fled on foot, but has since been located. The five other occupants were taken to Rapid City Regional Hospital.

The name of the deceased is not being released at this time.
