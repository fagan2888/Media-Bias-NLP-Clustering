Never one to shy away from low-hanging fruit, Jimmy Kimmel sent a correspondent onto the street to shove a camera in the faces of random people and ask them to pontificate on Obamacare — with a twist, of course.

Kimmel’s guy asks the interviewees which plan they support: Obamacare or the Affordable Care Act. They’re the same thing, of course, but that didn’t stop the subjects from picking one over the other, many of them stuttering through their explanations passionately.
