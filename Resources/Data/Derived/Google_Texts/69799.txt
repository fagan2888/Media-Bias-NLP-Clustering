Boca Raton authorities need your help finding two men who tried to steal a Rolex watch at Mayor’s Jewelers on Saturday. The employee was showing them a Rolex President watch when one of the males unsuccessfully tried to grab the watch from the employee’s hand.

The suspects ran out of the store into the mall and fled in different directions. Both suspects were Hispanic and only spoke Spanish during the incident. The employee described the males as well-dressed and one had tattoos on his arms.

These suspects may have been involved in a similar incident in the South Florida area. Please contact Detective Scott Hanley at (561) 338-1344 or Palm Beach County Crime Stoppers at (800) 458-TIPS if you have any information about this crime.
