Updated on Saturday Nov. 28: Thomas has been located, police said.

Original post: Dallas police are asking for the public’s help locating a missing 73-year-old man who was last seen Wednesday afternoon in Lake Highlands.

Police said Harrison Thomas was last seen around 4 p.m. leaving his daughter’s home in the 9700 block of Whitehurst Drive near Interstate 635. He was driving a silver Honda or Hyundai.

Thomas is described as a black man, about 5-foot-10, weighs 195 pounds. He is bald, has brown eyes and was last seen wearing a white sweater, blue jeans, white hat, and white shoes.

Anyone with information is asked to call 9-1-1 or contact the Dallas Police Department’s Missing Persons Unit at (214) 671-4268.
