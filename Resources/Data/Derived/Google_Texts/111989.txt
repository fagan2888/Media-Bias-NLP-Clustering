UPDATE: The Severe Thunderstorm Watch until 10 p.m. has been canceled for six counties in the KAKE viewing area.

A Severe Thunderstorm Watch until 10 p.m. has been issued for much of eastern Kansas. The watch does not include the city of Wichita.

Counties in the KAKE viewing area included in the watch are: Butler, Chase, Chautauqua, Cowley, Elk and Greenwood.

Large hail and damaging winds will be the primary threat along with heavy rainfall. Tornadoes are unlikely with these storms, but they cannot be ruled out.

Stay alert the changing weather conditions and updated forecasts. We will help keep you ahead of the storms with our Interactive Radar and LIVE weather coverage . And be sure to download the KAKE News app and sign up for text alerts .
