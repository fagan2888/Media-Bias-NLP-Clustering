Bowling fund raiser set

Basic training graduate

Air Force Airman Noah T. rathbun graduated from basic military training at Joint Base San Antonio-Lackland, San Antonio, Texas.

Rathbun completed an eight week program that included training in military discipline and studies, Air Force core values, physical fitness, and basic warfare principles and skills.

Airmen who complete basic training earn four credits toward an associate in applied science degree through the Community College of the Air Force.

Rathbun is the son of Daniel Rathbun of Eaton Rapids and Ambrerlee Bassett of Harrison.

He is a 2006 graduate of Hastings High School.

Chip Hills teachers get iPads

Teachers in the Chippewa Hills school district recently received iPads for classroom use.

Each building in the district also received a classroom set of iPads for student use.

The iPads were purchased through title monies as well as money given to the district by the Saginaw Chippewa Indian Tribe.
