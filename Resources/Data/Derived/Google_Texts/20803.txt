The past week was filled with anticipation, joy, disappointment, long hours of waiting in the merciless Washington, D.C., heat, and endless streams of analysis and commentary—all because we’ve reached the end of another Supreme Court term.

Decisions on the future of Obamacare and same-sex marriage left some jubilant and others scrutinizing the role of the judicial branch. The Daily Signal and other photographers captured these scenes from outside court.
