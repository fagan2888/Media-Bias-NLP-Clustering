UPDATE: A Wichita man is dead after crashing his motorcycle on the southeast side of town.

The accident happened just before 7 p.m at Washington and Bayley, south of Lincoln.

A witness told officers that two motorcycles were heading north on Washington at a high rate of speed. One of the riders crashed into the center median.

Investigators say the 62-year-old died around 10:45 p.m.

His name has still not been released.
