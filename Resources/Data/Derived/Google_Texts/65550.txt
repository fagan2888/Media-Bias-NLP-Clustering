The U.S.News and World Report has released its 2016 list for the best jobs in heathcare, and a theme common to the top 25 is an expected growth in the number of jobs in each respective field over the next several years. The Bureau for Labor Statistics estimates that job growth in the heath sector will outshine all other jobs in the period from 2014 to 2024.
