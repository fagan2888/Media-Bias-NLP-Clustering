Kaitlyn Greenidge received her MFA from Hunter College. She is the recipient of a 2016 NEA fellowship in literature, as well as fellowships from Lower Manhattan Community Council’s Work-Space Program and the Bread Loaf Writers’ Conference. Her work has appeared in the Believer, American Short Fiction, Guernica, Kweli Journal, the Feminist Wire, Afro Pop Magazine, Green Mountains Review and other places. She was born in Boston, and currently lives in Brooklyn. "We Love You, Charlie Freeman" is her first novel.
