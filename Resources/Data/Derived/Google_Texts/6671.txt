The artist formerly known as Prince, is releasing not one, but two new albums on September 30, 2014 — ending a four-year hiatus. His last record was “20Ten” four years ago. This year also marks the 30th anniversary of Prince’s groundbreaking album “Purple Rain,” which debuted in 1984.

The album “Age Official Age” is a 13-track solo album, and includes the tracks “Breakfast Can Wait” and “This Could Be Us,” among others.
