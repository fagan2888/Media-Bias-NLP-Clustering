TOMS RIVER Police are searching for a man who held a Dollar Tree store employee at knifepoint as he forced her hand over money from her cash drawer Friday.

The man walked into the store at 1354 Hooper Ave. at about 9:40 p.m. and pulled a knife on a 24-year-old woman working there, according to a news release from Officer Ralph Stocco.

The man demanded money from the register, to which the woman complied. He fled with the whole cash drawer.

Police describe the suspect as a six-foot-two white many with scruffy facial hair wearing a hooded sweatshirt with stripes down the arms and gray sweatpants.

Stocco said anyone with information should contact Detective Steve Bucci at 732-349-0150 extension 1300.
