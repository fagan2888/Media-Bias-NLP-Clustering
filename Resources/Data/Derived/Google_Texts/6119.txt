President Barack Obama clearly has several famous faces in his corner.

Since the launch on Tuesday of the Affordable Care Act, aka Obamacare, a number of celebs have answered the commander in chief's call to help promote the act by way of the hastag #GetCovered.

In fact, some stars have even taken it one step further and posted pics of themselves holding up signs with the hashtag written on them as well as a link to HealthCare.gov to learn more.
