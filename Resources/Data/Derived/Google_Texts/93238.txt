Police are investigating after finding a man’s dead body south of downtown Denver on Friday morning with “obvious signs of trauma.”

The body was found at West 10th Avenue and Acoma Street about 9:13 a.m.

Police are considering this to be a homicide, said Tyrone Campbell at a Friday news conference.

Campbell could not expand on the victim’s trauma or say whether he was shot.

The victim’s identity has not been released.

This is a developing story that will be updated as more information becomes available.
