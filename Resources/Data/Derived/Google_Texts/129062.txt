The Wayne County Sheriff's Office has arrested and charged a Seven Springs man with one count of murder.

Deputies say they were called to a reported assault at 113 Al Drive in Seven Springs around 6 a.m. Sunday.

Morris Holmes, 49, was transported to the Wayne Memorial emergency room where he was pronounced dead.

Details about how the crime happened weren't released Sunday.

Deputies say Craig Melvin, 55, is charged with murder. He's being held in the Wayne County Correctional Center without bond.
