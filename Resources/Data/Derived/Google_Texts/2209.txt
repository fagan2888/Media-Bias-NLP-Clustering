Panama City Police aren't revealing much information in the shooting death of a local man early Christmas morning.

Investigators say 56 year old Wilbert Faison was shot and killed Wednesday morning in the 500 block of East 13th Street.

About 1:00 Wednesday morning police responded to reports of shots being fired in the area and an injured person.

Faison was taken to Bay Medical Sacred Heart where he was pronounced dead about 1:30.

If you have any information that could help in this case, please call the Panama City Police Office and ask for Detective Rossomondo.

The number is (850) 872-3112.

If you'd like to report an anonymous tip call crimestoppers at 850-785-TIPS.
