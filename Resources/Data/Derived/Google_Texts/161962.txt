Two men were hospitalized Sunday morning after their car flipped and struck a tree in the northbound lanes of the Garden State Parkway near mile marker 68.7 at 8:24 a.m., State Police said.

One of the men was taken to Community Medical Center for treatment and another to AtlantiCare Regional Medical Center in Atlantic City, said Trooper 1 Michael Kleissler.

Their injuries were not life-threatening, he said.

State Police withheld their identities.

The crash remains under investigation.
