A 20-year-old Kenosha woman was killed and three others were injured in a single vehicle crash about 1:45 a.m. Saturday in Green Lake County, according to the sheriff's department.

Those injured in the crash were a 13-year-old girl from Princeton, a 21-year-old man from Kenosha and a 20-year-old woman from Princeton.

According to a news release, a 1995 Chevrolet Blazer crossed the westbound lane of traffic on state Highway 23 just west of Green Lake and overturned into a ditch.
