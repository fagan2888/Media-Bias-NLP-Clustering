Update 5:49 p.m.: Dallas police said Kevin Thornton has been located and is safe.

Original post 10:05 a.m.: The Dallas Police Department is seeking help from the public to locate a 72-year-old critically missing man.

According to police, Kevin Peter Thornton was last seen walking from his home at the 5300 block of Fleetwood Oak Avenue yesterday around 4 p.m.

Police say Thornton may suffer from on set dementia and could need medical care.

Thornton is 5’08″ tall and 160 pounds with grey hair and blue eyes. He was last seen wearing a grey polo shirt and blue jeans.

If you have any information regarding Thornton’s whereabouts, police ask you to call 911 or the Missing Persons Unit at (214) 671-4268.
