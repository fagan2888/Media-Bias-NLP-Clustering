A southeast Wichita home was damaged in a fire late Sunday morning.

Crews were called around 11:45 a.m. to the report of a house fire at South Terrace Drive and East Wilma Street, near Harry and Oliver. They reported seeing heavy spoke pouring from the home when they arrived.

Neighbors said the woman who lives at the home was at church at the time of the fire. No firefighters were hurt.

The cause of the fire and a dollar estimate of the the damage have yet to be determined.
