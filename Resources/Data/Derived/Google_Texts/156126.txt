Road rage leads to a man being stabbed and rushed to the hospital Saturday morning.

It happened at the IHop near Ridge road and Kellogg around 3 a.m. on Saturday.

It started on Maple and West street as a road rage incident. Leading to the IHop parking lot where the suspect confronted the victim, stabbing him once.

The victim was taken to the hospital. No arrests have been made.
