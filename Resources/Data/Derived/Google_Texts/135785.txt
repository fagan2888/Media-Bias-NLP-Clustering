A Hendry County teen died after a crash in Fort Myers early Sunday morning.

According to Lee County Sheriff's officials, the crash happened on Highlight Street at around 12:45 a.m.

When deputies arrived, they found the driver of a Chevy pick-up truck had been ejected from the vehicle.

The driver, identified as 19-year-old James Adam Barfield, was taken to Lehigh Regional Hospital where he died from his injuries.

Preliminary investigations revealed that Barfield was driving northeast on Highlight Street when he attempted to turn. Barfield lost control of the truck and hit a pine tree.

Investigators say speed and alcohol may have been a factor in the crash.
