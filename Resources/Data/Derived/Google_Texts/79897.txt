Police say a man who was shot twice in Kinston overnight is cooperating with the investigation.

The Kinston Department of Public Safety received a call at 1:16 a.m. Saturday in reference to a shooting near the Simon Bright apartments.

One man was found shot on East Shine Street outside of building 806.

He was transported to Vidant Medical Center in Greenville. His condition is unknown at this time.

Police do not have a motive or suspect information.

Anyone with information is asked to call police at (252) 939-4020.
