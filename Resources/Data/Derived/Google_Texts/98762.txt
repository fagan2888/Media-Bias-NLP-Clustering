A 45-year-old Northwest Florida woman is dead after a hit and run crash Sunday morning near the Seachase Condominiums on Front Beach Road.

Panama City Beach Police say the victim, Lora York, was walking west along the north side of Front Beach Road when she was hit.

After York was hit, the suspect drove off.

Investigators at the scene believe the vehicle that hit York is a lighter colored 2007-2010 General Motors Hummer H3.

A few hours into their investigation, police say the suspect was identified as 44-year-old David Wayne Turner.

Turner was taken into custody, and charged with leaving the scene of a crash with a death.

The investigation is on going.
