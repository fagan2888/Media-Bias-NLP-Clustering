One woman is dead after police said she was hit by a car near East Broadway Boulevard and South Kolb Road on Christmas Eve by an allegedly impaired driver.

Officers from the Tucson Police Department responded to the scene just after 11:30 p.m., according to a news release.

When they arrived, they saw a 34-year-old woman on the ground, and began rendering aid until crews from the Tucson Fire Department arrived.

The woman was taken to Banner University Medical Center with life-threatening injuries, where she later died. Her identity has not yet been released.

TPD detectives determined that the driver of a 2004 Honda CRV was heading eastbound on Broadway Boulevard approaching a green light at South Kolb Road.

The woman was crossing Broadway Boulevard when she was hit. She was not in a crosswalk.

Police at the scene determined the driver, 31-year-old Joedaniel Gutierrez, was allegedly impaired.

He was arrested for DUI - impaired to the slightest degree, a misdemeanor.
