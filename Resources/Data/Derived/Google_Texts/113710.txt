× Hobbs Island Road closed while police investigate crash

HUNTSVILLE, Ala. – Two drivers were transported to the hospital after a crash on Hobbs Island Rd just after 8 p.m. Thursday.

Witnesses said a car drifted across the center line and hit another car head on.

One driver appeared to have a broken arm and the other was unable to move from the car, according to witnesses.

There is no official word on the conditions of the drivers.

Hobbs Island Road is closed while police investigate the crash.
