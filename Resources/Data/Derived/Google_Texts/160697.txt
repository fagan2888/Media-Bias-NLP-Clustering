Sullivan's Island Police arrested a man for disorderly conduct during a church service on Sullivan's Island.

Police did not name the subject but arrests records show that the man is identified as James Goulah.

Goulah was taken to the Sheriff Al Cannon Detention Center. He is facing charges of disorderly conduct under the town code and disturbing a religious worship.

His court date is set for Jan. 5. According to Police, there were no firearms involved in the incident.
