Still reeling from the embarrassment of being on the losing end of rookie right-hander Chris Heston’s improbable no-hitter on Tuesday night, the Mets finally welcomed back a long-awaited reinforcement.

Catcher Travis d’Arnaud returned from the disabled list for Wednesday night’s 8-5 loss to the San Francisco Giants after missing more than seven weeks with a fractured right pinky finger.
