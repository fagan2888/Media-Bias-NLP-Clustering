Kristen Jean Childers and Benjamin Gene Mercer were married October 12, 2013, at Vesica Piscis Chapel in Catoosa. The Rev. Bernadine Dowdy officiated. A reception followed.

The bride is the daughter of Billie Bible Childers of Bixby and the late Kenneth Childers. She is the granddaughter of the late Andy and JoAnn Bible. The bride graduated from Bixby High School and Northeastern State University. She is a Blackjack Dealer with Riverspirit Casino.
