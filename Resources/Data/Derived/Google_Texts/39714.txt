Wichita police are looking for a man who fired shots from a car at another vehicle, crashing into a different vehicle then driving away late Saturday night in south Wichita.

Officers arrived at just before midnight to contact a 24-year-old male victim, who stated he was driving southbound on I-135 when an unknown man pulled alongside him in a gray Infiniti sedan and started firing at his vehicle.

The victim told officers he was not injured, but his car did receive damage.

Police say the Infiniti then exited off Hydraulic, colliding with another vehicle, before continuing to drive westbound.

Police say they were able to contact several witnesses at the scene of the hit and run, but are asking anyone who may have witnessed the shots being fired to call the WPD investigations division at 268-4407.
