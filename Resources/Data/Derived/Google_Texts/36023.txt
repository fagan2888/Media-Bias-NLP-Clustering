Covington Brings On Legislative Expert From White House

Law360, Washington (July 6, 2015, 4:57 PM ET) -- Covington & Burling LLP said last week that it had added an Obama administration official to its ranks with Layth Elhassani, a former special assistant to President Barack Obama in the White House's legislative affairs office.



Elhassani joins Covington's global public policy and government affairs practice based in Washington, D.C., where he will help business clients navigate the political and regulatory landscape, according to the firm. Elhassani has spent most of his professional life in Washington, having served in the White House from 2012 to 2015...
