Police are investigating a homicide after a male victim was found dead inside a vehicle at a Del City apartment complex Wednesday morning.

According to Del City police, at about 8 a.m., officers responded to the report of a vehicle with bullet holes in the parking lot of Oak Ridge Apartments, located at 3324 S. Bryant.

When officers arrived, they found a male victim dead inside the vehicle.

Police have not identified the victim at this time. No arrests have been made, and no suspect information is available.

Police said they also received a report of shots fired overnight. But it's unclear at this time if these two incidents were related.
