Box Elder, SD

Two Texas bikers in South Dakota for a motorcycle club event were arrested at a Box Elder bar.

At about 3 a.m. Saturday, Box Elder Police were called to the Belle Starr Gentlemen's Club for unwanted subjects who were possibly armed. Three officers took cover after hearing gunshots coming from the west side of the building.

Police arrested 34-year-old Gerardo Belloc, of Midland, Texas. He told police he was test firing a .45-caliber Kimber semi-automatic pistol and was arrested without incident. He is charged with possession of a controlled substance, possession a loaded firearm while intoxicated, reckless discharge of a firearm and disorderly conduct.

Officers also arrested 25-year-old Jose Juan Morales, of Midland, Texas. He is charged with driving under the influence.

Both told police they were attending the Bandidos National Run in Rapid City, and were members of a support club.

Belloc and Morales are being held in Pennington County Jail. No injuries were reported.
