Rachel Watrous and Nathan Litsey were married Sept. 27, 2014, in front of family and friends in the Old Main Chapel at the University of Colorado.

Pastor Ray Donatucci officiated as the couple exchanged personalized vows. The ceremony was followed by an outdoor reception at the CU Koenig Alumni Center.

Rachel is the daughter of Tom and Betty Watrous, of Windsor. Nate is the son of Bob and Judy Litsey, of Boulder. The couple live in Boulder.

The bride and groom both received bachelor's degrees from the University of Colorado. The bride also received a master's degree from the University of North Carolina and is an occupational therapist at The Peaks Care Center in Longmont. The groom is a commercial real estate agent with W.W. Reynolds in Boulder.

The couple celebrated with a honeymoon in Hawaii.
