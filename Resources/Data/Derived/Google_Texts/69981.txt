Nearly 100 House Democrats are asking Team Obama to rewrite its pending reform of retirement plans. ObamaSave would force many customers to pay an annual percentage of their assets to a financial adviser instead of trading commissions. A Journal editorial notes that the result will be a big price increase. A recent study “found that while the current commission model costs on average 0.5% of assets, the fee-based model would cost 1.1% of assets plus additional fund expenses.”
