Walmart has been named Pharmacy Innovator of the Year for 2015 by Chain Drug Review. Walmart has more than 140 million weekly customers and showed a commitment to consumer needs by holding "America's Biggest Health Fair," a 4,400-store event that included free blood glucose, hypertension, and vision testing, and information about coverage under the Affordable Care Act and Medicare. Walmart has also collaborated with Directhealth.com to help customers enroll in insurance plans that fit their needs. The company has redesigned its website to allow customers the chance to manage their health whenever they want, wherever they are, and it has worked to make healthy food more accessible and affordable.
