The Silicon Valley of today is very different from the one 20 or 30 years ago—and not just in terms of the hottest companies or newest technologies. Rather, something has fundamentally changed for the worse, and it is time to return to what made the Valley culture great. This begins with the restoration of equitable equity. This is the attitude—and the practice—that all contributors to a company’s success should have a material stake in that success.
