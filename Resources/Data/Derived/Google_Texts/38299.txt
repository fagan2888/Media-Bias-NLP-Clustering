Days after President Barack Obama appeared with Zach Galifianakis on the Funny or Die web series "Between Two Ferns" to promote the Affordable Care Act, Michelle Obama and a collection of celebrity moms have connected to do the same thing.

Sharon Feldstein (Jonah Hill's mom), Patsy Noah (Adam Levine's mom), Terria Joseph (Alicia Keys' mom) and Guadalupe Rodriguez (Jennifer Lopez's mom) star in the video, sharing stories of their kids' youthful days. (Fun fact: Hill flooded his elementary school.) "We nag you because we love you," Michelle Obama says at the end of the video, before plugging the Affordable Care Act website.
