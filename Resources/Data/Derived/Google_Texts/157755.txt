Indian Country Today Media Network’s pow wow kid of the week is three-year-old Marley Malnourie.



Marley is a member of the Arikara/Lakota tribes.



Her photo was submitted by Angel Young, a data techician at the United Tribes Technical College in Bismark, North Dakota.



If you know a child ICTMN can feature as a pow wow kid of the week, please submit your image to us at Editor@ictmn.com. In the email, include the child’s: name, age, parent's names, tribal affiliation, his/her dance style, and a little bit about the pow wow he/she attended.
