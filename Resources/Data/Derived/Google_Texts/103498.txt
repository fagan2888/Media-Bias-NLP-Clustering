Its a boy! The first baby to arrive in Meridian in the year 2015 is Kyrie Aamir Williams born at 5:23 this morning at Anderson Regional Medical Center and both mom and dad were happy to have their little bundle of joy arrive.

"Well I was not expecting it, I am very very excited to have the first baby born in Meridian and at the Hospital in 2015, very happy," explained mom Lashanna Jenkins.

"We have another bundle of joy in our life and we are thankful for that," said dad Unterio Williams.

Kyrie weighed 6 pounds 14 ounces and he was 21 inches long and both the baby and mom are doing fine.
