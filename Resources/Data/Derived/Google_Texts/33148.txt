The Tallahassee Police Department is investigating an afternoon shooting.

Officers tell us it happened at around 4:40 this afternoon. Officers responded to the Villa Cortez Apartments on Jackson Bluff Road. They found a victim suffering from a gunshot wound to the leg. He was taken to the hospital. His injuries are not believed to be serious.

Police are searching for the suspect. They have not released any other details at this time.

We'll bring you the latest on this case as it becomes available.
