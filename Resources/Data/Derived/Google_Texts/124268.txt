For free assistance with buying health insurance on healthcare.gov, call the Community Action Partnership of Mid-Nebraska at 308-865-5675.

Open enrollment in the Marketplace runs through Feb. 15. Consumers can review and compare health plan options at healthcare.gov. Those who sign up by Jan. 15 will have coverage effective on Feb. 1. Those who sign up by Feb. 15 will have coverage starting March 1.

Consumers who were automatically reenrolled in their 2014 policies can sign up for a different policy before Feb. 15.

For more information, visit www.Localhelp.healthcare.gov, or call the federally-facilitated Marketplace Call Center at 1-800-318-2596. TTY users should call 1-855- 889-4325. Translation services are available. The call is free.
