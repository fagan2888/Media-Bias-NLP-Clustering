COLUMBIA, SC (WACH) - For the second year running, BlueCross BlueShield of South Carolina will host their BlueDay at EdVenture.

This event provides free admission to all at EdVenture Saturday, October 24.

"There is no trick to this treat. We want South Carolina families; whether or not they have BlueCross insurance, to enjoy a free day at EdVenture, fill out their Passport to Health and consider treating themselves or a loved to a policy that is tailored just for them," said BlueCross President Jim Deyling.

BlueDay takes place prior to the November 1 date which is the start date for enrollment for the Affordable Care Act. Represents from BlueCross will be there to discuss potential insurance policies with visitors.

The act makes it so every citizen must have health insurance or will face a a fine.

Also Saturday, all visitors at EdVenture will receive a Passport to Health. Designated stop have been established at the museum and if a visitor gets a stamp at every spot they are then qualified to win tickets to the USC vs. Clemson game.
