Becky Reed of Sterling and Ken Reed of Sterling are pleased to announce the engagement of their daughter, Sara Reed, to Ben Hutchcraft of Dodge City, Kan.

The bride-to-be is the granddaughter of Jack and Phyllis Claver of Sterling and the late Ed and Beverly Reed of Sterling. Sara is a 2007 graduate of Sterling High School and a 2013 graduate of Kansas State University in Manhattan, Kan., with a Bachelor of Science in construction science and management. She works for Faith Technologies in Lenexa, Kan.., as a pre-construction specialist.

The future groom is the son of Virgil and Sherry Hutchcraft of Dodge City, Kan. He is the grandson of Robert Goodman and Beulah Cook of Sabetha, Kan., Bill and Mary Stanley of Topeka, Kan., and Deanna Baumgartner of Solomon, Kan. Ben is a 2007 graduate of Dodge City High School in Dodge City, Kan., and a 2012 graduate from Kansas State University with a Bachelors of Science in secondary education. He works for Comanche Middle School in Dodge City, Kan., as a Humanities teacher.

The couple will be married on Oct. 11, 2014, and will reside in Kansas City, Kan.
