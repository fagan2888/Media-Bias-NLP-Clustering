Assemblywoman Michaelle Solages, will be hosting an Affordable Care Act Education and Enrollment Workshop on Tuesday, November 19th, from 7:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. at Christ Evangelical Lutheran Church, 57 Spooner Street, Floral Park 11001. (The church is one block south of Jericho Turnpike and on the corner of Plainfield Avenue.)

{jcomments on}

Assemblywoman Solages has invited the New York State of Health to speak at the community forum in Floral Park to provide important updates and information on the Affordable Care Act health insurance marketplace for New York residents. Topics covered will include the individual mandate, impact on small businesses, the NYS Health Exchange, information on Income Tax Credits and Subsidies, Medicare & Medicaid and much more.
