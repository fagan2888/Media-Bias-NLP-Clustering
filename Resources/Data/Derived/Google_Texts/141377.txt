Precisely 12 years ago, a man in Pakistan, with a video camera running to capture the act, drew a knife across the throat of our Wall Street Journal colleague Daniel Pearl and murdered him. Danny—gifted reporter, elegant writer, incurable idealist—was a victim of Islamic extremists who either thought he was an Israeli spy or simply liked the idea of killing an enterprising American journalist.
