MILAN—Private-equity firm Blackstone Group LP is nearing a deal to buy a minority stake in Italian fashion house Gianni Versace SpA, a person familiar with the situation said on Wednesday, in what would mark a major milestone in the turnaround of a fashion house that was once on the brink of bankruptcy.

After months of negotiations, the Versace family—which controls the company founded in the 1970s by Gianni Versace—is near a decision to sell a 20% stake in the company to the investment fund for as much as €200 million...
