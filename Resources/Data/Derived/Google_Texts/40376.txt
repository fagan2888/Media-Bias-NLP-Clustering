The exhibit, which ends Sept. 7, includes animatronic dinosaurs, a puppet show and fossil dig boxes.

The special begins Monday and ends Aug. 31. Tickets are $7.50 for ages 12 to 64. Children ages 3 to 11 and seniors age 65 and up pay $6. Admission is free for members and children under 2.

The zoo is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. and is at 650 South R.L. Thornton Freeway.
