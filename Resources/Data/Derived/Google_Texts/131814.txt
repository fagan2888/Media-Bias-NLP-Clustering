Dell Rapids, SD

The Minnehaha County Sheriff's Office is investigating a fatal crash near Dell Rapids, SD.

Authorities responded to a report of a vehicle sitting in a bean field along 474th Avenue north of Dell Rapids just before 1 a.m Sunday morning.

The victim has been identified as 55-year-old Charlene Adams of Dell Rapids. Authorities believe she was traveling southbound on 474th Avenue when she lost control of her vehicle and rolled. She was ejected from the vehicle and was pronounced dead at the scene.

The accident is still under investigation.
