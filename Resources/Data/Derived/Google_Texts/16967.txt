Aberdeen, SD

Registration is open for a spooky 5K in Aberdeen on Halloween this year.

The Annual Boo-kin For A Cure Spooktacular Fun Run 5K will take place Saturday, October 31 starting at 2 p.m. in Wylie Park. The race is a fundraiser for Relay For Life and the American Cancer Society.

The race costs $20 per person through October 27 and $25 from October 28 through the day of the race. Anyone who registers before October 26 gets a t-shirt, and kids under 12 run free.

Costumes are encouraged, with the opporunity to win prizes.
