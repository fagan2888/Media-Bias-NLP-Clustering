HHS Finalizes ACA Plan Auto-Renewal Rule

Law360, Washington (September 3, 2014, 2:40 PM ET) -- The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services on Wednesday finalized a rule to automatically re-enroll participants in Affordable Care Act insurance exchanges in their existing plans unless individuals voluntarily opt out, saying the plan will help reduce administrative costs and streamline the program.



The final rule, which will be published in Thursday's Federal Register, claims it will bring individuals who sign up for coverage through the exchanges in line with most consumers who have coverage from outside the exchanges and are generally auto-enrolled each year....
