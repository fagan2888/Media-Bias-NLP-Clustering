The Meridian Police Department is investigating the shooting of a woman on Saturday night.

MPD says it happened about 7:30 at Frank Berry Courts apartments when a woman was shot in the lower leg.

Investigators say it does not appear the woman was the intended target and therefore, was shot accidentally.

Police are actively searching for two persons of interest.

If you have any information about this case, you are asked to call Crimestoppers at 601-485-1860.
