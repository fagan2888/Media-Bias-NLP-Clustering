All lanes were temporarily closed as of 2:32 p.m. because of the crash, according to the state Department of Transportation.

Port Authority police spokesman Joseph Pentangelo said a multi-vehicle crash occurred in the center tube at about 2:20 p.m., and that two people were been taken to Hoboken University Medical Center for minor injuries.

One vehicle was disabled in the crash due to airbag deployment, Pentangelo said.

As of 2:45 p.m., the center tube was expected to open "momentarily," Pentangelo said.

Heavy traffic and 30-minute delays were reported eastbound through the New Jersey side as of 2:45 p.m., according to the DOT.
