Huron, SD

For the owners of more than 100 vandalized vehicles in Huron looking for answers, Huron Police have arrested four people they believe were responsible.

Previous Story: Vandals Strike More Than 100 Vehicles In Huron

The Huron Police Department arrested four juveniles early Saturday morning after performing a traffic stop. The vehicle was pulled over for speeding around a cul de sac.

Four male juveniles were in the car, along with a cash register, BB guns, and BBs. The cash register had been reportedly stolen from a local Asian grocery store the night before.

All four boys were arrested and now face charges of burglary, theft, ingestion of a substance for the purpose of intoxication, and multiple counts of personal property damage. They are currently sitting in the Beadle County Juvenile Detention Center while they await their first court appearance.

The Huron Police Department were grateful for the community's help in bringing the crimes to an end.
