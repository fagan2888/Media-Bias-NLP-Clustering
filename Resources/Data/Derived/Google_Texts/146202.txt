'Gov will extend medical insurance to all Iranians,' Hassan Rouhani tweets. Iran president pushes 'Rouhanicare'

Iranian President Hassan Rouhani announced plans on Twitter on Wednesday that might sound a little familiar to Americans.

Gov will extend medical insurance to all Iranians. First step will be to cover 5mn uninsured Iranians by the social safety net #RouhaniCare Story Continued Below — Hassan Rouhani (@HassanRouhani) February 5, 2014

Rouhani’s announcement of universal health care for all Iranians makes a not-so-subtle nod to the U.S.’s Affordable Care Act, more commonly known as Obamacare.
