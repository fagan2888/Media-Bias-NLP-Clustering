JACKSONVILLE BEACH, Fla. - The Jacksonville Beach Police Department is searching for a man who was last seen earlier this month.

Antonio Porter was last seen at 2:20 p.m. March 6 in Jacksonville Beach, police said.

A family member talked to Porter on the phone March 11, but no further contact has been made.

Porter currently lives at an apartment in the 800 block of First Street South in Jacksonville Beach as a caregiver, police said.

Anyone who may know where Porter is or has information about his disappearance is asked to call the Jacksonville Beach Police Department at 904-270-1667.
