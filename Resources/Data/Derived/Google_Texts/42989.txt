Donald Trump lashed out a bit at CNN at a fundraiser tonight, saying rather bluntly, “CNN is terrible.”

A reporter asked him a question about all the protesters gathered outside the event, and Trump dismissed them as just a small number.

He dismissively told her that he expected her to ask that question because she’s from CNN and, as Trump said, “You people do not cover us accurately at all.”
