Shareholders OK Anthem-Cigna Merger; Antitrust Check Ahead

Law360, Los Angeles (December 3, 2015, 9:13 PM ET) -- Shareholders of Anthem Inc. and Cigna Corp. on Thursday overwhelmingly approved a previously announced $54 billion merger that will cover approximately 53 million medical members, although the consolidation still has to pass scrutiny by antitrust regulators.



For both health plans, approximately 99 percent of the votes cast Thursday morning were in favor of the unification, according to announcements from Anthem and Cigna.
