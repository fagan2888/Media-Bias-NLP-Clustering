House Bill Aims To Repeal ACA 'Belly Button Tax'

Law360, New York (November 15, 2013, 3:14 PM ET) -- A bipartisan bill introduced Thursday would repeal the Affordable Care Act's “belly button tax,” marking Capitol Hill's latest response to the health care law as its real-world impact becomes clearer.
