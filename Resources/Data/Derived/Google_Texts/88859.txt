Custer, SD

Authorities are searching for two teens who ran away from the STAR Academy near Custer Sunday.

Two boys ran away from the Brady Academy program before noon MDT Sunday.

Both boys are Native American. One of the boys is described as 5'11" tall, weighs around 145 pounds, with black hair and brown eyes. He is 16 years old.

The other boy is described as 5'2" tall, around 110 pounds, with brown hair and brown eyes. He is 15 years old.

Anyone who has seen these two boys or knows of their whereabouts is urged to contact law enforcement.
