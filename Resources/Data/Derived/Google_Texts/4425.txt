Troopers have identified a man who was killed in a crash Sunday afternoon on the Interstate 135 interchange in Wichita.

It happened around 12:45 p.m. on the ramp from westbound Kellogg to southbound I-135. The Kansas Highway Patrol said the driver lost control of his SUV, veered off the road and struck a bridge support pillar.

The man died at the scene. He is identified as 63-year-old James Depriest of Wichita.

The crash is under investigation.
