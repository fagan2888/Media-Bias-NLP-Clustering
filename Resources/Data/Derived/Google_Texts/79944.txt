The Aurora Police Department is asking for the public’s help in locating a man suspected of murder.

Lloyd Rickey Henderson — who Aurora police said goes by “Rickey” — was involved in an early morning fight Sept. 27 at 348 Potomac Way that led to the shooting death of Corey Clemons.

Police applied for and secured a warrant for first-degree murder for the arrest of Henderson in Clemons’ murder, according to an Aurora police news release.

Henderson is believed to be in the Aurora or Denver area, although his exact whereabouts are unknown.

Anyone with information about Henderson is asked to call 911 or Metro Denver Crime Stoppers at 720-913-7867.
