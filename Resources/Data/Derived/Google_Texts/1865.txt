Our judicial system is an adversarial one. Its "very premise," the Supreme Court explained years ago, "is that partisan advocacy on both sides of a case will best promote the ultimate objective" of justice and equity. Of course, partisan advocacy also makes for familiar story lines: good versus evil, rich versus poor, David versus Goliath, left versus right.
