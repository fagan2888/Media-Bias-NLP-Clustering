ORLANDO, Fla., May 4, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- PlanSource®, a leading provider of cloud-based health exchange and benefits engagement solutions, today announced that Chief Executive Officer Dayne Williams will present at the William Blair Benefit Technology Exchange and Broker Conference in Boston, Mass. on Tuesday, May 5, 2015.

The changing nature of the healthcare insurance landscape, driven by the passage of the Affordable Care Act and pressures from increasing costs and complexities, creates numerous opportunities for the key players in the benefits distribution space. The purpose of this conference is to help investors explore and begin to define the dynamics and key issues that will drive the sector over the next 12 to 24 months.

Financial services and technology analyst Adam Klauber is hosting the conference, which will consist of a series of panels from leading experts from across the healthcare spectrum, including benefits brokers and consultants, human resources and health insurance executives and leaders in the healthcare exchange field.
