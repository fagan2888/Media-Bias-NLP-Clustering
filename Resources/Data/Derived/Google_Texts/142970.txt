The victim of a shooting and stabbing Tuesday night in Oxnard was a 17-year-old Oxnard resident, authorities said Wednesday morning.

Oxnard police responded to a fight in the 5400 block of Roland Way about 9:30 p.m. Tuesday and found the victim in an alley. He had been shot once and had several stab wounds, officials said.

He was taken to Ventura County Medical Center, where he was in critical but stable condition, police said. He was expected to survive.

No arrests had been made. A possible motive for the attack was unknown, and police were continuing to investigate the incident.
