A 20-year-old driver fatally crashed into a guardrail and a tree early Sunday near White Rock Lake.

Police say Jose Monroy-Fuentes of Garland lost control when driving in the 8200 block of Garland Road around 2:50 a.m. His car spun, ran over the curb and collided with a guardrail and tree, according to police. He was pronounced dead at the scene.

No passengers were in the car and no other vehicles were involved. Police are investigating whether drugs or alcohol played a role.
