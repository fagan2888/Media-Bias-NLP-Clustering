A Salina woman was hospitalized after firefighters rescued her from a house fire Saturday afternoon.

Crews were called around 2:15 p.m. to a fire in the 2000 block of Harold Avenue. They saw flames when they arrived on scene.

Battalion Chief Kirk Stover told KSAL News that firefighters rescued one person from inside the attached garage of the home. She was taken to a local hospital. He current condition is unknown.

KSAL reports the fire was contained to mainly the garage area, but there was damage throughout the entire house.

The cause of the fire is under investigation.
