A car crash killed a driver and injured two passengers in Garland Sunday morning.

Garland police say the car was heading west on Interstate 635 near Jupiter Road around 5:30 a.m. when it spun clockwise and collided with a light pole. Police say the highway was not wet when the crash occurred.
