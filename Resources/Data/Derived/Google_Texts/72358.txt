An update to the shooting in Pratt County Saturday morning. The Pratt County Sheriff says the victim has died.

He is identified as 66 year-old Lewis R. Culver of Cullison, Kansas.

The shooting was ruled as a self-inflicted gun shot wound.

An investigation is underway into a shooting in Cullison that left one person hurt.

Just after 8 a.m. Saturday, the Pratt County deputies were called to the scene of a gunshot victim.

The 66-year-old man was taken to Wesley Medical Center in Wichita. His name was not released.
