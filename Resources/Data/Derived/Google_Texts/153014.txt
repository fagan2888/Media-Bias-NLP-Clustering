A woman was hit and killed by a vehicle Saturday night while crossing the street, making it the second pedestrian death in two nights in Colorado Springs.

The woman was crossing the street against a red light, according to police.

It happened at the intersection of North Powers and Dublin Boulevard in Colorado Springs around 11:30 p.m. Saturday.

The name and age of the victim has not been released.

The driver of the vehicle stayed on scene and is not being held on any charges. Drugs or alcohol are not considered a factor.
