A St. Petersburg man who was struck while crossing 54th Avenue N. in St. Petersburg died Friday night, the Florida Highway Patrol said.

The man, identified as 50-year-old Larry Eugene Day, did not have the walk signal when he tried to cross 54th Avenue N. at the intersection of 28th Street at around 11 p.m., troopers said.

The man walked into the path of a Ford SUV driven by Tabitha Lashay McMillian, of Atlanta, Georgia, and was struck, troopers said.

The man was taken to Bayfront Medical Center where he later died.
