Katy home to growing senior community Nearly 39,000 residents over age 60 call the area home

At age 84, Erwin Engel is happy with the life he's lived so far. His college years at Texas A&M University, his career selling paper across the state and his 40-year marriage to Beatrice Louise Bryan, his late wife, are all highlights. So when he wakes up sore from arthritis pain in his room at the new Orchard Assisted Living and Memory Care in Katy, he doesn't let it get him down.
