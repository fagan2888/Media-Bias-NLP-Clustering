Two men were killed in separate stabbings early Saturday in Philadelphia.

The first occurred at 2:54 a.m. in Northeast Philadelphia, when a 21-year-old man was stabbed in the chest, stomach, and right shoulder during an altercation in the 7300 block of Revere Street. He was taken to Aria Torresdale Hospital, where he died at 3:38 a.m.

No arrest has been made.

The second incident occurred at 3:09 a.m., when a 30-year-old man was stabbed once in the chest in the 2700 block of Oakford Street in the King Village section. He died there about 15 minutes later. Police said three women were being held for questioning.
