A stabbing on 16th Street Mall Sunday evening sent one person to the hospital, officials said.

One person stabbed another, said Denver police spokesman Sonny Jackson.

The victim was taken to the hospital, but is expected to survive, Jackson said.

Jackson said one person is in custody and the investigation is ongoing.
