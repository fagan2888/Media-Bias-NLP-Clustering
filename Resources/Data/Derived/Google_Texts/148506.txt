The basic steps in the AtlantiCare Community Healthcare Access Program, deadline for applying for federal marketplace Feb. 15:

1. If you lost your job as a result of a casino closing in 2014, call the Access Center at 888-569-1000.

2. Access Center will schedule a meeting with a financial counselor and send information and an appointment reminder.

3. Meet with counselor and sign up with federal and/or state health insurance programs, possibly with subsidies, as appropriate.

Source: AtlantiCare
