Police are investigating a shooting incident in which an armed security guard fired at a suspect.

It happened around 2:30 Sunday morning near Lincon and San Pablo streets, which is just west of Governeour.

Police said the suspect was shooting at a red car in the area when an armed security guard fired several rounds at him.

The suspect ran into a neighborhood and was not found.

A short time later, a man arrived at a Wichita hospital for treatment of a gunshot wound. Investigators are still trying to determine if he was involved in the shooting incident.

Police did give specifics of the security guard's employment.

Further details were not released.
