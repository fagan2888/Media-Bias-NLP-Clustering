How to throw a summer blow-out on a budget

Photo: ONLINE_YES Houstonian Will Matthews has released an event-planning book.

Do event planners hold the secrets to a happy life? Houstonian Will Matthews thinks so. Matthews discovered a passion for planning events when he threw his own high school graduation party and has been planning functions large and small on and off ever since. Six years ago, he launched RSVP With Will and has planned corporate events for clients including the University of Houston and the United Way.
