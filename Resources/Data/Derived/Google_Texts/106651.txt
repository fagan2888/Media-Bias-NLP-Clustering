A man is in serious condition after crashing his motorcycle on a stretch of highway in west Wichita.

The crash happened around 5:30 p.m. on West Kellogg near Dugan Road. Police say the motorcyclist was heading eastbound when he clipped the rear of the car in front of him.

The man was thrown off the motorcycle. He is currently listed in serious condition. His name and age were not released.

Police are still investigating the crash.
