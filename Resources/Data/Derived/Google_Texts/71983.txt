Does the success of the Ubers and the Airbnbs of the world signal the rise of a medieval-kind of feudalism, or simply the market’s invisible hand creating a new way for millions to find work? CIO Journal Columnist Irving Wladawsky-Berger thinks industry analyst and sharing economy expert Jeremiah Owyang has the right take: “[C]apitalism will find a solution to much of the dichotomy at the heart of the space.”
