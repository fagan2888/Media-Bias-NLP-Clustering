Brookings, SD

The Brookings Arts Council is inviting artists and art-lovers to come together for a unique event in town.

The Council's Bi-Annual 6 Squared Fundraiser is an event that collects hundreds of 6x6-inch pieces of art from all over the country for a gallery show. The pieces are displayed and put on sale for $20 each.

The gallery opened for viewing Wednesday, May 6th and will remain open until May 16th when the works of art will be available for purchase.

A music and ice-cream social event will begin at 4 p.m. on May 16th with a raffle for buying positions starting at 5 p.m. Raffle tickets are $5 and can be purchased up to and including the night of the event.

The Brookings Arts Council is located at 524 4th Street in Brookings. They are open Tuesday through Saturday from noon to 5 p.m.
