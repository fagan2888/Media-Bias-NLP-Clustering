Police are searching for a hit-and-run suspect that hit a pedestrian on Sunday afternoon on Board Street in Red Bank, said Capt. Darren McConnell.

Alphonso J. Tomaino, 95, of 335 Broad Street, was struck by a vehicle on Sunday afternoon near 362 Broad Street in Red Bank. He was crossing the road near the Broad street near the intersection of Pinckney Road, McConnell said.

Tomaino was transported to Riverview Medical Center in Red Bank by MONOC with non-life threatening injuries, McConnell said.

Red Bank police are still searching for a silver Nissan Altima, which they believe the hit-and-run driver was operating, McConnell said.

Anyone with information about this hit-and-run should contact the Red Bank Police Department at (732) 530-2700.
