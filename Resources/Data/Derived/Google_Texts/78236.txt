A single-engine plane traveling to Fort Worth Spinks Airport from Springfield, Missouri crashed in Oklahoma Sunday afternoon, killing a man, a Federal Aviation Administration spokesman said.

Related Plane Bound for Fort Worth Crashes in OK, Killing 1

The Beechcraft Bonanza was carrying a husband and wife when it went down five miles southeast of Colbert, Oklahoma around 3 p.m. Sunday, said spokesman Lynn Lunsford.

Authorities with the Oklahoma Highway patrol confirm with NBC 5 that one man on board the plane was killed. He was later identified as Charles S Fehr, 64, of Fort Worth.
