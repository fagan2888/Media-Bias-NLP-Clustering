Fresh Start Behavioral is helping people from near and far overcome addiction.Ryan Drake from Fresh Start Behavioral Services says overcoming barriers will help people receive the treatment they need to overcome their addictions.This program is called Recovery Supportive Services. Whether it be housing situations, insurance needs or concerns or transportation to their treatment appointments, Fresh Start has an answer and solution for all concerns.The first step is to come in and talk with one of their staff members. At this time, prospective patients can figure out a plan to see what actions need to happen in order to get their treatment started. Fresh start will also assess their needs, where they are at, and how Fresh Start can help overcome their barriers.
