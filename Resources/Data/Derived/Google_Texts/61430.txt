William Amaya

Fort Morgan Middle School eighth grader, William Amaya has been chosen as the October Rotary Junior Wheel. He is the son of Ana Reyes and William Amaya.

Amaya is a good student at Fort Morgan Middle School, carrying a 3.59 average. His educational goals are to be great at math, to get a 4.0 GPA, and to go to college someday.

It is Amaya's hope to attend CSU and to study to be a veterinarian or to do something to help animals.

He was honored by selection as pack leader on the Wolves team last year. He was also recognized as team player last year.

Amaya is a football manager at Fort Morgan Middle School. He likes to have fun in class. Amaya also likes animals (dogs are his favorite). His favorite food is chicken nuggets.
