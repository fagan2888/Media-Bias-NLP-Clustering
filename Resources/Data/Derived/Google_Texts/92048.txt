Regarding Lucette Lagnado's "Anti-Israel Jews and the Vassar Blues" (op-ed, Feb. 24): As two of the 15 Vassar Jewish Union (VJU) board members and active members of the Jewish community at Vassar, we voice our strong support of Israel and speak out against the ASA boycott. We aren't afraid to discuss these issues with people who don't agree with us. The VJU has always provided a community where we are encouraged to share our views and opinions regardless of where they fall. Our organization's consideration and vote to join the Open Hillel Movement results from the VJU's mission to provide open discourse....
