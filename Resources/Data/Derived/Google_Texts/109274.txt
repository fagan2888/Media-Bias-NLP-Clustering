Rancho Mirage, CA, Aug. 19, 2014 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Today, the world's leading surrogacy and in vitro fertilization (IVF) insurance solution provider, New Life Agency, Inc., announced that it has partnered with LightStream to provide financing options for all assisted reproduction insurance needs, including surrogacy and fertility medications through Walgreens Pharmacies.

"We have searched years for a financing option to help intended parents and patients seeking financial assistance. We are happy to have finally found a way to alleviate the upfront, out-of-pocket costs of the most important piece to their journey, securing peace of mind insurance for the liability of their future family," says Trish Taylor, New Life Chairman. "We are delighted to partner with LightStream, making it even easier for our clients to make their dreams of having children come true."

LightStream is a national online consumer lending division of SunTrust Bank, one of the nation's premier financial services companies. Their aim is to streamline the process of obtaining a loan, allowing intended parents to focus on building their family, rather than worrying about how to pay for the costs.

"This venture will further New Life Agency's mission to ensure the best fertility and maternity medical care in the world, by assisting clients in a completely different way and facilitating more future families," says Taylor.

Patients and intended parents with good to excellent credit can apply for a New Life Fertility Financing loan through LightStream. It is a streamlined, virtually paperless loan process that can provide funds for all assisted reproduction insurance needs, including surrogacy and fertility medications through Walgreens Pharmacies. LightStream loans may be obtained from $5,000 to $100,000 at fixed rates as low as 5.99% APR.* The loan is also unsecured, with no liens or collateral requirements. Upon submission, applicants receive a quick response during business hours. Once approved, funds can be deposited directly into the client's bank account as soon as the same day with no fees, down payment or prepayment penalties.
