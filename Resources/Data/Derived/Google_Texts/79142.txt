Get to the airport early if you’re taking Southwest today: The airline is having computer troubles, causing delays for some flights Sunday morning.

A statement from the company said that as of 10 a.m., fewer than 100 of its 3,600 flights scheduled for today were delayed.

The company is asking passengers to arrive at the airport two hours early to reduce delays.
