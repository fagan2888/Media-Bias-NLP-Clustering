Updated at 7:40 p.m. Sunday:

A pedestrian struck and killed early Saturday while crossing Loop 12 has been identified as 56-year-old Alvin Waites.

Original post at 9:26 a.m. Saturday:

A 56-year-old pedestrian was struck and killed early Saturday in southeast Dallas.

About 12:25 a.m., the man was walking southbound across the 6400 block of Loop 12 when a vehicle traveling eastbound struck him, police said.

He was taken to a hospital, where he later died. Authorities have not released his name because his relatives need to be notified.

The driver of the vehicle that struck him remained at the scene after the crash, police said.
