Panama City, FL---For the last 11 years, the News Herald and the Northwest Florida Daily News have matched up, East vs. West, picking the best senior high school basketball players, and competing in the Halifax All-Star Classic.

For the second straight year, the East has swept the West.

The boy's All-Stars from the News Herald beat the Daily New's finest in a stat-filled event, winning 112-102. It's the fifth consecutive victory for the East.

Bay High's Rayshawn Patterson scored a team-high 21 points on his way to the game's MVP honors, the first ever for a Tornado.

In the girl's game, Chipley's Amanda Paschal powered the East past the West with 29 points in her MVP effort, as they won 85-64.
