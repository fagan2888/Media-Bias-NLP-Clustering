A 39 year old Panama City man is in critical condition following at late Friday night traffic crash on Highway 388 just east of Northwest Florida Beaches Airport near West Bay.

Listed in critical condition at Bay Medical/Sacred Heart is Joseph Aric Guetter.

The Florida Highway Patrol says Guetter was heading east on Highway 388 when he ran off the road and into the trees and crashed into a utility pole.

His Toyota Four Runner overturned and threw him out. He was not wearing his seatbelt.
