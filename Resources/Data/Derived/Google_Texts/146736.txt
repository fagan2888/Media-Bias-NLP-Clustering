Wichita Police are searching for the suspect who robbed a West Wichita gas station late Saturday night.

The robbery happened at the Jump Start gas station located in the 3800 Block of West 21st Street.

Just before 10 p.m., police say a 49-year-man and a 13-year-old boy were shutting down the business when the suspect entered the store through an unlocked rear door.

The victims tell police the suspect was armed with a gun and demanded money. An undisclosed amount of cash was given to the suspect before he took off.

It is not known which direction the suspect left or what form of transportation was used.

There were no injuries reported from this crime.
