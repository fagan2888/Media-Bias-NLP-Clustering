The Sedgwick County Sheriff's Office has identified a woman who died after being hit by a car near Garden Plain.

The accident happened just after 7 a.m. Friday at 311th Street West and 31st Street South, southwest of Garden Plain.

The sheriff's office said 62-year-old Deborah Farrington, a resident of rural Cheney, was walking eastbound on 31st when she was hit by an eastbound car driven by a 17-year-old boy. Farrington died at the scene.

The teen who was driving the car was also identified by the sheriff's office, but his name is being withheld because of his age.

Lt. Lin Dehning said the case is still under investigation.
