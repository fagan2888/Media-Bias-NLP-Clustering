State Court Strikes NYC Rule For Taxi Driver Health Care

Law360, New York (April 14, 2014, 2:43 PM ET) -- A New York court last Tuesday struck down a fare reduction rule created under former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg’s administration to provide health care assistance for taxi drivers to meet requirements of the Affordable Care Act, ruling the city had exceeded its authority.



New York Supreme Court Judge Margaret A. Chan found, in a ruling released on Friday, that the city of New York and the New York City Taxi and Limousine Commission did not have the authority to issue the “Fare Reduction Rule”...
