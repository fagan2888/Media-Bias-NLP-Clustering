Q. Mr. Trump, what will you bring to our country if elected?

A. Hire me and I'll bring things to this country like you never seen! I'll be the best bringer of things.

Q. What will you do to fix the bad partisanship in politics?

A. Hire me and I'll show you partisan politics like you never seen! Democrats and Republicans will love me, and I'll love Democrats and Republicans.

Q. Mr. Trump, you appear to have a very limited vocabulary, do you consider this to be a detriment to this position?

A. Hire me and I'll show you words! I know words like you won't believe! I'll be the best word president ever!

Q. You talk a lot about the great wall you will build to keep immigrants out, even though for the past eight years there has been a net-zero number of immigrants crossing the border.

A. Hire me and I'll create so many new jobs they'll be begging to come across, and my wall will keep them all out!

Q. If you eliminate Obamacare, what will you replace it with?

A. Elect me, and we will have the best health care plan by far! People in Missouri will be able to go into Kansas or Oklahoma and get some of the best care! People in Kansas and Oklahoma will be able to come into Missouri and get some of the best care! I'll be the best health care president the country has ever seen!

Q. Mr. Trump, what type of energy policy will you promote?

A. Oh, I know energy, I'll show you energy! I'll be the best energy president you will ever know!

Thank you for your time, Mr. Trump.
