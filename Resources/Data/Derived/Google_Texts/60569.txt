A 19-year-old man lost his life after an accident in Berkeley County.

The Berkeley County Coroner's Office says 19-year-old Travis Kyle Jackson of Moncks Corner died following an accident on Saturday.

Authorities say Jackson was traveling on Highway 402 near 3 Mile Road when his Jeep ran off the roadway and struck a tree.

Jackson was wearing a seatbelt, according to the coroner's office.

His death has been ruled accidental and was investigated by the coroner's office and the South Carolina Highway Patrol.
