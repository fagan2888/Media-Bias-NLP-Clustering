A double shooting Friday in Kansas City, Kansas, on I-70 is now a homicide investigation.

Police officers were called to I-70 and I-670 about 5:15 p.m. and found two men in a black Camaro suffering from gunshot wounds.

Both victims were rushed to hospitals. One of the victim's died from his injuries. Police identified him as Deonta C. Jones, 22.

Detectives released a picture of a maroon GMC Envoy they said may be connected to the shooting. Authorities said the Envoy is believed to be a 2010-2014 model with a dark window tint and a yellowed plastic license plate cover.
