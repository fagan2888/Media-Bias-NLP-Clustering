GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. – A worldwide event is, for the fifth year in a row, making its way to Grand Rapids.

Ladyfest GR will be in town on April 2nd from noon to midnight at various different locations throughout the Heartside Neighborhood.

Workshops are free and will cover a variety of topics. Anyone who purchases a wristband for just $10 at any of the venues can attend.

For more information, visit the event’s website ladyfestgr.com
