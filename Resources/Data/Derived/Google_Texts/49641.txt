KANSAS CITY, Kan. - People in KCK and Wyandotte County will have a chance to get help signing up for health care insurance.

Enroll Wyandotte is hosting a Health Fair and Enrollment event on Saturday in an effort to help residents enroll through the health insurance marketplace.

Volunteers will be answering questions in both English and Spanish to help residents navigate the health insurance marketplace and understand the Affordable Care Act.

Screening services including blood pressure, body mass index, and blood sugar checks are also available.

The event, located at Memorial Hall at 600 N. Seventh St. in KCK, is open to the public, and child care will be provided.

The workshop continues until 5 p.m. Saturday.

The deadline for individual enrollment is Feb. 15, 2015.
