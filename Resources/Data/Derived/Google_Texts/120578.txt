A couple was found shot to death in an Algonquin home early Saturday, police said.

A man and woman were discovered by police responding to a shooting call around 4:20 a.m. Saturday in the 1800 block of West Hill Street, according to Louisville Metro Police spokeswoman Office Carey Klain.

MORE CRIME NEWS

Aaron Combs, 30, and Julie Clemons, 28, were pronounced dead on the scene of gunshot wounds, said chief deputy coroner Jo-Ann Farmer Saturday afternoon.

Combs and Clemons were a couple and lived in the home, Farmer said.

The Homicide Unit is investigating the deaths as a double homicide, but no suspect information was available as of Saturday afternoon.
