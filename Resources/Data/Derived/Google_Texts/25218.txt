One man was arrested in connection with a stabbing that killed a Cushing man, Friday night.

According to the Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation, 36-year-old Nathaniel Becenti was stabbed to death around 8 p.m. at his home in the 1200 block of E. Pecan.

Authorities said 21-year-old Noel Bryan was arrested overnight on a murder complaint and taken to the Cushing City Jail, where he is waiting to be transported to the Payne County Detention Center.

Agents said Becenti and Bryan were acquaintances.
