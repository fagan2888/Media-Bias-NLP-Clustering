Teresa Marie Zebrowski, of Boulder, and Roshan Dahal, of Biratnagar, Nepal, were married on July 16, 2014, in Bhaktapur, Nepal.

Teresa is the daughter of Charlie Zebrowski and Francis Zebrowski, of Fort Myers, Florida. Roshan is the son of Sharada Dahal and Damber Bahadur, of Biratnagar, Nepal.

The couple plan to honeymoon in Pokhara and Bandipur, Nepal.
