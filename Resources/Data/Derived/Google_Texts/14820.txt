It looks ever more likely that the world's largest tech IPO since Facebook will have to wait until next year. China's Alibaba, an online trading portal, expects to raise at least $10 billion when it lists, but it is struggling to figure out where it will sell its shares. New York and Hong Kong are the main competitors.
