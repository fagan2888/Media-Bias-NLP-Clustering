Former CBS News investigative reporter Sharyl Attkisson, a senior independent contributor to The Daily Signal, spoke to Fox News’ Bill O’Reilly tonight about her new book, “Stonewalled.” Attkisson revealed details about how her computer was hacked and why she decided to leave CBS News after 20 years at the network.
