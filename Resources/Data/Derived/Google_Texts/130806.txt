Tens of thousands of visitors poured into Belmar Friday through Sunday to sample seafood at the 28th annual New Jersey Seafood Festival, held Friday through Sunday in Silver Lake Park.

Food at the festival ranges from the traditional — shrimp, lobster and calamari — to the not-so-traditional — alligator sausage and seafood paella. Cajun, shrimp-on-a-stick and coconut shrimp, crab cakes and stuffed crab are some of the festival's most popular draws.
