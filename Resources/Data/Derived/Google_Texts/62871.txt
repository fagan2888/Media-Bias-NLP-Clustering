Hays police responded to 229 West 10th Street in an alley behind a nightclub called The Home.

Upon arrival they found a 20-year-old man had been stabbed in the chest. Investigation revealed that the stabbing occurred during a brief altercation inside of the nightclub.

The victim was transported to a local hospital to be treated for his injuries.

There are no suspects, and the investigation continues.

Police ask that if anyone has information in reference to this incident they are asked to contact Investigator Jeff Ridgway at (785) 625-1011 or email him at jridgway@haysusa.com
