7 Criticisms Of Medicare's Epic Nursing Home Rule

Law360, New York (October 19, 2015, 7:57 PM ET) -- A 400-page proposal to overhaul nursing home regulations under Medicare and Medicaid for the first time in a quarter-century is attracting extensive criticism from health care providers concerned about vague policies and potential perks for the plaintiffs bar.



The pushback was contained in correspondence posted online after last week's deadline to comment on a regulation published in July by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services. More than 9,000 comments were submitted, and they expressed a mix of support, concern and outright hostility.
