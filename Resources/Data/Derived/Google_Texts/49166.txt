Ted Cruz has a tongue-in-cheek ad out just in time for Christmas.

The Texas senator, seated with his wife and two daughters, highlights his colorful time so far in Congress by announcing the debut of some of his new books, "Told by the senator who once read 'Green Eggs and Ham' on the Senate floor."

His books include "How Obamacare Stole Christmas," and "Rudolph, The Underemployed Reindeer."
