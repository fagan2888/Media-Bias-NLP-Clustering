Lykke Li performs onstage during day 1 of the 2015 Coachella Valley Music & Arts Festival (Weekend 1) at the Empire Polo Club on April 10, 2015 in Indio, California.

Swedish indie-pop act Lykke Li delivered an entrancing set on Friday night (April 10) at the 2015 Coachella Valley Music & Arts Festival, the artist's first show since cancelling her last tour and going on hiatus in January.

Full Coachella Coverage

A highlight of her Coachella set was her cover of Drake's "Hold On, We're Goin' On," which she's been performing during her European tour.

Lykke Li stopped by the Billboard studio at Coachella to discuss her appreciation of Drake, learning how to get over insomnia by playing tennis, the direction of her new music, and much more. Watch the interview in the video below.
