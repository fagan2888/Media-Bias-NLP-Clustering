UPDATE: After a thorough search of the parking garage, Phoenix police reportedly found no evidence of the suspect, the weapon, or the suspect’s vehicle.

PHOENIX — KTAR.com received news Sunday afternoon that Phoenix police have closed off a parking structure near 12th Street and Willetta as they search for a suspect reportedly wielding a shotgun.

According to police, officers received information that an armed suspect in a white pickup truck was seen in the parking garage near Banner Good Samaritan Medical Center.

Officers are currently going through the garage looking for anyone matching the description.
