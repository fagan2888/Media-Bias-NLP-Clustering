New information is being released Monday in a death investigation in Estherville.

Thomas Bortvit, 19, of Estherville, was first reported missing and later found dead.

Officials reported that Lee Samuel Christensen, 18, of Estherville, was arrested Sunday night and charged with first-degree murder in the case.

Christensen is accused of using a firearm to kill Bortvit on Saturday in rural Emmet County.

Christensen was taken into custody in Minneapolis and taken to the Hennepin County Jail, where he is awaiting extradition back to Iowa.

The homicide investigation continues.
