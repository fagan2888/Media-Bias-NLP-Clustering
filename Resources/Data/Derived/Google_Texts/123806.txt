Wally Hudnall died Friday night. Hudnall was a long-time, well-known member of the community.

Hudnall has run for mayor a few times. The last was the 2013 election as an independent candidate. He's run for several other offices over the years. Last year, Mayor Percy Bland appointed Hudnall to serve as Ward Five's Civil Service Commissioner. Hudnall had been fight ALS since 2012.

Mayor Percy Bland released this statement:

“Wally Hudnall passed away last night with Dianne at his side, our thoughts and prayers are with her and their family. Wally's love for the citizens of Meridian and her citizens was profound and unwavering and we appreciate his service as a Civil Service Commissioner, he was a true civil servant and friend to us all.”

-Mayor Percy Bland
