The atrocities committed across South Sudan since December have ranged from mass killings and rape to the murder of hospital patients lying in their beds. With civil war engulfing the country, farmers have been unable to plant crops, raising the specter of famine. U.N. Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon warned last month that by the end of the year, half the country's 12 million people could be either fleeing, starving or dead.
