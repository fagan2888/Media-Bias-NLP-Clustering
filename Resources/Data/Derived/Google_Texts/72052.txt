December is arriving in a rush, ushered in by a chilly November. Thanksgiving is upon us with Christmas close on its heels.



The following schools are presenting their Christmas programs shortly after returning from the Thanksgiving break.



Tuesday, Dec. 2, 6 p.m. Mary Beth King will present Burlington Elementary School students in a fantastic Christmas concert filled with familiar favorites and some fun new songs to move to.



The concert will take place at Burlington High School.



Liberty School

Liberty School’s Christmas music program is slated for Wednesday, Dec. 3, from 6:30 to 8:30 p.m.



Everyone from PreK through the 12th grade has been working on different songs and musical presentations. Some numbers are just vocal and others are instrumental.
