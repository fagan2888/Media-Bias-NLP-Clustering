LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- Humana has several hundred temporary jobs available.



The company is looking for nearly 600 enrollment specialists.



The employees would be responsible for reviewing and processing Humana Individual and Medicare Advantage member applications, responding to inquiries and reconciling enrollment information.



Employees will work for Humana for six months or less and will be based in downtown Louisville.



Start dates for the employees will range between September 14 and go through the end of October.
