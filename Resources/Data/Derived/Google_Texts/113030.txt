A local woman is dead after a traffic crash around 8 p.m. on Christmas Eve.

Panama City Police say Amber Martinez, 43, of Panama City, was driving south on Martin Luther King, Jr. Blvd. when Amanda Mincy, 51, of Panama City, walked out in front of her vehicle.

Police say Mincy was trying to cross the street from the Cannon Oil Mini Mart when she was hit.

Mincy died from her injuries.

Police are still investigating the crash. If you have any information about what happened, you're asked to contact the Panama City Police Department at (850) 872-3112.
