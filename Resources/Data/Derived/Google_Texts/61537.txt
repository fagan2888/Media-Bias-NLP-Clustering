Pasco County sheriff’s deputies are investigating after a woman was found dead this morning along a Dade City road.

The body of Naomi Jean Robbins, 36, was found on Ruffing Road near the intersection of Ramsey Road.

Deputies received a call about 11:30 a.m. that a body was found.

Detectives found no obvious signs of trauma, and the cause of death will be determined by the medical examiner.

Deputies are asking for the public’s help to determine Robbins whereabouts for the past day. Anyone with any information is asked to contact the sheriff’s office Crime Tips Line at 1-800-706-2488
