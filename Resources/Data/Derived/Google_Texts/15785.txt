Wichita hospitals announcing the arrivals of the first babies of 2016.

The first baby born at Wesley Hospitals, a baby boy who arrived at 12:46 Friday morning.

Sydney Clark gave birth to her daughter, Mackenzie, around 7:30 Friday morning at the Via Christi NewLife Center.

This is Sydney's second child, but the first time she's gone through a natural childbirth. She said the process left her exhausted.

Mackenzie weighed in at five pounds, eleven ounces. Mom and baby are expected to go home later today.
