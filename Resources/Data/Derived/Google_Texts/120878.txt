The Broncos will wear their alternate all-blue uniforms for their primetime game against the San Francisco 49ers on Sunday at Sports Authority Field at Mile High.

The game will be the Broncos’ 13th in the “throwback” uniforms; they own a 8-4 record with the all-navy look.

PHOTOS: Broncos wearing blue jerseys with blue pants

The blue uniforms were the team’s predominant home jersey from 1997-2011, before they returned to the traditional orange as their primary color in 2012.

The Broncos will revert back to the orange uniforms for their remaning four home games.
