Donald Trump told the National Rifle Association on Friday that “the Second Amendment is on the ballot in November” and that Hillary Clinton “wants to abolish the Second Amendment.” A Journal editorial says he’s right. The political left has long held that the right to bear arms belongs only to a “well regulated Militia.” And Supreme Court liberals keep voting against decisions that recognize an individual’s right to own a gun. A Clinton appointee would provide the critical fifth vote to eviscerate this constitutional right....
