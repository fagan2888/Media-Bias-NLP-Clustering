OSBI agents are assisting the Anadarko Police with a homicide.

Just before 1:00 p.m. Friday, Anadarko Police responded to a report of a body found inside a home at 510 E. Georgia.

Upon arrival, officers found a deceased woman and an injured man. The man was transported by med-flight to an Oklahoma City hospital.

Police requested the assistance of the OSBI to investigate the crime scene.

Names of the victims have not been released at this time.
