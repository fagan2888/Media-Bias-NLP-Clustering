BRECKENRIDGE — A Frisco snowboarder died last week after he struck a tree.

David Carr, 32, was on an intermediate run near Claimjumper at the time of the accident on Wednesday. Summit County Coroner Regan Wood said he wore a helmet.

The crash was the second fatal ski accident of the week. On Monday, John Sherwood of Randolph, N.J., died after he collided with another skier. The Summit County Sheriff’s Office is looking for anyone who might have witnessed the hit-and-run collision between the skiers.
