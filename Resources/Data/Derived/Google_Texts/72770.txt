Monument Police Department is investigating after a male suspect robbed a hair salon at gunpoint Sunday evening, officials said.

Around 4:35 p.m., police said a male suspect entered Great Clips on Highway 105 at 574 W. Highway 105.

Lt. Steve Burk said the man was in the shop for a few minutes before brandishing a hand gun, robbing the store, possibly taking items from customers and fleeing.

No one was hurt, Burk said.

The suspect is described as being bald with a goatee.

Monument police remain on scene.
