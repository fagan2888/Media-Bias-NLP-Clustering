The mayor of York confirms a City Council member in York was killed Saturday evening.

The York Police Department says Ecoles Cook was shot multiple times Saturday at his home. YPD Communications Officer Alicia Washington says Cook's wife called police at about 7:50 p.m. Washington says they don't know yet who the shooter was or why this happened.

Mayor Gena Robbins says Cook died from his injuries. Mayor Robbins is asking for the community's prayers for the family during this time.
