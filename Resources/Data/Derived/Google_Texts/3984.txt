Loveland police are searching for two male suspects accused of breaking into a woman’s home, trying to take items and hitting the woman with a baseball bat as they fled, officials said.

Two unidentified males allegedly forced their way into a 20-year-old woman’s home at 510 2nd Street SE, according to a Loveland Police news release.

The woman confronted and chased the suspects while they were taking items from her home. On their way out, the suspects struck the woman with a baseball bat before fleeing in a dark-colored pickup truck, the release said.

Detectives are now working to follow up on leads to identify the suspects. Anyone with information about the investigation is asked to call Detective Kevin Havel at 970-962-2244 or Crime Stoppers of Larimer County at 970-221-6868.
