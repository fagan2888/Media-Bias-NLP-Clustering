Man stood behind girl in line at Batman ride

Ronnie U. Lacy is accused of criminal sexual contact against a girl in line at Six Flags (Photo: Courtesy of Ocean County Prosecutor's Office)

A Manhattan man is accused of committing an act of criminal sexual contact against a 13-year-old girl as he stood behind her in line for the Batman ride at Six Flax Great Adventure.

At about 10 p.m. Sunday, police arrested Ronnie U. Lacy, 21, charging him with criminal sexual contact and endangering the welfare of a child, according to a release from Ocean County Prosecutor's Office. Witnesses and the girl reported to police that the alleged crime happened at about 4:45 p.m. Sunday.

Lacy was taken to Ocean County jail with a $100,000 bail and no option to pay 10 percent.
