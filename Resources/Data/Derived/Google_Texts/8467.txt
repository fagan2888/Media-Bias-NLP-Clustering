Lynn Haven, FL --- Saturday at Mosley some of the best high school wrestlers in the area competed in the finals of the Panhandle Championships.

Two locals competing in the 113 weight class final, Chance Layfield of Bay facing Garrett Jones of Arnold.

Layfield, able to control the pace of the match and take the title via points.

But Arnold found a great deal of success on the day including at the 120 weight class where Richie McClannahan dominated. The freshman able to own the match against Dy'Juan Carney of Wakulla. McClannahan avoided a late potential pin fall though to win via points as well.

Then, Arnold took home another title, this time in the 126 weight class as Brock Carlan defeted Nick Stavinski of Gulf Breeze.
