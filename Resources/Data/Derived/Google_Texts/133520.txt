Fleenor, Geneva Elizabeth Bolling King

Geneva Elizabeth Bolling King Fleenor, 83, went to her Heavenly home on Tuesday, May 24, 2016. She was preceded in death by her parents, Issac and Audrey Conner Bolling; sister, Joyce Huddleston Roden; niece, Sherry Roden Ferguson; and special friend, Paul Jarvis. Geneva was born in Roanoke and was a lifetime member of the First Church of the Nazarene where she sang in the choir, and worked with children and women's ministries. She was an artist who loved to give her art away to family and friends. A highlight of her life was traveling with Linkous Tours to the Southern Gospel Conventions. She was a beloved mother and grandmother, and she will be missed dearly.She is survived by her son, Harry C. King Jr (Sonny); and daughter, Glenna King Dunn (Mike); grandchildren, Catina Wright (Jammie), Chad Dunn (Colby), Sonya Mullins, Suni Nicole Hefflen (Cameron), Apryl Dunn Butler, Jessica Tolley (Brian); 14 great grandchildren; niece, Debbie Bobbitt; and nephews, Eddie and Walter Roden; best friend, Ruby Huffman; and numerous nieces and nephews.The family wishes to thank Good Samaritan Hospice and especially Sharon, Cary, and Kathy for their assistance in her care.A celebration of life will be at 11 a.m. held on Friday, May 27, 2016 at First Church of the Nazarene, 8th and Highland Ave. SE, Roanoke, Va. followed by a luncheon, visitation on hour prior to service.Ms. Fleenor is entrusted to Valley Funeral Home.
