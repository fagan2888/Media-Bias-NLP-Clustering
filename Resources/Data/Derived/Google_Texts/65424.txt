Health Co. Execs In Criminal Crosshairs, DOJ Atty Says

Law360, New York (July 2, 2014, 6:48 PM ET) -- Prosecutors are increasingly looking to hit large corporations and their executives with criminal health fraud charges that so far have mostly been brought against small businesses, a top official in the U.S. Department of Justice said Wednesday.



The comments were among many insights that government officials shared about their enforcement priorities on the final day of the American Health Lawyers Association’s annual meeting — here, Law360 explores five takeaways.
