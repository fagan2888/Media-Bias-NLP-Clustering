Police responded to a call of a cutting in the 2000 block of W. McCormick just after 12 a.m. Saturday morning.

"Upon arrival, the officers located the victim in front of his apartment with multiple stab wounds", Sgt. Keith Fort said.

The victim, a 48-year-old man, was taken to St. Francis in critical condition with stab wounds to the upper torso and is currently undergoing surgery.

Wichita Police have a person of interest, a male suspect, who they are currently trying to locate. The relationship between the victim and the suspect is unknown.

There were several witnesses in the area at the time who are currently being interviewed by police as they continue to investigate.
