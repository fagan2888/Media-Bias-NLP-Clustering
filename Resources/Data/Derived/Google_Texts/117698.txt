(TRUNEWS) A secretive ‘John Doe’ is at the center of the illegal hacking and leak of the infamous ‘Panama Papers’ from the law firm Mossack-Fonseca. The dumping of over 11.5 million documents has resulted in a world-wide domino-like effect, impacting foreign leaders and high profile wealth-builders.

London’s Independent newspaper is reporting that the mysterious individual contacted German reporters in 2014 with a mysterious online message:

“Hello. This is John Doe. Interested in data?”

The anonymous source then began the process of off-loading terabytes of confidential data, including corporate agreements, internal company emails, private lawyer-client communications, and even copies of passports.

Among the thousands of people named in the documents are former Iceland Prime Minister Gunnlaugsson, Ukrainian President Petro Poroshenko, King Salman of Saudi Arabia, Chinese President Xi Jinping’s family members, close associates of Russian President Vladi­mir Putin, British Prime Minister David Cameron’s late father, Ian, and soccer superstar Lionel Messi.
