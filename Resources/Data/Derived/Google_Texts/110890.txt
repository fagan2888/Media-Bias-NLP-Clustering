Longmont police on Friday responded to the area of 4505 Redmond Drive on the report of a physical domestic violence incident occurring between a male and a female. The man fled the scene, prior to police responding. He was later contacted and arrested for domestic violence charges. The case was cleared by arrest.

At about 10:30 p.m. Thursday, Longmont police officers made an arrest in the 400 block of Main Street in reference to a DUI investigation. The driver was later released on a summons.

A Frederick resident on Friday reported her silver 2002 Nissan Xterra missing from the 2200 block of Main Street between 1:30 and 10 a.m. The vehicle was later located in the 3800 block of Colo. 119 in Weld County. The case is closed.

Just before 2 a.m. Fri day, Longmont police conducted a traffic stop in the 1200 block of Alpine Street One man was arrested for DUI-related charges without incident.

Longmont police contacted a vehicle on Friday for a traffic violation in the area of Pace Street and Whitehall Drive. The man driving the vehicle was found to have an active warrant for his arrest, and to be in possession of a quantity of narcotics. The driver was taken into custody without incident, and transported to the Boulder County Jail.

The report of a disturbance brought Longmont police at about 3:40 a.m. Friday to the 600 block of Elliott Street. One woman was arrested on an outstanding warrant without incident.
