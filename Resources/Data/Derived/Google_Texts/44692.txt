HHS Home Health Authorization Rule Survives Industry Protest

Law360, New York (November 3, 2015, 6:13 PM ET) -- The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services can require physicians to submit a detailed narrative about why they are authorizing Medicare patients for home health care services without overstepping its authority under the Affordable Care Act, a D.C. federal judge ruled Tuesday.
