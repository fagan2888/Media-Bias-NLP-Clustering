ABERDEEN, S.D. - Tyler Bossert, a standout hockey player for the Fargo South-Shanley high school team, has signed a contract with the Aberdeen (S.D.) Wings of the North American Hockey League.

Bossert, a 5-foot-8, 170-pound forward, scored 23 goals and had 28 assists his last season with Fargo South-Shanley. The Wings play in the NAHL, a Tier II junior league.
