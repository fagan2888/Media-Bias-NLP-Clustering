Dave & Buster’s Can’t Duck Obamacare ERISA Class Action

Law360, New York (February 9, 2016, 5:10 PM ET) -- A New York federal judge on Tuesday said amusement chain Dave & Buster’s can’t duck a proposed class action over claims it slashed employees’ hours to avoid increased health care costs stemming from Obamacare, saying the employees have a case under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act.
