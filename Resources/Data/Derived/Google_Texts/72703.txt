Police have identified an elderly woman who was found dead inside her southwest Oklahoma City home.

According to police, at about 5:30 p.m. Monday, officers responded to a welfare check at a home in the 3100 block of S.W. 54th Street. When they arrived, they found an elderly woman dead inside the home.

Police said the woman, later identified as 82-year-old Ikuko George, had trauma to her body consistent with homicide. No arrests have been made and police do not have a suspect description at this time.

If you have any information, call the homicide tip-line at (405)-297-1200.
