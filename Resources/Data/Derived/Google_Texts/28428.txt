Season ends at Longmont's Sunset Pool

Brody Emler, 9, of Longmont, splashes down the water slide on Labor Day 2013 at Sunset Pool in Longmont. (Matthew Jonas/Times-Call)





Julien Mauldin of Longmont splashes down the water slide on Monday, Sept. 2, 2013, at Sunset Pool in Longmont. It was the last day of the season for the city pool. (Matthew Jonas/Times-Call)











LONGMONT -- Hundreds of residents spent at least part of their Labor Day at Sunset Pool. Pool employees estimated that about 600 swimmers were enjoying the pool as it neared its 5 p.m. closing.
