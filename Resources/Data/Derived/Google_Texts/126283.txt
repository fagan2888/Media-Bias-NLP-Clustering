State Route 83 has reopened about 10 miles south of Interstate 10 following a motorcycle crash at milepost 48, according to the Arizona Department of Transportation.

A spokesperson from the Arizona Department of Public Safety confirms there was a fatality in the wreck.

Details are still scant, but a Tucson News Now crew is en route.
