A man is in critical condition after suffering a medical condition at a Wichita State University softball game.

The incident happened just before 1 p.m. Sunday at WSU's C. Howard Wilkins Softball Facility. A WSU official said the man had suffered a heart attack. Dispatchers originally reported the man fell from the bleachers.

EMS rushed the man to a nearby hospital.

The Wichita State softball match-up with St. Gregory got underway shortly after the incident.
