Two men are behind bars, each with bond set at $2 million, for multiple drug offenses.

Williams (left), Alexander (right)

Davarus Williams, 29, and James Alexander, 45, were booked in the Pitt County Detention Center Friday.

Officers at the jail say the arrests were made by the Greenville Regional Drug Task Force.

Wiliams is from Grimesland. He faces 10 felony charges for trafficking opium or heroin, conspiring to traffic opium or heroin, and eluding arrest.

Alexander is from Greenville. He's facing 5 felony charges, which also include trafficking opium or heroin and conspiring to traffic opium or heroin.

It isn't known at this time if the two drug cases are related. Both men are scheduled to appear in court August 5.
