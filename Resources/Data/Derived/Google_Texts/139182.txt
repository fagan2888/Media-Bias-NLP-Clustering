Police are searching for a suspect who attacked a woman in the parking lot of a fast food restaurant in west Wichita.

It happened around 6:45 p.m. Friday outside the Wendy's near Central and West Street.

Police say the 21-year-old victim was sitting in her car when a man opened the passenger door and got in. He then pointed a handgun at the woman and demanded money.

The victim was able to use her stun gun on the suspect. The two struggled until the suspect got out of the car and ran.

The victim suffered a broken nose.

Police did not provide a description of the suspect.
