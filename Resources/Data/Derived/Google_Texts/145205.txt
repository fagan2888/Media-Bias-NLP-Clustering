Qualcomm Inc. promoted its No. 2 executive to the top job at the chip maker, a surprise move whose timing may have been influenced by the CEO search at Microsoft Corp.

The San Diego-based company said Steve Mollenkopf, Qualcomm's 44-year-old president and chief operating officer, was chosen Thursday as its next chief executive and will assume the title in March. He will succeed Paul Jacobs, 51, who has led the chip maker since 2005...
