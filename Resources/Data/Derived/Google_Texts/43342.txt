Sarah Wood and Jason Kirk were married during a poolside wedding on the evening of May 3, 2014, at the Aria Resort in Las Vegas, Nevada, with reception following. The couple will travel to the Caribbean in August for their honeymoon.

Sarah is the daughter of Sam and Barbara Wood of Boulder. Jason is the son of Mark Kirk, of Reno, Neveda, and Nicolle Alida, of Seattle, Washington.

The couple are graduates of the University of Colorado-Boulder. Sarah is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in nursing at Regis University's Loretto Heights School of Nursing. She expects to graduate in December 2014. Jason works as an accountant in the oil and gas industry. The couple live in Lakewood.
