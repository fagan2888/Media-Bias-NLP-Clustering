Tucson police are investigating after a car crashed into two motorcycles on Catalina Highway just north of Tanque Verde Road. The accident happened around 3:30 p.m. Sunday.

According to Sgt. Pete Dugan, a vehicle pulled out of a private drive and hit the two motorcycles.

One motorcycle had a passenger. In total, three people were taken to the hospital. All three have serious non-life-threatening injuries.

Catalina Highway between E. Dawn Post Road and E. Tanque Verde Road was closed and has now reopened.
