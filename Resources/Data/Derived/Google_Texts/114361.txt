Genesee hopes to hire at least 25 people for various positions. A spokesman tells us some of them will be seasonal but could turn into full-time jobs. He says it's all due to an increase in production demands.

“The beer business is seasonal. People like to drink during the summer when the weather is warm so we're gearing up for that. And then we have some great new products that are doing well on the market,” said Harvey Beldner, North American Breweries.
