The golf movie “The Squeeze” is scheduled to make its Denver premiere Monday as a fundraiser for local chapter of The First Tee youth golf organization.

Actor Chris McDonald and the film’s writer and director, Terry Jastrow, will conduct a question-and-answer session following the movie’s 6:30 p.m. showing at Wellshire Golf Course and Event Center.

McDonald’s acting credits include “Requiem for a Dream” and “Thelma and Louise.” Jastrow is a seven-time Emmy winner for his work on “Wide World of Sports” and Olympic Games coverage.

Registration — $85 for individuals and $300 for a group of four — and can be accessed at www.TheFirstTeeofDenver.com. Seating is limited. Doors open at 5:30 p.m. with a reception.
