The accident happened just after 1 a.m. near the I-135 and I-235 interchange in South Wichita.

The driver was inside a car that lost control and ended up in the ditch.

"We've got a witness that the vehicle was possibly traveling at a high rate of speed, passed him and then lost control and then struck the guardrail," said Trooper Paul Perez, Kansas Highway Patrol.

The individual was transported to a local hospital with serious injuries.
