Havelock-native and former WITN Pepsi Player of the Week Pharoh Cooper hauled in a school record 233 receiving yards Saturday night in South Carolina's 45-42 overtime loss to Tennessee.

Cooper also ran for a touchdown and threw for another in the shootout loss to the Vols. His 256 all purpose yards were the most for a Gamecock this season. South Carolina amassed 625 total yards in the loss.

The Gamecocks dropped to 4-5 on the season. They'll travel to Florida on November 15th.
