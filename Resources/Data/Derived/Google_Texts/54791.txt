The Quad Cities will be getting visits from the Vice President, the U.S. Speaker of the House, and a former First Lady.

On Monday, October 27, 2014 Vice President Joe Biden was scheduled to be in Davenport promoting early voting.

Biden was set to attend an event at Modern Woodmen Park at 10 a.m. Congressmen Bruce Braley and Dave Loebsack were also scheduled to attend.

After his stop in the QC, Biden will head to Rockford, Illinois for an event with Congresswoman Cheri Bustos.

Also visiting the QC Monday will be U.S. Speaker of the House John Boehner. Boehner was scheduled to stop by the Scott County GOP Office across from NorthPark Mall at 5:15 in support of Dr. Mariannette Miller-Meeks.

On Wednesday, October 29, Hillary Clinton was set to join Bruce Braley at a campaign event in Cedar Rapids and Davenport.
