One person was killed and a child was ejected in a rollover accident near Goodyear early Sunday morning, Arizona Department of Public Safety trooper said.

The victim in the crash was the driver of the vehicle, the DPS duty office said.

No names were provided this early in the investigation.

The rollover happened in the eastbound lanes.

Troopers said two other people were also in the vehicle and reportedly trapped for a time. Their conditions aren’t known.

No other information was available.
