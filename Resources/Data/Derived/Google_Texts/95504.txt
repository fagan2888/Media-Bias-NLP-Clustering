Kayla Jones scored 26 points and pulled down 10 rebounds in a 67-47

Eastern Regional Final victory over Rosewood to send the Knights to the girls 1A State title game in Chapel Hill.

Remi Simmions scored 16 points and Takwana Bland added 18 points for the Knights in the win. Riverside jumped out to a 16 point first period lead and never looked back.

Lexi Mercer scored 23 points for Rosewood. She was the only Eagles player to score in double figures.
