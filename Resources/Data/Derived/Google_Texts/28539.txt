Sioux Falls, SD

More than 1,000 runners laced up their sneakers and braved the very cold weather for the St. Patty's Day Mile. The three races kicked off under the arch at Falls Park throughout the day.

The runner who breaks the course recorded in the five mile, 5K, and Irishman wins up to $2,000.

The Team Competition winners have $200 donated to the charity of their choice.
