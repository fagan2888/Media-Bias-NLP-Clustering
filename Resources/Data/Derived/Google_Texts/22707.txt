Panama City Beach Police responded to shots being fired Saturday morning at a home on West Gulf Boulevard.

When they arrived on scene, they arrested 17-year-old Dyllon Alexander Combs.

According to reports, Combs and his estranged wife, 20-year-old Elizabeth Menzie, got into an altercation involving a car.

Menzie, and three other people went over to Combs home to get the car. When they got to the home they confronted Combs about the car and that is when they began to argue.

Reports state, Menzie and her friends left the home, and Combs came outside and allegedly fired four shots.

Police were in the area, heard the shots, and responded immediately.

Combs was arrested for three counts of aggravated assault with a deadly weapon. He has been booked in the Bay County Jail.
