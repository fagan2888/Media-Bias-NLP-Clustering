The Rev. C.J. Bell will be honored at 4 p.m. Saturday at St. Matthew Baptist Church, 5605 Old Boyce Road in the Rapides Station area. (Photo: Town Talk file photo)

The public is invited to a reception honoring the Rev. C.J. Bell, the founder of the Alexandria News Weekly, at 4 p.m. Saturday at St. Matthew Baptist Church, 5605 Old Boyce Road in the Rapides Station area.

The Dr. Joe S. Green Leadership Institute will host the reception.

Bell will be honored for the work he did through the newspaper, which primarily serves the black community.

Various dignitaries will be on hand for the reception.
