On June 2, Heartland Institute Science Director Jay Lehr was on on “Your World with Neil Cavuto” on Fox News Channel. Cavuto asked Lehr to come on the show to discuss the Obama administration’s new power plant emissions rule. The administration instituted the arbitrary rule to meet the president’s goal to ensure energy bills “necessarily skyrocket.”

As Lehr said to end the segment … “what are they smoking?”
