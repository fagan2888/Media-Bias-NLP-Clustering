Emergency crews shut down parts of northbound Interstate 35 in Norman due to a semi-truck crash Friday morning.

A semi-truck reportedly hit the concrete barricade and rolled over off the roadway. The northbound I-35 off-ramp to eastbound SH-9 in Norman will remain closed through Friday evening for ongoing clean-up of the accident, according to the Oklahoma Department of Transportation.

ODOT officials said the right lane of northbound I-35 will remain closed at the State Highway 9 interchange in Norman, until 1 p.m. Friday.

Authorities encourage drivers to look for an alternate route or plan for heavy congestion.

No injuries have been reported at this time. Stay with us for updates.
