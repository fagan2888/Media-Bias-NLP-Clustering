According to the Florida Highway Patrol, one person is dead after a car crash involving a motorcycle.

Officials with FHP said it happened around 9:40 p.m. on Copper Lane in the Holt Community.

The driver of the car was heading east on US 90 on Copper Lane in the Holt community. The driver hit the motorcyclist head on. Officials said the driver, 21-year-old Alvaro Moreno-Colorado, drove for another 500 feet beyond the initial point of impact before stopping.

Joey Lock, the driver of the motorcycle, was pronounced dead at the scene. The driver that hit him was taken to the North Okaloosa Medical Center and treated for minor injuries.

After being released from the hospital he was arrested for DUI manslaughter and driving without a valid license.
