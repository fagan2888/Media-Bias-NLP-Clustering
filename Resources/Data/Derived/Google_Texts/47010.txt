Athens Montessori School invites Athens and surrounding communities to join its annual fall festival on Saturday from 10 a.m. until 3 p.m.

There are many activities planned including music, magic shows, a petting zoo, book fair, cake walks, bake sale, moonwalks, arts and craft vendors from across the state and loads of kids crafts.

Admission to the festival is free and free parking is available at on the AMS campus as well as next door at St. Gregory’s Episcopal Church. AMS is located at 3145 Barnett Shoals Road.

For more information regarding the festival, email Laura Gibson at lauraegibson@gmail.com.
