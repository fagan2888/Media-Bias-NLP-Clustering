New Mexico State Police responded to a fatal vehicle crash near Gallup Friday.

NMSP say Harry Brown, 85, from Shonto, AZ, was traveling eastbound on State Road 264 when he lost control of his vehicle. The vehicle exited the roadway and hit the side of a hill.

According to NMSP, Brown sustained fatal injuries as a result of not wearing his seatbelt and was ejected from the vehicle.

Brown was pronounced dead at the scene, NMSP say.

Police are still investigating the scene of the crash.
