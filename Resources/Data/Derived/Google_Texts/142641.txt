BOULDER, Colo. (AP) — Boulder County search teams are looking for an overdue hiker in Eldorado Canyon State Park.

The Sheriff’s Department says 53-year-old David Robles of Westminster failed to return from a hike in the park on Saturday.

Crews resumed the search on Monday after hunting for Robles Saturday night and Sunday.

Robles’ car was found in the park but investigators say they have no other leads.
