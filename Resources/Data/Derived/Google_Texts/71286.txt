I have great respect for Floyd Abrams’s First Amendment advocacy, but he is wrong when he asserts in his Nov. 11 letter that requiring donor disclosure by nonprofits isn’t “at odds with the First Amendment.” Forcing such disclosure as a condition of speaking goes far beyond the already stringent campaign disclosure requirements currently in place. It violates fundamental rights of free speech, privacy and association.
