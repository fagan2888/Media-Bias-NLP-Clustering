On Aug. 10 the Des Moines City Council voted in a 6-1 decision to keep Hull Avenue open until construction on the Guthrie viaduct is completed. The permanent closing of Hull Avenue has been a contentious issue all along, but this decision has been encouraging.

This was a matter of public safety because closing of both corridors would have created long detour routes. This council protected the welfare of our east-side neighborhoods by maintaining more direct routes for emergency services and this is truly refreshing.
