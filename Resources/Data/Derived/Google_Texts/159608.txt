How The Candidates Stack Up On Immigration Policy

Law360, New York (November 5, 2015, 7:51 PM ET) -- With the presidential cycle accelerating, Democratic and Republican candidates have spoken about how they would fix the U.S. immigration system, touching on everything from border security to high-skilled immigration. Here, Law360 takes a look at where the candidates stand on immigration issues.



To determine who to include on this list, Law360 chose those candidates who, as of Nov. 4, polled above 3 percent in the RealClearPolitics average of major national polls conducted by organizations such as Pew Research Center, CNN, NBC and Fox News.
