The state fire marshal is investigating the cause of an early morning house fire this weekend in DeFuniak Springs.

The fire was at a home on Hidden Lakes Trail.

Firefighters responded to the fire around 6:30 Sunday morning after they say a witness saw the home engulfed in flames.

According to first responders, the fire caused thousands of dollars in damage.

Firefighters say no one was at home at the time of the fire.

No injuries have been reported.
