Fatal accident on 37th St. N.

Police have identified a man who was killed in a crash early Sunday morning in northeast Wichita.

The single-vehicle accident happened on 37th Street North around 3:30 a.m. Sunday. Police said the car was eastbound on 37th street from Hillside toward Oliver, when the driver lost control on a curve.

The vehicle crossed over onto the other side of the roadway, hit a curb, went into the grass and struck a tree. The driver, the only occupant in the vehicle, was trapped in the vehicle and pronounced dead when emergency crews arrived.

Monday morning, police identified him as 22-year-old Ryan Hamilton of Wichita. Police said speed was a factor in the crash, along with wet roadways.
