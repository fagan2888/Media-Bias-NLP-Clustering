The chairman of the Congressional Task Force on Terrorism says the top two GOP presidential contenders need a better understanding of intelligence-gathering on suspected terrorists.

ADVERTISEMENT

"I am concerned with Mr. Cruz and Mr. Trump both. I think they've got to come to reality with the fact that we have to track known terrorists; who are they talking to? What are the conversations about? We need to know," Rep. Robert Pittenger (R-N.C.) says in an interview with The Hill's Molly K. Hooper.
