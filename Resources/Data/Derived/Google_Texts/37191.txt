A motorist was killed after hitting a group of horses on State Route 77 near Winkleman Sunday morning, Arizona Department of Public Safety troopers said.

There were eight horses on the roadway. Four of them were hit.

Troopers said three of those horses died and one had to be put down due to severe injuries.

The driver was pronounced dead at the scene.

A second vehicle, a pickup truck, struck once of the dead horses, troopers said.

SR 77has reopened milepost 132.

The name of the victim has not yet been released.
