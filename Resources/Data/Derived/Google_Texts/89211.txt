A man suffered serious injuries in a stabbing Friday night in west Wichita.

It happened just after 10 p.m. in the 4100 block of West 3rd Street, which is a block from West Street.

Police said a 34-year-old man was stabbed in the abdomen outside of his home after an argument with a 58-year-old man turned into an altercation.

The victim was rushed to a local hospital with serious injuries.

The suspect was also taken to the hospital where he was treated and then booked into jail for aggravated battery.

Police said it is unclear what led up to the incident, but said the victim did know the suspect by name.
