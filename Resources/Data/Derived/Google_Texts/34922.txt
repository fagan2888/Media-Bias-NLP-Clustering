Diane (Gawne) and Bob Greenlee were married on August 14, 1965, at St. Michael's Church in Orland Park, Ill., a Chicago suburb.

The Rev. Monsignor John Desmond presided, as he had in 1942 for the marriage of Diane's parents, Dan and Shirley Gawne.

Diane and Bob moved to Boulder in 1975 with their two children, AnneMarie Kemp and Robert Christopher Greenlee.

Diane and Bob now reside in Lafayette, as do their two adult children and four grandchildren.
