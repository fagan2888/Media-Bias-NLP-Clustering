Sioux Falls, SD

South Dakota authorities are asking for your help in finding a state prison inmate who didn't return from her work release job in Sioux Falls early Sunday morning.

33-year-old Jovan Prince is a Native American woman, five-foot-eight inches tall and weighing about 130-pounds. She has brown hair and brown eyes.

Prince is serving a five-year sentence for forgery out of Pennington County. She's also been sentenced to her fourth drunk driving offense out of Hughes County.

Anyone who sees Prince, or knows where she is, should call local law enforcement.
