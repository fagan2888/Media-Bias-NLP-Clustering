Members of the Philadelphia Baptist Church are on their way home Saturday after Friday's bus crash in Walton County.

A little before 5 p.m. Friday, a number of agencies responded to a crash at Interstate 10 and mile marker 83 in Walton County. Reports say the bus, which was carrying a number of people, left the roadway and hit a tree.

Several of the passengers were taken to the hospital. The crash did not cause any life threatening injuries. Members of the Philadelphia Baptist Church were taken in Friday night by Walton County officials and sheltered at the DeFuniak Springs Community Center.
