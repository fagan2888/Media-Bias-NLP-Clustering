KNOXVILLE ? An early morning fire Monday destroyed a house in North Knox County, but the residents escaped without injury.

The fire at 7815 Miken Lane was reported at 4:38 a.m.

An occupant had awakened and had gone into the kitchen when she saw the flames at the back of the house, according to Rural/Metro Fire Department Capt. Scott Roberts.

She awoke others in the house, and they fled outside.

The house was declared a total loss, Roberts said. The cause of the fire is under investigation.

The Red Cross was called and is assisting the house's six occupants, he said.
