The President of the United States came to Milwaukee Thursday to congratulate the city for its engagement in a major health care initiative.

He spoke at the United Community Center about how the city won an Affordable Care Act competition to get the most people enrolled per capita. Milwaukee just defeated Chicago, his hometown. About 89,000 were enrolled in the Milwaukee area.
