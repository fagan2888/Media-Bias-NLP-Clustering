NEW YORK (WPIX) — A young man died in Brooklyn Thursday afternoon after he was scalped by a model helicopter.

Paramedics were called to the scene of the horrifying accident at Shore Parkway and Bay 44th St. where the 19-year-old man was pronounced dead on arrival.

According to sources, the blades of the chopper took off the top of the man’s head.
