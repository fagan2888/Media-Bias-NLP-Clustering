At least one person was injured in a shooting in southeast Colorado Springs early Sunday morning.

Officers responded to the 4000 block of Colony Hill Circle just after 2:30 a.m. on reports of at least eight gunshots fired in the area. Police found several shell casings in the street, but didn't spot any damaged property. At the time the officers were investigating the neighborhood, they didn't come across any shooting victims.

An hour later, Memorial Hospital alerted the police department that someone had just checked in with a gunshot wound. After officers interviewed the man, they determined he had been injured in the shooting on Colony Hill Circle. At this time, no suspects have been identified.

If you have any information that can help police, you are asked to call CSPD at 444-7000 or Crime Stoppers at 634-STOP.
