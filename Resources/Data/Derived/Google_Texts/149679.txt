A man who the Pima County Sheriff's Department says physically attacked a deputy Friday night died after the deputy shot him, according to a PCSD media release.

A deputy responded around 11:30 p.m. to a disturbance call at The Lodge at Ventana Canyon, 6200 N. Club House Lane, and on arrival the deputy approached a man to question him, according to the release.

The man attacked the deputy, and the situation escalated until the deputy shot the man, who was transported to Banner University Medical Center, where he was pronounced dead, according to the release.

The sheriff's department has requested that media inquiries be sent via email, with responses to come on Monday.
