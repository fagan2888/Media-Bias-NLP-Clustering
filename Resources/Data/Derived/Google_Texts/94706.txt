JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - The Jacksonville Sheriff's Office is looking for a man wanted for intent to sell cocaine and marijuana near a church.

Parris Devon Young, 26, has active warrants for possession with intent to sell cocaine within 1,000 feet of a church, possession with intent to sell marijuana within 1,000 feet of a church and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon.

Young, who is 5-feet, 10-inches tall and 160 pounds, has a $225,000 bond and his last known address is in the 6100 block of Arlington Expressway.

Anyone with information on Young's location is asked to contact JSO at 904-630-0500 or email JSOCrimTips@jaxsheriff.org. To remain anonymous and receive a possible reward up to $3,000, contact Crime Stoppers at 1-866-845-TIPS.
