(KANSAS CITY, Mo.) The Kansas City Royals announced a chance for fans to buy tickets to possible World Series games played at Kauffman Stadium.The tickets are limited and fans have to register online for a chance to be part of a random drawing.Click here to register.The drawing will take place on or about Oct. 11.Fans have until Friday at noon to register.Winners will be notified by email.The Royals will play the Baltimore Orioles in the ALCS.
