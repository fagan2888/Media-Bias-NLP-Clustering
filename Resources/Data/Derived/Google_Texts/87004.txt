Rising Star: McDermott Will's Anne Hance

Law360, New York (April 3, 2015, 1:07 PM ET) -- McDermott Will & Emery LLP partner Anne Hance has successfully guided health insurers, hospitals and pharmacy benefit managers through Medicare’s dense thicket of regulations as they pursue more lucrative contracts and next-generation reimbursement arrangements, earning her a slot among Law360’s top health lawyers under 40.



Hance, 38, lands on the Rising Stars list as co-chairwoman of McDermott’s payors group and sought-after strategic counsel to an array of companies coping with fast-changing government policies related to Medicare Advantage, Medicare Part D and the Affordable Care Act....
