A police chase Saturday afternoon ended with the suspect crashing his car in Haysville.

The 44-year-old man was attempting take steel cable from Westar near 79th Street South and Broadway, said Sedgwick County Sheriff's Sgt. Conrad Janssen.

Haysville officers responded to the call, and a pursuit ensued. The short chase ended when the suspect crashed around 2 p.m. at 71st and Broadway.

No one was hurt.

The suspect was booked for theft and other felony charges associated with the chase, Janssen said.
