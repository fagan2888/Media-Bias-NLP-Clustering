Traffic is moving again over the Mantoloking Bridge after the partially opened span was fully lowered.

The problem, first reported shortly before 9 a.m., was resolved about 10:40 a.m., according to a dispatcher with the Ocean County Road Department. He did not know the cause of the malfunction.
