Welcome to 2014. If you’re an extraordinarily pale teenager in Illinois or Oregon, you may not use a tanning facility without express medical reasoning from a doctor. And if you’re 21+ in Colorado, you may now legally buy and sell marijuana. Those are just a few of the new laws that went into effect today: Jan. 1, 2014.

According to the National Conference of State Legislatures, roughly 40,000 new state laws take effect today. Last year we chronicled a handful of strange rules and regulations, and in continuing that tradition, here are a few highlights from this year’s batch of new laws.
