Understanding Health Insurance

Written and produced by the Kaiser Family Foundation, the YouToons aim to help consumers better understand health insurance. These short videos explain important health insurance concepts, such as health premiums, out-of-pocket health costs, and provider networks.

This year, open enrollment begins on Sunday, November 1, 2015, and ends Sunday, January 31, 2016.
