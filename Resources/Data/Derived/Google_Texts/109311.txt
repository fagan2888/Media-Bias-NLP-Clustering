Ted Cruz opened a campaign office in Ellington on Saturday. (Joel Leyden)

Republican presidential candidate Ted Cruz opened a Connecticut headquarters on Saturday morning.

Cruz’s campaign said they opened office in Ellington. The office is located at 4 West Rd.

Cruz’s campaign said his office is “staffed by dozens of volunteers using phone banks, social media and distributing signs and literature.”

A grand opening event will be held at 1 p.m. on Sunday.

Spokesperson would not confirm if Ted or Heidi will be there. Cruz is currently in Indiana.

Building is owned by a Muslim property owner and the campaign is securing religious leaders to support Cruz.

The office hours are from 8 a.m. to 11 p.m.
