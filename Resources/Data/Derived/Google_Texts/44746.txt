An Altha man is dead after a car crash early Saturday morning on State Road 71 in Jackson County.

Florida Highway Patrol officials say around 1 a.m. Saturday, 31-year-old Hector Hernandez was driving southbound on State Road 71 when he began traveling into the west shoulder.

Hernandez car then became airborne. Then the front of his car hit a grass culvert, the car overturned, and then struck a tree.

Florida Highway Patrol says Hernandez was not wearing a seatbelt, and they are still investigating if this was alcohol related.
