Know Your Candidates: Darin Smith







Submitted by Aaron Groff (agroff@k2tv.com)U.S. House candidate Darin Smith sits down with K2TV to discuss his campaign and the issues important to him.In this edition of "Know Your Candidates," Smith explains his stance on the coal industry and increasing demand for natural gas, along with working to repeal Obamacare, his support of what he calls "Trump 2.0," and why he is the best candidate to represent Wyoming in Washington.
