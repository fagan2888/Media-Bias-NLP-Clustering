Open enrollment for Obamacare ends Jan. 31. Coverage will begin March 1 for those that sign up by then..

If you go without insurance and are not exempt from the federal requirement to have coverage, you could face a penalty of $695 per adult (plus $347.50 for each child younger than 18 not covered) or 2.5 percent of your household income, whichever is higher.

Go to www.ChooseHealthDE.com for more information and to connect to www.HealthCare.gov.

Free in-person enrollment assistance is available at the locations below. Help by phone is also available by calling the federal Marketplace at (800) 318-2596.
