Jones Day, WilmerHale, Bancroft Clean Up At High Court

Law360, New York (July 8, 2014, 5:49 PM ET) -- Behemoths Jones Day and WilmerHale and the boutique law firm Bancroft PLLC have earned appellate bragging rights, putting up the best records before the U.S. Supreme Court this session.



Just seven law firms argued at least four cases before the high court this term. Jones Day, which has more than 1,700 attorneys in the U.S. alone, and the 13-attorney Bancroft — buoyed by former U.S. solicitor general Paul D. Clement — won nine of the 10 combined cases they argued.
