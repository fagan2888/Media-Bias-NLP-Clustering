One person was stabbed at a Greenville apartment complex Halloween night, prompting an ECU off-campus crime alert.

Police say the person was stabbed following a fight between two groups in the visitors parking lot of North Campus Crossing.

Police were called to the scene around 11:37 p.m. The alert said the victim was being treated in the hospital, but no further information was released.

Around 1:00 a.m., police were called to the Province Apartment Complex near 11th and Lawrence streets for the report of an assault.

One person was taken to the hospital. There is no word on that person's condition.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Greenville Police Department at 252-329-4315. Information can be provided anonymously to Crime Stoppers at 252-758-7777.
