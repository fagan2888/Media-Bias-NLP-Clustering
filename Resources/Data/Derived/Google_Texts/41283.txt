A missing 22-year-old autistic man who walked away from his group home on Saturday night was found Sunday afternoon in Kenosha County

Robert Parrott left his group home at 3614 Sovereign Drive shortly before 9 p.m. Saturday, according to a Racine police news release.

He was found safe and unharmed near County E and Highway 32 about 1:15 p.m. Sunday, police said.

Authorities described Parrott, who has limited verbal skills, as a missing endangered adult and urged the public to call with information about his location. On Sunday, Racine police thanked those who assisted in locating him.
