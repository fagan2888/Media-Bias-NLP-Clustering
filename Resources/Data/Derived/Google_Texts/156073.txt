Supreme Court could take up another Texas case

The U.S. Supreme Court building in Washington, D.C.

Last week, when the U.S. Supreme Court decided to take up the Texas abortion law, supporters and opponents cheered because their time finally had come. After decades of state actions and lawsuits over Republican-led abortion restrictions across the country, the highest court in the land said it would weigh in on the topic for the first time in more than 20 years.
