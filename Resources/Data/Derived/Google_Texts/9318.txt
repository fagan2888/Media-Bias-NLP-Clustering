A recent study conducted by the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation about the impact of the Affordable Care Act in Texas reminds us of a particularly egregious political syndrome. Whether it has an official Latin appellation, we're not sure, but it manifests itself by self-mutilation of the nose (in order to spite one's face).
