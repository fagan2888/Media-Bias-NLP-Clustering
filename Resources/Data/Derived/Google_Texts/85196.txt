It was an early Saturday for some who participated in one of the oldest races in town: the Anderson Cup Road Race. This is the 32nd annual Anderson Cup 5k Race benefiting the pediatric therapy department. A total of 197 racers crossed the finish line this morning all for a good cause.

"It just provides equipment for the children which is great and it lets them have things that they might not would have had otherwise, and it's just great for the community."

There was also a fun run for the kids a balloon-releasing ceremony bringing awareness to national Donate Life Month by the Mississippi Organ Recovery Agency.
