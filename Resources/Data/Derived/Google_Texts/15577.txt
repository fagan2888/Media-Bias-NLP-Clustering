Ina Road has reopened at the train tracks on the east side of Interstate 10 after being closed for much of the day.

A train hit and killed a pedestrian Saturday morning just before 7 a.m., Northwest Fire District spokesman Adam Goldberg said in an email. The victim was a male.

Marana Police officials say this is being investigated as a suicide.
