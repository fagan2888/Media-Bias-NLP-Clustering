The shooting began around 12:30 p.m. local time at Arapahoe High School—a school of about 2,000—reported the Denver Post. Reports are now saying at least two people have been injured at the suburban Denver high school.

Around 1:15 p.m. local time, Arapahoe County Sheriff Grayson Robinson told CALL7 that he believed the shooter was still inside the school. Leading police to call it an “active shooter situation.”

The Denver Post reported that nearby schools are locked down and that reports of the shooter having also been shot are unconfirmed.
