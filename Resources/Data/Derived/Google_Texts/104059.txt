There were several good deals to be had at 15th Avenue Baptist Church Saturday.

The bi-annual Mothers of Preschoolers Mega Yard Sale was going on. This is the biggest fundraiser for MOPS. MOPS is a ministry for moms who can come together in fellowship providing a much-needed breaks for moms of young children. One of the organizers Madeline Kennedy says today's event was packed.

"We've sold out of spots. We have over 50 vendors here and it's really going great," Kennedy says.

The next event will be later this year.
