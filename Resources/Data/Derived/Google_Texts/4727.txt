This past weekend, the Obama Administration announced they would be replacing CGI Federal with Accenture as the primary contractor in charge of the HealthCare.gov website. As Jimmy Kimmel explained on his show last night, Accenture is one of those companies that you see ads for, “mostly at airports,” but have no idea what they do.

Kimmel showed his audience a new ad from Accenture that aims to introduce them to Americans and explain why they are “the right company to get this done.”
