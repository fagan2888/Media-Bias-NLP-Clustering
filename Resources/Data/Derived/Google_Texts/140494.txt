MERCER COUNTY - Enroll America will be available to answer questions about getting health coverage and guide individuals through the process of signing up for health care during four information sessions in Trenton and Hamilton next week.

The assistance will be available:

• Tuesday, noon to 7 p.m. -- Mercer County Connection, Hamilton Square Shopping Center, Route 33

• Wednesday: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. -- Mercer County Connection, Hamilton Square Shopping Center, Route 33

• Thursday and Friday, 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. - Trenton City Hall, 319 East State Street.

Those interested in signing up for coverage will need to do so before the enrollment period ends on Feb. 15. The assistance is free and offered in both English and Spanish.

To schedule an appointment for enrollment assistance at the Mercer County Connection call 609-890-9800 and for an appointment at Trenton City Hall call 609-533-1871.

During the last enrollment period, 5,502 Mercer County residents enrolled in a healthcare plan through the Affordable Care Act.
