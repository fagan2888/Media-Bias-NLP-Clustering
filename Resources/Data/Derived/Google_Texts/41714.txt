Updated Aug. 3 at 4:15 p.m.: The pedestrian killed in an accident Sunday has been identified as 83-year-old Billy Kimbrough of Arlington, according to the Tarrant County Medical Examiner’s office.

Original item Aug. 2: A pedestrian was struck and killed by an SUV early Sunday, police say.

The victim, who is in his 80s, was walking on the frontage road of U.S. Highway 287 near Median Way about 4 a.m. and was struck by a northbound Chevrolet Tahoe, police spokeswoman Tiara Richard said.

The SUV’s driver called 911 and stopped to render aid, but the man was pronounced dead at the scene, she said.

The victim’s identity will be released by the Tarrant County medical examiner after his next of kin has been notified.

Police have said the driver is unlikely to face charges.
