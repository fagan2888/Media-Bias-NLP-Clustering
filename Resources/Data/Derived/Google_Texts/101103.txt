For individuals, Obama has proposed targeted tax help, including breaks for college students paying tuition, low-income people without children and those paying for child care. And the president appeared unconcerned with how the GOP might see his budget.

“I am not going to accept a budget that locks in sequestration going forward,” Obama said at the Department of Homeland Security’s National Operations Center – a venue that underscored the ongoing battle with Congress over DHS funding for the current fiscal year.

“I want to work with Congress to replace mindless austerity with smart investments that strengthen America. And we can do so in a way that is fiscally responsible," the president said.
