× Car & Train Collide in Huntsville; Woman Injured

HUNTSVILLE, Ala. (WHNT) – Police are on the scene of a wreck at a railroad crossing in Huntsville.

It happened at intersection of Pratt Avenue and Meridian Street.

We’re told a train and car collided. One woman was taken to Huntsville Hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.

Police issued an alert just before 7:30 a.m. regarding the wreck.

The train has come to a stop as a result.

We have a crew on the scene gathering the breaking details.
