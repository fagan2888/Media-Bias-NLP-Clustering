Kristina Vaccarelli and Chase Merrigan were married Aug. 2, 2014, at First Congregational Church in Boulder with reception following at the Rembrandt Yard.

The couple honeymooned in Kauai and Hawaii the first two weeks of August.

Kristina is the daughter of Kelly Vaccarelli, of Boulder, and Bill Conboy, of Golden. Chase is the son of Sean and Patty Merrigan, of Boulder.

The couple attended Fairview High School, where they were great friends. They began dating shortly after college.

Kristina works for Datalogix in Broomfield, and Chase works for Sea to Summit in Boulder. The couple plan to reside in the greater Boulder area.
