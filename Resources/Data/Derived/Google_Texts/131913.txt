Editor's note: To mark this month's 125th anniversary of The Wall Street Journal, we offer a compilation of notable op-eds from various contributors. Last week we published excerpts from editorials, which have been appearing in our pages for more than a century as the unsigned expression of the paper's editorial board. But for much of its history the Journal published few op-eds, which carry individual bylines. We have excerpted below some memorable contributions from recent decades.
