Ways And Means Floats 4 Bills On Hospital Medicare Pay

Law360, New York (July 29, 2015, 8:12 PM ET) -- The Republican-controlled House Ways and Means Committee on Wednesday introduced four bills to alter Medicare reimbursement for hospitals and various specialty providers. The bills would create a new bonus system and tweak payments for inpatient and outpatient admissions, treatment of the uninsured and indirect medical education.
