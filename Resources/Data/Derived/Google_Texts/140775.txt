A man has died following a two-vehicle crash Friday night in McPherson County.

It happened just before 10:30 p.m. on northbound I-135 near Smoky Valley Road. That's just southeast of Lindsborg. The Kansas Highway Patrol released few details about the crash but confirmed the crash involved two vehicles.

Troopers said a man was killed, and another person was hospitalized with unknown injuries.

The Patrol will provide additional information once the victim's next of kin have been notified.
