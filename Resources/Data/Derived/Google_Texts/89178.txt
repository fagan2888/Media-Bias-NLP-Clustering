Three teenagers are hospitalized after an ATV crash on Sunday afternoon.

Hood County Sheriff Roger Deeds confirms, the teens were injured when the ATV they were riding in crashed head-on with a Ford pick-up.

The accident happened on Apache Trial in Indian Harbor according to the Hood County news.

The victims — a 13-year-old girl 14-year-old boy and 15-year old boy — were taken by air ambulance to John Peter Smith Hospital in Fort Worth.
