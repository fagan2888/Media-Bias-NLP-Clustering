Hundreds participated in an annual race designed to help raise money for rehab services for adults and children with stroke, spinal cord injury, acquired brain injury, orthopedic injuries and neuromuscular disorders.

The 8th annual "Run, Walk, and Roll Race for Rehab" was held Saturday morning in Greenville.

The 5k and one-mile event took place on a course through downtown and ECU with over 140 race participants and over 100 volunteers.

Net proceeds from the race will benefit programs and services at the Vidant Health Rehabilitation Center.
