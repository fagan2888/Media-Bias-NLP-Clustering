BELDEN, Miss. (PRESS RELEASE) – Registration is underway at Itawamba Community College for a “Building Girls for a Better Tomorrow” construction camp scheduled for 8:30 a.m.-2 p.m., June 7-9, at the Belden Center (3200 Adams Farm Rd.).

Seventh through ninth grade girls are eligible to participate in the camp, which offers hands-on activities, food, fun and girl power.

The cost is $25, which includes a t-shirt.

For more information, contact Kala Hudson at (662) 407-1389.
