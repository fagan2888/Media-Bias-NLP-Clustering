HICKORY, N.C., Nov. 17, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- Dale Jarrett Racing Adventure, Inc. (OTC: DJRT), is pleased to announce that it's proxy for the disposition of the company's racing adventure has been approved. The company is now in the process of preparing and filing the necessary documents to change the name to 24/7 Kid Doc, Inc. and to obtain a new stock symbol more appropriate to the company's new identity.

24/7 Kid Doc, Inc. is an online Pediatric care provider committed to providing fast, easy and cost-effective access to Pediatricians and Pediatric Nurse Practitioners. Patients can have video visits with these providers from their smartphones, iPads or computers when they need it most, at any time day or night.

Setting itself apart from other online medical providers, 24/7 Kid Doc will not only be offering pay-as-you-go Doctor visits, it also offers monthly memberships and has created a value-added program for the rapidly expanding number of companies developing cost effective alternatives to the Affordable Care Act. By integrating 24/7 Kid Doc's online Pediatric care within their offered services, these associations will be able to significantly reduce their delivery costs thereby providing them with the opportunity to reduce total premiums to all families.

According to CEO Tim Shannon, "We are in the final stages of preparing to launch our online services and to implement our nationwide marketing plan. Beta-testing of our services begins January 1st 2016 in conjunction with 24/7 Pediatric Care Centers of Florida."
