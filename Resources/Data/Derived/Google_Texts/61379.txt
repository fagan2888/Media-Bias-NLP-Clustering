Authorities are looking for a man accused of robbing a southwest Oklahoma City bank, police said.

The bank was robbed about 1:20 p.m. Saturday at MidFirst Bank, 7500 S Western Ave. A man walked into the bank and demanded money and the teller gave an undisclosed amount of money. The robber threatened to shoot employees but no gun was seen.

The FBI said the robber was seen leaving the bank on foot and walking east.

The robber was described as a black man in his late 50s, about 6 feet tall, weighing about 180 pounds and with salt and pepper gray hair.

No one was injured in the robbery.

Anyone with information is asked to call 911.
