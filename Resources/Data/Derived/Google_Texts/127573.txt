Jena Garlinghouse, daughter of Patsy and Bruce Garlinghouse, of Boulder, is engaged to be married to Patrick Thurmer, son of Veronika and Mathias Thurmer, of Boulder.

Jena and Patrick grew up three houses away from each other and started dating at Boulder High School. After graduating from high school, they both attended the University of Colorado-Boulder and have since moved to San Francisco.

Jena is an account director at Hatch, a communications and design agency. Patrick is a senior analyst at AKQA, a digital advertising agency.

The couple plan to be married Oct. 2, 2015, in Kauai, Hawaii, at a botanical garden on the North Shore. After the wedding, the couple will return to San Francisco, where they reside with their 4-year-old dog, Charlie.
