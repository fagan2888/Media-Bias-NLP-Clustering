Another fire – this one dubbed the Back 30 Fire – popped up in the Santa Fe National Forest Friday. The new fire is located near Glorieta Mesa.

The US Forest Service says the fire began just after noon on Friday and that crews are hiking in to the fire at the moment. One structure is threatened, according to the Forest Service.

28 people are responding to the fire at this point.
