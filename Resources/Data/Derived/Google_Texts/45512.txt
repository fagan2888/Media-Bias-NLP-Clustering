Family and friends are looking for a Salina woman who went missing on Christmas day.

KSAL reports 25-year-old Shyla Bisterfeldt has not been seen since 6 a.m. Christmas morning. She was last seen leaving Kenwood View Nursing Home, wearing green scrubs.

Shyla is 5’3″ tall, and weighs about 150 pounds. She has brown eyes. Her hair is originally brown, but was recently dyed partially multi-colored.

Anyone with any information on where Shyla Bisterfeldt might be is asked to contact Salina police at 785-826-7210.
