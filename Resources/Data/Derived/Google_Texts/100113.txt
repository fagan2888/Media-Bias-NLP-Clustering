A 12-year-old boy, shot in the side with a .22-caliber bullet Sunday evening, was listed in stable condition at Children’s Hospital in Aurora, Weld County authorities said.

The shooting happened near Keenesburg at 29593 Weld County Road 20, a dispatcher said.

The authorities declined to discuss the circumstances surrounding the shooting. The boy was carried by ambulance to Aurora, Weld County Sheriff’s Office Cpl. Matt Turner said.

“Latest word is that the hospital has the patient in stable condition,” Turner said.

Children’s Hospital officials declined comment.

When deputies arrived on scene at about 6:15 p.m., they found the wounded boy, he said. Sheriff’s deputies are investigating the cause.
