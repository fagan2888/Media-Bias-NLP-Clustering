5 Issues For Employers To Consider When Shaping Benefits

Law360, New York (October 23, 2014, 10:49 AM ET) -- The workplace that we know today is rapidly changing. Competition for highly skilled workers is fierce, employees have become more mobile, in part due to alternative work arrangements or outsourcing, and there are often several generations of employees working alongside one another with different workplace approaches and perspectives. Developing employee benefit and compensation programs that are meaningful to a diverse group of workers with varied needs will be challenged with greater frequency.
