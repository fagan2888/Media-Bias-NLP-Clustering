SAN LUIS OBISPO, Calif. - The FrenchWay App will allow patients to maneuver through hospitals, find doctors and receive appointment reminders at French Hospital Medical Center in San Luis Obispo.

The new app is the first of its kind in San Luis Obispo County and is part of a 90-day pilot program.

If it has a successful test run, the FrenchWay App will be available to use in all Dignity Health Hospitals.

The FrenchWay App is available to download for free on iPhone and Androids.
