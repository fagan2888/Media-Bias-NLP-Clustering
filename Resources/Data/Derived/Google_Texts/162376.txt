Several roads in Holmes County are currently flooded or have been made impassable due to excessive water.

Holmes County Emergency Management crews urge caution to drivers traveling on or near the following roads:

Francis Lane

Buck Rogers Road

Blue Springs Road

Bonifay-Gritney Road

River Road

Curry Ferry Road

Crews report that just south of the Gritney Fire Dept is covered with water. Officials say drivers should turn around if they come upon a road covered in water and not attempt to drive through it.

Caryville River is currently at 14.64 and expected to crest at 16.1 on Monday and Tuesday.
