High winds and dry conditions continue pose increased risk of fires across KAKEland.

The National Weather Service has issued a Red Flag Warning for Ellsworth, Russell, Saline, Lincoln, McPherson, Barton and Rice counties in central Kansas until 6 p.m. Sunday.

The Red Flag warning denotes extreme grassland fire danger with winds in excess of 20 miles per hour, and low humidity.

The rest of central, south central and eastern Kansas are under a hazardous weather outlook. The fire index is expected to remain high into the evening, increasing fire danger.
