Ford O'Connell Contributor

Ford O'Connell is a Republican strategist and political analyst. A frequent commentator on Fox News, CNN, and other media, he worked on the 2008 McCain-Palin presidential campaign. In 2010, Campaigns & Elections magazine named him a “Rising Star.” He is chairman of CivicForumPAC, managing director of Civic Forum Strategies and author of “Hail Mary: The 10-Step Playbook for Republican Recovery.”
