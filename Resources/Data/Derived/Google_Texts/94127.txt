Choctaw Co. Sheriff Scott Lolley says it began Friday night around 11:00 pm.

He says the 911 call came in as a domestic disturbance, upon arrival, Lolley says deputies couldn't make contact with the husband or wife at the home. Sheriff Lolley says after watching the home for a while, they found out the man was holding his wife hostage with a rifle. The Alabama State Trooper Tactical Team was called in. Sheriff Lolley says contact was eventually made and the woman was released. Around two this afternoon state troopers made entry and took Jeremiah Presley into custody.

Jeremiah Presley is charged with kidnapping - first degree and domestic violence - third degree.
