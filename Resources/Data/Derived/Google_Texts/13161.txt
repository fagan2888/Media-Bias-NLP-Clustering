By almost any measure, Kirk Rademaker has a dream job. As a professional sand carver, he is paid to travel to beautiful beaches to create huge sand sculptures. He also starred on "Sand Masters," which ran on the Travel Channel from 2011 to 2013. The show filmed six professional sand artists as they created elaborate carvings around the world.
