Double Take 'Toons: Unfair Accounting?

Depending on your point of view, the latest Congressional Budget Office report provides evidence that the Affordable Care Act is either a "job-killer" or frees millions of Americans from "job-lock." Gary Varvel thinks it's a killer and drives his point home, while Jeff Darcy believes some Americans can afford to leave jobs now that they have portable health care.
