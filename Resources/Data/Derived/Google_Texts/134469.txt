SANTA BARBARA, Calif. -

A stabbing at Ortega Park in Santa Barbara sent one man to the hospital and landed another man in jail.

Santa Barbara Police responded to the scene Friday around 10:15 p.m.

Officers found a 42-year-old man with a stab wound to his stomach.

Witnesses told police the attack was unprovoked.

They also provided police with a description of the suspect.

Officers saw a man matching that description running in the area.

They chased him down and took him into custody.

The suspect is identified as 19-year-old Josue Siguenza of Santa Barbara.

Siguenza was booked into the Santa Barbara County Jail for assault with a deadly weapon.

Officers also found a knife believed to be the weapon used by Siguenza.

The unidentified victim was taken to Cottage Hospital and is expected to survive.
