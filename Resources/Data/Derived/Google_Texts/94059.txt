Shannon O'Brien (right) dances with student Ara Shammian during the Matheny School prom in Randolph, NJ, on Thursday, May 14, 2015. Dawn J. Benko for the Daily Record MOR 0515 Matheny School Prom (Photo: DAWN J BENKO)

The Matheny Medical and Educational Center in Peapack held their 2015 Prom, "Arabian Nights," on Thursday at The Skylands.

Matheny is a special hospital and educational facility here for children and adults with medically complex developmental disabilities. The prom is an example of Matheny's efforts to enhance the quality of life for the people it serves, according to a press release from the school.

The evening was coordinated by Matheny's recreation therapy department, which is responsible for providing students and patients with a variety of recreation opportunities and resources to improve their physical, emotional, cognitive and social well-being, the release said.
