The next sign-up season for 2017 health coverage under the Affordable Care Act will begin just as the 2016 election campaigns draw to a close, under draft rules released by the Obama administration on Friday afternoon.

The open enrollment period for people buying insurance on their own for 2017 will start Nov. 1, 2016, and end Jan. 31, 2017, federal officials outlined in the draft rules.
