Update at 12:20 p.m.:

Dallas police have located Deontre Coleman, 9.

Original post:

Dallas police have issued a critical missing alert for a 9-year-old boy last seen Saturday evening.

Police say Deontre Coleman was last seen near his home along the 8000 block of Rothington Road at 5 p.m. Saturday. That’s one block from Skyline High School.

Per the alert, Coleman stands 4-foot-8, weighs around 100 pounds and was last seen dressed in a blue shirt, grey pants and blue Nikes.

If you have any information about Coleman’s possible whereabouts, police are asking you to call 911 or DPD offices at (214) 671-4268.
