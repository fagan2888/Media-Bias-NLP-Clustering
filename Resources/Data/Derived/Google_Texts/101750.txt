HANOVER, Pa. – People in York County walked their way to a healthier lifestyle on Sunday.

The Hanover hospital hosted it’s annual Hanover Heart Walk and Festival to raise funds for the American Heart Association.

The event included 1 to 3 mile route options. It also featured lots of family fun activities such as, mini-golf, a soil/herb station, and a chance to build your own healthy snack.

People of all ages come out to take part in the walk.

FOX43 Photojournalist, Derrik Bobb, introduces us to special little girl named Mia.

Her story is inspiring others to join in the fight, one step at a time.
