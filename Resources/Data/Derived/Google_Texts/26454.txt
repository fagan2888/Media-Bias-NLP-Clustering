A Lenexa woman who went missing Thursday was found dead Saturday in Kansas City, Mo., police said.

The body of Diana Langford, 50, was found in the area of 46th Terrace and Broadway, Lenexa police said Saturday in a news release.

The cause of death has not been released.

Langford was last seen leaving a Cracker Barrel restaurant in Olathe around 11:30 a.m. Thursday.

Lenexa police issued an alert for the Lenexa resident Friday, believing she may be endangered due to mental health issues.
