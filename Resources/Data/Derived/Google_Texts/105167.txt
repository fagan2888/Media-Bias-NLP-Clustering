The 53rd annual Carl Casper Custom Auto Show will feature classic cars, celebrities and music Friday through Sunday at the Kentucky Exposition Center.

The show features an array of vintage and custom vehicles as well as offering a music festival in Freedom Hall, with different concerts each day.

On Friday, the Louisville Crashers will take the stage at 8:30 p.m., with Caribou playing at 8 p.m. on Saturday and The Monarchs performing on Sunday at 3:30 p.m.

Low rider cars will compete in Freedom Hall Friday and at 7 p.m. Saturday with finals at 2 p.m. Sunday.

Autograph sessions with celebrities are also available throughout the weekend, meet-and-greet opportunities are scheduled with WWE Diva Charlotte, David Constock from “Street Outlaws” and Cincinnati Bengal’s cheerleaders.

More than 100 pinstripers will paint objects to be auctioned for charity. Many of the pieces will be created at the show. and there are eight different auctions throughout the event. Specialty vehicles, including a Batmobile, will also be on display.

For more information on tickets, visit http://www.carlcasper.com.
