A Grammy-winning artist is coming to the Northland this summer. James Taylor will be in Duluth June 1 for a concert at AMSOIL Arena.

Over the course of his career, Taylor has sold more than 100 million albums and won multiple awards for songs ranging from "Sweet Baby James" to "October Road."

Taylor's latest album, "Before This World," was released last year and debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200.

Tickets go on sale Friday, February 5 at 10 a.m. at ticketmaster.com

To reserve tickets by phone, call 800-745-3000.
