Denver detectives have arrested a woman for allegedly stabbing a man to death late Friday night, according to police.

Shandrea Bowie, 32, is being held for investigation of first-degree murder.

The stabbing happened in the 5400 block of Quari Street, said Raquel Lopez, Denver police spokeswoman.

“It appears to be a domestic,” Lopez said.

The name of the victim has not been released.

This is a developing case, and it will be updated as new details are available, she said.
