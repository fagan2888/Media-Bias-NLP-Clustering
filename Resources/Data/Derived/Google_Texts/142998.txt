An Oklahoma Highway Patrol trooper was killed Saturday night while responding to a jack-knifed semitrailer on Interstate 40 near Shawnee.

Two troopers were at the scene when another vehicle hit the semitrailer, an OHP spokesperson said. After hitting the semitrailer, the vehicle hit both troopers.

One of the troopers died at the scene, and the other trooper was taken to OU Medical Center in serious condition, according to a spokesperson. The names of the troopers were not released.

The driver who struck the troopers was transported to an Oklahoma City area hospital in serious conditions.

The semitrailer driver sustained minor injuries from the initial accident.
