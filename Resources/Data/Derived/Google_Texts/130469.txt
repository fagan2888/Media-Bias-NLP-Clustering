A woman has died after a car and semi collided late Sunday morning on a highway in Cowley County.

The Kansas Highway Patrol reporting Janice A. Tandarich, 79, of Oologah, Oklahoma died after the Toyota Camry she was driving was struck by a semi-truck at around 11:50 a.m. Sunday.

The KHP reports Tandarich was attempting a u-turn at the Highway 166, 211th Road intersection, east of Arkansas City when the vehicles collided.

No one else was hurt. The KHP reports Tandarich was wearing a seat belt at the time of the crash.
