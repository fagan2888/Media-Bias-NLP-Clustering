Mississippi State quarterback Dak Prescott and Mississippi cornerback Senquez Golson are among the 10 finalists for the C Spire Conerly Trophy, which is awarded to the state's top college football player.

Each school in the state is allowed to nominate one player for the award.

The other nominees are: Mississippi Valley State receiver Julian Stafford, Alcorn State quarterback John Gibbs Jr., Millsaps defensive end Zach Bell, Delta State quarterback Tyler Sullivan, Southern Miss defensive end Dasman McCullum, Jackson State receiver Daniel Williams, Mississippi College defensive back Dylan Klibert and Belhaven receiver Greg Livingston.

The winner will be announced Dec. 2 at the Mississippi Sports Hall of Fame.
