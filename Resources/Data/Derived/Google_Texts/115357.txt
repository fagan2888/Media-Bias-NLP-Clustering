Louisville Metro Police continue to investigate Saturday shootings that killed one and left two wounded.

The LMPD Homicide Unit is following up on leads in the fatal shooting of a man around 5:30 p.m. Saturday in the 2400 block of Nichols Meadow Court, said police spokesman Dwight Mitchell. The man, who has not yet been identified, was found dead inside a vehicle on the Jacobs neighborhood street.

No arrests have been made as of early Sunday afternoon, Mitchell said.

Around 6:30 Saturday evening, officers from the First Division responded to reports of a shooting in the 900 block of Esquire Alley in the Russell neighborhood.

Two men were found shot and taken to the University of Louisville Hospital. They are expected to survive their injuries, Mitchell said.

No suspects have been arrested in that case.

Anyone with information on either incident is asked to call LMPD's anonymous tip line at 502-574-LMPD.
