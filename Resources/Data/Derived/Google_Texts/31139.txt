A female pedestrian was hit and killed by a tow truck Saturday evening.

Tempe fire officials say it happened just before 9 p.m. at Scottsdale Road and McKellips.

The victim was a woman in her 30s. She died at the scene.

Police say impairment was not a factor.

Scottsdale Road was closed in both directions at McKellips.
