Wichita police are looking for a suspect in a Friday night shooting that sent two people to the hospital.

Police say the victims walked into Wesley Medical Center at around 11:10 p.m. They tell officers the shooting happened in the 1600 block of N Lorraine. That's near 15th and Hillside.

One victim told police a man had shot him in the left knee with a purple handgun. The other was shot in the rib cage on his left side.

The victims tell police the suspect is a mid-20s man about 5-foot-10, 150 pounds. He was last seen wearing a NIke jacket, polo-style shirt, blue jeans, and was armed with that purple .380 handgun.

Anyone with information on this incident is asked to call police.
