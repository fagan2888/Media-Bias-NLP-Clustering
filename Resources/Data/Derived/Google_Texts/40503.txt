A Topeka girl who was discovered missing Saturday morning has been located.

As of 8:30 pm Saturday, the Topeka Police Department has been blocking off the 2600 block of Southeast Ohio and the 2100 block of Southeast Pioneer way in an attempt to find answers surrounding the missing girl report from this morning.

Eight year old Samara Herrera was located Saturday in the 2500 block of SE Ohio in Topeka after last being seen at 11:30 Friday evening in the 2100 block of SE 34th Terrace.

Police have not released any information that details the reasoning behind her disappearance or if she was with a family member.

Herrera was taken to a local hospital to be medically cleared and police continue to investigate why she went missing.
