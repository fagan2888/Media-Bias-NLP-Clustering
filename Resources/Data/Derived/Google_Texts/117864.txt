DOI's 2017 Budget Gives Boost To BIA, Indian Education

Law360, New York (February 9, 2016, 5:15 PM ET) -- The Obama administration’s proposed 2017 budget features $13.4 billion for the U.S. Department of the Interior, including a heavy investment in Indian education and a bump of almost 5 percent for the Bureau of Indian Affairs.
