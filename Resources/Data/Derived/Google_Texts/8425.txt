At rally in Valdosta, Georgia, NASCAR owners and drivers took the stage with Donald Trump and announced that Nascar Inc. has endorsed the Republican frontrunner.

Chairman Brian France said, "I've known Donald for over 20 years. I'm here to tell you, he wins with his family."

Bill Elliott, one of Nascar's legends, said, "We need a change guys, that's all there is to it. And I think this is the man for the job."
