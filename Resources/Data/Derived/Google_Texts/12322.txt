Colorado health groups are urging Millennials to sign up for the new insurance exchanges with a series of raunchy and ridiculous ads with taglines like, "Keg stands are crazy. Not having health insurance is crazier." The "affordable" part of the Affordable Care Act will only be successful if young healthy people sign up for insurance to offset costlier care for the elderly. But will these ads convince you to get insurance?
