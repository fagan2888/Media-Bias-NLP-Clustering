Andover, SD

One man is dead after a two-vehicle crash east of Andover, South Dakota.

Around 12:30 a.m Sunday, a 50-year-old man was driving his mini-van eastbound in the right westbound lane on US Highway 12 when it struck an RV traveling westbound in the same lane.

Five people were inside the RV; only one person received minor injuries. The driver of the mini-van was pronounced dead at the scene.

Debris from the crash site resulted in another crash when a car hit a tire that had come off the RV. The two people in the vehicle suffered non-life threatening injuries. Highway Patrol closed down the westbound lanes of US Highway 12 for over four hours.
