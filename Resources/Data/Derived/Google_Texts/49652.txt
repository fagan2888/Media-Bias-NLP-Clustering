President Obama promised that Obamacare “won’t add a dime to the deficit?”

It’s not a dime; it’s over a trillion dollars deficit for just the first 10 years, reports the Congressional Budget Office. Costs will be double what Obama claimed when the health care law was passed. After offsetting new revenue from the law’s new taxes, we’ll still suffer a deficit of $1.3-trillion purely due to Obamacare.

After he told such a whopper lie to get Obamacare passed, who could trust anything that the President says today? Only incurably gullible people—and Obamacare doesn’t cover that.
