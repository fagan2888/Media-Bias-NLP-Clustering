Susan J. Demas is the CEO of Susan J. Demas Communications and Editor and Publisher of Inside Michigan Politics. She has covered Michigan politics since 2004 and writes a weekly syndicated column. She is the only Michigan journalist to have been named to The Washington Post's list of "Best Political Reporters," The Huffington Post's list of “Best Political Tweeters” and The Washington Post's list of “Best Political Bloggers.” Her political columns can be found at SusanJDemas.com.
