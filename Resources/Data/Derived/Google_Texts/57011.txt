Emergency crews are on the scene of a serious crash near Lake Hefner in northwest Oklahoma City, Wednesday afternoon.

First responders were called to the scene at South Shore Dr. and N. MacArthur Blvd.

Authorities have shut down MacArthur Blvd. in both directions while crews work the scene.

Two vehicles were involved. The cause of the crash has not yet been determined.
