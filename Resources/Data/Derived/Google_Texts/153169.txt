FOX 13 News is teaming up with IASIS Healthcare to raise awareness regarding health issues, and on Thursday those efforts centered on tips for healthy hearts, including the HeartView Scan.

During a HealthFix Info-Thon Thursday, volunteers took calls to answer your questions about heart health. Cardiologist Adam Elhaddi spoke to FOX 13 News' Nineveh Dinha about ways people can improve the health of their heart and the qualifications for a HeartView Scan.
