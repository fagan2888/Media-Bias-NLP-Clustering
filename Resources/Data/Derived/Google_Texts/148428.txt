The Netherlands

A Dutch politician has caused a furore in Iran after wearing a headscarf that left some of her hair, neck and ears exposed. One leading Iranian conservative said MEP Marietje Schaake, visiting Tehran on a European Parliament delegation, looked as if "she were going to a party".

Denmark

Danish police "basically never investigate crime" on the island of Funen – population 466,000 – due to a lack of manpower. Police officers have admitted: "It is relatively risk-free to deal cannabis and other drugs," adding that drug prices on the island have dropped dramatically as a result.

Austria

A Salzburg brothel is struggling to cope with demand after offering free drinks and free sex in protest at what its owner calls "unfair taxation". Hermann "Pascha" Müller, who is paying prostitutes out of his own pocket told an Austrian paper he no longer wants to be "the tax office's pimp".

Spain

Spanish lawmakers have rectified a "historic mistake" by finally granting dual citizenship to Aus – descendants of Jews expelled from Spain in 1492. "We hope this will make Spain a better place," said the president of Spain's federation of Jewish communities.
