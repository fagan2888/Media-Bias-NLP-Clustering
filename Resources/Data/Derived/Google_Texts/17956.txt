FIRST FAMILY—First Baptist Church in Penn Hills, 7450 Chadwick St., recently hosted its 24th Pastoral Anniversary of Rev. James Emory Hunt and Family. The three-day celebration included a First Lady’s Night honoring Rev. Dr. Shelia Johnson-Hunt with Rev. Dr. David Lee and his congregation; a service with guest preacher Rev. Marco A. Tinor and the University of Pittsburgh “Some of God’s Children” Gospel Choir; and a service with guest preacher Rev. Dr. J. Van Alfred Winsett. Pictured above, the First Family of First Baptist Church of Penn Hills: Dr. Julian E. Hunt, Minister Teresa R. Hunt, Rev. Dr. Sheila Johnson-Hunt and Pastor James E. Hunt. (Photos by Erin Perry)
