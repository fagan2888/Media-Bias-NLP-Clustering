After 10 years here, Pacha New York, once an indispensable venue for DJs and producers of electronic dance music, will host its final New Year’s Eve show. The club is closing at the end of January and Pacha, perhaps the best-known brand in the EDM world with clubs in countries including Argentina, Brazil, China, Germany, Portugal and Poland in addition to its flagship in Ibiza, Spain, will no longer have a franchise in the U.S.
