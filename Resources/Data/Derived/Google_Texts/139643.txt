A section of I-235 was shut down as emergency crews responded to an injury accident. The accident was reported on I-235 near 25th Street North after 9:30 a.m. Friday.

The Kansas Highway Patrol said a SUV driven by Tracee Tipton of Lincoln, Nebraska, drove through the median and struck a southbound SUV head on. Troopers said Tipton reached down to grab something and realized her SUV was drifting across the median.

The southbound lanes of I-235 were closed at K-96.

Tipton was taken to Via Christi St. Francis.

Four people in the other SUV were hurt. James, Connor and Alex Demastes and Theresa Spradling were also taken to Via Christi St. Francis. All four are from Waterloo, Iowa.
