The fire was reported just after 4:30 a.m.

Two people were able to escape when their house caught on fire early Friday morning in Moore.



According to the fire department, just after 4:30 a.m., crews responded to a large home fire in the 200 block of S.E. 26th Street.

According to News 9's Bill "Fig" Newton, two people were inside the house when the fire started, but they both escaped without any injuries. Live pictures from the scene show flames shooting through the roof of the house, and firefighters are using a ladder truck to try and extinguish the flames.

The cause of the blaze is under investigation.
