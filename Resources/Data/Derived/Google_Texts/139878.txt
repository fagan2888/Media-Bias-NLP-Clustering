The House may vote as early as tomorrow on a bill to give the president what is formally called Trade Promotion Authority, or TPA, and informally “fast track.” There’s a man-bites-dog aspect to the debate over TPA, in that President Obama faces more opposition from Democrats than Republicans. When the Senate approved the measure last month, 62-37, just 14 Democrats joined 48 Republicans in favor.
