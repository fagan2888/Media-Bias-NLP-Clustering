If you're looking for work, Walmart is going to hire up to 95 associates in Newport.

The new Walmart Neighborhood Market is expected to open this summer in the Carteret County town.

A temporary hiring center will open this Monday in the Oceanway Plaza Shopping Center on Highway 70.

Through Walmart's Veterans Welcome Home Commitment, the company will offer a job to any qualified Veteran who has been honorably discharged within the past 12 months.

Applications will be accepted from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Monday through Friday, and after 6:00 p.m. by appointment. You can also apply online.
