Northwest Fire officials say that a home near Dove Mountain was struck by lightning Saturday evening.

Captain Adam Goldberg says crews are at the house that caught fire in the 12000 block of north Lost Shadows Court.

Officials say there were no injuries.

The damage is estimated at $7,000 with most of the damage being to the roof and attic.

The call came in just before 8 p.m.
