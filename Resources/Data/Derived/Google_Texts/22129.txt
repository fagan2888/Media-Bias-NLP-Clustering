Several presidential candidates and their surrogates will speak Monday in North Carolina.

Bill Clinton: Will speak on behalf of Hillary Clinton at the Hilton Hotel at 207 Greenville Boulevard SW in Greenville at 8:30 a.m.

Donald Trump: Will speak at Lenoir-Rhyne University in Hickory at 10 a.m.

Bernie Sanders: Will speak at the PNC Music Pavilion in Charlotte. Doors open at 1:30 p.m.

Ted Cruz: Will speak at Fayetteville Technical Community College at 3 p.m.

Hillary Clinton: Will speak at Grady Cole Center at Central Piedmont Community College in Charlotte at 9:15 p.m.
