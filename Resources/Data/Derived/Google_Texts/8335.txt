HHS Rule Provides Broad Anti-Discrimination Protections

Law360, New York (September 9, 2015, 1:22 PM ET) -- On Sept. 8, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services' Office of Civil Rights published a sweeping proposed rule implementing Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act. The rule provides that individuals cannot be subject to discrimination based on their race, color, national origin, sex, age or disability in certain health programs and activities.
