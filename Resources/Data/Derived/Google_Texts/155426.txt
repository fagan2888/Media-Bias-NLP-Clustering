Charleston County authorities responded Friday to Station 26 on Sullivan’s Island, where the body of a Mount Pleasant woman was found in the surf.

An autopsy has determined that a woman whose body was found Friday morning in the surf on Sullivan’s Island died from drowning, authorities said.

But the manner of death for Joanna Joly, 25, of Mount Pleasant, is still under investigation, Charleston County Deputy Coroner Kimberly Rhoton said in a news release.

Authorities don’t know why she drowned. The Charleston County Sheriff’s Office is investigating the death, Rhoton said.

Anyone with information about Joly’s death should call the Sheriff’s Office at 202-1700.
