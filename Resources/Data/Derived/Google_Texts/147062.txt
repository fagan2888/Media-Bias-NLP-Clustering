Tucson Police is on the scene of an accident involving two vehicles at Pima and Craycroft.

One of the vehicles is a motorcycle.

The westbound lanes of Pima are blocked.

Tucson Police Department's Media Relations Officer Kris Goins the motorcyclist was taken to the hospital with life-threatening injuries.

Reporter Monica Grimaldo is at the scene gathering more details.
