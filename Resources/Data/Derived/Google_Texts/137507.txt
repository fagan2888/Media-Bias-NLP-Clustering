Updated at 2:11 p.m.:

Police located Ramirez at 2:06 p.m. after he had been missing for almost five hours. He has been transported to a local hospital.

Original post:

Dallas police are asking for the public’s help in locating 24-year-old Francisco Ramirez, who has been missing since Sunday morning.

According to police, Ramirez was last seen walking in the 400 block of South Clinton Avenue in the Winnetka Heights neighborhood at about 9:15 a.m.

Ramirez is described as 5-foot-3, weighs 150 pounds and has black hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a black shirt, black shorts and black high-top Nike shoes.

According to police, Ramirez is “distraught and is considered a danger to himself.”

Anyone with information on Ramirez’s whereabouts is asked to call 911 or contact the Dallas Police Department at 214-671-4268.
