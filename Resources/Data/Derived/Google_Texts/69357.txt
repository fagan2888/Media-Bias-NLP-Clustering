Family members have identified the man found dead in a Martin County mobile home fire on Saturday.

Family at the scene of the fire tells WITN that the victim is believed to be 49-year-old Neal Manning.

Drew Robinson of the Martin County Sheriff's Office tells WITN that only one person was in the home when they received the call around 5 a.m.

He tells us that it happened at 1166 Penny Lane, near Everetts.

The cause of the fire is being investigated by the Martin County Sheriff's Office along with the State Bureau of Investigation. Robinson tells us that his department will not release the name of the victim until after an autopsy is performed.
