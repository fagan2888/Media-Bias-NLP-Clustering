Police are looking for a man who allegedly robbed a southeast Wichita business at knifepoint.

The robbery happened just before 9:30 Saturday evening at Patricia's, an adult store near Pawnee and Rock Road.

The clerk told officers a man with a knife entered the store and demanded cash. He was able to get away with an unknown amount and was last seen running south from the business.

The clerk was not hurt.

Police described the suspect as a white man, standing about 5-foot-10 and weighing 150 pounds. He was wearing blue jeans and a black jacket.

Detectives are currently reviewing surveillance video. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (316) 267-2111.
