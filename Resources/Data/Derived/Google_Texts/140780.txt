A man will appear in a Sydney court charged with indecently assaulting seven teenage girls at the Sydney Olympic Park Aquatic Centre.

The 33-year-old allegedly assaulted the girls when they were swimming in the rapids section on Sunday afternoon, police say.

The victims, aged between 14 and 18, reported the incident to pool security who called police.

The man is in Australia on a bridging visa after arriving six months ago, police say.

He was charged with five counts of indecent assault of a person aged under 16 and two counts of assault with an act of indecency.

He was refused bail and will appear at Burwood Local Court on Monday.

The Department of Immigration and Border Protection has been informed.
