Wichita police are investigating after toddler was taken to the hospital after ingesting a marijuana pill.

Police were called to Wesley Medical Center after a woman took her 17-month-old to the hospital after noticing it was lethargic and unresponsive.

The child's mother told police she had dropped the child off at a babysitter's house in the 2600 block of south Emporia when she went to work.

Police said the child ingested a prescription synthetic marijuana pill while at the babysitter's house.

The condition of the child hasn't been released. Police are investigating the incident and are not ruling out possible charges.
