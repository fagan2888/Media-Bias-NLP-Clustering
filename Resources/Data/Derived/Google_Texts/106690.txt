Wichita police say a 26-year-old man is in custody after allegedly robbing a Walgreens pharmacy at gunpoint.

The robbery happened around 3 a.m. Sunday at the Walgreens at Harry and Hillside. An employee told officers the suspect jumped the pharmacy counter, pointed a gun at him and demanded prescription drugs.

The robber ran off with "several pills," police said, but officers quickly found and arrested him. He was booked for aggravated robbery.
