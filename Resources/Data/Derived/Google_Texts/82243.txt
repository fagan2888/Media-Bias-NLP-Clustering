House damaged by fire in the 2100 block of N. Homestead

A fire caused significant damage to a home in northeast Wichita Sunday morning.

Just before 1 a.m.. Sunday, firefighters responded to a house fire in the 2100 block of N. Homestead. When crews arrived they found heavy fire in the house. Firefighters were able to get the blaze under control.

The fire caused an estimated $200,000 in damages to the home.

One person was injured from the fire and is being treated at St. Francis.

The cause of the fire is still under investigation.
