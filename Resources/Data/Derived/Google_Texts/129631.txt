Rosholt, SD

A Rosholt, SD man is dead after a two vehicle crash near Rosholt on Saturday.

61-year-old Larry Sands was heading eastbound on 112th Street in a Chevrolet Blazer. He entered the uncontrolled intersection on 112th and 474th Avenue at the same time as Kevin Compaan. Compaan was heading south in 474th Avenue in a Mercury Sable. Compaan struck the driver’s side of Sand’s vehicle.

Sands was thrown from the vehicle and died at the scene. He was not wearing a seat belt. Compaan and a minor passenger were wearing seat belts and received minor injuries.

Compaan was cited for failure to yield the right-of-way.

No further information is available.

The South Dakota Highway Patrol is investigating.
