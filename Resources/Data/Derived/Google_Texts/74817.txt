A new South Tulsa business is getting a jump on its grand opening. Workers at Sky Zone trampoline park were putting on the final touches Monday.

When it's done, kids of any age can bounce around or jump in the foam pit. There's even a basketball dunking station and party rooms.

Sky Zone has locations all over the country, but this will be the first one in Oklahoma.

"Sky Zone is an all inclusive, 3-D play experience. It's good for all ages, just about any size, age, and ability," said Manager Keith Snyder.

Sky Zone officially opens December 16, near 61st and Memorial. We're told they're still looking to hire more employees.
