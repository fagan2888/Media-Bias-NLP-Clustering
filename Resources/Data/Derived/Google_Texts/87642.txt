Walton County Commissioners are excited to see new growth in Northern Walton County.

Friday, Commissioners agreed to sell almost 25 acres of land in the Mossy Head Industrial Park to Saad Development.

The cost, $19,000 an acre.

Commissioners will not disclose what business is actually coming in, but they did say it would be a 24/7 distribution facility.

According to Saad Developments website, they have had many projects with FedEx, restaurants, banks, and shopping center.
