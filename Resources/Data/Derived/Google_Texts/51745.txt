A new lineup of fall TV shows and big events like the Republican presidential debates and football season kick-offs weren’t enough to boost television ad dollars in the most recent quarter.

Television ad spending was flat in the third quarter from a year earlier, according to new data from Standard Media Index, which reports on 80% of U.S. ad agency spending.
