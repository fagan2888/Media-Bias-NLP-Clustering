Dallas police are looking for a 27-year-old man believed to be a danger to himself.

Donjuwan Lenier Baker was last seen driving a 2001 Ford Escape in the 3500 block of Timberglen Road, in far north Dallas. The SUV had the Texas license plate BSX2510.

He was wearing a blue dress shirt with a while collar, a black jacket and dark blue slacks with pinstripes. He appeared distraught, police said.

He is described as black, 5-foot-6 and 160 pounds.

Anyone who knows Baker’s whereabouts is asked to call Dallas police’s missing person’s unit at (214)-671-4268.
