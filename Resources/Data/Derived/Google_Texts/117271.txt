Medline Escapes Contract Suit Over ACA Tax

Law360, Chicago (February 12, 2015, 5:49 PM ET) -- Distributor Medline Industries Inc. was within its rights to back out of a contract after supplier Chemence Medical Products Inc. attempted to pass on to Medline the cost of the Affordable Care Act’s medical device tax, a Georgia federal judge ruled Wednesday.



Under its supply contract for Chemence’s Octylseal surgical adhesive, Medline was permitted to terminate the pact in response to a price increase, which was essentially what the tax amounted to, U.S. District Judge Thomas W. Thrash said in an opinion granting Medline summary judgment...
