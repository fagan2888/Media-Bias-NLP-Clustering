A man was taken into custody when an alleged theft victim was able to tell police where some of his property was.

Police say the items were stolen during a vehicle break-in. They didn't specify what was stolen, but say the victim was able to "track" some of it. That lead officers to the 400 block of West Garden of the Gods Road in Colorado Springs.

Police say when they arrived, they found Robert Mack, 20, in a vehicle and some of the missing items reportedly were in plain sight inside the vehicle.

Mack was taken into custody.
