<p>Emergency light on vehicle</p>

A 54-year-old Camarillo man was killed Sunday night after being hit by a vehicle at the intersection of Mobil Avenue and East Ponderosa Drive in Camarillo.

Police said the incident was reported at 7:47 p.m. when a motorist is suspected of hitting the pedestrian and then speeding off. The driver was apprehended nearby.

Police said the incident remained under investigation Sunday night and they were looking at whether drugs or alcohol may have been involved in the incident.

It was unclear if the man was crossing the street or was on the sidewalk when he was hit.

Police said the man was declared dead at the scene. His name was not released pending notification of next of kin.
