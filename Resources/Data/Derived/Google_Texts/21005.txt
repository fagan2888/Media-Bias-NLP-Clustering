Bethel Police say that a man is behind bars after he struck a person in the face with an end table leg.

33-year-old Latavis Wiggins is currently being held in the Pitt County Detention Center and is charged with felony assault with a deadly weapon inflicting serious injury.

Police officials say the attack happened on February 8th when Wiggins got into an altercation and struck the victim in the face with an end table leg.

That victim was taken to Vidant Medical Center for further treatment.

Wiggins is being held on a $25,000 secured bond.
