1095 Mate® is 1094-C and 1095-C filing software for U.S. employers and tax professionals required to comply with the shared responsibility provisions of the Affordable Care Act (ACA). 1095 Mate supports paper printing and electronic filing of Forms 1095-C (Employer-Provided Health Insurance Offer and Coverage) and Forms 1094-C (Transmittal of Employer-Provided Health Insurance Offer and Coverage Information Returns). The software supports unlimited employers, employees, covered individuals and ACA tax forms.

1095 Mate® is designed to be installed on stand-alone computers, over a network or hosted in the cloud. Trial download is available from www.realtaxtools.com .
