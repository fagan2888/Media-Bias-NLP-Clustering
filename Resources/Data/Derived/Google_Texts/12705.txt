The tangled web of South Asian relations was visible on the sidelines of the global climate summit in Paris last week. Pakistan’s Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif met with Afghanistan’s President Ashraf Ghani. More dramatically, Mr. Sharif sat with India’s Prime Minister Narendra Modi at a time when no formal talks were being held between the two countries.
