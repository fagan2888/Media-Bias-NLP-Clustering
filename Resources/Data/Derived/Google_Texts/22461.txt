When making New Year’s resolutions for 2015, be sure to add learning about Ohio’s Sunshine Laws.

The Sunshine Laws appropriately refer to the rules and regulations for public bodies when conducting public meetings, as well as the public records laws.

But laws can be complicated things. Even though the Attorney General’s office will give you a “Yellow Book,” as the free publication documenting the laws is called, it’s not always easy to know what your local council or school board should and shouldnt’ do.
