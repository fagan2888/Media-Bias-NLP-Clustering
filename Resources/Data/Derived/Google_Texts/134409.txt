One person was killed in an Elizabeth City neighborhood Sunday.

Police say 38-year-old Willie James, Jr. was shot around 5:45 this morning at 311 Harney St.

James ran from that location to a home on Maple Street where police found him suffering from an apparent gunshot wound to his upper body.

James was transported to Albermarle Hospital then to Norfolk Sentara by Nightingale where he died.

Police are following up on leads and the investigation is ongoing.

If you have information that could help police, you're urged to call (252) 335-4321 or (252) 335-5555. All information received will remain confidential.

The Elizabeth City PD will have a news conference tomorrow at 2 p.m. at the H. Rick Gardner Municipal Building to provide an update on the investigation.
