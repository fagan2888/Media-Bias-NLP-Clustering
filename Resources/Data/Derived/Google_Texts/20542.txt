Philadelphia

‘Available Light” (1983)—a 55-minute, two-part dance work with choreography by Lucinda Childs, music by John Adams and design by Frank Gehry—is having a revival this year. First shown in its current restaging in June in Los Angeles, the 11-dancer presentation played Philadelphia’s 2015 Fringe Festival last week in the cavernous Drexel Armory; touring plans take the production to international venues into next year.
