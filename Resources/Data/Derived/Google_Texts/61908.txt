Panama City, FL---Saturday at Tommy Oliver Stadium, track and field teams from the area lined up against each other as they competed in the Panhandle Open.

Final heats in the running events began at one, including the girls 4 x 100 relay. In the race shown, the Bay Tornadoes crossed the finish line first.

In the boy's 4 x 100 relay, Marianna's team able to come up with their win. The winners of all races were determined by time trials across all the finals.

Field events going on alongside throughout the day including the pole vault and the long jump.
