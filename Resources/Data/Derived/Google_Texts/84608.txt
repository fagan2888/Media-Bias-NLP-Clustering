PHOENIX, July 15 (UPI) -- One person was fatally stabbed early Wednesday in a fight between coworkers at a US Airways training center near Sky Harbor International Airport in Phoenix.

Police said the altercation between two flight-simulator technicians ended violently about 4 a.m. Wednesday. The victim was taken to a local hospital, where he was pronounced dead.

No further details about the incident, including names or possible arrests, were immediately available.

The facility is located west of the airport. The airport operations were not disrupted.
