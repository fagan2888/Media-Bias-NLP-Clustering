Sioux Falls, SD

Bill Maher is not directly affiliated with any Democratic Party fundraiser as an earlier version of this story suggested. His performance is open to the public.

Political commentator, comedian, and television show host Bill Maher will be stopping in Sioux Falls this fall for a stand-up comedy performance.

Maher will be performing at the Washington Pavilion September 13 this year. Tickets run for $85, $78, $68, $55, and $20.

You can get tickets on Washington Pavilion's website, through their box office or by calling (605)-367-6000.
