The Lubbock Police Department reports a man stole a case of beer from a local convenience store early Sunday morning.

The incident occurred at the 7-Eleven, located at 5002 Quaker Avenue, after 1:00 a.m.

Authorities tell EverythingLubbock.com the suspect, described as a Hispanic male, entered the convenience store and was believed to have been armed.

Lubbock Police said the suspect did not take any money but did take a case of beer.

Officers later located the suspect's vehicle and a chase ensued. The chase was canceled after it was deemed too dangerous by officers.

The Slaton Police Department later picked up the pursuit of the suspect vehicle. The suspect was eventually captured outside of Lubbock County.

Authorities have not released the name of the suspect.
