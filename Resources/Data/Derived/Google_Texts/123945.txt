A Ness City man is dead after losing control of the van he was driving and crashing it into a Ness City home.

The Kansas Highway Patrol reports at just after 4 p.m. Saturday, Henry Hildebrandt, 61, was driving southbound when he crashed into the home in the 500 block of S Topeka.

Hildebrandt was taken to a hospital where he was later pronounced dead.

The KHP reports he was wearing a seat belt at the time of the crash. no one else was hurt.
