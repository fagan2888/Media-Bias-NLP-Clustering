A DeFuniak Springs man is behind bars after police charged him Saturday for attempted robbery and burglary.

Reports state 28-year-old Bobby Lee Chaney was arrested and has been charged with attempted robbery, two counts of burglary, and two counts of criminal mischief.

Police state, Chaney admitted that he tried to rob That Pizza Place in DeFuniak Springs. He also told officers he burglarized Thriftway Supermarket and H&M hot dog stand back in March.

Chaney is also on probation for grand theft.

He was booked in the Walton County Jail.
