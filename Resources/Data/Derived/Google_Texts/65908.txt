Springfield's Kallysa McGeary clears the hurdle on her way to first place in the 100 hurdles in 17.06 during the Holly Invitational on Saturday, April 18. Granada's Kylee Holden (let) placed second and Ciarra Tate of Baca County (right) was third. McGeary also took first in the 300 hurdles in the Holly meet. McGeary ranks first in the 100 hurdles and third in the 300 hurdles in the Class 1A state track rankings. (John Contreras/The Lamar Ledger) Whittney Westphal anchors Springfield's 800 medley relay team to victory with a time of 2:00.07 in the Holly Invitational on Saturday, April 18. Otther members of the team included Ally Loflin, Demi Ownbey, and Brenna Hendricks. Their time of 2:00.07 moved the team into first in the event in the Class 1A state rankings.
