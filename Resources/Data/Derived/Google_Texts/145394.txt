Texas Insider Report: AUSTIN,Texas — (Lakeway)– The Dr. Dawn Buckingham for Texas State Senate Campaign announces the endorsement of Peggy Venable, leading Texas Conservative and former Reagan Administration appointee.

“Dawn Buckingham is the true fiscal conservative senate district 24 needs” said Peggy Venable. “She has a proven conservative track record of working to cut state spending and will cut government regulations, lifting burdens off Texas businesses. I am pleased to give her my endorsement.”

Peggy Venable has worked tirelessly as an advocate for Texas taxpayers and promotes free market policies. She was convention director for the 1984 Republican National Convention and served in the Reagan Administration as White House Liaison for the US Departments of Education and Interior. A former TV and radio talk show host, Venable is a speaker and commentator.
