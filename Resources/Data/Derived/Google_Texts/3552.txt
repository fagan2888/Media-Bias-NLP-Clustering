3 Hottest Industries For Deal-Making In 2016

Law360, New York (January 5, 2016, 3:54 PM ET) -- The health care, technology and energy industries are ripe for deal-making in the coming year as industry-specific trends are set to drum up mergers between competitors, merger and acquisition exits and distressed deals, experts say.



Although it is questionable whether deal-making will be able to match up to the record pace of 2015, the year ahead is still expected to be a strong one for M&A as evolving industries leave companies in fear of being left behind.
