LAHORE: Pakistan Baitul Maal on Thursday announced financial help for a patient of Thalassemia after his plight was reported by Geo News.



Geo News had aired a news story from Lahore, showing the plight of a 19 year boy who could not afford the treatment. His brother had earlier died of Thalassemia.



Pakistan Baitul Maal took notice of the news and announced financial help for the patient.
