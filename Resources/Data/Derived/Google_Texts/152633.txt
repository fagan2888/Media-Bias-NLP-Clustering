NEWPORT NEWS, Va. (WAVY) – Police are investigating a shooting at a gentleman’s club in Newport News early Wednesday morning.

Sgt. Randal Bailey with the Newport News Police Department told WAVY.com officers responded to the Liquid Blue Gentleman’s Club located at 606 Dresden Dr. just after 1 a.m. after hearing several gunshots. There, they found a 34-year-old man with a gunshot wound to the leg.

Bailey said it looks as though there was an altercation in the parking lot. The victim was treated and released at a local hospital and his wound was minor.

The victim was reportedly uncooperative in the investigation.
