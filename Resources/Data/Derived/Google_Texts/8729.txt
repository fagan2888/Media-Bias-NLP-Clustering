Two Tallahassee men are dead, and a woman was injured after their helicopter crashed into a Wakullt County Marshland.

Investigators say the three were flying back to Tallahassee after visiting a restaurant near the coast.

53-year-old Curtis Robert Clifford and 49 year old Terry Bryan Ooten were found dead in the Tallahassee Helicopters wtxl labeled chopper.

The lone survivor is 45 year old Sheri Noelle Devore.

Officials believe the helicopter had just taken off when it hit trees close to the Wakulla County Airport's runway.

The helicopter then cut nearby power lines, and came to rest in a marshy area.
