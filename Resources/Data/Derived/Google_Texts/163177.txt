An expectant father has drowned following a boat accident Sunday morning in western Sedgwick County.

Rescuers were called around 8:20 a.m. to the report of people in the water at a private pond at Fidelity Farms near North 391st Street West and Central. That's about two miles south of Cheney Reservoir.

Authorities said two people were duck hunting in a small boat when it tipped over. One person was able to swim to shore. He was not hurt.

Divers recovered the body of Aaron Travis at around 9:30 a.m. Family say Travis was expecting his first child in May.
