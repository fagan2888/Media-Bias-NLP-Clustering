The Lake County Liberty Coalition will meet at 7 p.m., July 16, in the Fellowship Hall of the Faith Lutheran Church, 8125 Mentor Ave. in Mentor.

Executive Director Chuck Laughlin will be speaking on the topic What Do We Do Now? and the public is invited to attend. This is in light of the recent Supreme Court rulings on ObamaCare and on same sex marriage.

The Lake County Liberty Coalition is a grass roots, non-partisan organization whose purpose is to defend and promote traditional values as well as the principles of constitutionally limited government, fiscal responsibility, and free markets. More information on the organization is available at www.lakecountylibertycoalition.com/.
