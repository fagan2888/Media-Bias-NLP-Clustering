Two people are in the hospital after a crash Friday in Jackson County.

20 year old Kayla Smith from Sneads was trying to make a left off US 231 onto Tendell Road when 56 year old Sue Kennedy from St. Louis Missouri hit the side of her car.

Smith was transported to Bay Medical Sacred Heart Hospital with critical injuries.

Kennedy was sent to Southeast Alabama Medical Center with serious injuries.

The Florida Highway Patrol says alcohol was not a contributing factor.

Charges are pending further investigation.
