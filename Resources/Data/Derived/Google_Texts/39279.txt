A passerby is recovering from knife wounds after choosing to intervene when he overheard a man and woman arguing inside a Wichita home.

It happened at about 10 p.m. Saturday. Wichita Police say the 44-year-old man was walking by a home in the 200 block of West Lincoln when he heard the screams of a woman from inside a home.

He went inside to home and was confronted by a 28-year-old man. A confrontation ensued. The younger man grabbed a knife, cutting the victim on the shoulder and causing minor injuries. EMS treated the victim on scene.

Police arrested and booked the suspect on charges of aggravated battery. The 61-year-old woman inside the home was not injured.
