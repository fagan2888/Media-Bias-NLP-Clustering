Authorities responded to a call of shots fired and found a man with gunshot wounds early on Sunday, March 27.

Sgt. Pete Dugan, spokesman for the Tucson Police Department, said the call came in just before 5 a.m. from an apartment complex in the 720 block of South Tucson Boulevard.

Officers immediately began first aid on the man in the parking lot, according to Dugan. He said firefighters took over the care when they arrived, but the man was pronounced dead soon after.

The man has been identified as DeAntae Lee Fuller, according to Dugan.

There is no suspect description at this time and no arrests have been made.

Anyone with information should call Tucson Police Department or report tips anonymously through 88-CRIME.
