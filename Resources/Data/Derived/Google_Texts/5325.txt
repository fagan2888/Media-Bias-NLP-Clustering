The South Carolina Highway Patrol responded to a deadly wreck shortly before 7:30 p.m. Saturday.

Troopers said the wreck involved two vehicles on SC Highway 225.

Greenwood County Coroner Sonny Cox said Jeremy Dean Cockrell, 27, of Greenwood, was driving one of the vehicles. He died at the scene from blunt force trauma, Cox said.

Troopers said Adam Rowell, 26, of Greenwood, drove a 2005 Chevy truck. Investigators said Rowell, who was not wearing a seat belt, had to be extricated and was flown to Greenville Memorial.

The Highway Patrol continues to investigate.
