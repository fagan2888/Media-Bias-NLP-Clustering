Millennium Practice Management Associates of Upper Saddle River, which manages medical practices, has been acquired by a Texas company, according to the Dallas Business Journal today.

Plano-based MediGain LLC bought Millennium as part of its aggressive growth strategy, which is getting a boost from the Affordable Care Act, the journal said. Financial details of the deal were not disclosed, the journal said.

The deal will "allow us to help providers around the country to obtain the reimbursement they deserve," Millennium Chief Executive Officer Gregg Stolzberg told the journal.
