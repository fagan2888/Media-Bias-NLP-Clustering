McDermott Will & Emery Launches Health Care Lobbying Arm

Law360, Washington (April 2, 2014, 7:04 PM ET) -- McDermott Will & Emery LLP announced Wednesday that it has launched a new lobbying and consultancy firm focusing on helping the health care industry deal with the Affordable Care Act and other legislative challenges, the law firm's first industry-specific lobbying arm.
