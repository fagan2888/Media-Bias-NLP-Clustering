YouTube user Gigawipf made a thing of beauty when he programmed a bunch of floppy disk drives to play Soft Cell’s 1980s hit “Tainted Love,” itself a cover of Gloria Jones’s 1964 hit. Though Gigawipf has programmed several songs to play on floppy disks, this video recorded in 2012 is by far the most successful — most likely because the old technology adds another layer to the nostalgia. But there’s one thing missing in Gigawipf’s version — the vocals.
