(Photo : Instagram) Katy Perry and Charli XCX will be touring together for the European leg of the Prismatic World Tour!

Katy Perry and Charli XCX were quite friendly at the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards but nobody could've guessed the incredible surprise of the duo's friendship!



'This Is How We Do' singer Katy Perry announced via Twitter that the "Boom Clap" songstress will serve as her opener for the European leg of her upcoming Prismatic Tour, MTV Reports.
