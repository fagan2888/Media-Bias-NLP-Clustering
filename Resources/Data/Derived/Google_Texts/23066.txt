Scene of semi overturned on I-71 northbound near mile marker 21 in Oldham County. (Photo: WHAS11.com)

According to a report from WHAS11.com, emergency services are responding to an overturned semi on I-71 northbound near mile marker 20 in Oldham County.

The Kentucky Transportation Cabinet's website reports that both lanes are currently blocked and to use the shoulder as a lane.

Authorities told WHAS11.com that "long-term closure" is possible.
