Off the Couch: If you need motivation to exercise, consider Walk With a Doc. The nonprofit program has 148 chapters, mostly in the U.S., offering free 30- to 45-minute doctor-led walks in parks and malls. The goal: to encourage people to get the 150 minutes a week of moderate aerobic activity recommended by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Most Walk With a Doc chapters have weekly walks, often on Saturday mornings.
