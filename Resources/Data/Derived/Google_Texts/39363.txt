Car damaged by fire on E. Magill St.

Man injured in early morning vehicle fire in northeast Wichita.

It happened around 3:30 a.m. Saturday in the 7000 block of E. Magill, where fire crews responded to a report of a car fire.

Lt. Troy Thissen stated that upon arrival, firefighters found the car on fire. As they went to open the passenger compartment, firefighters found a man inside the vehicle.

The victim was transported to St. Francis for treatment. His condition is currently unknown.

The cause of the fire is still under investigation.
