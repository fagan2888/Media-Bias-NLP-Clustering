The 2015 LGBT Health Fair will be Nov. 17 from 3 to 7 p.m.

The Council on Aging of Buncombe County will join with Western Carolina Medical Society at First Congregational UUC on 20 Oak Street, Asheville for 2015 WNC LGBT Health Fair on Tuesday, November 17th from 3 pm to 7 pm. (Photo: File photo)

ASHEVILLE - The Council on Aging of Buncombe County and the Western North Carolina Medical Society want to make sure the region's LGBT population has access to health care.

The 2015 LGBT Health Fair will be Nov. 17 from 3-7 p.m. in downtown Asheville at the First Congregational United Church of Christ, 20 Oak St. It is free and open to the public.

Organizers intend to promote the physical and mental health of the LGBT community in Western North Carolina by providing access to services, knowledge about area resources and information on the Affordable Care Act.

For more information, contact Susan Wilson at wnclgbthealthfair@gmail.com.
