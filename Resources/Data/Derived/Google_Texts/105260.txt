ATLANTA—With the Nets trailing the top-seeded Atlanta Hawks, 1-0, in their first-round playoff series, coach Lionel Hollins delivered some surprisingly positive news at Tuesday’s practice, describing reserve forward Mirza Teletovic as “probable” for Wednesday’s Game 2.

It was only three months ago that Teletovic’s season was supposedly over, according to the team. There was no guarantee, in fact, that he would ever play again.
