A man was killed Saturday afternoon after being struck by a car while crossing Parker Road outside of the crosswalk area, officials said.

The man, who has not been identified, was pronounced dead on the scene of southbound Parker Road just south of Interstate 225. The driver who hit him while he was crossing the road in front of traffic, said Colorado State Patrol spokesman Nate Reid.

The driver who hit him stayed on scene after the crash, state patrol said.

Drugs or alcohol are not expected to be involved, Reid said.

Two lanes of traffic are shut down due to the crash.
