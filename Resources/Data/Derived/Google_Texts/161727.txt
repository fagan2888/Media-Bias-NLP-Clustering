Three adults and a child were hospitalized following a rollover crash Friday afternoon in southwest Kansas.

The crash happened around 5:45 p.m. on K-156 just east of Kalvesta. The Kansas Highway Patrol said a BMW sedan was heading east on the highway and lost control of a curve. The car went off the road and into a ditch before it overturned.

The driver and a passenger suffered serious injuries. They are identified as 45-year-old Dion Harley of Shawnee and 22-year-old Jadell Blevins of Dodge City.

A four-year-old girl, Coey Harley, was also seriously hurt.

A fourth person in the car, Desiree Harley of Shawnee, was treated for unknown injuries.

The crash is under investigation.
