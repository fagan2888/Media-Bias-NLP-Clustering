Cigna Agrees To End Controversial HIV/AIDS Drug Program

Law360, New York (October 9, 2015, 1:58 PM ET) -- Cigna Health and Life Insurance Co. on Thursday agreed to remove certain HIV/AIDS medications from a list of drugs that must be obtained through mail order, settling a proposed nationwide class action in Florida that alleged the list was discriminatory.
