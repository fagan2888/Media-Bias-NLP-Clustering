Two people sustained minor injuries early Sunday morning in separate, but similar, shooting incidents in Wichita.

The first shooting happened around 3:30 a.m. at Hillside and Roseberry, which is south of Pawnee. Police said a 20-year-old woman was driving in the area with a friend when someone stated shooting at her vehicle.

The woman was hurt by flying glass from the driver side window.

Officers found several shell casings in the area. No suspect information was made available.

The second shooting happened about an hour later on South I-135 near Hydraulic. Two men, ages 20 and 27, were driving down the interstate when someone in a black 2000s model Dodge Charger fired one shot at their vehicle. the bullet shattered the driver side window. The driver suffered minor injuries from flying glass.

Police had no other suspect information.
