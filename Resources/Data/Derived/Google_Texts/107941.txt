The town of Marana just announced a job fair to staff the new premium outlet mall that's slated to open in the fall on Twin Peaks Road near Interstate 10.

More than 80 retailers will need hundreds of staff to be ready in time for grand opening Oct. 1, according to a post on the town blog, Marana 365.

The job fair is set for 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. Aug. 20 at the El Conquistador resort in Oro Valley.
