MRI maker Fonar Corp. reported third quarter net income jumped 17 percent to $2.5 million as revenues rose 0.3 percent to $17.1 million.

The Melville-based firm reported income from operations rose 15 percent to $2.7 million as it continued to market an operate its Upright Multi-Position MRI.

President Raymond Damadian said he was happy that the “periodic cuts in MRI reimbursements brought about by the Affordable Care Act have subsided, at least for now.”

The firm on Jan. 8, 2015, acquired 20 percent of the Class A members ownership interest in Health Diagnostic Management for $5.0 million, increasing its stake to 60.4 percent from 50.5 percent.
