Heavy rain is blamed for a crash that killed a Kansas woman Saturday afternoon.

The crash happened just after 12 p.m. on highway 54 near Pratt. The sheriff's office said a Pontiac Vibe was heading west when the driver crossed the center line and hit an oncoming car. Witnesses told deputies it was raining heavily when the crash happened.

The 87-year-old woman driving the Pontiac was ejected and died at the scene. She is identified as Velma Base of Garden City.

Two people in the other car, 52-year-old Sang Ngoc To and a 53-year-old Que Thi Nguyen were taken to a nearby hospital for treatment of serious injuries. They are also from Garden City.

The Pratt County Sheriff's Office is still investigating.
