On the heels of the second main-stage Republican presidential debate, a veteran GOP campaign strategist says candidates should be reviewing Wednesday night's game tape as they prep for the next stage of the race.

Chris Henick, founder and president of Blueprint Advisors, tells The Hill's Molly K. Hooper that each candidate and campaign team should pay close attention to how "suitable" and "likable" they came off during the three-hour debate.
