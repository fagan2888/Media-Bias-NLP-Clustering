Two people are dead after a shooting around 12:30 a.m. Saturday near Churchill Downs.

When police arrived on the scene in the 800 block of West Evelyn Avenue, they found one man dead in the street. The second victim was taken to University of Louisville Hospital where he later died, said Louisville Metro Police spokeswoman Alicia Smiley.

The Homicide Unit is investigating the incident. Anyone with information is asked to call the crime tip hotline at 502-574-LMPD.
