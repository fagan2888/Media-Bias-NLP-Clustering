A trial for Cyr has been postponed.

A trial has been postponed for a suspect in the death of a pregnant Blanchard mother.

Joseph Cyr, 34, is facing two counts of first-degree murder in the death of Jaymie Adams and her unborn child.

1/25/2016 Related Story: Jury Selected In Joseph Cyr Murder Trial

A jury of eight men and four women was selected for his trial on Monday. However, the trial had to be postponed on Tuesday, because the public defender is in the hospital.

A new trial date has not been determined.
