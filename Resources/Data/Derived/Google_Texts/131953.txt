“Demystifying the Hipster”

College: Tufts, Medford, Mass.

Course description: “The hipster is a divisive cultural figure that elicits both envy and outrage, and some argue that it has run its course—but what exactly is (or was) the hipster? Are hipsters part of a counter-culture, or are they just another marketing niche in the mainstream? How can we tell the difference?”
