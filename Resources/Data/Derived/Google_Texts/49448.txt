A woman was arrested late Saturday morning after allegedly dragging her boyfriend with her car, leaving him with severe road rash.

The incident happened around 11:15 a.m. in the 6000 block of Croyden Circle, near 21st Street North and Woodlawn.

Police say couple, both 22 years old, were arguing when the man reached in his girlfriend's car to get his cell phone.

The woman drove off, dragging her boyfriend for about half of a mile before falling off. He suffered severe road rash to most of his back. He was treated and released.

The girlfriend was arrested for aggravated battery domestic violence.
