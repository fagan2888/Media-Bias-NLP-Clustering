Bicycle Group Kent Buys Stoneridge Cycle

The buyer sells bikes and accessories under the Kent, BCA, Goodyear, Razor and X-Games brands

Kent International Inc. has acquired Stoneridge Cycle Ltd., a bicycle supplier, for an undisclosed amount.

Ontario, Canada-based Stoneridge supplies bicycles and accessories through Canadian retailers. Terms of the deal were not disclosed.

Kent, headquartered in Parsippany, New Jersey, supplies imported bicycles. The company also designs bicycles and related accessories, which it sells through stores including Toys 'R' Us, Walmart (NYSE: WMT) and Amazon (Nasdaq: AMZN).
