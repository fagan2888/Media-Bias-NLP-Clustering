An Arlington man was fatally struck early Saturday morning by a drunk driving suspect, Arlington police said.

Around 2:15 a.m. police responded to a report of a crash in the 2600 block of West Division Street, near South Bowen Road, and found Terry Dow lying in the roadway.

Police said investigators determined Dow was struck by a pickup truck while trying to cross West Division Street. He was transported to an area hospital, but later died of his injuries.
