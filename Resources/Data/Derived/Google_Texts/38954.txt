June 20, 2014

[Washington, D.C.] U.S. Rep. Paul Ryan (R-Wisconsin) showed his displeasure with the IRS on Friday when he ripped into Commissioner John Koskinen for losing Lois Lerner's emails and what he called a "pattern of abuse." The outburst came during a House Ways and Means Committee Hearing investigating the IRS for its targeting of conservative groups.
