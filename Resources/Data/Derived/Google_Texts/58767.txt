There's a movement afoot to reform the U.S. patent system to combat "patent trolls," businesses that buy patents from inventors and then sue firms that use them without payment. The core claim is that trolls discourage innovation through expensive and wasteful litigation that often extracts exorbitant payments from manufacturing companies.
