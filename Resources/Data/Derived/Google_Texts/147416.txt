Cuyahoga GOP Lincoln Day dinner to feature senators, straw poll: U.S. Sens. Joni Ernst of Iowa and Rob Portman of Ohio are set to speak at the Republican Party of Cuyahoga County's 24th Annual Lincoln Day Dinner in Cleveland. During the event, the county party plans to hold its first-ever presidential straw poll, open to those who buy a ticket to the dinner.

Republicans to debate in Florida: The 12th GOP presidential debate is set to start at 8:30 p.m. Eastern time at the University of Miami. The event will be aired on CNN.

Abortion rights leader to campaign for Clinton: NARAL Pro-Choice America President Ilyse Hogue is set to campaign for Hillary Clinton in Akron and Kent, holding organizing events with local students and community leaders.

Portman subcommittee to release Obamacare report: The U.S. Senate Permanent Subcommittee on Investigations, chaired by Portman, is expected to release a report alleging waste and mismanagement within the Obamacare co-op loan program. The subcommittee is scheduled to meet at 9:30 a.m.

Cincy lawmakers to hold public forum: Four Cincinnati-area Democratic state lawmakers are planning a public community forum on issues affecting working people and families in Ohio. The forum is scheduled to take place from 5:30 p.m. until 7:00 p.m. at New Prospect Baptist Church in Cincinnati.

Strickland to attend GOTV phone bank: Democratic U.S. Senate candidate Ted Strickland plans to join volunteers for an afternoon "get-out-the-vote" phone bank at Ohio Democratic Party headquarters in Columbus.

Medical Marijuana Task Force meets: The Ohio House's Medicinal Marijuana Task Force, created by state lawmakers to study the issue, is expected to hold a 7 p.m. meeting at the Statehouse.

Others to watch: Hillary Clinton is expected to campaign in Florida and North Carolina. Bernie Sanders plans to spend the day in Florida.
