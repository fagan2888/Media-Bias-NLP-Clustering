The Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office is searching for a 16-year-old inmate who escaped from the Department of Juvenile Justice (DJJ)Facility on Straight Line Road in Crestview Saturday night.

Noe Dominguez escaped around 7:15 p.m. and was seen heading east into the woods near the facility.

Deputies were unable to locate Dominguez following an extensive search by multiple agencies. Dominguez is a Hispanic male, 5'10" tall weighing 150 pounds with black hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a purple shirt, blue shorts and black tennis shoes.

Dominguez was being held at DJJ on burglary and theft related charges. Anyone who sees Dominguez should contact the Okaloosa County Sheriff's.
