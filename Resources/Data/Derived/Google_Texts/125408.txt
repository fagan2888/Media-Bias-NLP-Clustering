Authorities searched for a missing woman with dementia

Jean Vesey, an 89-year-old Brick woman who has dementia, went missing Monday. (Photo: NEW JERSEY STATE POLICE)

BRICK - Authorities found an 89-year-old woman with dementia who went missing Wednesday afternoon.

Jean Vesey was last seen at her house in Brick at 3 p.m. Monday, driving a tan 2011 Chevrolet Malibu, according to a release from New Jersey State Police.

She was found safe, according to a post on the police department's Facebook page.
