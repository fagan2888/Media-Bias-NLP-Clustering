Photo: Getty Two of the largest insurers in the pension risk transfer market, MetLife and Prudential, have issued new reports on sponsors’ mounting interest in moving pension liabilities from their books. MetLife’s Pension Risk Transfer Poll, a new survey of about 230 defined benefit plan sponsors, found that 45 percent of large plan sponsors have taken proactive steps toward an eventual pension risk transfer.
