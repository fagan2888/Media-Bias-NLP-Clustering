People celebrate in front of the Supreme Court after the ruling was announced on Obamacare subsidies on June 25, 2015. (Photo: Mark Wilson, Getty Images)

The Supreme Court on Thursday upheld the insurance subsidies created under Obamacare, ruling that federal subsidies can be offered in both state and federal health care exchanges. Chief Justice John Roberts wrote the 6-3 decision.
