A family is safe after a fire at their home early Saturday morning.

Crews were called around 6 a.m. to the home near Chalet Drive and Capri Lane, which is northeast of Pawnee and Rock Road. When firefighters arrived, they found heavy fire emanating from the front of the garage area.

Lt. Jeff Holmes said eight people and two dogs got out safely. He added that crews did a good job keeping the fire contained to the garage.

The fire caused about $40,000 in damage. The cause is still under investigation.
