Hospitals don't have to be scary places. Actually, many are quite beautiful. A new survey by Soliant Health, in fact, has named the Top 20 Most Beautiful Hospitals of 2015, highlighting a mix of facilities from across the country.

Soliant, an Atlanta-based healthcare staffing firm, said it received more than 388,000 votes in this year's survey. In reality, 21 hospitals made the list since two hospitals tied with votes for the number 20 spot. Texas and Florida both had the most hospitals on the list.
