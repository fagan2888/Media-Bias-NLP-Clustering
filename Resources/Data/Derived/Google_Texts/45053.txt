A Waukesha County traffic accident near Nashotah on Sunday night killed two motorists.

State Highway 16 was closed in both directions at County Highway C following the crash around 7:20 p.m. Westbound traffic was diverted at County Highway C while eastbound traffic was diverted at Sawyer Road.

Details of the crash were not released late Sunday by the Waukesha County Sheriff's Department.

Also Sunday, a one-vehicle fatal accident was reported in Milwaukee near the intersection of S. 35th St. and W. Hayes Ave. around 8:30 p.m. Details on that crash were also not released late Sunday.
