× 15-pound baby born in California

CALIFORNIA — A 15-pound baby was born in California Thursday.

Andrew Jacob did more than tip the scales when he entered this world on Thursday, weighing in at 15 pounds, 2 ounces and measuring 24 inches long.

He was delivered by cesarean section at Desert Valley Medical Center.

Andrew Jacob did not break the world record for the biggest baby born, but he is believed to be one of the largest babies delivered at the hospital, according to Victor Valley News.

His proud parents are Vanessa and Daniel Cervantez of Hesperia, Calif.
