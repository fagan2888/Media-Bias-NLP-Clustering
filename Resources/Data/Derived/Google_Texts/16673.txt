A man wanted on an arrest warrant for second-degree murder, aggravated motor vehicle theft and lesser charges surrendered at the Weld County Jail Saturday night.

Dmitri Sebastian Manzanares, 24, was wanted in connection with a fatal stabbing early Saturday morning in the 4900 block of 11th Street Road, Greeley police said.

Officers entering the home found the body of a man with a stab wound, police said.

Investigators determined Manzanares and the victim, who has not been identified, were fighting. During the altercation, Manzanares allegedly stabbed the victim and then fled the scene.
