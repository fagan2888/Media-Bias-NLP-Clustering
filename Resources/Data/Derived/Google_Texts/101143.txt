A second suspect has been arrested in the deaths of two men found in a car in Raleigh earlier this month.

Raleigh police said in a statement that 18-year-old Denzel Rashad Dancy was arrested Thursday night in Goldsboro.

Dancy is charged with two counts of murder in the deaths of 24-year-old Reyes Diaz of Raleigh and 25-year-old Allan Uriel Rodriguez of Wendell.

The men were found dead in a car in Raleigh on March 10.

Dancy was arrested by Wayne County sheriff's deputies and Goldsboro police officers. It was not clear if he has an attorney.

Twenty-year-old Daekwon Javon Ragland was arrested March 17 and charged with two counts of murder.
