Wichita Police were initially called out to the report of a shooting and found a man stabbed multiple times in south Wichita.

The incident happened at 3:16 a.m. at Washington and Marion Road, near Pawnee and Broadway.

Police got a call of a shooting and were dispatched to the neighborhood.

"The victim, we don't believe that he sustained any gunshot wounds but possibly has some stab wounds," said Sgt. Christopher Marceau, Wichita Police.

They found the 22-year-old man and rushed him to a hospital. Police say he is in stable condition.

Police are still looking for a suspect.
