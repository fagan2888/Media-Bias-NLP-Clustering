Aberdeen, SD

A Colorado man was arrested in Aberdeen in connection to another man being shot in the head early Saturday morning.

According to the Aberdeen Police Department's Facebook page, police responded to a call of a male being shot in the head at the Hampton Inn & Suites just after 5 a.m. Saturday morning.

Investigation into the incident led to the arrest of 19-year-old Jordan Mercure from Fort Collins, CO. He faces aggravated assault charges.

The mother of the man who was shot tells The American News that her son is out of surgery at Avera McKennan Hospital in Sioux Falls. She said he is in critical but stable condition and is in the intensive care unit.
