Kenneth Faried looked up from his seat in the visitors’ locker room at Talking Stick Resort Arena in Phoenix late Saturday night.

“I’m tougher than you think,” the Nuggets power forward said. “I don’t let much stuff keep me out.”

And yet a back injury can take even the toughest person down. Faried has been dealing with back soreness for a while now. Playing down low, he gets hit in the back several times every game, so he has proved to be tough. But back spasms cropped up in the first half against the Phoenix Suns on Saturday night, forcing him from the game.

Faried is listed as day to day but has designs on playing when the Nuggets resume their road trip at New Orleans on Tuesday night.

Faried, who started strong this season, had two points, seven rebounds and a steal in 14 minutes, and then his back forced him out.
