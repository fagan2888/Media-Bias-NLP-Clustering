DLA Piper Vets Create Health Policy Firm With Akin Gump

Law360, New York (November 21, 2014, 4:05 PM ET) -- Two former DLA Piper strategists have created a new consulting firm that will team up with Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP to advise clients on health policy issues, the strategists said Thursday.



Krista Drobac and Kristen Ratcliff formed Sirona Strategies to focus on health care strategy matters after recently leaving DLA Piper, where they worked in the health care regulatory and policy group.
