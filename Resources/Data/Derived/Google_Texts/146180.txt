DENVER -- The Broncos are making the change from a 4-3 to a 3-4 defense under new Broncos Defensive Coordinator Wade Phillips.

Von Miller & T.J. Ward had the chance to meet with Phillips and they’re excited about the new Broncos defense. Broncos Defensive Tackle Mitch Unrein and former Broncos Tight End Joel Dreessen both played for Broncos Head Coach Gary Kubiak when they were with the Texans.

Dreessen spent five years with Kubiak in Houston, Unrein was with the Texans his rookie year before he was released. They’re excited about Kubiak.
