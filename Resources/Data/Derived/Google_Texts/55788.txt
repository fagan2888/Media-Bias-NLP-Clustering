[This post was updated at 12 p.m.]

A motorcyclist died Saturday morning after falling from the Interstate 45 overpass onto a street below.

The motorcyclist was driving around 9:28 a.m. on I-45 when he swerved to avoid an accident, lost control of his bike and fell off the overpass onto Live Oak Street. He died at the scene, police said.

The man has not been publicly identified.

Live Oak Street remained closed for several hours while police investigated the crash.
