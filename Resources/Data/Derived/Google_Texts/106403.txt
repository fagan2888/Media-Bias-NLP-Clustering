A woman was killed in a crash Saturday night in southeast Kansas.

The crash happened just after 10 p.m. at K-39 and Tulakes Road in eastern Wilson County. The Kansas Highway Patrol reports a Buick sedan was heading west on K-39 when the driver left the roadway and struck the embankment of a creek bed head-on.

The passenger in the vehicle died at the scene. She is identified as 51-year-old Darla Elwood of Chanute.

The driver, 45-year-old Kevin Elwood, was taken to Wesley Medical Center in Wichita. He was listed in serious condition Sunday afternoon.

Further details were not provided.
