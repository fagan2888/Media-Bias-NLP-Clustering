HOLMDEL â€” A state elevator inspector was injured Tuesday after falling about 5 feet down an elevator shaft at Bayshore Health Care Center.

At about 11 a.m., Holmdel Police got a 9-1-1 call that a man fell in the elevator shaft, according to a release. They determined the man, described as a state inspector for elevators, was climbing up a shaft after inspecting it when he slipped off a ladder and fell back down into the shaft. He traveled about 5 feet.

He was treated at the scene by Holmdel First Aid Squad and taken to Jersey Shore Medical Center with back and leg injuries.
