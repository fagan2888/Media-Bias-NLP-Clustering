Washington (CNN) President Barack Obama is traveling to the West Coast Wednesday, and is scheduled to take part in multiple DNC fundraisers, including one at famous actor/director/media mogul Tyler Perry's home in Los Angeles.

But scooping up big donations isn't the only thing on the President's agenda.

According to a White House official, the President will also participate in an hour-long plus interview with comedian Marc Maron.

Friday morning, the President will stop by the headquarters of Maron's "WTF with Marc Maron" interview podcast, located in a garage in Los Angeles. Maron tweeted this Wednesday, hinting at an announcement of the Presidential interview Thursday morning.
