Victim identified in fatal auto pedestrian crash in Hollywood

Charleston County Deputies are investigating a fatal auto pedestrian accident that occurred late Saturday night in Hollywood.



Deputies say the driver of a Chevrolet Blazer hit a woman who was walking along Highway 162. According to Charleston County Deputies, the driver was trying to pass another vehicle on the Highway when the incident occurred.



The victim, 34-year-old Crystal Jackson of Charleston, was pronounced dead on the scene. Officials with the Charleston County Coroner's Office say the cause of the death was blunt trauma.



Charleston County Deputies continue to investigate the accident.
