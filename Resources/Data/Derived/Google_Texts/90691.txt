Berkeley County, South Carolina, sheriff Wayne Dewitt is facing DUI and other charges after leaving the scene of an accident and failing a field sobriety test, reports ABCNews4.

South Carolina Highway Patrol told ABCNews4 that Sheriff Dewitt was driving his county-owned truck early Sunday morning when he crashed into another vehicle and fled the scene.

Police found Sheriff Dewitt at his home where he failed a field sobriety test, and was arrested after officers confirmed that he had been driving his truck at the time of the accident, the news website reported.
