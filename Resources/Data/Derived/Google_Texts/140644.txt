× Cullman drug court officer faces sex abuse charges, lawsuit

CULLMAN, Ala. (WHNT) – Cullman County Drug Court Officer Winfred Eugene Vance faces sex abuse charges, including sodomy.

Court records show a grand jury indicted Vance after a December arrest.

Our newspartners at al.com report that a lawsuit alleges Vance used his position with the Cullman County Drug Court to make unwanted sexual advances.

The lawsuit has nine plaintiffs and seeks $25 million in damages. You can read more about the suit from our newspartners here.
