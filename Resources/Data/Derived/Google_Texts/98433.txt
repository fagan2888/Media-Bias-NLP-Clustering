Florida Forestry Service officials, South Walton Fire officials and Walton County Sheriff's deputies responded to a wildfire Saturday afternoon in South Walton that was threatening many homes.

The fire was near Oak Avenue and Juniper Street and has since been extinguished.

Officials evacuated ten homes, just as a precaution. But there was no damage to any structures.

Forestry officials say a residents yard debris caught fire due to dry weather conditions and they are reminding residents to not burn anything this weekend because of the weather.
