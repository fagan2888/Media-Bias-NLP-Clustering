UPDATE 4/10/16 9:29 P.M.

The Tallahassee Police Department and FDLE has cancelled the search for Eliza Rollen.

Officials say she was found Sunday evening and was unharmed.

No other information has been released at this time.

----------------------------------------

The Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) is asking for your help to find a missing girl last seen on Belmont Road in Tallahassee.

FDLE officials say Eliza Rollen is a black, 11-year-old girl, she is 5' tall and weighs 80 pounds.

The FDLE says Eliza might be with Lamona Rollen, a black, 38-year-old woman. She is 5' 10" tall and weighs 145 pounds.

Law enforcement officials advise they might be traveling in a 2007, red Mercury Milan with the Florida license plate number 942HG.

If you have any information about where Eliza or Lamona Rollen might be, you are asked to contact the Tallahassee Police Department at 850-606-5800 or 911.
