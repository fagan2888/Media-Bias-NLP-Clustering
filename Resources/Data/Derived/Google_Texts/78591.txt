About 30 residents were displaced after a fire at an apartment complex in Euless Saturday morning, KXAS-TV (NBC5) reported.

Eight units at the complex in the 200 block of West Bear Creek Road sustained heavy fire damage.

Firefighters were able to rescue four people and six animals from the fire, the department posted to Twitter. One resident was transported to a local hospital for smoke inhalation.

The fire department said on social media one firefighters suffered minor injuries, though no other details were immediately available.

The Red Cross was assisting residents at the complex.
