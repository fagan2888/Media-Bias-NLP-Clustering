A Year in Provence

By Peter Mayle (1989)

1. Lurking inside every Englishman is a Mediterranean homeowner screaming to get out. As the rain pelts London, he dreams of sun-blasted stone terraces, olive trees and gallons of EU-subsidized plonk. Peter Mayle gave up a career in advertising, moved with his wife to an old farmhouse in Provence and nailed the fantasy with his opening line: "The year began with lunch."
