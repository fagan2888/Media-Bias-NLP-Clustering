Rapid City, SD

Rapid City law enforcement are searching for a state prison inmate who failed to return to the Rapid City Minimum Security unit after a funeral furlough.

30-year-old Dace Lunderman did not return to the prison at the scheduled time Saturday, March 14 from Mission, SD, where he was attending a funeral.

Lunderman was serving time for simple assault against a law enforcement officer in Pennington County. He is a Native American male, standing 6 feet 4 inches tall and weighs about 260 pounds. He has black hair and brown eyes.

Anyone who has seen Lunderman or knows of his whereabouts is urged to contact authorities immediately.
