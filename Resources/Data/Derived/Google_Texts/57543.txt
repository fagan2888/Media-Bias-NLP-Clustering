A 26-year-old is in critical condition yet stable after losing control of his motorcycle Sunday evening on West Twin Peaks Road just east of North Sandario Road.

The man was not wearing a helmet and was transported to the hospital, according to Capt. Adam Goldberg, spokesman for the Northwest Fire District. The crash happened shortly after 4:30 p.m. in the 10000 block of West Twin Peaks Road.
