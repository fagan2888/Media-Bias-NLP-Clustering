Intrigued by the "Stewarts & Hamiltons" cast? We know the feeling. Each week we'll be dishing on a new cast member — read on for five fast facts about Alana and Rod Stewart's son Sean Stewart:

1. He has a bit of a bad boy reputation, thanks to a three-month stay in prison after a bar fight in 2002 and his appearance on Season 2 of "Celebrity Rehab With Dr. Drew" in 2008, alongside Gary Busey and Amber Smith.



2. He also starred alongside Aaron Spelling's son Randy and David Weintraub in the A&E series "Sons of Hollywood" (for which he wrote the theme song) in 2007.

4. He recently started a clothing line called Sean's Dirty Weekend, described by The Mirror as "affordable" and "not high end, not flash."

"I'm in such a new chapter in my life where my health and wellness is my main concern," he told Daily Mail. "I'm feeling good, I'm feeling healthy."
