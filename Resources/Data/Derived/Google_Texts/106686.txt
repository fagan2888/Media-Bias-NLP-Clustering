Sioux Falls, SD

A Sioux Falls minimum-security prison inmate has been placed on escape status after walking away from the unit Sunday morning.

Thirty-six-year-old David Holloway was reported missing Sunday from Unit C at the South Dakota State Penitentiary.

Holloway is described as a white male with brown hair and brown eyes. He is 6 feet tall and around 180 pounds. He is currently serving time for grand theft and eluding police.

If you have seen Holloway or know of his whereabouts, you are urged to contact police.
