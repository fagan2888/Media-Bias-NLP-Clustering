UHC names Supply Chain Performance Excellence award winners

The UHC, an organization representing academic medical centers across the United States, this week named its top hospitals for managing supply chain at its annual conference in Orlando, Florida.

According to the organization, the group analyzed data from its UHC Operational Data Base, as well as members' CMS cost reports, to measure supply utilization at the facility level and across clinical areas including cardiology, surgical services, inpatient medications, laboratory services and imaging services to determine the winners.
