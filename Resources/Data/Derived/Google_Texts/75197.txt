Police are investigating a shooting in which a Milwaukee man was shot during an altercation with a tow-truck driver that had just towed his vehicle early Saturday.

The incident happened at 12:53 a.m., according to police. The driver of the tow truck realized he was being followed and drove to the District 2 Police Station, in the 200 block of W. Lincoln Ave., where the incident happened. The victim, 35, of Milwaukee was taken to the hospital and is expected to survive.

According to police, there were three people in the tow truck and one of them was the shooter. The shooter, 27, from Milwaukee stayed on scene and is cooperating with police. A firearm was recovered.
