RICHMOND, Va. (WTVR) - A man considered the "prime suspect" in the attempted abduction, robbery and sexual assault of a Georgia teen was last seen at a Richmond Walmart, according to the U.S. Marshals Service.

Investigators are looking for 54-year-old Zachary Cobb.

A parole violation warrant for Cobb, who served nearly 30 years in prison for a 1981 double murder, was issued on Dec. 18. Cobb was last seen at the Walmart on Sheila Lane in South Richmond on Dec. 15, according to the U.S. Marshals Service.

Cobb is believed to be driving a silver 2006 Toyota Camry with Georgia tags BUV-5838.

Anyone with information about Cobb's whereabouts is asked to call the United States Marshals Service Headquarters at 1-877-926-8332.
