UPDATE: December 25, 10:20 a.m.

Okaloosa County Sheriff's Officials have identified the man who they believe went missing Thursday while paddle boarding near Okaloosa Island.

Sheriff's officials believe 51-year-old John Frye, a paddle board enthusiast from Fort Walton Beach, disappeared in the strong surf on Christmas Eve behind the boardwalk on Okaloosa Island.

The U.S. Coast Guard is involved with the search, along with beach safety crews. If you have any information about what happened, please call the Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office at (850) 651-7410.

--------------

The search is on for a missing paddle boarder in Okaloosa County.

According to the sheriff's office, witnesses reported a man paddle boarding behind The Boardwalk on Okaloosa Island Thursday morning when he disappeared in the heavy surf.

We're told his paddle board washed ashore sometime around 10:30 a.m. Authorities believe the man to be a 51-year-old paddle board enthusiast from Ft. Walton Beach.

The Coast Guard is currently searching the waters off shore while Okaloosa County Beach Safety crews check the Gulf's shoreline.
