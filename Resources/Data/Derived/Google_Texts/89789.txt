A growing list of federal investigations and inquiries threatens to become a serious embarrassment for Caterpillar Inc., which has long projected a squeaky-clean Midwestern image.

The Peoria, Ill.-based maker of heavy equipment and engines faces a federal grand-jury investigation in Illinois involving cash transfers within the company and another in California focused on railcar repairs.
