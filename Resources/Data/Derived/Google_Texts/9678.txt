Chef Paz, the Peruvian restaurant at 9039 W. National Ave., West Allis, will begin serving weekend breakfast April 3.

Breakfast will be served from 7 to 11 a.m. on Fridays, Saturdays and Sunday, chef-owner Maritza Paz said. Peruvian and American breakfast dishes will be on the menu.
