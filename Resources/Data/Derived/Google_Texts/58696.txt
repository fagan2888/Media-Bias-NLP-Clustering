Colorado Springs police officials are investigating a possible kidnapping that occurred Saturday afternoon, according to a news release from the Colorado Springs Police Department.

Officials received a call at 2:10 p.m.notifying them that a man had forced a woman into his gold Acura MDX. The car may have a Colorado license plate with the letters “QEO.”

Investigators obtained surveillance of the possible kidnapping that shows the the suspect forcing the victim into the car. A witness told officials the victim was in the suspect’s car as it drove away from the scene on the 4000 block of Austin Bluffs Parkway.

The Colorado Springs Police Department has asked anyone with additional information to call Pikes Peak Area Crime Stoppers at (719)634-7867.
