Summerville authorities evacuated Azalea Square shopping center on Sunday to contain a gas leak. (Photo Source: @SpendorThought via Twitter)

Summerville fire crews say they contained a gas leak at the Azalea Square shopping center.

Officials evacuated the building as a precaution Sunday afternoon, according to Summerville Fire and Rescue Chief Richard Waring.

Authorities ventilated the building until SCE&G crews could arrive, Waring said.

A witness said the evacuation lasted for about an hour.

No injuries were reported.
