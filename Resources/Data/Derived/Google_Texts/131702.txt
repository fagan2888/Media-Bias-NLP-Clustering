Donation to Park Vista Elementary

Sara Melancon, a second grade teacher at Park Vista Elementary, received a check for $300 from Tim Hale of FO-CAL fiber optic lighting to purchase items for her classroom. With the money she was able to purchase novel sets from Scholastic Book Club, which also sent a free poster and bookmark for every child, along with 10 additional free books. With those, Melancon was able to and make a prize bag for the top reading average in her morning and afternoon classes. Her benefactor Tim Hale added camouflage caps to add to the prize bags.

Back to School Summit

Southern University Agricultural Research and Extension Center’s Southwest Center for Rural Initiatives is hosting its 15th annual the Back to School Summit at the Blackham Coliseum in Lafayette Sept. 3 from 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. The theme for this year’s event is “S.T.A.R.: Stop, Think, Act, and Review.” The event is designed to provide a neutral platform for open dialogue among teens and service providers, assists teens in making healthy life choices, prevents teen HIV/STD transmission, pregnancy, school drop-out, violence, substance abuse and tobacco use and provides a panel of professionals to discuss importance of healthy decision making. For more information, contact the center at at 337-943- 2410 or Krystle_Washington@suagcenter.com.
