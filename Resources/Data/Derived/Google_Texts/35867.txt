A fatal crash involving a motorcyclist forced the closure of the westbound 134 Freeway near the 5 Freeway Tuesday morning in the Glendale area.

Traffic Updates, Map

Southern California Traffic Headaches

Details regarding the crash were not immediately available. The motorcyclist was pronounced dead at the scene.

Three lanes were blocked at 7 a.m. Westbound traffic was backing up to Glendale Avenue, about four miles from the site of the crash.

Two lanes remained closed at 10 a.m., but the road reopened before mid-day.
