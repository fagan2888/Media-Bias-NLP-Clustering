Hospital Mergers Cut Competition, Efficiency, FTC Panel Says

Law360, New York (February 25, 2015, 7:09 PM ET) -- While the Federal Trade Commission has put a magnifying glass to the growing trend of vertical hospital mergers, a panel of experts at an FTC conference said those mergers frequently are not necessary and can quash local health care competition.



The indictment of the acquisition trend comes toward the end of a two-day joint conference with the Justice Department on competition in health care.
