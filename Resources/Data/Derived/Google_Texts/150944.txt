Artist Annu Palakunnathu Matthew showcases her photography at the Wichita State University Ulrich Museum of Art.

'Postdate: Photography and Inherited History in India' is featured at the museum from September 12 to December 13.

Matthew was born in England and raised in India and the United States. Her works of art are inspired by her experiences living in various countries.

Her work has been featured in The New York Times, CNN Photo Blog, and Buzzfeed. Her work has been exhibited at galleries such as the Guangzhou Biennial of Photography and The Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History.

Admission to the Ulrich Museum is free.
