A 47-year-old man was hit by a vehicle and killed while on a walk in York, SC, Christmas night.

The man was identified as Anthony Shannon, Sr., of York, according to the York County Coroner's Office.

York police say the collision happened around 10 p.m. Friday night on Railroad Avenue near the East Jefferson intersection.

According to police, Shannon was walking on Railroad Avenue when he was struck. The driver drove away. No vehicle description has been released.

The South Carolina Highway patrol was called in to collect evidence from the scene to recreate what happened. They are hoping the evidence will lead officials to the vehicle and driver who fled the scene.
