Two people escaped a mobile home fire in the 300 block of North Elm, near Oak Street. in Douglass.

"We got a call around 1:32 this morning that there was an explosion and people were yelling outside the structure," said Douglass Fire Chief John Ford.

When crews arrived, they found flames coming out of the roof and windows.

Two people were home at the time but they were outside when the explosion occurred.

Investigators are determining the cause of the fire.
