Meridian police still need your help to find a woman that has been missing for several months.

Police say Cheryl James has been missing since April 10th. She is a white female in her mid-30s who could have blond or brunette hair. James is 5 feet 4 inches tall, and weighs about 120 pounds. This is the last photo police have of her. If you have any information about where she might be located, you can call Meridian police at (601) 485-1893 or Crimestoppers at (855) 485-TIPS.
