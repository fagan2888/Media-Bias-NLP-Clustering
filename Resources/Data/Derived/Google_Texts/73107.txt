Though an estimated 650,000 people attended Saturday's Thunder Over Louisville, police say they made only 10 arrests during the festivities.

The arrests included six adults and four juveniles, according to a press release from the Louisville Metro Police Department. They consisted of:

- one adult arrested on a domestic violence charge

- three adults arrested on alcohol intoxication charges

- one adult arrested on alcohol intoxication and disorderly conduct charges

- one adult arrested on a DUI charge

- one juvenile arrested on disorderly conduct and assault in the third degree charges

- three juveniles arrested on failure to disperse charges

Also during Thunder, emergency medical service personnel made 50 runs, the Louisville Fire Department made three runs and the police department made 74 runs. Six vehicle collisions were reported, including one officer-involved incident at 5th and Liberty streets, when an officer collided with a vehicle that ran a red light.
