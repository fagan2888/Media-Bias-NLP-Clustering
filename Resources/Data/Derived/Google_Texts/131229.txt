Aurora police are investigating a homicide in an apartment complex parking lot after finding a dead man with a bullet in his head in a vehicle on Saturday.

Police found the victim when they responded to a report of a possibly deceased man in a vehicle in the lot at Silverbrook apartments on North Idalia Court about 7:45 p.m., police said in a news release on Sunday.

The victim was pronounced dead at the scene.

Anyone with information can call agent Tom Welton at 303-739-6107 or Metro Denver Crime Stoppers at 720-913-7867.
