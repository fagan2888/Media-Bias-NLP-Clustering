A week after the passing of beloved actor and comedian Robin Williams, “Late Show” host David Letterman paid tribute to his friend of 38 years. The 10-minute segment includes entertaining stories and tearful moments.

Letterman remembered Williams’ kindness — getting him a part on “Mork & Mindy” — and his incredible talent. Letterman described seeing Robin Williams for the first time at the Comedy Store: a “hurricane” of humor.
