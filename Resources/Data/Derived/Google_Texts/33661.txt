SARASOTA MUSIC HALF MARATHON LANE CLOSURES

The inaugural Sarasota Music Half Marathon kicks off at 7 a.m. this Sunday, and some roads will be closed, or partially closed during the four-hour event.

The course begins at the Van Wezel Performing Arts Hall and over the John Ringling Causeway and around St. Armands Circle, circles back and then continues along Orange Avenue into the Cherokee Park neighborhood and back through Selby Gardens to the hall.

Here is a list of lane closures for the Sarasota Music Half Marathon, according to the department.

Tamiami Trail (U.S. 41):

6:30 a.m. — 11 a.m.

• Southbound traffic will be merged into one lane from 10th Street to South Orange Avenue.

• Northbound traffic will be merged into one lane from Hudson Avenue to Gulfstream Avenue.

John Ringling Causeway and the Ringling Bridge:

6:30 a.m. — 11 a.m.

• Westbound traffic will be merged into one lane from Tamiami Trail (U.S. 41) to Bird Key Drive.

• Eastbound traffic will be merged into one lane from Adams Drive to Bird Key Drive.

• Eastbound traffic will again be merged into one lane from Hart’s Landing to Tamiami Trail (US 41).

• East and west traffic will be redirected as two-way traffic in the south side lanes from Adams Drive to Bird Key Drive.

St. Armands Circle:

6:30 a.m. — 9 a.m. (estimated)

• St. Armands Circle — All lanes completely closed.

• Southbound lane of North Boulevard of the Presidents will be closed from Madison Drive to St. Armands Circle.

• Northbound lane of South Boulevard of the Presidents will be closed from Monroe Drive to St. Armands Circle.

• All traffic will be re-routed via Washington Drive to Monroe Drive to South Adams Drive for access to the John Ringling Causeway and Ringling Bridge.

South Orange Avenue:

6:30 a.m. — 10:30 a.m. (estimated)

• All of South Orange Avenue is completely closed from Mound Street (U.S. 41) until it becomes McClellan Drive and continues to be closed to the intersection of South Osprey Avenue.

South Osprey Avenue:

6:30 a.m. — 10:30 a.m. (estimated)

• All of South Osprey Avenue will be completely closed from McClellan Drive to Webber Street.

• Southbound lane of South Osprey Avenue will be closed from McClellan Drive to South Drive.

Cherokee Park:

6:30 a.m. — 10:30 a.m. (estimated)

• Westbound South Drive will be closed from South Osprey Avenue to Cherokee Terrace.

• All of Cherokee Terrace will be closed from South Drive to North Drive.

• Westbound lane only of North Drive will be closed from Cherokee Terrace to South Osprey Avenue.
