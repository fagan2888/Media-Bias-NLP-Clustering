A Panama City man is in serious condition after a motorcycle crash on Saturday night.

According to Florida Highway Patrol, 47-year-old Anthony Martini was driving his motorcycle northbound on Star Avenue in Callaway when the accident occurred.

Officials says as Martini was approaching the intersection of Star Avenue and State Road 22 when the light turned yellow, he then accelerated to make it through the intersection, but struck the east-side curb of Star Avenue.

Reports state he then lost control of his motorcycle, hit a second curb and fell from the motorcycle.

Officials say Martini is being treated for possible head injuries.

Florida Highway Patrol says Martini was not wearing a helmet.
