An original painting by Tom Lynch, an acclaimed watercolorist from Naperville, has graced the Daily Herald front page each Christmas Day for decades.

Suburban watercolor scenes by acclaimed Naperville artist Tom Lynch have graced the Christmas Day front page of the Daily Herald since at least 1992. This year's peaceful, lighted streetscape evokes feelings of family and home.

Lynch often leads watercolor painting workshops, with the schedule online at tomlynch.com.

He has written seven books, has received two lifetime achievement awards and was listed as one of the nation's top 20 teachers by American Artist magazine.
