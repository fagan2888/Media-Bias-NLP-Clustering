For the 32nd time, people will jump into Boulder Reservoir to celebrate the New Year with the Boulder County Polar Plunge.

The cost to take the plunge is $25 for adults and $20 for children 12 and under — provided registration is done in advance online.

Jan. 1 registration will also be offered at the reservoir, located at 5565 N. 51st St., starting at 11 a.m. Online registration is also available at bit.ly/1uy7Uxi.

The plunge website also allows for friends and family members to donate to plungers, with money benefiting the Boulder County AIDS Project.
