Wagner Law Group Opens 2nd Fla. Office In Tampa

Law360, Miami (September 16, 2014, 6:15 PM ET) -- Boston-based employment and real estate law firm Wagner Law Group announced Tuesday that it is opening its second Florida location with an expansion to Tampa.



The 22-attorney firm, which also has offices in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, and San Francisco, specializes a variety of employment areas, including Employee Retirement Income Security Act matters, labor and human resources, employee benefits and welfare benefit plans, as well as corporate, tax, estate planning and administration, real estate and litigation.
