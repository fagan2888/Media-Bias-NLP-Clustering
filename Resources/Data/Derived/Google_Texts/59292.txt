A Canon City man died Friday night when the vehicle he was driving traveled off Shelf Road near Teller County.





The Colorado State Patrol says John Marsicano, 69, died on scene, and his passenger, Deloris Burns Marsicano, 56, was transported to Penrose Main with serious injuries.





The accident happened at about 9:22 p.m. about 1.8 miles from Teller County.
