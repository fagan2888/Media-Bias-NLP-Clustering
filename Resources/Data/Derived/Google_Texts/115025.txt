A brush fire one acre in size on Locust Street and New Hampshire Avenue in Lakewood has been contained and a brush fire a quarter of an acre in size on Squankum Yellowbrook Road in Howell is under control, New Jersey Forest Fire Service spokesman Bill Edwards said Sunday afternoon.

Edwards said the Lakewood fire was discovered by the Lakewood Forest Fire Tower at 2:42 p.m. and was contained by a combination of air craft dropping water and ground forces.
