Ex-HHS Official Joins Squire Patton Boggs' Health Policy Team

Law360, New York (June 16, 2015, 3:15 PM ET) -- Squire Patton Boggs LLP has added the former deputy director for congressional oversight and investigations at the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services as a partner in its health care public policy practice in Washington, the firm announced Monday.



Kristine Blackwood served as the liaison between HHS and Congress during the implementation of the Affordable Care Act and wrote provisions of the act while serving as chief counsel for the Senate Special Committee on Aging.
