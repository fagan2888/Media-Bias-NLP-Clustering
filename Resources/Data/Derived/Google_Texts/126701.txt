SEE Molly’s fashion influence

“Diamonds in the Daytime: The Changing Fashions of Margaret Brown’s World” opens the 2015 exhibit calendar at the Molly Brown House Museum. On display through Aug. 30, guests can see the museum’s fashion collection and how Brown influenced styles during her day. A handful of programs including a fashion show on June 13 are offered in connection to the exhibit, call or visit the website for the lineup. The museum is open Tuesday through Sunday, offering guided tours that start every 30 minutes. Admission is $5-$8. 1340 Pennsylvania St., 303-832-4092, mollybrown.org
