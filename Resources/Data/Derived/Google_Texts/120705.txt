The Shins’ “New Slang” went hand-in-hand with Zach Braff’s “Garden State.” The band is also being featured in Braff’s directorial follow-up “Wish I Was Here.” The Shins shared their new song “So Now What,” via NPR Music.

The movie’s soundtrack also includes Bon Iver, and Cat Power with Coldplay. The soundtrack will be out on CD and digital download on July 15, and on vinyl on Aug. 5.
