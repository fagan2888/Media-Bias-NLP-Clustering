Police were looking for a man who robbed a woman outside her apartment complex Tuesday in Ventura, officials said.

The incident was reported about 7:45 a.m. in the 6600 block of Webster Street, Ventura police said.

The victim was walking to her vehicle in the complex's parking lot when the suspect startled the victim and pointed a gun at her, demanding she give him her property, authorities said. The victim complied then the man ran away from the area south on Webster Street, officials said.

Officers checked the area but could not find the suspect, who was described as a man in his 20s wearing a blue beanie and bandana, police said.

The victim was not injured during the incident, authorities said.
