Four people are dead after a crash on Weld County Road 48 at U.S. 85 Sunday night, officials said.

The accident occurred around 10 p.m. between a Dodge truck and a Honda SUV, said Cpl. Heather Cobler with the Colorado State Patrol.

Another person involved in the crash was transported to a hospital with serious injuries, Cobler said.

Accident investigators are on scene. Cobler said both U.S. 85 and Weld County Road 48 are closed during the investigation.
