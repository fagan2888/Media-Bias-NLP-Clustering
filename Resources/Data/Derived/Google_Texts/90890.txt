A 56-year-old Valrico man was killed Saturday night when the 2006 Harley Davidson motorcycle he was operating struck the back of a livestock trailer on McIntosh Road, authorities said.

Hillsborough County sheriff’s deputies identified the victim as Steven Michael O’Neal, 2310 Durant Road, who was riding south on McIntosh Road. Deputies said he ran into the rear of a trailer attached to a 1999 Ford F-350 pickup driven by Robert Alton Henderson Jr., 38, 8309 Franklin Road, Plant City.

The pickup was stopped to turn left on to Franklin Road around 8:10 p.m. Saturday, deputies said, when the bike, at a speed of about 50 mph, struck the rear of the trailer .
