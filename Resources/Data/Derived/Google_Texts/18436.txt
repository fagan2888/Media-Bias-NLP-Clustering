Rates going up for Texans in health marketplace Houstonians to pay an average of nearly 5 percent more next year

Photo: Manuel Balce Ceneta, STF Health and Human Services Secretary Sylvia Burwell will be in town... Texans buying coverage through the federally mandated health insurance marketplace will face an average 5.1 percent rate increase for benchmark plans in 2016, a new Health and Human Services analysis released Monday afternoon showed.
