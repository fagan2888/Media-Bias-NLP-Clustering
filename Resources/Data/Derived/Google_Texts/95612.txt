× Progressive to Hire 900 Employees

MAYFIELD VILLAGE, OH — A Northeast Ohio company is looking to hire more than 900 employees by the end of the year.

Progressive Insurance says it has a number of positions in locations from Cleveland to New York.

The jobs include IT, analyst, legal, call center, claims and corporate. The positions require work experience in customer service and a stable work history.

Progressive says it offers flexible work arrangements, performance bonuses, a casual dress code, on-site medical facilities for employees and their families and a fitness center.
