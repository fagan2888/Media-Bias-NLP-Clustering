A local teenager has died after an overnight crash in Kemper County.

The Mississippi Highway Patrol tells us 18-year old Cardashia Jackson of Meridian died from a crash that happened on Highway 16 near Dekalb about 4:30 Sunday morning. Sergeant Andy West says Jackson was a passenger in a 2002 Nissan Altima that ran off the road and overturned.

Three other people were in the car and one of those three was critically injured.
