A Fond du Lac County man has pleaded not guilty to charges he drugged a 13-year-old girl, sexualy assaulted her and videotaped the acts, according to the Fond du Lac Reporter.

Jeffrey G. MacMillan, 44, of Empire, remains in the Fond du Lac County Jail on a $2 million bail.

The criminal complaint against him says a laptop computer seized from the home that contained more than 200 images of the girl being sexually assaulted.
