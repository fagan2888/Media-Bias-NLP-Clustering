Health insurer Cigna's operating revenue rises 5.6 pct

May 6 (Reuters) - U.S. health insurer Cigna Corp, which is being bought by larger rival Anthem Inc, reported a 5.6 percent rise in quarterly operating revenue as more customers in its Medicare Advantage business boosted premiums.

The company said shareholder's net income fell to $519 million, or $2 per share, in the first quarter ended March 31, from $533 million, or $2.04 per share, a year earlier.
