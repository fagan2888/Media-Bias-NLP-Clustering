It's no time for minced words in Cleveland -- at least as far as this trio of young ladies is concerned. The Browns suck, they say, and the only answer is one of their own: Youngstown native and former Ohio State coach Jim Tressel.

The girls implore the Browns organization to look at Tressel, a "proven winner" that has put dozens of players in the league.

Cleveland-area reporters have already suggested that the team appears to be run by The Three Stooges; why not just leave your front office decisions to a few 10-year-olds?
