One rider was killed and another seriously injured after two motorcycles crashed Sunday afternoon near Carter Lake west of Loveland.

Colorado State Patrol Trooper Josh Lewis said the injured rider was en troute to Medical Center of the Rockies in Loveland.

State Patrol dispatchers received the call a few minutes before 5 p.m. rushed to the scene and were launching an investigation into the crash, Lewis said.

The crash happened on Larimer County Road 8e, he said. No further details were available.
