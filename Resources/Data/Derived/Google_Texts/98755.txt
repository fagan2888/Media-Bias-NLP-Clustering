PMC 2015 Nearly 6,000 cyclists are riding in the 36th annual Pan-Mass. Challenge this weekend with the goal of raising $45 million for cancer care and research. To honor the late Attleboro resident and youth girls' basketball coach David Parker, who died from colon cancer in October 2014, Team Parker set up an unscheduled water and snack brake near Dodgeville Field on South Main Street. Kevin Cincotta offers water to riders.
