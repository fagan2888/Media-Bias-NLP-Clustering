ACA Expert Joins Holland & Knight From IRS

Law360, New York (March 8, 2016, 8:17 PM ET) -- A former senior director of operations at the Internal Revenue Service who oversaw the implementation of the Affordable Care Act has joined Holland & Knight LLP as a partner in Washington, D.C., the firm announced on Tuesday.



Nicole Elliott joined the public policy and regulation group, and will continue her work with the ACA after helping create and implement the regulations for the act at the IRS.
