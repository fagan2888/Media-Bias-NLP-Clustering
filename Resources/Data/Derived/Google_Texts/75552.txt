Anthem Hit With Latest Suit Over Narrow ACA Network

Law360, New York (August 20, 2014, 7:51 PM ET) -- A group of California consumers on Tuesday sued Anthem Blue Cross in state court for allegedly tricking them into buying health insurance policies with small provider networks, the latest litigation over cost-cutting moves inspired by the Affordable Care Act.



Tuesday’s complaint — which echoes a similar suit filed last month against Anthem — alleges that the insurance company “systematically misrepresented” the size of its networks by falsely confirming that certain providers were included.
