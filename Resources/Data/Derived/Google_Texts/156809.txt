Torrey Banks and Danny Patterson were married Oct. 10, 2015, at St. John's Church in Boulder.

Torrey is the daughter of Brit and Candace Banks, of Boulder. Danny is the son of Bruce and Carol Patterson, of New London, Conn.

Paige Banks, the bride's sister, was the maid of honor. The groom had two best men, brother Gil Patterson and friend Brendan Dier.

Torrey and Danny graduated from the University of Colorado-Boulder in 2011. They reside in Boston, where Torrey is a first-grade teacher and Danny a financial planner.

They are planning a Christmas holiday honeymoon to Paris with skiing in Chamonix, France.
