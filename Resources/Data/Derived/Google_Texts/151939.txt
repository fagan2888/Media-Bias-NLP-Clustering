A major winter storm with possibly a foot of snow and major coastal flooding is becoming more likely for Saturday, forecasters say.

A blizzard watch has been issued for 14 counties in New Jersey from Friday night through Sunday morning, according to the National Weather Service.

Union, Somerset and Middlesex counties are part of the blizzard watch. Hunterdon is still under a winter storm watch.
