Fight Over Health Insurance Mergers Hits Capitol Hill

Law360, New York (September 10, 2015, 3:41 PM ET) -- The rapidly escalating antitrust fight over proposed megamergers in the health insurance industry came to Capitol Hill on Thursday, where representatives of hospitals, doctors and insurers sparred over likely fallout if the U.S. Department of Justice lets the deals proceed.



Thursday’s tussling played out at the antitrust panel of the House Judiciary Committee and featured representatives of the American Hospital Association, the American Medical Association and America’s Health Insurance Plans.
