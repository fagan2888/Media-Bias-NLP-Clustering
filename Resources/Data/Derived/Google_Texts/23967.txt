Oklahoma City Police are investigating following a fatal shooting at an apartment complex Sunday afternoon.

Around 1:20 p.m., police responded to the reported shooting at the Chapel Ridge Apartments in the 5700 block of SE 48th.

Upon arrival, police discovered one victim who died from injuries sustained in the shooting.

At this time, police have yet to release the name of the victim or the description of the suspect. But they say the suspect was seen leaving in a black Nissan Maxima.
