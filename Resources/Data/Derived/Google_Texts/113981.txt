Sioux Falls, SD

Head to the Great Plains Zoo and Delbridge Museum of Natural History for a free lunch and other fun activities at Hy-Vee Day Saturday, July 11.

Hy-Vee will provide free lunches of hot dogs, chips, watermelon, cookies and water from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. Saturday. You can also get up close with the animals, see stilt walkers, and get your face painted.

Hy-Vee also sponsors the zoo's Face-To-Face Farm, where you can ride a camel, feed goats, and have other unique animal experiences.

Saturday's event is free for zoo members or with paid admission to the zoo.
