Authorities are seeking the public’s help in locating a missing juvenile from Hillsdale, a statement this afternoon from the State Police said.

Demaris D. Martinez, described as a Hispanic/white female, 16, was last seen by her family Friday morning, the statement said.

Martinez is otherwise described as having black hair, brown eyes and a medium brown complexion. She was last seen at 10:30 a.m. at her home wearing a black Burberry jacket. State Police said she might be in the Bronx.

Anyone with information is asked to contact Hillsdale Police at (201) 664-4200 or the State Police’s missing persons unit at (800) 709-5464.
