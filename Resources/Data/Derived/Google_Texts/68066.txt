Nov. 14-15. To bolster the movement to shop local during the holiday season, the Jackalope Art & Craft Fair returns to Denver for the second time this year. The namesake of the fair mirrors the unique handmade items crafted by local artisans that will be available at the EXDO Event Center. The fair will feature more than 100 juried vendors selling specialty creations including ceramics, paper goods, fashion and jewelry design, home decor, art, photography and one-of-a-kind accessories. There will also be a series of free workshops for all ages throughout the weekend. 10 a.m.-5 p.m. daily. The event is free, and free parking is available in the lots around the EXDO Event Center, 1399 35th St., Denver. jackalopeartfair.com or 323-989-2278.
