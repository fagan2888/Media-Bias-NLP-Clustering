An elderly man with dementia has been reunited with his family in Pima County, according to sheriff's deputies.

Ralph Nickerson, 73, left his home a little before 2 p.m. Sunday, March 6 in the family car, even though he hasn't driven since October.

At approximately 11 p.m. a release from the sheriff's department stated that Nickerson is safe and with his family once again.
