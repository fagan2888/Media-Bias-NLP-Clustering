Health Insurance Startup Oscar Valued At $1.5B In Financing

Law360, New York (April 20, 2015, 4:06 PM ET) -- Health insurance carrier Oscar Insurance drew $145 million in its latest fundraising round, the company announced Monday, earning it membership in the growing club of startups valued at more than $1 billion.



The fundraising round was led by Peter Thiel and Brian Singerman from Founders Fund, and included investments from Li Ka-shing of Horizon Ventures, the Wellington Management Company and Goldman Sachs, as well as all previous investors, the carrier announced on its blog.
