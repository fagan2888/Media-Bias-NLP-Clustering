Hospitals Rip DOJ's FCA Suit Over 60-Day Overpayment Rule

Law360, New York (September 23, 2014, 5:01 PM ET) -- New York’s Mount Sinai Health System on Monday attacked a high-profile False Claims Act suit alleging failure to return Medicaid overpayments within 60 days of identifying them, saying the U.S. Department of Justice and a whistleblower failed to show violations of the Affordable Care Act time limit.
