Shining A Light On Conn.'s Version Of The Sunshine Act

Law360, New York (May 29, 2015, 12:23 PM ET) -- On May 11, 2015, Connecticut Gov. Dannel Malloy signed into law Public Act 15-4, “An Act Concerning Reporting Payments by Manufacturers to Independently Practicing Advanced Practice Registered Nurses,” which became effective upon signing. The reporting amendment modifies Section 21a-70f of the Connecticut General Statutes (report of payment or transfer of value by manufacturer to advance practice nurse), which was passed in 2014.
