Maintenance work will disrupt light rail service north of the Broadway station from April 15 into the early hours of April 18, according to an RTD alert.

There will be no light rail service north of the Broadway station on the C, D, E, F and H lines from 1 a.m. April 15 through 3 a.m. April 18.

Service on the W line will be unaffected, RTD said.

Bus shuttles will run to the Auraria West and Downing stations on April 15 and to Union and Downing stations on April 16 and April 17 during normal light rail hours.
