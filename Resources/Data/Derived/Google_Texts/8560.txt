Panama City Police investigated a car crash that hit a furniture store early Friday morning.

It happened at Airport Furniture on 15th and Lisenby around 3am. Panama City Police say a man pulled into the parking lot, hit a curb, then slammed into the building.

Authorities administered a breathalyzer test. The driver did not appear to be hurt. The store's owner was called in and said the damage was minimal, and they will be open Saturday.
