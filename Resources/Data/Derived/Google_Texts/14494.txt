Defense and security are among the less-discussed questions in the Scottish independence debate. First Minister and Scottish National Party (SNP) leader Alex Salmond has promised to work with London "to ensure that there is a seamless, secure transition and for the continuing security of both countries," should the Scots choose independence. In reality, an independent Scotland will be a cuckoo in the Western nest.
