A man is locked up after lawmen say he waved a gun around inside a bar in the east.

The New Bern Police say that 24-year-old Vincent Fruhling was arrested Friday night around 11:18. Officials say he waved the weapon around inside of the Mesh Sports Lounge at 2500 Trent Road.

Fruhling has been charged with carry concealed weapon and going armed to the terror of the public.

Officials say there were no injuries or shots fired, and that the suspect was taken into custody without incident.
