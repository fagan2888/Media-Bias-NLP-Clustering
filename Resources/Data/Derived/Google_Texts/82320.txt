Stephen Colbert reminded us on Friday night’s “Late Show” that the Iowa Caucuses are just three short weeks away. But the Jeb Bush countdown might take a little longer. He’s still isn’t giving up. While campaigning in New Hampshire this week, Bush gave a 13-year-old-boy a tiny toy turtle which he apparently keeps in his pocket.

“I’m not a grievance candidate,” he told a room of fans in Longwood, Florida earlier in the week. “I’m the tortoise in the race — but I’m a joyful tortoise.”
