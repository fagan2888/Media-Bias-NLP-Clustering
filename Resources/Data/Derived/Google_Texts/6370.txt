A woman is accused of shooting a man in the head late Friday night in Crosscreek Township, police said.

Tracy Chechuck faces several charges, including attempted homicide. She allegedly shot Louis Ruscello after the two got into a fight, according to state police in Washington County.

Ruscello was treated at a hospital. His condition was not released.

Ruscello was living with Chechuck and her fiance, police said.

Chechuck was held in the Washington County Jail.
