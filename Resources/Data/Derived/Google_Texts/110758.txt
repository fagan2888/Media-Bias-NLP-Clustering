Also on the Web

A four-wheeler off-road vehicle rolled over and killed a 4-year-old boy on Sunday, Feb. 21, near Benson, according to the Cochise County Sheriff's Office.

A post on the CCSO Facebook page said the incident happened near Ocotillo Road and Little Rincon Ranch around 11:55 a.m.

The boy was taken to a nearby hospital, where he was pronounced dead, according to the post.

An update from the sheriff's office corrected it's initial report, saying the child was a girl. It is now known the child is a boy.

The boy's family was nearby when the accident happened, according to the update.

A family friend was driving a Side By Side UTV, when it rolled over and the boy was ejected.

The Sheriff's Office Accident Investigation Team is continuing the investigation.
