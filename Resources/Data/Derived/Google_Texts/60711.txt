There has been a revolving door at McKenna Long & Aldridge LLP as it prepares to combine with global mega-firm Dentons. The most recent in a series of partner departures at the firm include Daniel Jarcho, a litigator who moved to the Washington office of Alston & Bird LLP; Michael Durkee, a land-use lawyer now with Nossaman LLP in San Francisco; and bankruptcy lawyer Christopher Graham, now with Eckert Seamans Cherin & Mellott LLC in White Plains, N.Y.
