The top local stories this morning from the KERA Newsroom: Officials in Ellis and Rockwall counties say they've been told to expect more than 800 unaccompanied minors from Central America.

The children, mostly boys between the ages of 12 and 18, will be in the two privately-owned camps for 21 days until they can be placed with sponsoring families.
