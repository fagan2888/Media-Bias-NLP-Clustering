Brian Burgoon, a 2014 graduate of Topeka West High School, will receive his Eagle Scout award at a Court of Honor ceremony Sunday, July 13, at Faith Lutheran Church.

Burgoon began his Scouting career with Cub Scout Pack 59 at Faith Lutheran Church, where he earned the Arrow of Light award as a Webelo. He joined Troop 7 at First Christian Church where he earned 21 merit badges and became a member of the Order of the Arrow. He completed National Youth Leadership Training and has held several leadership positions. His high adventure trips included travels to Boundary Waters and the Philmont camp.

His Eagle service project involved redoing the playground at First Christian Church. He planned and led the project, acquired all materials and recruited assistance to complete the project.

Burgoon lettered in band at West, where he was drum captain. He plans to continue in marching band at Washburn University.

He is the son of Suzanne Sawyer-Burris and Clint Burgoon.
