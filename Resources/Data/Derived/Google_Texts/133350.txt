A man was hospitalized Friday night after accidentally shooting himself in the leg at a Salina movie theater.

The incident happened around 8:30 p.m. at Starplex Cinemas located in the Central Mall at 2259 South 9th Street.

The man was taken to a local hospital with a gunshot wound to the leg. His injuries were not life-threatening, police said.

No one else was hurt.

Further details about the accidental shooting were not immediately available.
