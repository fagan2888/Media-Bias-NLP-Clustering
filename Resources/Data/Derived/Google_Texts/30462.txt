Forecasters are calling for a slight chance of a rain and snow mix in Denver on Thursday as temperatures are set to top out at 53 degrees before a sunny, warm weekend.

The National Weather Service in Boulder is calling for a low Thursday night of 36 degrees.

The weather service forecasts a high of 64 degrees on Friday.

Temperatures are expected to top out at 72 degrees on Saturday and 69 degrees on Sunday, the service says.
