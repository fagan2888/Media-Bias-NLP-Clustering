The Professional Staff Congress, the union that represents more than 25,000 faculty and staff at CUNY and its Research Foundation, plans to hold a strike authorization vote.

The union has been working without a contract for five years and has received no raises in six.

“A strike authorization vote — even though it is not a vote to strike — is a significant escalation of our campaign,” wrote PSC President Barbara Bowen to the union’s membership. “There will be several months of preparation before the vote is taken. The union’s mass meeting on November 19 will offer an opportunity to discuss and plan for the vote.”
