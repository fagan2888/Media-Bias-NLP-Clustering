Seventeen-year-old soccer sensation Mallory Pugh not only made her senior U.S. Women’s National Team debut on January 23 against the Republic of Ireland in San Diego, but she also scored a goal in the U.S.’ 5-0 victory.

Pugh scored the team’s fifth goal in the 83rd minute while she was in for Alex Morgan.
