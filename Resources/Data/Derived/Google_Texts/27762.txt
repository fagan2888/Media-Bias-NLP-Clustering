NORTH LITTLE ROCK, Ark. (KARK) -- Police are investigating a deadly shooting after shots were fired in Northern Little Rock.

37-year-old Beau Abrams died from gunshot wounds and another unidentified man was injured.

It happened in the 1400 block of Rockwater Boulevard shortly before 2:00 a.m.

Police responded to a shots-fired call and found two victims.

Abrams died on the scene.

The other man is suffering from head injuries.

The investigation is ongoing.

So far police have not released any information about suspects or a possible motive.
