Brookings, SD

You may not be ready to think about decorating trees just yet, but the Brookings Arts Council is ready to accept tree submissions for its annual Festival of Trees.

The Brookings Arts Council is asking for submissions of specially-decorated holiday trees from different groups and organizations to be displayed.

The deadline to participate in the festival is November 6. You can register online or stop by the Brookings Arts Council at 524 4th Street in Brookings. The trees will be on display from November 28 through January 1.

Proceeds from the event go to different educational programs in Brookings.
