By Staff Reports

Two people were arrested early Saturday in Oxnard on suspicion of gun and narcotics violations, authorities said.

The arrests occurred after officers began investigating a suspicious vehicle parked near Yucca and E streets about 2:20 a.m.

The passenger, Atimalala Burgess, 31, of Oxnard, threw a loaded .32-caliber handgun onto the street, authorities said. Also, officers found a quarter ounce of methamphetamine packaged for sale, police said. Burgess was arrested on suspicion of several weapons and narcotics charges, police said.

The driver, Fernando Solis, 23, of Oxnard, also was arrested, police said.
