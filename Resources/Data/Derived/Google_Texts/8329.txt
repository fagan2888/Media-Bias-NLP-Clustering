The Crestview Police Department is investigating a stabbing death from early Saturday morning.

Police have identified 35-year-old Samuel Cornish as the stab victim. Investigators say he was stabbed one time in the upper chest area.

Witnesses say Cornish was walking on the shoulder of South Ferdon Boulevard north of Duggan Avenue when he collapsed from his injuries.

He was transported to North Okaloosa Medical Center where he was pronounced dead.

Investigators are asking any witnesses, or persons who may have information regarding this incident to contact the Crestview Police Department at (850)-682-3544.
