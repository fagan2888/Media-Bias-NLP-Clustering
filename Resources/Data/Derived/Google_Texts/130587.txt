In the City of Marion a Tuberculosis outbreak has taken place today. Over 1,000 people were tested and of those tested 47 came back positive for the disease.

It's declared an outbreak by the public health officials.

A source say it is possible to have the disease but not the infection and symptoms include prolonged cough (longer then a few days), chest pains, night sweats and fever.

The city is using government funding to test and officials in Marion say for those who complete the whole course of medication will receive $100.
