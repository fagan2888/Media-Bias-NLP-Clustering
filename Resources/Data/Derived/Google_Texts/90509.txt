A 49-year-old Queens, N.Y., woman was arrested last week for allegedly engaging in prostitution in a township massage parlor, police said.

Jin Hauzi was charged with one count of prostitution for providing sexual activity in exchange for money on Nov. 26 at 35 Spa, on Route 35, Aberdeen Capt. Joseph Cole said.

Hauzi was issued a summons and released, Cole said.

Authorities are still investigating whether the spa’s ownership was also involved, Cole said.
