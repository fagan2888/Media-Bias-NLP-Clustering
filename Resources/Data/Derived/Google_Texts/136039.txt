Walton County Sheriff's Deputies arrested a Niceville man Sunday after they say he exposed himself to several teenagers.

Deputies say they found Brian Mitchell, 41, at the Loves Travel Shop on Highway 90 around 8 p.m. Sunday.

According to witnesses, Mitchell exposed himself inside Loves and walked only a commercial bus, where he exposed himself to students between 15 and 18 years old.

Deputies pulled out a taser after Mitchell refused to get on the ground. They say Mitchell grabbed the deputy's wrist holding the taser before the deputy was able to get him on the ground.

Mitchell's charges include battery on law a law enforcement officer, indecent exposure, resisting an officer with violence, lewd and lascivious behavior and trespassing.
