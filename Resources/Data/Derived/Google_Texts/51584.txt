PARIS--French producer prices dropped in January as refining prices collapsed and food and energy prices declined, statistics showed Friday.

France's producer price index fell 0.8% in January on the month and 2.5% on the year, national statistics bureau Insee said.

Prices of coking and refining output sank 14% on month and 30% on year. Food prices fell 0.4% on month and on year.

Only transport, information technology and electronic equipment prices posted small gains in January.
