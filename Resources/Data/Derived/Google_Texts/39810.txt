A 25-year-old Hustisford woman died late Saturday night after being hit by a driver running a red light and being ejected from her car in Brown Deer.

A passenger and the driver of the other car suffered non-life threatening injuries and were taken to the hospital.

The 25-year-old man who was driving the other vehicle was believed to be under the influence. Charges will be referred to the Milwaukee County District Attorney's office on Tuesday, police said.

The accident happened in the 6000 block of W. Brown Deer Rd.
