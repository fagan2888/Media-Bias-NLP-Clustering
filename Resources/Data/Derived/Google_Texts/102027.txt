A man was killed in a crash Sunday morning in southeast Kansas.

It happened just after 9:30 a.m. at the junction of highways 169 and 400 in Montgomery County.

The Kansas Highway Patrol says an SUV was crossing Highway 169 and did not stop at the stop sign. It was then hit by a northbound pickup.

The driver of the SUV, 47-year-old Lane Marten, later died at the hospital. Reports state he was from Battle Creek, Iowa.

The driver of the pickup was also taken to a hospital with “possible injuries.”
