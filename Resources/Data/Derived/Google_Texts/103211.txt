A mother and three children were hospitalized early Saturday afternoon after an explosion and fire at a Kansas home.

Crews from several fire stations were called around 12:30 p.m. to a home near High and Ash streets in Riley, northwest of Manhattan.

According to Fire Chief Pat Collins, the the garage door was blown off in the explosion, and fire that followed left the home in ruins.

A 30-year-old woman, identified as Amber Farnsworth, and three children were taken to a local hospital. They were treated and released.

investigators are trying to determine the cause of the explosion. Damage is estimated at $125,000.
