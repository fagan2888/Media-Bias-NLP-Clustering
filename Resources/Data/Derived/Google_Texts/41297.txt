What are the most conservative and liberal towns in your state?

This map, compiled by Business Insider, depicts the most left-leaning and right-leaning towns in each state based on data provided by the research organization Clarity Campaign Labs.

Clarity Campaign Labs surveyed users’ responses to a series of questions on issues like guns, abortion and taxes to determine a community’s political views.

You can also use Clarity Campaign Labs’ online quiz to see which community in your state is the best fit for your political beliefs.

“Don’t forget to share this with your friends! Maybe they should be your neighbors…or not,” they caution.
