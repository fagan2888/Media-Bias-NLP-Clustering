The city of Meridian egg hunt was this morning at 10 in Highland Park. Hundreds of children of all ages were a part of the hunt. Over 4,000 eggs were placed with special prizes. Kids also enjoyed carousel rides.

Not long after that the first carter foundation egg hunt was underway at George Reece courts. Many children turned out for that event. Kids were given prizes.

Ma'lysia Granger is the winner for this year's Easter egg hunt. James Carter says he will continue to have the Carter Foundation egg hunt every year.
