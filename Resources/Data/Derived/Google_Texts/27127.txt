Austin Powell is safe and being returned to Oklahoma.

A Muskogee teenager who's been missing since Friday has been found safe Tuesday afternoon. Deputies tell us Austin Powell's biological father abducted the boy from his foster family.

Austin has a sensitive medical condition and did not have his medications.

Using Facebook posts, investigators tracked Powell and his father to Bad Axe, Michigan where the two were staying with one of the father's friends.
