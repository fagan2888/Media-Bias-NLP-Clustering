Fisher, Audrey Kathryn Lantz

Audrey Kathryn Lantz Fisher, of Botetourt County, Va., departed this life on Monday, May 23, 2016, after a lifetime of love, service and devotion to God, to her family, her friends and neighbors, her church, her home and garden, her pets over the years and even the birds and woodland creatures close by.Audrey was always a stylish and active lady who held many responsible and varied jobs since she started working at Woolworth's at 18 years of age in downtown Roanoke.She was the beloved wife of William Fisher for 53 years and leaves behind to cherish her memory her loving immediate family and many close relatives and friends.Audrey loved owls, cross-stich, reading her Bible and other inspirational books, working in her garden, as well as feeding and watching the birds around her home. Additionally, she was a devoted member of Parkway Wesleyan Church since her childhood and loved her Sunday School Class. She was well-known for her love of baking special treats for others, especially cakes. She will always be remembered as a generous, kind, caring, nurturing and independent person who lived a faithful, healthy and joyous life.A Celebration of her Life will be held at 1 p.m. on Thursday, May 26, 2016, at Parkway Wesleyan Church with Dr. John Ott and Pastor Mike Lauridsen officiating. Interment will follow in Evergreen Burial Park. The family will receive friends from 7 to 8 p.m. on Wednesday, May 25, 2016, at Oakey's Roanoke Chapel. Online condolences may be made at www.oakeys.com.
