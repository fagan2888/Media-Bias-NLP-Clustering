Duncan, James Thomas

James Thomas Duncan, 91, of Salem, went to be with the Lord on Wednesday, May 25, 2016.He was preceded in death by his wife of 67 years, Avalee Mills Duncan; parents, Lewis Madison Duncan and Minnie Manning Duncan; three brothers, Ural, Carl and Buck Duncan; and two sisters, Doris Gearheart and Georgie Duncan.He is survived by his three daughters and their husbands, Martha and Ricky Ellis, Nancy and Everett (Tubby), Cindy and Eddy Jones; grandchildren, Lauren Ellis McGuire and husband, Matt, Tyler Ellis and his fianc√©, Laura Love, Kevin Jones, Sarah Jones; great-grandchild, Tate McGuire; his sister and her husband, Geneva and Warren Blosser; sisters-in-law, Millie Mills and Wanda Duncan. He also leaves behind many nieces and nephews.He was born in Roanoke, Va. on January 13, 1925. He served in the Navy during World War II and afterward, worked at American Bridge Works, and retired from Eaton Corporation. He lived in the Wabun community most of his life. He was a faithful member of Green Hill Church of the Brethren. He was well known for his impeccably manicured lawn, and his beautiful garden.A special thanks to The Joseph C. Thomas Center and their staff for their exceptional loving care over the last year. Also, to Good Samaritan Hospice, and the special care takers form Generation Solution.Funeral services will be held at 11 a.m. on Saturday, May 28, 2016 at Lotz Salem Chapel with the Reverend Jim Worline officiating.Interment will follow at Blue Ridge Memorial Gardens.The family will receive friends from 2 to 4 and 6 to 8 p.m. Friday, May 27, 2016 at Lotz Funeral Home Salem; and the family will receive friends one hour prior to Saturday service.In lieu of flowers, the family suggests memorial contributions be made to Green Hill Church of the Brethren, 2699 Harborwood Road, Salem 24153 or the charity of your choice.
