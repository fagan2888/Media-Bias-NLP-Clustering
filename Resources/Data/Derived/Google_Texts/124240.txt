Penn State Hershey, PinnacleHealth To Fight FTC Merger Suit

Law360, Chicago (December 17, 2015, 2:15 PM ET) -- Penn State Hershey Medical Center and PinnacleHealth System announced Thursday they will fight the U.S. Federal Trade Commission and the Pennsylvania attorney general's efforts to block their merger, saying the tie-up will benefit the community and keep health care costs low.
