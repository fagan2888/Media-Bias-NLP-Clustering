DOMINIC BARTON: What are the top five issues that every CEO faces these days?

Boldly reallocating capital and people to growth opportunities each year (e.g., more than 10% change from previous year). Digitization and adapting to new technology. Building agility and resilience in the organization to react to shocks and capture opportunities. Revamping leadership development to focus more on “who leaders are” rather than “what they do.” Regulatory strategy and execution.

Dominic Barton is the global managing director at McKinsey & Co.
