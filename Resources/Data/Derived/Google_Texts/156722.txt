By: ICTMN Staff

Take a few seconds to flip though ten beautiful photos from the 27th Annual Black Hills Powwow in the Rushmore Plaza Civic Center in Rapid City, South Dakota.

The Black Hills Powwow has become one of the premier American Indian cultural events in the United States, attracting hundreds of dancers, singers, artisans and several thousand spectators from across several U.S. states and Canadian provinces.

The theme for this year’s pow wow, which was held on October 11-13th, was “Come Dance With Us.” And many of the guests in these photos did just that—dance!

From drummers, drumming, to smiling babies, and ladies wearing colorful feathers, check out these photos, which are sure to make you smile.
