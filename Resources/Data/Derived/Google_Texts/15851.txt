A 4.2-magnitude earthquake centered in Oklahoma was felt in Wichita this morning.

The U.S. Geological Survey confirms a 4.2-magnitude quake 12 miles north of Crescent, Oklahoma.

The quake was recorded at 8:21 a.m.

KAKE's Tanner Swift and Lily Wu were on-set in the studio and broke the news to viewers as they felt it during a commercial break.

We have received numerous calls and messages from people who felt the quake in Wichita and Rose Hill.

Other communities have reported feeling the quake, as far north as Salina, as far east as Pittsburg, and as far west as Haviland.

If you have reports of damage from the quake, KAKE News would like to hear from you.
