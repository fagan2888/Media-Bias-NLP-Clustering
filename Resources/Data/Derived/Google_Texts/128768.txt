If you’re worried about the cost of health insurance premiums in the exchanges, it might help to know that – thanks to the law’s generous subsidies – your premiums could end up a lot lower than you expect.

More than half of the uninsured don’t know that financial assistance in the form of subsidies is available in the exchanges. A few more quick facts about subsidies:

An impressive 85 percent of Americans shopping in the exchanges will receive subsidies.

Subsidies can lower your premium significantly, but the law also provides subsidies that can reduce your cost sharing.

The average after-subsidy premium paid by the millions of enrollees who got premium subsidies during the first open enrollment was $82.

It pays to calculate your subsidy!
