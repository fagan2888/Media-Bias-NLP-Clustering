The outcome of 21st Century Fox's takeover battle for Time Warner Inc. will likely come down to which CEO Time Warner's shareholders trust more: Fox chief Rupert Murdoch or Time Warner's Jeff Bewkes.

That is a stark choice. The two men symbolize opposing ends of the Hollywood mogul spectrum. Mr. Murdoch, 83 years old, is in some ways a throwback to an older generation of studio chief, exercising control of an entertainment empire he built himself from a single newspaper in Australia, by taking enormous business risks...
