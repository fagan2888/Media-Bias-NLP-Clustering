The Arapahoe County Sheriff’s Office is searching for two suspects after a gas station in Arapahoe County was robbed at gunpoint, officials said.

Two men went into a Western Convenience gas station at 1370 S. Parker Road around 11:40 p.m. on Friday night and demanded money from the clerk while brandishing semi-automatic handguns, according to Lt. Mike Mitchell with the Arapahoe County Sheriff’s Office.

The clerk gave the suspects $100, and the suspects ran away.

Authorities did not have any leads on the suspects’ whereabouts or a possible vehicle description.
