Panama City Police are investigating a rollover accident that occurred Saturday night at the intersection of Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. and 11th Street.

Around 5:40 p.m., Panama City Police received responded to the accident.

According to reports, a blue mini-van was traveling north on Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. and a silver four-door sedan was traveling east on 11th Street, when the driver in the blue van ran a red light, and crashed into the other vehicle.

This caused the sedan to overturn.

Both drivers and one passenger were taken to the hospital. They are all in stable condition.
