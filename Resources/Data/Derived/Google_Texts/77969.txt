Mom Can't Sue Employer, Insurer For Gender Bias Against Son

Law360, New York (May 11, 2016, 8:24 PM ET) -- A Minnesota nurse can't bring gender bias claims against her employer for providing insurance that denied her son’s gender-reassignment surgery and related prescriptions, nor can she sue insurer HealthPartners Inc., as they weren’t involved in the matter, a federal judge ruled Wednesday.
