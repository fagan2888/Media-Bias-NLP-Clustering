Police are investigating two shootings in Baltimore on Wednesday evening.

Authorities said four people were injured in a shooting in the 1100 block of Carroll Street just after 6 p.m.

Officials said three victims were at the scene of the attack when they arrived. An 18-year-old man suffered a hand injury, and a 23-year-old man and a 47-year-old man suffered injuries to their feet.

Investigators said a 23-year-old man was also taken to a hospital with multiple gunshot wounds.

A second shooting happened just before 7 p.m. in the 800 block of Venerable Avenue where a 19-year-old man was injured and taken to a hospital for treatment.

The victim's injuries were not considered life-threatening.

Anyone with information about the shootings is asked to call the Baltimore Police Department at 410-396-2221.
