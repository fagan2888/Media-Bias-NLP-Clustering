Sen. Bernie Sanders' campaign event will continue in Tucson Sunday with a town hall meeting that will focus on immigration issues.

"The Future of Immigration Reform," is set for 2 p.m. at 225 E. 26th Street, Suite 1 The event is free and open to the public but RSVPs are encouraged.

The announcement comes after Sanders wrapped up a visit to the U.S.-Mexico border in Nogales on Saturday.

Sanders will not be present at the town hall.

Erika Andiola, National Latino Press Secretary, and Isabel Garcia, a local immigration activist, will discuss Sanders' immigration platform and what it means to Arizona families.
