Syracuse, N.Y. -- James Reed, senior vice president of sales and marketing for Excellus BlueCross BlueShield, has been named regional president of the health insurer's Central New York region.

Reed will replace Dr. Arthur P. Vercillo, who is retiring from the position July 1. Vercillo will devote his time to his local surgical practice.

Reed has been with Excellus 20 years. In his new role he will combine his leadership of marketing and sales with the additional responsibility of supporting and maintaining business and quality health and provider networks in Central New York.

Reed was regional president in 2008 and 2009, then took on the sales and marketing role to prepare the company for changes related to the federal Affordable Care Act.
