The Friends of Williamson County Public Library will hold its first book sale of the year.

On March 7-9, thousands of new and used donated books will be for sale at the Williamson County Public Library in Franklin.

Friends of Williamson County Public Library members will get an opportunity to participate in a jump-start sale that starts at 9 a.m. on March 7. The general public will enter at 10:30 a.m. and will be allowed to shop until 5 p.m. The March 8 sale time is 1-5 p.m., and March 9 will be a general public $5 bag sale from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. Members will have a special bag sale that begins at 3:30 p.m. March 8.

Paperbacks, hardcovers, fiction, nonfiction, children’s books, antique books, coffee table books, audio books, CDs and DVDs will all be on sale. Items range from 50 cents to $5.

Cash, check and Visa or MasterCard will be accepted as payment.
