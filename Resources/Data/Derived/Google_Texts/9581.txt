Marinan Reese, 79, passed away late Friday due to injuries sustained in a car crash involving an OKC police officer.

Friday afternoon, emergency crews responded to the scene of an accident involving officer Bryan Holloway and Reese on NW 150th Street between May Avenue and Pennsylvania Avenue.

Both were transported to the hospital.

Officer Holloway has been placed on medical leave, according to Oklahoma City police.

The crash is still under investigation at this time.
