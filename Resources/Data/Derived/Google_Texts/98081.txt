Wonder how much money your hospital makes? The Palm Beach Post asked for records Gov. Rick Scott showed select legislators, and the newspaper created a searchable list here.

Find out which Florida hospitals have profit margins of 25 percent, 30 percent or as much as 55 percent statewide, according to the documents.

Locally, Palms West Hospital in Loxahatchee cleared $12.1 million, or 8.1 percent of revenues, and Delray Medical Center posted $19.8 million or 7.4 percent, the records show.
