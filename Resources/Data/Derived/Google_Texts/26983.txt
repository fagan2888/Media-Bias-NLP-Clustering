A fatal three-vehicle accident on U.S. 285 near Fairplay has closed the highway in both directions, according to the Colorado State Patrol.

The accident, which occured around midday, involved a semi-truck and resulted in the death of man, said Trooper Josh Lewis, a spokesman for the patrol.

The highway is closed at mile marker 182.

Information regarding further injuries, the identities or ages of those involved in the crash and an estimated time of road reopening was unavailable, Lewis said.
