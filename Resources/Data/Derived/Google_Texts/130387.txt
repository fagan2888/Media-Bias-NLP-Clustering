A two-car accident at Route 33 and Millstone Road sent two people to hospitals Monday morning, according to authorities.

The crash between a Ford Econoline van and a Lexus was reported around 7:30 a.m. State Police said the crash was cleared by 9 a.m.

One person involved in the accident was taken to Jersey Shore University Medical Center in Neptune and the other was taken to CentraState Healthcare System in Freehold Township, according to the fire department. A medevac helicopter initially called to the scene for serious injuries was called off, said the department.
