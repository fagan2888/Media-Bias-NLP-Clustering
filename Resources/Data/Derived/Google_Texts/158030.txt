A man from Richmond, Virginia was arrested in Greenville Saturday night for a crime committed in Northampton County.

Tremayne McKeever, 49, is charged with felony assault with a deadly weapon.

Officers at the Pitt County jail say Greenville policemen served McKeever and brought him into custody around 7 p.m.

Details on McKeever's charge weren't immediately available. A court date or plans to bring McKeever to Northampton County hadn't been made Sunday night.
