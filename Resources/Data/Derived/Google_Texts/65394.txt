Activist investors, as well as strategic risks and contracting CEO tenures are top issues for corporate boards, according to Dr. Deenadayalen (Len) Konar, who has more than 20 years’ experience as a public company director in South Africa. Dr. Konar discusses how boards address reputation and regulatory risk, as well as the two-sides of business model disruption—risk and opportunity. He also considers what corporate directors likely will focus on during the next year, identifying a wide variety of issues—from severe weather and the changing demographics of the workforce to energy efficiency and security measures.
