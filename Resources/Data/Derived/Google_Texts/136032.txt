Wichita police are looking for a culprit after an armed robbery early Saturday morning at a southeast Wichita convenience store.

It happened at about 1:45 a.m. at the Kwik Shop near S Oliver and I-35.

A store employee told arriving officers a man came into the store pointing a handgun at him, and demanding cash from the register. After taking the after, the robber ran away eastbound on foot.

The employee described the crook as Hispanic, about 6-foot, 170 pounds, wearing dark clothing and armed with a semi-automatic handgun.

The employee was not hurt. The amount of cash taken was undetermined.
