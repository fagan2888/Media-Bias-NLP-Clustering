With less than two weeks to go before Britain’s election, HSBC is performing a public service by threatening to leave London. The bank said Friday it is conducting a new review of whether to keep its headquarters in the U.K., the first time it has reconsidered that question since 2010. British tax policies will weigh heavily on the decision.
