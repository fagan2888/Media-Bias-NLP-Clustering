Rep. Pushes Effort To Nix ACA 'Belly Button' Tax

Law360, New York (April 16, 2015, 5:35 PM ET) -- U.S. Rep. Pat Tiberi, R-Ohio, strengthened his push on Thursday to repeal the Affordable Care Act’s so-called belly button tax, or reinsurance fee, by reintroducing legislation to nix the measure, which he says unnecessarily increases the cost of health care.



Under the ACA, insurance providers must pay a fee for each covered person — $63 in 2014 — to fund a government reinsurance program that pays insurance companies to offset high-cost enrollees. Last year’s fee raised $10 billion.
