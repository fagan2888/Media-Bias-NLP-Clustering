UPDATE 7:30 p.m.

According to the Florida Forest Service (FFS), both lanes on Back Beach Road have reopened.|

The fire is 100 percent contained, but smoke is still lingering around the roadway. Motorists are asked to drive with caution.

The cause of the fire is still under investigation.

Officials from the FFS say the gusty winds certainly helped spread the fire quickly Sunday afternoon.

---------------------------------------------------------------------

Crews are currently battling a fire at the west end of Panama City Beach.

The fire broke out Sunday afternoon at the intersection of Back Beach Road and Le Grand Drive.

Officials have shut down the road from Kelly Street to Downing Street.

That road could be closed for several hours.

No structures are endangered at this time.

We will have more information as it becomes available.
