A Panama City Beach minor is in the Department of Juvenile Justice Saturday after he was caught with felony amounts of marijuana in his bedroom.

Police discovered marijuana on 14 year old Brian Bruhmuller after a traffic stop. Bruhmuller was in the car at the time. During the course of the investigation, police say Bruhmuller gave statements indicating he was storing more in his bedroom.

A search revealed he had more than a quarter pound packaged for sale.

Police say there was also evidence of narcotic distribution. He's facing charges of possession of marijuana, intent to distribute, and possession of drug paraphernalia.
