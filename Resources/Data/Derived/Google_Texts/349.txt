Butler County Fatal Accident

A male teenager is dead and another with serious injuries after a pickup and semi collide on US 400 in Butler County.

The accident happened around 3:45 p.m. east of Augusta or three miles west of Leon.

A 2000 Chevrolet Silverado was driving west-bound when the driver entered the wrong side of the road and crashed head on into a semi.

The driver of the Silverado died and has been identified by the Kansas Highway Patrol as Leon Williams of Wichita, 19-years-old.

The semi truck rolled over in the ditch south of the road; the driver was taken to the hospital with serious injuries.

Both the semi truck driver and the driver of the Silverado were wearing seat belts.
