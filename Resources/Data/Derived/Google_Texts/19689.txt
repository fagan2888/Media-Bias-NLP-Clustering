Police say local authorities are responding to a hazardous materials incident in Howell (Photo: Amanda Oglesby)

police evacuated homes at Balsam Court in Pointe O' Woods after finding what they believed to be hazardous materials in a home during a first aid call.

Police, local fire departments and the Monmouth County Hazardous Materials Unit blocked the neighborhood Sunday morning and were seen entering the home while wearing blue latex gloves.

Howell Police notified the Monmouth County Prosecutor's Office Major Crimes Unit, the county Office of Emergency Management, the New Jersey State Police Bomb Squad and hazardous materials officers, among other agencies. The Howell Township Office of Emergency Management and fire departments from Howell, Adelphia and Southard Fire also responded to the neighborhood.
