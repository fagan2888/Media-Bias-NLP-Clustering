Richard Scott Coleman, 32, is accused of second degree murder in the shooting death of his brother, Andrew Brice Coleman, 34. (Photo: Pima County Sheriff's Dept.)

Authorities have identified a man killed on Friday night in the 7400 block of West Bopp Road as 34-year-old Andrew Brice Coleman of Peoria, Ariz.

They have arrested Coleman's brother Richard Scott Coleman, 32, according to a media release from the Pima County Sheriff's Department.

Deputies were called to the residence around 7:20 p.m. Friday. There, they found the victim, who had been shot, along with his brother, said Deputy Tom Peine, PCSD spokesman.

Richard Coleman is being charged with second degree murder.
