LITTLETON —A homeowner was involved in a shooting with two other men Sunday morning, according to the Jefferson County Sheriff’s Office.

One of the suspects was shot and taken to the hospital.

Deputies are searching for a man in gold Mustang. It is about 8 to 10 years old and may have damage and bullet holes in it.

The mobile crime lab and investigators were en route to the shooting scene at 10:05 a.m., deputies said. There is a large crime scene in the area, the sheriff’s office tweeted.
