Friday, September 11, 2015

Darick Isom, 36, was arrested on Thursday after he allegedly cut another man who tried to stop a fight he was having.

Police responded to the 3400 Block of Amnicola Highway and contacted the victim, Kelvon Watkins, who said that he found the defendant in an altercation with his cousin.

He said that he told the defendant to stop the argument and turned to walk away, but that Isom struck him in the face with a glass bottle or a knife.

The victim said that he was cut on the face and hand and lost consciousness immediately after.
