Crowdclinical.com tracks hospital trust and satisfaction through what has become a routine personal habit: Tweets on social networking platform Twitter. Analyzing over 400,000 tweets from September 2012 to October 2013, the site flagged messages that expressed an emotion one way or another and created an algorithm to tag them, creating a database that showed the overall patient experience based on the outpouring.
