COMMERCE CITY — Police are investigating after they discovered two people dead at a home in the 1900 of block of Joliet Circle.

Commerce City police Cmdr. Mark Morgan said a 62-year-old man and a 43-year-old woman were found dead of gunshot wounds when officers responded shortly after noon on Saturday. A third victim was taken to the hospital with non-life-threatening, non-gunshot wounds.

Police are not looking for a shooter or other suspect in connection with the deaths, Morgan said. “The community is safe,” he said.

The street is block off and police tape surrounds the house.

This is a developing story and will be updated as more information becomes available.
