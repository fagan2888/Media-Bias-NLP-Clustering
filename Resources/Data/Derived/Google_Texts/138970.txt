Some 26,400 Ford heavy duty trucks are being recalled because their seat belts may not be anchored in the correct position, reports The Detroit News.

The models include F-650 and F-750 trucks in model years 2011-2013 and 2015.

Ford is not aware of any accidents or injuries related to the issue, the Detroit News story said.

All of the trucks were built in Mexico. Ford announced last week that production of the trucks is shifting to Ohio.
