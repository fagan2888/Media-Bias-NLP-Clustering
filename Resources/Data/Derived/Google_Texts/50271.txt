HACKETTSTOWN — Hackettstown Police discovered a driver under the influence of marijuana after an officer stopped a car on Third Ave for failure to use a turn signal on Sunday, Jan. 19, at 11:17 p.m.

Police said that driver Jeffrey Prudente, 24, of Somerville was under the influence of the controlled dangerous substance and had a bag containing suspected marijunana in his poessions.

Passenger Carly Lorenz, 19, of Princeton Junction was also allegedly in the possesion of a bag containing suspected marijuana.

Both were charged with possesion of under 50 grams of marijunana, and Prudente was additionally charged with driving under the influence, reckless driving and failure to use a turn signal.

They were released pending a court appearance.
