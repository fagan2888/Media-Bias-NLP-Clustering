A study out this week finds that liberals and socialists drink considerably more alcohol than everyone else. As the authors of the study note, “Our findings are relatively consistent with the recent sociological studies showing that people with more socialist views tend to engage in more unhealthy behaviors such as excessive drinking.” But this only begs the question: do people become left because they drink too much, or drink too much because they’re leftists in the first place and are coping with the disastrous results by self-medicating? And left unanswered (heh) is the all-important question: do conservatives drink better booze than leftists? I’m certain I know the answer to this.
