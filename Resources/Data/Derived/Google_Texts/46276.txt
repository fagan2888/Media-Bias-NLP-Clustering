U.S. Coast Guard crews are searching for a missing diver off the coast of Captiva.



Coast Guard officials tell us they responded to a call about the missing diver around 2 p.m.

We're told a person was scuba diving near the USS Mohawk artificial reef -- 28 miles from Captiva. The diver never resurfaced.

Search crews have been in the area for several hours. A helicopter is aiding in the search.

Officials tell us weather and the current may play a role in finding the missing diver.
