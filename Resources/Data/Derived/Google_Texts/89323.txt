MANCHESTER — The state's official health insurance marketpplace, Access Health CT, is to host an informational and enrollment session on Saturday at Manchester Memorial Hospital.

The event is set for noon to 4 p.m. in the hospital's conference room at 71 Haynes St.

Access Health CT was developed to satisfy the requreiments of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act. The event will offer information on financial assistance and the affects of health care reform, according to a news release. People attending also may receive help in enrolling for health care coverage.

A checklist of information people should bring to the session is available at http://www.learn.accesshealthct.com.
