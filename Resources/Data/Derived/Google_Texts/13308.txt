A Montana tax board said Tuesday that real-estate developer Tim Blixseth, embattled founder of the ultra-luxurious Yellowstone Club ski and golf resort, owed the state $56 million in back taxes.

The Montana Tax Appeal Board ruled in favor of the state department of revenue that Mr. Blixseth failed to report all required income on his tax returns from 2002-06.
