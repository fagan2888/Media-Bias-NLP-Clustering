A Lafayette man suspected of shooting a 65 year old to death in his home on Kelly Drive was arrested Tuesday.

Joseph Lilly, 55, was apprehended by police following an arrest warrant. He is accused of shooting 64-year-old Paul Robertson, who was found dead in the doorway of his home on Kelly Drive in Lafayette.

Lilly is charged with first-degree murder, and was booked into the Lafayette Parish Correctional Center Tuesday without bond.
