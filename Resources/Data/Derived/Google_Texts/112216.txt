Three people were injured Saturday night in a boating accident on Lake Arlington, a MedStar spokesperson said.

Around 7:30 p.m., emergency crews responded to the 5700 block of Shoreline Circle where they three patients were already on shore.

Matt Zavadsky, a MedStar spokesman, said all three patients suffered minor injuries. They all declined transport and were treated and released at the scene.

No details about the boating accident were available.
