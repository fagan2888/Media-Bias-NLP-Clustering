Florence council meets Monday

During its regular meeting Monday evening, the Florence City Council will have a public hearing and consider first reading of an ordinance vacating a portion of Sylvanite Street.

The council also will elect a mayor pro-tem to replace former Councilman Mel Edwards who died unexpectedly June 26; consider a resolution approving a grant application with DOLA for the East Main Street water line, curb, gutter, sidewalk and drainage project; a resolution adopting the Eastern Fremont County Trails, Open Space and River Corridor Master Plan; and make appointments to the Historic Preservation Commission.

The meeting will be at 7 p.m. at the Florence Municipal Center, 600 W. Main St. in Florence.
