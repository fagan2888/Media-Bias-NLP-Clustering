Officers are investigating a report of bones being found on Monday near Lake Stanley Draper, police said.

Oklahoma City police said a person at the lake called police after finding bones. Investigators are not sure if the bones are human remains, Oklahoma City police Sgt. Ashley Peters said.

Oklahoma City police and the state medical examiner's office were at the scene Tuesday near Midwest Boulevard and Lake Stanley Draper Drive.
