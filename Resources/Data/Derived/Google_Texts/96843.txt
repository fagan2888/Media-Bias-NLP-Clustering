How to Watch

You can watch the keynote presentation too in several ways, by downloading Apple’s app from iTunes or following a live video stream here.

You’ll want to have Safari 4 or later version of the browser on at least OS X 10.6, Apple’s Mac operating system. On your iPhone and iPad, you need at least iOS 4.2. You can also stream on a second- or third-generation Apple TV with at least version 5.0.2.

On Windows, make sure you download the latest versions of Safari and QuickTime.
