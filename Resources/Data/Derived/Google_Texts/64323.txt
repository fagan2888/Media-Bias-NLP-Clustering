This year may be the most consequential one for trade agreements in a generation. It is all but certain to be the most combative—although America’s top negotiator is optimistic about the outcome.

Unless the negotiations unexpectedly run off the rails, a draft Trans-Pacific Partnership agreement should be on the table for congressional consideration by Labor Day.
