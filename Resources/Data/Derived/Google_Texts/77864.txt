Orthotic Co. Beats Claims That Audits Caused Stock Drop

Law360, New York (April 1, 2016, 8:07 PM ET) -- A Texas federal judge on Friday tossed allegations that an orthotics company lied to investors about the impact of federally mandated Medicare billing audits, saying the investors had not shown the national provider had wrongdoing in mind.



U.S. District Judge Sam Sparks dismissed the suit against Hanger Inc. and two of its executives that alleged they had covered up how increased audits for orthotics providers mandated by the Affordable Care Act, known as RAC audits, would affect the business.
