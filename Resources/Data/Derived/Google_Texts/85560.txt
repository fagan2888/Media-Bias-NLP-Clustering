An eighth grade student was pronounced dead Friday after he was found lying on the floor of a bathroom at Carson Middle School, officials say.

The boy was discovered unconscious in the bathroom just after noon. He was taken to Evans Hospital, where he was pronounced dead, according to a news release by Fort Carson.

“Fort Carson is saddened by the loss of one of our young family members, a student at Carson Middle School,” the Saturday news statement by Dorraine McNutt, spokeswoman for Fort Carson.

Fort Carson and school district news releases did not indicate the cause of the child’s death, according to a news release.

Counselors will be available to speak with students because of the incident.

“There is no danger or threat to the community at this time,” the news release says. “A thorough investigation is being completed into the events surrounding yesterday’s tragedy.”
