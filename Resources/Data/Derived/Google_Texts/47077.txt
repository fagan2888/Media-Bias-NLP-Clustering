A 25-year-old Summerville woman died in a three-vehicle accident just blocks away from her home Friday night.

Jasmine Reid was struck by a pickup truck at 10:30 p.m. Friday, according to Berkeley County Coroner Bill Salisbury.

Reid was turning left on North Main Street from Longstreet Street when she was hit, Salisbury said.

A third vehicle was also involved in the accident.

Salisbury said Reid was not wearing a seat belt and the accident happened not far from her home.

Reid died at the scene and her death was ruled accidental, Salisbury said.
