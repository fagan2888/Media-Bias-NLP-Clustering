A Greenville man is accused of physically hurting his wife and locking her in a closet on July 4th.

Greenville police say Quincey Wooten and his wife were involved in a domestic dispute at their home on the 1200 block of Park West Drive in Greenville. Police haven't said what triggered the argument.

Police received an emergency call from the woman around 10:50 p.m. Saturday. Police say the woman had a phone with her and used it to dial 911 while inside the closet she was forced into.

She received medical treatment at Vidant Medical Center for non-life-threatening injuries.

Wooten is charged with felony second degree kidnapping and assault on a female. He is currently being held in the Pitt County Detention Center without bond.
