Delmont, SD

From volunteering to donations of money and other needed items, there are several ways you can help the town of Delmont as they pick up the pieces left by an E-F2 tornado.

Previous Story: Delmont Accepting Volunteers Starting Friday

You can make financial donations by contributing to a fund set up at First State Banks in Delmont, Tripp, Geddes, and Armour.

Survivors are currently in need of non-perishable food items, household cleaning supplies, personal care items, can openers, and clean-up gear like gloves, shovels, and wheelbarrows.

Donations can be taken to the Donations Management Center at the Delmont American Legion. You are asked to enter Delmont from the east, via 284th Street.
