One person is dead following a crash on I-70.

The Kansas Highway Patrol confirms that a person has died, following a collision involving two semi trucks. The accident happened around 5:30 p.m. Saturday.

According to KHP records, Rodney Hongsermeier, was killed in the crash near Colby. Hongsermeier, 52, is from Nebraska.

A spokesperson says that both trucks caught on fire, and one of them burned to the ground. The highway had to be shut down for several hours, but opened up early Sunday morning.
