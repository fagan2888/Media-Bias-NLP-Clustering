EDITOR'S NOTE: This story has been corrected to report that Rebekah Jones was arrested on suspicion of attempted murder. The original story also misreported that she is from Denver.

A 39-year-old woman is facing an attempted-murder charge after being arrested New Year's Day in Lafayette, according to Boulder County Jail records.

Rebekah Jane Jones was arrested by Lafayette police Friday and booked into the jail early Saturday on suspicion of attempted second-degree murder, prohibited use of a weapon, child abuse, harassment, felony menacing and domestic violence, according to jail records.

Lafayette police on Sunday said the case is still under investigation and declined to provide any further information.

Jones is due to make her first court appearance at 1:30 p.m. Monday at the jail.
