Police in northern Vermont are investigating the death of a 13-year-old juvenile.

In a media release, Hardwick Chief of Police Aaron Cochran said the department and the Hardwick Rescue Squad were called to a Hardwick home at 9:07 a.m. on Friday for a report on an unresponsive juvenile.

The juvenile was pronounced dead at the scene.

An autopsy will be performed to determine the cause and manner of death.

The investigation remains active.
