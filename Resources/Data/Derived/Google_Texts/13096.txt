Planet Fitness members who have a PF Black Card membership may pay an additional tax this year as part of the Affordable Care Act, also known as Obamacare. The act imposes a tax on all tanning salons, and a Planet Fitness Black Card membership, which runs $19.99 per month compared to the club's basic $10 per month rate, includes tanning services.
