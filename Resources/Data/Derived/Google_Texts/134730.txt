Obama Signs ACA Tweak To Aid 'Small Employers' With Costs

Law360, New York (October 8, 2015, 9:15 PM ET) -- President Barack Obama on Wednesday night signed a bipartisan bill aimed at protecting small and midsize businesses from potential health care premium increases under the Affordable Care Act, according to the bill’s original sponsor in the U.S. Senate.
