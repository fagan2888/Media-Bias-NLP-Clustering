Police have identified a woman found dead inside her northwest Oklahoma City home.

According to police, at about 2:29 p.m. on Friday, February 26, officers responded to a trouble unknown call at a home in the 3000 block of N. Robinson. When officers arrived, they found the body of 54-year-old Joyce Raelyn Grant inside the home.

Police said Grant had injuries consistent with homicide. Her vehicle was missing from her home, but was later found unoccupied near N.E. 23rd and Broadway.

There have been no arrests in this case and police do not have a suspect description. If you have any information, call the Homicide tip-line at (405)-297-1200.

This is Oklahoma City's 12th homicide of 2016, according to police.
