Crimefighters of Louisiana Inc. plans a joint forum Tuesday night (Sept. 1) for candidates for Jefferson Parish president. NOLA.com will be covering the event live in the comments stream of this post.

The race features Kenner Mayor Mike Yenni, who aims to project youthful energy, and Parish Councilman Elton Lagasse, who emphasizes decades of experience in parish government. Incumbent John Young is leaving office to run for lieutenant governor.

Our live coverage begins at 6 p.m. Readers are encourages to join the comments stream with questions and observations.
