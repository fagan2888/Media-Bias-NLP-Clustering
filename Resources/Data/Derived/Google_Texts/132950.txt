A car crashed into a home in SW Oklahoma City Wednesday afternoon.

The Oklahoma City Fire Department responded to the home at 8701 Hillcrest Dr. just before 4:00 p.m.

Authorities said the incident occurred after an altercation in the street. The driver of the vehicle accidentally backed into the home attempting to get away from the altercation.

The driver is in police custody at this time. His name has not been released.

No one was home at the time of the crash and no injuries have been reported.
