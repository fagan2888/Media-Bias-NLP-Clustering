× Get Covered Tenn to help residents during open enrollment period

NASHVILLE, Tenn. — There are only four days until the open enrollment period of the Affordable Care Act begins, and one organization wants to help you get ready.

Get Covered Tenn will once again be helping people go through the different options you have when it comes to getting enrolled.

The assistance will be free and in-person, and can be utilized by anyone living in Tennessee.

It is recommended that everyone who is uninsured, underinsured or has questions go ahead and set up an appointment with an enrollment adviser.

You can log on to Healthcare.gov or the Get Covered Tenn website to schedule an appointment, or call (844) 644-5443 for assistance.

Open enrollment will begin on November 1, 2015 and will run until January 31, 2016.
