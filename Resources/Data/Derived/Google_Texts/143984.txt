JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - A woman was taken to the hospital after she was shot Saturday afternoon, according to the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office.

Investigators said the shooting happened at about 3:52 p.m. on Indian Lakes Court on the Westside.

When police arrived, they found a woman who was shot. The location of the gunshot wound was not clear, but her injuries are said to be non life-threatening.

Her identity has not been released.
