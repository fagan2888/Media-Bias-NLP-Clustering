Entrapment results from car accident on Hwy 17

Photo Source: AP

According to officials, the Awendaw-McClellanville fire department responded to an accident on Highway 17 involving two vehicles resulting in an entrapment shortly before 10 p.m. on Saturday.



The report states that the incident took place on the 7400 Block of Highway 17 near Semper Fi Road.



According to authorities, one individual was transported to MUSC.



The Charleston County Sheriff's office is currently investigating the cause of the accident.
