Dr. Ben Carson is never a man of few words. Today on “NewsOne Now” with Roland Martin, the neurosurgeon was in the hot seat, to address his latest controversial remark: comparing Obamacare to slavery.

The health insurance law, said Carson, “places the government at the pinnacle. Now it’s placed on a road to control your health … and I think most of us are asleep at the wheel. We don’t realize what’s happening.”
