Quian Davis works out at the Vendetta MME Gym in Millville. He is training for his second professional match at the Claridge in Atlantic City on Saturday December 5th. Thursday December 3 2015 Quian Davis of Millville was the former all-time leading rusher at Buena Regional High School for football and also had a short stint in baseball's minor leagues with the Miami Marlins. He now is a professional boxer training with Irving Muse Sr. (The Press of Atlantic City / Ben Fogletto)
