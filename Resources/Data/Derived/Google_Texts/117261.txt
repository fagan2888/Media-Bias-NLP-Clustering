Comedian Tracy Morgan has sued Wal-Mart Stores Inc. for negligence, after one of its truck drivers crashed into Mr. Morgan's limousine van last month, killing one passenger and injuring three.

The lawsuit, filed in U.S. District Court in New Jersey, claims the driver of the Wal-Mart truck was speeding when he fell asleep at the wheel and crashed into the entertainer's vehicle after being awake for more than 24 hours.
