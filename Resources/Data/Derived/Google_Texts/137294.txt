A motorbike wreck in Aliquippa left two people injured Sunday afternoon, according to a Beaver County emergency dispatcher.

The accident happened around 4:30 p.m. at Morrell Park off Maratta Road.

One victim was critically injured and transported to a Pittsburgh hospital in an emergency helicopter. EMS units transported the other to a city hospital in an ambulance.

The dispatcher did not know which hospitals the victims were taken to.
