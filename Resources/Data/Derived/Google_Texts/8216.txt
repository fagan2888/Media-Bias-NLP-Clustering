ADEN (Reuters) - At least five Yemeni policemen were injured on Sunday when they foiled a suicide car bomb attack on the airport in the southern city of Aden, police said.

They said the policemen opened fire at a car that was traveling at high speed towards the airport. It exploded near the checkpoint wounding five policemen.
