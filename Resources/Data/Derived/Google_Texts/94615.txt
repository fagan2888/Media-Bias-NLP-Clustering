Des Moines, IA

Classic car enthusiasts converge in Iowa this weekend for the annual Des Moines Concours d'Elegance.

The premier automotive exhibition features more than 100 classic cars from years past, including a 1912 Star Car Packard Touring bus.

People attending the event say they have always had a love for vintage vehicles, adding it reminds them a much simpler time in life.
