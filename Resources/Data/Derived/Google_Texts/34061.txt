DOD Rule Expands Tricare To Reserve Members Under 60

Law360, New York (January 2, 2015, 7:20 PM ET) -- The U.S. Department of Defense has published a final rule that will permit reserve members under the age of 60 who qualify for nonregular retirement to purchase insurance through U.S. military health care provider Tricare.
