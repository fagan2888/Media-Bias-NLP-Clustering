A Derby man, suffering from dementia, who disappeared Saturday morning has been found.

Derby Police say 84-year-old Floyd Tiffany was found by police in Enid, Oklahoma. Floyd was not hurt and will be reunited with his family.

Derby Police are asking for the public's help in locating a missing 84-year-old man.

Floyd E. Tiffany was last seen around 10 a.m. Saturday at his home in the 200 block of West Tall Tree in Derby. Tiffany left driving a 2001 metallic beige Chrysler Concorde with Kansas license plate 087 BDK.

Mr. Tiffany has dementia and will need help returning home. He is 6 feet tall, weighing 200 pounds and has grey hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a purple turtleneck, blue jeans and either white tennis shoes or brown shoes.

The last time Mr. Tiffany left under similar circumstances 6 months ago, he was found at Towne East Mall. Anyone who sees Mr. Tiffany should call 911 or contact Derby Police at (316) 788-1557.
