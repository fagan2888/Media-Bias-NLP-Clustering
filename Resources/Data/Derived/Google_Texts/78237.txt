Slide the City, a 1,000 foot water slide traveling around the country, came to Chattanooga on Saturday.

The family-friendly slip-and-slide water party included music, food and drinks.

The event is traveling the country, with many dates sold out. The Chattanooga stop was held in downtown on 5th Street, between Broad Street and Lookout Street.

Tickets ranged from $10-$55.
