James J. DiNicolantonio Contributor

James J. DiNicolantonio, PharmD, is a cardiovascular research scientist at Saint Luke’s Mid America Heart Institute. Dr. DiNicolantonio is currently completing his third-year as a cardiovascular research scientist at Saint Luke’s Mid America Heart Institute, Kansas City, Missouri. Dr. DiNicolantonio’s research interests focus on cardiovascular health and disease, including topics such as diabetes, obesity, hypertension, insulin resistance, lipids, antithrombotics, anticoagulants, as well as nutrition (with particular focus on refined sugars and salt) and nutritional supplements. He is the author or co-author of more than 150 medical publications, encompassing opinion pieces, review articles and systematic reviews and meta-analyses.
