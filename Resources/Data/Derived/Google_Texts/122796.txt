Joe Biden has certainly put his foot in his mouth more than his share of times. But his Oscar appearance was pitched just right, as he spoke about sexual assault. This could have gone very wrong, but he delivered it just right.

“I’m asking you to pledge: I will intervene in situations when consent has not or cannot be given,” he said. “Let’s change the culture.
