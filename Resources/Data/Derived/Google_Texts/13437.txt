PARIS—French car maker PSA Peugeot-Citroën SA said Thursday Thierry Peugeot will leave the company's supervisory board and be replaced by another representative from one of the family's holding companies.

Marie-Helene Roncoroni will replace Mr. Peugeot as a representative of the holding company Etablissements Peugeot Freres, Peugeot said in a statement.
