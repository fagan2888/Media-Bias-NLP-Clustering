× Coach in Cullman county accused of having sex with a minor, charged with rape

CULLMAN, Ala. (WHNT) – According to our news partners at Al.com Spencer Jackson Byrd, a volunteer basketball coach for Fairview High School is being investigated by the Cullman County Sheriff’s Department.

Law enforcement concluded through that investigation that Byrd had sex with a minor. Byrd has been charged with second-degree rape, sodomy and enticing a child.
