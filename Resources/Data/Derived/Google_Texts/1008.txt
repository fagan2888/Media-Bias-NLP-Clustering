Two people were evacuated after an apartment fire in the 2300 block of N.W. 12th St. in Oklahoma City, overnight.

According to the fire department, firefighters arrived at a two-story apartment complex overnight with flames showing on the first floor. Firefighters entered the rear of the structure and made their way to the room that caught fire. Firefighters were able to contain the fire to just that one room, but two people were evacuated from the building.

Residents claimed that somebody threw an object in their window, which started the fire. Fire Investigators were called to the scene to investigate. This fire is still under investigation.

No injuries were reported.
