LOVELAND —Officials announced Elizabeth Anderson as Loveland’s new Parks and Recreation director Wednesday. She was among seven finalists.

Anderson, who is the operations manager for the city of Sacramento’s Parks and Recreation Department in California, will assume her new role in Loveland on Feb. 17. She previously worked as the city of Flagstaff’s Parks and Recreation director in Arizona and as the community enrichment services director from 2005 to 2013. Before that, from 2002 to 2004, she was Yuba City Parks and Recreation director in California.

“She was very well-received by everyone in the whole selection process,” Loveland City Manager Bill Cahill said.
