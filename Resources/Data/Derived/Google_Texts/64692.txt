Dakota Andersen | Frederick Lemons | Jordan Leffel

Two men suspected several violent crimes in Hutchinson earlier this year have been bound over for trial in one of the cases.

Dakota Anderson, 19, (left) is charged with aggravated kidnapping, two counts of aggravated robbery, one count of aggravated battery and two counts of aggravated burglary. Frederick Lemens, 20, (middle) is charged with aggravated kidnapping, aggravated robbery, aggravated burglary and two counts of aggravated battery.

The Hutch Post reports both were bound over for trial in the invasion of a 56-year-old man's home. They are also accused in a second home invasion involving a 91-year-old man.

Jordan Leffel, 19, (right) who waived his right to a preliminary hearing, faces a total of eight counts including aggravated kidnapping, aggravated robbery and aggravated burglary, the Post reports.

Brendan Jones, 17, is also charged with his case in juvenile court.

The home invasions happened in April and May in the 3600 block of Rockwood Drive and the 3400 block of North Waldron. The four suspects are also accused of robbing the Family Affair Novelty & Gift Shop on East 4th Avenue.

Police conducted a number of search warrants in association the crimes and allegedly found items from all the crimes leading to the four arrests.
