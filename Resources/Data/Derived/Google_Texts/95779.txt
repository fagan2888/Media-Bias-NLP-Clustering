A Southport man is dead after a crash early Sunday morning in Bay County.

According to Florida Highway Patrol officials, 33-year-old James Thorndyke was standing by his motorcyle on the east shoulder of Highway 77 and Spikes Road around 6 a.m. Sunday when he was struck by a car.

The driver, 19-year old Brent Taylor was charged with DUI Manslaughter and was taken to the Bay County Jail.

Thorndyke was pronounced dead at the scene.
