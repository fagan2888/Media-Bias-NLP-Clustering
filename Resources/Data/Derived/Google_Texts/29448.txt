There were few "likes" but plenty of angry emoji when Gizmodo reported earlier this month that Facebook workers were suppressing conservative news stories in its "trending" section. According to the article, which sourced an anonymous former journalist who worked on the Facebook project, news curators would prevent stories about the right-wing CPAC gathering or Republican politicians from appearing in the prominent section at the top of every user's page.
