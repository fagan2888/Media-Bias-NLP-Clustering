October 20, 2013

A Randolph, Kansas teen died in a one vehicle accident early Saturday. The accident happened two miles northeast of Leonardville in Riley County.

Three teens were in a car that went into a skid. The car entered the ditch, struck a rock wall then overturned. One of the passengers was ejected.

Trevor Redding, 17, of Randolph, died in the crash. The driver, Garrett Olson of Leonardville and another passenger Lane Howe of Riley were taken Mercy Health Center for treatment of their injuries.
