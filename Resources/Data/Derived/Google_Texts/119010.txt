Blunt, SD

A three-vehicle crash left one woman dead and five people hurt Friday.

Authorities say 79-year-old Marcella Larsen of Huron died when the car she was riding in collided nearly head-on with another vehicle at the intersection of US Highway 83 and US Highway 14, about 14 miles east of Pierre.

A third car was also hit by the vehicle Larsen was a passenger in. The South Dakota Highway Patrol is still investigating.
