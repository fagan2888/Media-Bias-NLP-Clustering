A Wall man is accused of sexually assaulting two children he knew who were younger than 10 years old at the time.

Gavin Alexander, 40, was arrested Thursday, according to a release from Ocean County Prosecutor’s Office. The release said the alleged sexual assaults took place in Toms River.

Alexander is charged with aggravated sexual assault, sexual assault and endangering the welfare of a child, the release said. He’s being held at Ocean County Jail, Toms River on a $500,000 bail.
