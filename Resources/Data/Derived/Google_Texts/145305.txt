The NONA Theater grand opening was Feb. 10,1948, because it featured “You Gotta Stay Happy” and that was the year it debuted. The flick starred Jimmy Stewart, Joan Fontaine and also Eddie Albert (whom for another generation altogether, is known for the TV sitcom “Green Acres” co-starring Eva Gabor) and a chimpanzee — if the movie poster is any indication. The NONA was owned by Milton Guidry.
