A woman crossing the street midblock was struck and killed by a motorist who remained at the scene, police said.

The driver showed no signs of impairment, Phoenix Police Sgt. Vince Lewis said.

The accident happened at 9:45 p.m. Saturday in Ahwatukee.

Lewis said a Mazda Miata was heading south on 48th Street from Ray Road when it struck the pedestrian.

The woman was rushed to the hospital and later pronounced dead.

Her name has not been released pending notification of family and relatives.

Phoenix fire officials said she was in her 60s.
