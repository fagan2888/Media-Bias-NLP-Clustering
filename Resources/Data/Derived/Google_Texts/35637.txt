Wichita police are searching for the suspect in an overnight stabbing that left a man with serious injuries.

Sgt. Kevin Tronsgard said some people found a man in his 20s with stab wounds lying on the sidewalk near Central and Grove around 7 a.m. Saturday. The man told officers he was stabbed Friday night during an altercation in the area.

EMS took the man to the hospital where he underwent surgery. He was last listed in serious condition.

Tronsgard said the victim didn't provide much information on what happened, but investigators hope to speak with him further at the hospital.

Police did not provide a description of the suspect. Anyone with information is asked to call 911.
