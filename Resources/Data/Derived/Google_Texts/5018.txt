The Department of Defense announced the passing of a 31-year-old Fort Bragg soldier from Bay City, Michigan.

Sgt. 1st Class Michael Cathcart died Friday of wounds received from small arms fire during dismounted combat operations.

He was in the Kunduz Province.

He served two tours of duty in Iraq in 2003 and 2005 and volunteered for special forces in 2007.

Cathcart was a part of the 3rd Battalion, 3rd Special Forces Group at Fort Bragg.

The Bay City Times reports there will be a memorial at Fort Bragg with a service in Bay City to follow.

Cathcart was well-decorated with three Bronze Star medals and a Purple Heart. A fourth Bronze Star medal was posthumously awarded to him by the U.S. Army.
