Spearfish, SD

A world-renowned photographer will be speaking in Spearfish at Black Hills State University.

The university is hosting its annual Canon Explorer of Light event, a free and public lecture featuring an esteemed photographer associated with the Canon Explorer of Light Group.

This year's speaker will be Jennifer Wu, an internationally known landscape photographer that specializes in the night sky.

Along with the lecture, the BHSU photography department will host an open house featuring photography from students.

The event is Thursday, September 24 at 7 p.m at the Clare and Josef Meier Recital Hall.
