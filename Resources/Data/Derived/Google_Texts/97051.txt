Julissa Trevino reports:

An Arlington man died just past 11 p.m. Saturday after colliding with a vehicle at the intersection of Arkansas Lane and Carter Drive in Arlington.

The man has been identified as 25-year-old Stephen Hall.

The accident happened after the car turned south onto Carter Drive and struck the motorcycle, which had been traveling east on Arkansas Lane, said Arlington Police Department spokesperson Tiara Richard.

Police responded to the scene of the accident at 10:36 p.m. after multiple witnesses called 911, said dispatch supervisor Ruben Garcia.

Hall was transported to the Medical Center of Arlington and died shortly after.

The driver of the vehicle has not been identified, but initial 911 reports indicate he or she was not arrested.

The accident is still under investigation.
