A missing 16-year-old girl was found safe Sunday and no foul play is suspected, Tempe police said..

Alize Romero was last seen Friday, officers said. She may have been with an unknown man, according to police.

Authorities never believed she was in any danger but added it was unusual for her to be gone so long without contacting anyone.
