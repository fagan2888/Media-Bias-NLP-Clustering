Covering more of California

Photo: Paul Chinn, The Chronicle Rich Manuzon sets up a Covered California health care coverage...

California has been a national success story when it comes to implementing the Affordable Care Act. While other states — and, for a time, the federal government — were fumbling their website rollouts and dealing with political pushback from their own citizens and legislators, California was calmly setting up its health care exchange and expanding its Medi-Cal rolls. Following the long, agonizing Washington battle to achieve passage of the Affordable Care Act, Covered California (the state administration’s offshoot) seemed to be one of the nation’s brightest stars.
