Center Seat: Senator Kelly Ayotte (R-NH)

New Hampshire Senator Kelly Ayotte will join Bret and the panel of Katie Pavlich, Kirsten Powers and Charles Krauthammer for another edition of Center Seat.

We’ll talk to Senator Ayotte about the budget deal, particularly her effort to find alternative savings in the budget so that retirement benefits for current and future retirees remain intact.

Along with the budget, we’ll talk to Senator Ayotte about Obamacare and foreign policy, particularly the nuclear deal with Iran.
