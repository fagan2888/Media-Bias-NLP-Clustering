Lead, SD

A Fort Collins, CO man was killed in a single-motorcycle crash near Lead on Friday.

At about 6:10 p.m., a 46-year-old man was driving a 2005 Harley Davidson motorcycle east on U.S. Highway 14A one mile south of Lead.

The driver crashed at a curve and was separated from his motorcycle. He was transported to Deadwood Regional Hospital where he died from his injuries.

The name of the victim was not released pending notification of his family.

The South Dakota Highway Patrol said that alcohol is not believed to be a factor. The driver was not wearing a helmet.
