× Police investigate attempted bank robbery in Manchester Township

Northern York County Regional Police say a white male suspect attempted to rob an M&T Bank in Manchester Township Thursday afternoon.

Police say the suspect entered the bank located at 425 Loucks Road, just before 12:30 p.m, approached a teller station with a note demanding money. When the teller hesitated in fulfilling the demands of the note, the suspect fled empty handed, police said.

The suspect is being described as a white male, 5’6″ to 5’8″ tall, thin build with no facial hair, wearing a dark-colored hat with contrasting bill, grey jacket with black sleeves and hood, and blue jeans.

Anyone with any information and can who identify the male is asked to call the Northern York County Regional Police Department at 717-292-3647.
