An anti-freeze leak was reported Thursday night at a Costco in Westlake Village, officials said.

The leak was reported about 5 or 6 p.m. at 5700 Lindero Canyon Road.

The leak came from one of Costco's coolers, according to Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department Lost Hills Station officials.

Employees and shoppers were evacuated by the Los Angeles County Fire Department, officials said. No further details were available.
