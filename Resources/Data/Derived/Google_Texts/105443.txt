One who died was in band Fated Endeavor

The mosh pit clears for Chris Bevacqua of Fated Endeavor. at the Stone Pony on Sunday, Nov. 22. (Photo: Chris Jordan)

After a crash that killed two people, a Woodbridge man was issued a summons for careless driving.

Nishank Shaw, 24, was cited, according to State Police Sgt. Jeff Flynn.

“There’s no criminal charges pending," Flynn said.

Rob Critelli, 21, of Summit, and Christopher Calderone, 20, of Brick, were killed in a three-car crash on the New Jersey Turnpike in Woolwich Nov. 9, according to a report by the Associated Press.
