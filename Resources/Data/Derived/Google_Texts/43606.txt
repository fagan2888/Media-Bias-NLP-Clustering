Emergency crews are on the scene of a rollover crash with one person reported to be trapped in a vehicle on Interstate 35 in Edmond.

First responders were called to the scene on the northbound side of I-35, near the exit to E. 2nd St. According to scanner traffic, one person was trapped in the rolled vehicle and had to be freed by rescuers.

The crash involved a white SUV and a semi. The driver of the SUV was transported from the scene by ambulance in unknown condition. The cause of the crash has not yet been determined.

Authorities have shut down all northbound lanes at E. 2nd St. while crews work the scene. Traffic is being diverted off I-35 at E. 15th St.
