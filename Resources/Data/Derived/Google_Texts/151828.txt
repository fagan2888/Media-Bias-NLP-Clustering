Amanda Arlene Parsley, 36 of California, was identified by Detectives from the St. Mary’s County Sheriff’s Office, Vice/Narcotics Division as a distributor of Oxycodone.

Parsley was indicted by St. Mary’s County Grand Jurors and charged, with “Distribution of Oxycodone” and “Conspiring to Distribute Oxycodone”.

She is currently awaiting an arraignment date in the Circuit Court on October 17th, 2014.
