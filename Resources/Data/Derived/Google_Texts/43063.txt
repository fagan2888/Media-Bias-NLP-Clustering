Sioux Falls, SD

Food, fun, bowling and all for a good cause.

The Strikes for Special Olympics fundraiser brings teams of special athletes and supporters out for some bowling fun. The event helps to raise money and support for the special olympic activities which allows seventeen hundred athletes take part in throughout the year.

"We're raising money for funds for like going to Pierre, going to Rapid City for softball tournaments." Special Olympics Athlete Derek Smith said.

This is the first year of Strikes for Special Olympics and organizers say they hope to host more Strikes events across the state next year.
