John M. Orobono Jr.

Jenny Worob

Mr. Steven Worob and the beloved Mrs. Brenda Worob of Morrisville, Pa. are pleased to announce the engagement of their daughter, Jenny Worob to John M. Orobono Jr., son of John and Karen Orobono of Holland, Pa.

Jenny is a graduate of Conwell Egan Catholic High School in Bensalem, Pa. and Kutztown University and is a sales representative for Glaxo Smith Kline.

John is a graduate of Holy Ghost Preparatory School in Bensalem, Pa. and Lehigh University and is a Partner at Gilbert, Wilson & Hunter LLP in Newtown, Pa.

Last summer, right before the 4th of July fireworks, Johnny popped the question. This summer, they will marry. Their reception will be in Philadelphia and they will honeymoon in Italy. Afterwards, the couple will reside in Trevose, Pa.
