Timebends: A Life

By Arthur Miller (1987)

1. The memoirs of one of America’s greatest playwrights have many of the qualities of his drama. At their heart is the scene in which Miller, newly married to Marilyn Monroe, is subpoenaed to appear before the House Un-American Activities Committee.
