The teenager accused of snatching an iPad from a toddler has now been charged as an adult. (Published Wednesday, Aug. 26, 2015)

New information has been released on the teenager investigators said was caught on camera snatching an iPad out of the hands of a toddler.

Aaron Sapp, 16, was in Broward County Bond Court on Wednesday, where he was charged as an adult.

Prosecutors said Sapp was caught on surveillance video a month ago, robbing the boy at a salon in Tamarac.

Sapp is facing strong armed robbery charges. He's due back in court on Monday.
