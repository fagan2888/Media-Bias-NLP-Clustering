One person has died after being hit by a vehicle Friday night near the intersection of Bardstown Road and Hurstbourne Parkway.

Donald Ray Benningfield, 68, died around 10 p.m. at the scene from multiple blunt force injuries, according to an email from deputy coroner Larry Carroll. The accident occurred at 5300 Bardstown Road.

The man was crossing east across Bardstown Road when he was struck by an unknown vehicle headed north, said Louisville Metro Police spokesman Dwight Mitchell. He said the vehicle continued going north and that police are looking for the vehicle involved in the accident.

Louisville Metro Police are investigating. Funeral arrangements are pending.
