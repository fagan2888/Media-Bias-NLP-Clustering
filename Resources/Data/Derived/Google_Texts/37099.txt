Amid the debate of whether President Obama should hold off on appointing a replacement judge after Antonin Scalia passed away, the president has been moving forward.

Related: Debate heats up over replacement of late Supreme Court Justice

President Obama has mentioned Iowa judge Jane Kelly as a possible replacement, according to a report by KCRG.

Kelly is one of 10 judges serving in the 8th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals. She was appointed in April of 2013 and serves out of Cedar Rapids.
