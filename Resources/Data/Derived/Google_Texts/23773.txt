Trinity Healthcare

Matt Floyd is the founder, president and CEO Trinity Healthcare Staffing Group based in Florence that was recently ranked, for the fourth time since the business began in 1999, to the Inc. 5000. Rankings for the current list are compiled based on a private companies revenue from 2009 to 2012. The company first got on the list in 2004 when the list was still just 500 companies and has reached it in 2008 and 2009. This year the company ranked 2,519 with revenue growth of 141 percent.
