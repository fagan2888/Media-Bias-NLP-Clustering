People started lining up early Friday in Tulsa for some free dental work.

The first 100 people at the event at Perfect Smile near 53rd and Lewis got a free extraction, filling or teeth cleaning.

Friday's event was part of Tulsa's 3rd annual "Dentistry From The Heart" program. "Dentistry From The Heart" has grown to include over 200 annual events nationwide and provided more than 45,000 people with free dental care services.
