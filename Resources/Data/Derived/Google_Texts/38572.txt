Two men wanted in connection with a shooting at a Canton store that killed a 13-year-old boy and injured the boy's father have been captured by U.S. marshals.



Tony Terrell Clark, 34, and his nephew, Teanota Clark, 19, were arrested about 10 a.m. Monday in Dallas, Canton police said.



Police have been searching for the men since the Oct. 27 robbery and shooting at Fat Boy on Roby Street. Mrhad Saeed died of a gunshot wound to the head. His father, Fahd Saeed, was shot in the stomach, police said.
