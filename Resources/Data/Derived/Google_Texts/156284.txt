On Thursday, July 30, 2015, the White House and AIDS.gov will live stream the release of the National HIV/AIDS Strategy: Updated to 2020 during an all-day event in Atlanta, Georgia. Even if you can’t attend the launch event, digital tools are a great way to get involved!

Tweet your questions to @AIDSgov in advance of the July 30 live stream. Join the conversation using #NHAS2020 and #HIV 2020.

Get up-to-the-minute news on the roll-out of the update by following AIDS.gov’s blog, Twitter, and Facebook.

This is a national strategy, not a Federal one. This is your Strategy. This is our Strategy. Get involved and encourage others to join the discussion with ideas about how, together, we can end the HIV epidemic in the United States.
