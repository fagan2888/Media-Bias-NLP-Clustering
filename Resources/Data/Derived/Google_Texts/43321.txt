Officials are looking to determine a cause of a fire that damaged an apartment complex Sunday.

Clarksville and Jeffersonville firefighters are on the scene of a fire at an apartment complex in Clarksville, Indiana, according to a WHAS report.

The Meadows at Green Tree apartment complex is located on the 1400 block of Marlowe Drive.

The status of any injuries is currently unknown.
