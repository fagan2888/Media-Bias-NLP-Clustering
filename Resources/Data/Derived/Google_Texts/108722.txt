A fire engulfed a lumber yard in east Fort Worth this morning.

Fort Worth Fire responded to the 2500 block of East Lancaster Avenue around 5:30 a.m. No injuries were reported as of 8:40 a.m., fire officials said in a news release.

Officials restricted train access to nearby tracks and closed off East Lancaster, near the fire, to traffic. They said they plan to reopen the area by 10 a.m.

The cause of the fire remains under investigation.
