An overturned tractor trailer caused major delays Saturday afternoon at the intersection of Back Beach Road and Richard Jackson Boulevard.

According to officials on scene, the driver of the tractor trailer was trying to take a right onto Richard Jackson Boulevard, when a tire blew, and then the tractor flipped on its side.

The accident caused traffic delays in both directions on Back Beach Road, and drivers could not travel south on Richard Jackson Boulevard as well.

Officials say no one was injured when the tractor trailer overturned.
