Bankrupt MedLab To Sell Units To LabCorp For $10.5M

Law360, Wilmington (January 28, 2014, 7:34 PM ET) -- Bankrupt Laboratory Partners Inc., also known as MedLab, said in court papers Tuesday that it had landed a stalking horse for the planned auction of two of its Indiana-based units, stating that Laboratory Corp. of America Holdings would set the floor at $10.5 million.
