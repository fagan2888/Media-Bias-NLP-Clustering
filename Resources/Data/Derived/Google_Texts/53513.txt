A Diamond Shamrock was robbed just before 11:30 p.m. Saturday.

Officials say a male suspect entered the business at 3290 Austin Bluffs Parkway with a semi-automatic pistol and demanded cash. A store clerk as well as one customer were present at the time of the robbery and were not injured.

The suspect left the scene on foot, headed eastbound, with an undisclosed amount of money.

According to police, the man was wearing a black ski mask with dark sunglasses and was in all black clothing.

Officials searched the area but were unable to locate the suspect.

The incident is currently under investigation.
