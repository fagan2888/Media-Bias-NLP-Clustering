Jeff Daneff has been named chief financial officer at Terre Haute Regional Hospital.

A native of Omaha, Neb., Daneff earned his undergraduate degree in business administration from Creighton University, where he is expected to complete his master's of business administration, master's of finance and master's of business analytics and intelligence degrees in 2015.

Before joining HCA and Terre Haute Regional, Daneff served as the assistant CFO and controller at Oro Valley Hospital and Northwest Medical Center in Tucson, Ariz., part of Community Health System. Prior to that, he was a senior analyst in CFO Support Services for Alegent Creighton Health System in Omaha.
